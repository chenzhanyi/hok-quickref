# Flash / Flicker Combos

## What Is a Flash Combo?
Using Flicker (Flash) during an ability's cast animation to reposition its effect. This creates plays the enemy can't react to.

## Hero-Specific Flash Combos

### Angela
- **Ultimate + Flash:** Flash during Inferno Beam channel to reposition without interrupting

### Lu Bu
- **Flash + Ultimate:** Flash during Ultimate cast to reposition the landing zone — makes it nearly undodgeable

### Diaochan
- **Skill 2 + Flash:** Extends dash range to reach enemies who think they're safe

### Zhang Fei
- **Ultimate + Flash:** Flash behind the enemy before roaring — knocks them INTO your team instead of away

### General
- **Flicker over walls:** The most basic and essential Flash use. Know which walls are flashable

## Flicker Cooldown: 120 seconds
Track enemy Flicker cooldowns. If enemy ADC Flickers at 5:00, they won't have it again until 7:00. Punish this window.
