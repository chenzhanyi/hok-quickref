# Animation Canceling (Attack Reset)

## What Is It?
Animation canceling is interrupting the recovery frames after an ability or basic attack, allowing your next action to start sooner. This increases your DPS and makes your movements smoother.

## Common Animation Cancels

### Lu Bu
- Skill 1 can be cast **during** Ultimate leap animation — damage lands instantly on arrival

### Luna
- Auto-attack → immediate dash (Ultimate) cancels the auto recovery animation
- The timing window is tight (~0.3s) but essential for maximum DPS

### Li Bai
- Auto-attack jungle camps 4 times to pre-stack passive
- Skill 1 → Ultimate → Skill 1 return = hit-and-run with zero counterplay

### Basic Attack Reset (All Heroes)
Attack → Move (cancels recovery frames) → Attack
This rhythm should be second nature for every player.

## Practice Method
In training mode, practice: Attack → immediate move → Attack on a stationary target. Your hero should smoothly alternate between attacking and moving with no wasted frames.
