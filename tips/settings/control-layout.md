# Control Layout Guide

## Essential Settings to Enable
- **Target Lock Mode:** Advanced — shows hero/minion/tower attack buttons
- **Attack Minion Button:** ON — prevents accidentally hitting heroes when last-hitting
- **Attack Tower Button:** ON — crucial for taking objectives in crowded fights
- **Avatar Lock:** ON — shows enemy hero icons for precise targeting
- **Auto-Attack:** OFF — you want full control over when you attack
- **Skill Cancel:** Slide to cancel — most forgiving cancel method

## Pro Player Layout
- Place the **Attack Minion** button near your right thumb
- Keep the **Attack Tower** button adjacent
- Move **Summoner Spell** to easy-reach position
- Minimize button size slightly for more screen visibility
- Enable **edge screen rejection** to prevent accidental swipes

## Per-Role Adjustments
- **Junglers:** Prioritize Smite button placement
- **Supports:** Enlarge heal/shield buttons
- **Marksmen:** Attack minion button is most important
