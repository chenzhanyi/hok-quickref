# Pro Control & Layout Settings

## Target Lock Mode — Must Switch to "Advanced"
The default "Free Attack" mode is unreliable: when you want to hit a tower, your hero often auto-targets nearby minions instead.

**Fix:** Switch to **Free Attack Mode (Advanced)**. This unlocks two extra small buttons — **Last-Hit** and **Push Tower**. These buttons are the difference between a missed objective and a clutch tower takedown during dives.

## Auto Chase Distance — Set to "Close-Range"
Default "Far" range causes your hero to automatically walk forward when a target leaves attack range. This is deadly for marksmen and assassins — you'll unintentionally walk into the enemy team.

**Fix:** Set **Auto Chase Distance** to **Close-Range**. Your hero will stop attacking when the target exits range, leaving movement fully under your left-thumb control.

## Skill Wheel & Casting
- **Skill Wheel Position:** Set to **Fixed Position**. Prevents UI drift when your finger slides off the screen edge during intense fights, which can cause skills to fire in the wrong direction.
- **Release Without Target:** Set to **Cancel Cast**. The default "fire in facing direction" wastes key abilities — especially ultimates — when no target is locked.
- **Camera Follows Skills:** Enable for long-range heroes (Gan & Mo, Shouyue). The camera automatically pans in the direction of your skill, giving you extended vision for blind shots.

## Movement Joystick Center — Set to "None"
During sweaty teamfights, the default centered joystick can fail to register or cause reverse input. Setting the center point to **None** allows your left thumb to engage movement from anywhere on the left screen — no more dead zones.

## Mini-Map & Equipment Shop — "Right Side Swap"
Move **In-Game Equipment Position** and the shop filter to the **right side** of the screen. Your right thumb (skill buttons) can quickly purchase items while recalling or rotating, while your left thumb continues controlling movement. Zero downtime.

## Pro Button Layout
- Place **Attack Minion** and **Attack Tower** buttons near your right thumb
- Keep **Summoner Spell** in easy-reach position
- Minimize button size slightly for more screen visibility
- Enable **edge screen rejection** to prevent accidental swipes

## Per-Role Adjustments
- **Junglers:** Prioritize Smite button placement
- **Supports:** Enlarge heal/shield buttons
- **Marksmen:** Attack Minion button is your most-used tool
