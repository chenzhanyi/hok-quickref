# Auto-Translate In-Match Chat

## The International Server Problem
In Honor of Kings global, your teammates may speak Thai, Arabic, Russian, Malay, or broken English shorthand. Without translation, you're playing blind — you can't tell if your jungler is calling "take Dragon" or "retreat."

## The Fix
Enable **Auto-Translate In-Match Text Chat** in your game settings.

The system automatically converts all chat messages into your selected language in real time. While machine translation isn't perfect, it's more than enough to understand key calls:

- "kailong" → "Open Dragon"
- "撤退" → "Retreat"
- "埋伏" → "Ambush"

**One toggle. No more language barrier.**
