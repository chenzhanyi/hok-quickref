# Vision & Map Awareness

## Ward Timing
- **1:15-1:20:** Ward river bush (enemy Jungler ganks at ~1:30-2:00)
- **30-45 seconds before objective spawn:** Ward Dragon/Tyrant pit
- **When sieging:** Ward enemy jungle entrances behind the tower
- **When defending:** Ward your own jungle to spot flanks

## Ward Placement Priority
1. **Enemy buff camps** — tells you exactly where enemy Jungler is
2. **River entrance bushes** — protects your lane from ganks
3. **Objective pits** — Dragon, Tyrant, Overlord
4. **Tribush (Clash Lane)** — most common gank path

## Reading the Minimap
- **Enemy missing from lane?** Ping "missing" and play defensively
- **Enemy Jungler shows top?** Bot lane can play aggressive, take Dragon
- **All 5 enemies visible?** Make the most aggressive play possible
- **Multiple enemies missing?** They're doing an objective or setting an ambush

## The 3-5 Second Rule
Glance at the minimap every 3-5 seconds. Make it a habit: every time you last-hit a minion, check the map.
