# Anti-Heal Items — The "Three Sacred Vessels"

## The Problem
Solo queue teammates often ignore enemy healing. If the enemy team has Cheng Yaojin, Cai Yan, or Dasiming, and nobody builds anti-heal, your team's damage becomes irrelevant — they'll out-sustain everything.

## The Three Anti-Heal Items
Every role has an anti-heal option. **Build yours. Every game with enemy healing.**

| Item | Role | Effect |
|------|------|--------|
| **Scorched Cape** (红莲斗篷) | Tanks / Warriors | Passive AoE burn + heal reduction aura |
| **Mortal Punisher** (制裁之刃) | Marksmen / Assassins | Basic attacks apply heal reduction |
| **Venomous Staff** (梦魇之牙) | Mages / Enchanters | Ability damage applies heal reduction |

## When to Build
- Enemy has Cheng Yaojin, Cai Yan, Dasiming, or any hero with significant self-healing
- Enemy Support has healing abilities
- Enemy team has 2+ life-steal items

## Golden Rule
**Build your anti-heal item as your 2nd or 3rd item — not as a last-minute 6th slot.** Healing heroes are strongest in the mid-game. If you wait until full build to counter them, the game is already lost.
