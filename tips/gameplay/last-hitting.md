# Last-Hitting Masterclass

## Why Last-Hitting Matters
You earn ~30% more gold from minions you last-hit. At 10 minutes, 80 CS vs 50 CS = ~900 gold difference = nearly a full item.

## Lane Farming
- **Watch HP bars** — only attack when one hit will kill
- **Attack-Move rhythm:** Attack → Move → Attack → Move (cancels backswing animation)
- **Use the Attack Minion button** to prevent misclicking on heroes

## Tower Farming
| Minion Type | Tower Hits | Your Action |
|------------|------------|-------------|
| Melee | 3 | Tower ×2, then you last-hit |
| Ranged | 2 | Pre-hit once, tower ×1, then finish |
| Cannon | 7 | Always secure — use abilities if needed |

## Practice Routine
- **Week 1:** Empty practice mode, 30 min/day. Goal: 70 CS at 10 min
- **Week 2:** Add bot opponent. Goal: 75 CS at 10 min
- **Week 3:** Practice tower-farming specifically
- **Week 4:** Ranked-ready — CS should be automatic

## Gold Equivalent
**15 CS ≈ 1 Kill** (both worth ~300 gold). Minions don't fight back and respawn every 30 seconds. Consistent CS is the most reliable path to getting fed.
