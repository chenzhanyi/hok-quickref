# Sun Bin Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Must hit **40% CDR** as fast as possible. With 40% CDR, S2 has only a few seconds of downtime. Needs **tankiness** to survive positioning near the frontline. Pure utility build.

---

## Recommended Utility Support Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Star Spring (极影·星泉)** | Shadow Aegis aura: +15% CDR + Mana regen for whole team. **Star Spring active:** sustained HP/Mana restore for lane phase. |
| 2 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | +15% CDR. Combined with aura + runes = 40% CDR cap by 5-6 minutes. |
| 3 | **Rising Sun (旭日初光)** | S1/S2 spam easily procs Rising Sun's shield on Sun Bin + lowest-HP ally. Perfect synergy. |
| 4 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Reduces enemy AS on-hit — survives Assassin ambushes. |
| 5 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** or **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** | **Magic Cloak:** vs poke Mages. **Eternal Night:** burst-heal vs Magic Assassins (combined with S2 refund = instant full HP). |
| 6 | **Frost Breath (凝冰之息)** or **Nightmare Fang (梦魇之牙)** | **Frost Breath:** S1/S3 apply perma-slow for kiting. **Nightmare Fang:** Grievous Wounds vs heavy heal comps (Cai Yan, Cheng Yaojin). |

### CDR Breakdown

| Source | CDR |
|--------|-----|
| Runes (Compassion) | 10% |
| Shadow Aegis aura | 15% |
| Calm Boots | 15% |
| **Total** | **40% (cap)** |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **CDR (rush 40%), Max Health (survival), Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "10% CDR Tank" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |
| 🟢 Green | Compassion (怜悯) | ×10 | Cooldown Reduction +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** **+10% CDR**, +737 Max Health, +23 Physical Defense, +4% MS, +52 HP/5s. Hits 40% CDR by mid-game.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Sun Bin's S2 HP refund is NOT healing — it ignores Grievous Wounds. Copy the **CN #1 Sun Bin** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
