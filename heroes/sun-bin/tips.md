# Sun Bin Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S2 Timing: Right Before Burst, Not After

S2 refunds 40% of damage taken as HP. The timing window is tight:
- **Defensive:** cast S2 when you see the enemy Assassin about to engage or a Mage charging ultimate
- **Offensive:** cast S2 as your frontline/Assassin dives in — the MS + CDR + HP refund lets them survive and keep fighting
- The refund **ignores Grievous Wounds** — it hard-counters anti-heal

### 2. S3 Silence: Interrupt Channeled Abilities

S3's 1-second Silence on deployment is Sun Bin's highest-impact CC. Use reactively:
- Lu Bu S3 (aerial strike) → S3 his landing zone
- Wang Zhaojun S3 (blizzard) → S3 to cancel
- Zhang Fei S3 (roar) → S3 to silence during wind-up
- Mozi S3 (channel) → S3 to interrupt

### 3. Kite Rhythm: S2 Retreat → S3 → S2 Re-engage

Sun Bin's macro pattern: **When enemies engage → S2 your team backward (they can't catch you, their abilities hit S2's buffer) → S3 Silence → S2 off cooldown → re-engage.** This cycle destroys short-range comps.

### 4. S1: Bush Check + Vision

S1 reveals enemies hit. Throw S1 into suspicious bushes. If you hear the hit sound or see damage numbers → enemies inside. Also great for checking Dragon/Baron pit.

### 5. S2 + Team Diving: Enabler, Not Initiator

Sun Bin doesn't initiate — he **enables.** S2 your frontline Fighter/Assassin as they go in. The MS + CDR + HP refund makes them unkillable. Without you, they die. With you, they 1v3.

## Signature Combo

### Teamfight Engine: **S2 (engage) → S3 (silence) → S1 (slow) → S2 (chase/retreat)**

1. S2 — team rushes in with +MS + CDR
2. S3 — Silence enemy backline (interrupts their response)
3. S1 — slow priority target
4. S2 (off cooldown from CDR) — chase or retreat as needed

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S2's HP refund is NOT healing.** It bypasses Grievous Wounds entirely. Tell your teammates this — they'll stop flaming you for "not healing enough."
- **Max CDR first.** 40% CDR = S2 every few seconds. Rush Compassion runes + Calm Boots + Shadow Aegis.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in extended kiting fights. Struggles if your team has no frontline to enable.
