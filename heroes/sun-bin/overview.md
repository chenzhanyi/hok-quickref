# Sun Bin（孫臏） — Mage / Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage / Support (Team Utility / Kiting Engine)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 逆流之時 (Time's Flow)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 孙膑)

> Sun Bin is a strategic utility Support who doesn't tank — he **controls time and speed.** His S2 grants allies massive MS + CDR, and **40% of damage taken during S2 is refunded as HP** (NOT affected by Grievous Wounds!). His S3 is an AoE **1-second Silence** that interrupts channels. He's the undisputed "king of late-game kiting" in CN server.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S2: Time Rewind (The Soul Skill)

S2 grants allies: +30-60% MS, +20% CDR, and **40% of damage taken during S2 is refunded as HP.** The refund ignores Grievous Wounds! Timing is everything:
- **Too early:** the buff expires before damage lands = wasted
- **Too late:** ally dies before S2 activates
- **Perfect:** cast right before enemy burst lands

### S3: 1-Second Silence (Interrupt Everything)

S3 creates a field that **Silences enemies for 1 second** on deployment. After 5 seconds, explodes for massive damage. Use to interrupt: Lu Bu S3, Wang Zhaojun S3, Zhang Fei S3, Dun Shan S1, Mozi S3.

### S1: Bush Check + Slow

S1 can be thrown into bushes. If it hits a hidden enemy, it makes a distinct sound and reveals them. Also triggers Frost Breath slow for kiting.
