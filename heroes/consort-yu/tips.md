# Consort Yu Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S2: Never Waste It on Movement Speed

S2 is your life. It grants: **2s Physical immunity + CC cleanse on activation + slow removal + double-arrow on next AA.** Never use it to rotate faster. Without S2, enemy Assassins (Li Bai, Jing, Lam) WILL tower-dive you. Only cast S2 when:
- An Assassin engages you
- You see a CC projectile flying at you (Daji charm, Hou Yi bird)
- You need to immune a tower shot

### 2. S2 Cleanses CC on Activation Frame

The first ~0.1s of S2 grants **CC immunity**. If you time it right as a CC hits, you negate it entirely. Practice this timing — it's the hallmark of a great Consort Yu.

### 3. S3: Anti-Dive + Offensive Tool

When dived by melee Assassins: **S3 their face.** The stomp stuns them, then you auto-jump backward while CC-immune. You're now at safe range with +4s Attack Speed buff. Turn and kill them.

### 4. S1: Farm Safe vs Lane Bullies

Vs oppressive laners (Arli, Goya): max S1 and farm from long range. S1's damage and range let you last-hit safely without entering their threat zone.

### 5. Magic Damage = Your Kryptonite

S2 blocks **only Physical Damage**. Magic burst (Kongming, Mai Shiranui, Sikong Zhen, Luna) and True Damage (Lu Bu) ignore it entirely. When facing magic-heavy comps, build Magic Cloak early and position extra carefully.

## Signature Combos

### Anti-Dive Counter-Kill: **S3 → AA → S1 → AA**

1. Enemy Assassin dives you
2. **S3** stomp them → stun + auto-jump backward
3. **AA** with S3's Attack Speed buff
4. **S1** as they try to flee
5. **S2** saved for if another enemy joins or you need Physical immunity

### Full Burst Engage: **S2 → S3 → AA×n → S1**

1. S2 (Physical immunity + double-arrow ready)
2. S3 stomp (stun + jump back + AS buff)
3. AA repeatedly with S3's 4-second AS steroid
4. S1 to finish at range

### Bush Ambush: **S1 → S3 → AA**

1. S1 from bush (full damage, no warning)
2. S3 immediately after (stun + AS buff)
3. AA until dead

## Advanced Tips

- **S2 double-arrow passive triggers on-hit effects.** Use it to get an extra Doomsday/Ice Grip proc.
- **S3 can cross thin walls.** If positioned right, you can jump over jungle walls to escape or chase.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs Physical comps. Struggles vs Magic-heavy teams.
