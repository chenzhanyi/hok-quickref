# Consort Yu Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Meta has shifted from "Penetration-Black Cleaver" to **Crit-Attack Speed**. Her S3 and passive maximize crit/AS scaling. Build path: **Crit + Attack Speed**.

---

## Recommended Crit-Speed Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early AS for laning. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC.* |
| 2 | **Lightning Dagger (闪电匕首)** or **Shadow Blade (影刃)** | **Lightning Dagger:** chain lightning for safe wave clear. **Shadow Blade:** on-crit AS + MS for aggressive kiting. |
| 3 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | Crit core. First major damage spike. |
| 4 | **Dawn (破晓)** | Marksman ultimate item. AS, Crit, doubled Penetration. *(If behind: build Cloud Piercer (穿云弓) first, finish later.)* |
| 5 | **Blood Weeper (泣血之刃)** | Physical Lifesteal. With S2 Physical immunity, you can drain-tank through fights and reverse-kill at low HP. |
| 6 | **Sun Bow (逐日之弓)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | **Sun Bow:** +Range active for safe backline deletion. **Magic Cloak:** vs heavy magic burst (Shangguan, Gan & Mo, Xiao Qiao). S2 doesn't block Magic — this is your safety net. |

---

## Critical Warning

S2 does NOT block Magic Damage or True Damage. When the enemy has Magic carries (Kongming, Luna, Sikong Zhen, Mi Yue): build **Magic Cloak** early and save S2 for Physical threats only.
