# Consort Yu（虞姬） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Anti-Physical / Duelist ADC)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 森之靈風 (Forest Spirit Wind)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 虞姬)

> Consort Yu is a uniquely designed, high-ceiling Marksman. Her signature mechanic is her S2: **2 seconds of complete Physical Damage immunity**. This makes her the natural counter to every Physical Assassin, burst Fighter, and even most enemy ADCs. She also has a built-in CC-cleanse on S2 activation and a S3 that stuns + creates distance + grants Attack Speed.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S2: Physical Immunity (The Entire Kit)

S2 grants: **+MS, 2s Physical Damage immunity, slow cleanse, and CC immunity on the activation frame.** If Daji's charm or Hou Yi's bird is about to hit, S2 right before impact negates the CC entirely. **Never waste S2 for movement speed** — it's your only survival tool.

### S3: Stomp + Create Distance

S3 dashes to target, **stuns 0.5s**, slows, then jumps backward. During the jump, **CC-immune**. Landing grants **+4 seconds of massive Attack Speed**. When Han Xin, Wukong, or Lanling Wang dives you: S3 stomp → auto-jump backward → S2 if needed → auto-attack them down from safety.

### S1: Long-Range Poke

S1 is a penetrating arrow with high base damage and extreme range. Use to farm safely vs oppressive laners (Arli, Goya), bush-check, or finish fleeing enemies.

### Late-Game Transformation

Early game: S1 poke mage. Late game with crit/AS items: your AAs become devastating. S2 Physical immunity + S3 self-peel means you win duels other ADCs can't.
