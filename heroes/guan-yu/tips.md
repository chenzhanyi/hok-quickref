# Guan Yu Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Never Release the Left Joystick

Guan Yu's Charge bar resets if you release the joystick or get CC'd. Always circle at the edge of fights — never stand still. If you stop, you're useless.

### 2. Horse Radar: Best Bush-Check in the Game

In Charge Stance, moving toward enemies grants **+20% MS** and the horse glows. Approach bushes at an angle — if you suddenly speed up, someone's inside. No ability needed.

### 3. S2: Save It for Cleanse

Non-Charge S2 is your only self-cleanse. If Wang Zhaojun freezes you or Daji charms you, S2 breaks it instantly. **Never waste Charge-S2 for damage if you might need the cleanse.**

### 4. S3 Bulldozer Mode

S3 reduces Charge distance by 50% for 10s. You can chain-push every 2 steps. Optimal combo: **Charge-S3 (push back) → two steps → Charge-AA (push) → two steps → Charge-S1 (slash + push) → S2 saved for cleanse or chase.**

### 5. Sprint = Anti-Slow Insurance

99% of Guan Yu players take Sprint. During Sprint, you're immune to most slows — your horse can't be stopped. Pop Sprint before engaging and trample their entire backline.

---

## Signature Combo

### Classic Flank → Bulldoze: **Charge-S3 (push back) → Charge-AA (push) → Charge-S1 (slash + push) → S2 (chase/cleanse)**

1. Flank from the side or behind
2. Charge-S3: push enemies toward your team/tower
3. Two steps → Charge-AA: push again
4. Two steps → Charge-S1: slash + third push
5. S2: chase or cleanse if CC'd

### Bush Snipe: **Charge-S1 (slash from bush)**

1. Build Charge in bush
2. Charge-S1 blind-slash into the enemy
3. S2 away to disengage
4. Take zero return damage

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Sprint + S3 simultaneously = unstoppable bulldozer.** Pop both and the enemy formation collapses.
- **Berserker's Armor + Overlord's Platemail are core.** Both provide Movement Speed — Guan Yu's most important stat.
- **Matchup:** Destroys immobile backlines. Struggles vs point-and-click CC (Daji, Zhang Liang) if you waste S2.
