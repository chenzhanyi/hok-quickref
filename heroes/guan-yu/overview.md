# Guan Yu（關羽） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Charge-Based Roamer)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 一騎當千 (One Rider, A Thousand Enemies)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 关羽)

> Guan Yu is a uniquely designed, high-ceiling roaming Fighter. All of his gameplay revolves around **Charge Stance** — build energy by continuously moving; at full Charge, all abilities and AAs gain displacement and knockback effects. If his horse stops, his threat level drops to zero.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Charge Stance (Never Stop Moving)

Keep moving to build Charge. At full Charge, abilities become enhanced with knockback/push effects. If you **release the left joystick** or get hit by hard CC/slow, the Charge bar resets. Always circle at the edge of fights.

### Horse Head Radar (Anti-Bush)

When in Charge Stance, moving toward an enemy Hero (including invisible ones in bushes) grants **+20% Movement Speed** and the horse's head glows. This is the best bush-checking tool in the game — if you accelerate toward a bush, someone's there.

### S2: Your ONLY CC Cleanse

S2 (non-Charge) is a self-cleanse. If Wang Zhaojun freezes you or Daji stuns you, S2 instantly removes the CC and grants Movement Speed. **Never waste Charge-S2 for engage unless you're certain you won't need the cleanse.**

### S3: Bulldozer Mode

S3 shortens Charge distance by **50% for 10 seconds**. You only need ~2 steps to reach full Charge. Chain-push enemies across the map like a bulldozer.

### Summoner Spell: Sprint (Mandatory)

99% of Guan Yu players take Sprint. During Sprint, Guan Yu is **immune to most slows** — effectively 10 seconds of "horse protection." Pop Sprint in teamfights and trample their entire formation.
