# Guan Yu Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Two viable paths. Both require **Movement Speed** as the #1 stat. **Semi-tank** is meta in CN server.

---

## Recommended Semi-Tank Build (CN Standard)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | **Core boots.** 35% Tenacity prevents mild slows from breaking Charge. |
| 2 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Perfect for Guan Yu. Taking hits grants stacking **MS (up to 5%) + damage output**. He gets faster as fights progress. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core damage item: CDR, Health, flat Penetration. One Charge-S1 deletes squishies. |
| 4 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** | Massive HP + continuous regen. Guan Yu constantly roams the map — Overlord's Platemail means he never needs to recall. |
| 5 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Ominous Premonition:** vs heavy ADC. **Pure Sky:** active DR for diving + CDR. |
| 6 | **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Penetration vs armor stacking. Magic defense vs heavy magic burst. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Zero Attack Speed or Critical needed. **Physical Penetration, Attack Damage, Movement Speed** only.

### Setup 1: "Stealth 100-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +45 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, **+10% Movement Speed**.

### Setup 2: "Harmony Sustain" (vs Lane Bullies)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, +100 Physical Penetration, **+450 Max Health**, +4% MS. Better sustain vs Kuang Tie, Ji Xiaoman in lane.

## Summoner Spell: Sprint (Mandatory)

Sprint makes Guan Yu immune to most slows for 10 seconds — your horse can't be stopped. Always take Sprint. No exceptions.
