# Dharma Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** S3's second cast has **%missing HP execute.** Stack Physical Attack + Penetration for one-combo kills. CN server consensus: **full burst with one defensive item.**

---

## Recommended Clash Lane / Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Dharma dives into melee — getting CC-locked during S1 = death. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core #1. CDR + flat Penetration + Health. Perfect early-game spike. |
| 3 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** or **Master's Power (宗师之力)** | **Frost Grip:** S1→AA slow + CDR + defense. **Master's Power:** S1→AA one-shot burst for snowballing. |
| 4 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Dharma's soul item!** S3 second cast = %missing HP execute. Siege Breaker's +30% vs <50% HP stacks multiplicatively. S3 execute becomes a guillotine. |
| 5 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Defense + stacking MS/damage while fighting. Perfect for extended brawls. |
| 6 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** or **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | **Pure Sky:** active DR for surviving focus fire during engage. **Sanctuary:** resurrection. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Physical Penetration, Attack, Movement Speed.** Skills grant innate AS — no Attack Speed runes needed.

### Setup 1: "Stealth 100-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +45 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% MS. One S3 wall-kick deletes squishies with 2 items.

### Setup 2: "Dream Crit Hybrid" (Lane Trading)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×9 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×1 | Critical Rate +1.6% ("dream crit" on enhanced AAs) |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×7 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×3 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +100 Physical Penetration, **+1.6% Crit Rate**, ~+9% MS, +3% AS. That one crit during AA×2 wins lanes.
