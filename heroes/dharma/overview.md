# Dharma（達摩） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Wall Kick / Burst Engage)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 拳僧 (Fist Monk)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 达摩)

> Dharma is a high-burst, creative Fighter/Jungler. His S3 kicks enemies into walls — if they collide with ANY obstacle (terrain, turret rubble, Nuwa's matrix, Dun Shan's shield, Yi Xing's cage, even the crystal), S3 unlocks a second cast with **%missing HP execute damage.** Post-rework, his S1 now stores multiple charges, dramatically improving his engage range and consistency.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S3: Wall Kick (Any Obstacle Counts)

S3 kicks an enemy backward. If they hit **any obstacle**, S3 unlocks a second cast with **%missing HP execute.** Obstacles include: all terrain, turret rubble, Nuwa matrix, Dun Shan shield, Yi Xing's S3 cage, and the crystal.

### S1: Multi-Charge (Post-Rework)

S1 now stores charges. "S1 → S1" gives Dharma two long-range dashes without needing Flash. This dramatically improves his engage reliability.

### Passive: AA Heal + Defense

After S1/S2, the next 2 AAs become **fast double-punches** with bonus AS + HP restore. Always weave: S1 → AA×2 → S2 → AA×2. The healing is substantial — never dump all skills without AAs between them.

### S1 Angle → S3

Enemies rarely stand against walls conveniently. Technique: **S1 knockup → walk to their side/front during the airborne → S3** kicks them into the nearest wall. The S1 float gives you time to reposition.
