# Dharma Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S3: Any Obstacle = Wall

S3's wall-kick works on: terrain, turret rubble, Nuwa's matrix, Dun Shan's shield, Yi Xing's S3 cage, and the crystal. **If it blocks movement, it's a wall.** Look for these in every fight.

### 2. S1 Knockup → Reposition → S3 Wall Kick

Enemies rarely stand conveniently against walls. Technique: S1 knockup → during their airborne frames, **walk to the side or behind them** → S3 to kick them into the nearest wall. The S1 float time gives you the window to reposition.

### 3. Always Weave S1/S2 → AA×2

S1 and S2 each grant 2 enhanced fast-punch AAs with bonus AS + HP restore. Never S1 → S2 → S3 in one burst. Always: **S1 → AA×2 → S2 → AA×2 → S3.** The healing from those AAs wins trades that pure abilities lose.

### 4. S1 Multi-Charge (Post-Rework)

S1 stores charges. "S1 → S1" gives two long-range dashes. This means Dharma no longer depends on Flash for engage. Use double-S1 to gap-close from off-screen.

### 5. S3 Second Cast = Execute

S3's second cast deals **%missing HP damage** — the lower they are, the harder it hits. Always full combo BEFORE S3 second cast to maximize the execute damage.

## Signature Combos

### Long-Range Engage: **S1 → S1 → S3 → AA → S2 → AA×2 → S3 (execute)**

1. S1 double-dash into enemy backline
2. S3 kick into wall
3. AA (enhanced)
4. S2 armor shred + AA×2 (heal)
5. S3 second cast (execute)

### Bush Ambush: **S2 → S3 → AA×2 → S1 → S3 (execute)**

1. S2 from bush (armor shred)
2. S3 kick into wall
3. AA×2 (enhanced + heal)
4. S1 chase if they Flash
5. S3 second cast (execute)

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S1 float time is your S3 setup window.** Don't rush S3 after S1 — use the airborne frames to reposition.
- **S2 shreds armor.** Always S2 before the full S3 combo for maximum damage.
- **Matchup:** God-tier near walls and in jungle corridors. Struggles in open-field fights with no obstacles.
