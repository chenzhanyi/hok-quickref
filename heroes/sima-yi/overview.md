# Sima Yi（司馬懿） — Assassin / Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin / Mage (Magic Diver)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 寂滅之心 (Heart of Silence)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 司马懿)

> Sima Yi is a terrifying Magic Assassin/Jungler. He has one of the best global-range engage tools (S3), and after using any ability his next basic attack **teleports behind the target** — turning him into a "melee reaper" that deletes squishies in two hits. No Mana bar; abilities consume Energy. Nearby enemy spell casts restore his Energy.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Behind-the-Target Auto-Attack

After any ability, Sima Yi's next basic attack **teleports him behind the target**. Use this to dodge directional skillshots (Mai Shiranui, Gan & Mo) by automatically repositioning.

### Energy Management

No Mana. Abilities cost Energy. Kills/assists permanently increase Energy cap. Early game: Energy runs out fast. S2's suppression field **refunds Energy after 2 seconds** — stay in it.

### S2: Silence + Magic Damage Absorption

S2 silences enemies AND **converts 30% of incoming Magic Damage into HP over 5 seconds**. Time S2 against Shangguan, Zhuge Liang, or Daji — you'll take zero net damage.

### S1: Clone Decoy

S1 sends a clone forward while the real body stays behind **CC-immune**. Use the clone to bait Donghuang/Zhang Fei ultimates or enemy Flashes. If things go wrong, simply don't auto-attack — the real body stays safe.
