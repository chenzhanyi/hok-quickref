# Sima Yi Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Sima Yi's enhanced AA does NOT crit. It deals full Magic Damage. He needs **Attack Speed** (faster AA animations) and **Magic Burst**. Build path: Magic Attack Speed.

---

## Recommended Mid / Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Rune Greatsword (符文大剑)** or skip | Jungle: Blue knife for Magic Attack stacking. Mid: skip directly to boots. |
| 2 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | **Sima Yi's signature boots!** Attack Speed dramatically smooths AA animations. *Late game: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC.* |
| 3 | **Golden Sword (金色圣剑)** | Attack Speed + Movement Speed. Bonus Magic Damage on-hit. Perfect synergy with Sima Yi's enhanced AA playstyle. |
| 4 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage Magic Attack burst. After this, 1A2A combo deletes squishies. |
| 5 | **Spellblade Staff (巫术法杖)** | Core damage amplifier. "Power Strike" passive stacks on the first enhanced AA after ability — that single hit deals 50%+ of a squishy's HP. |
| 6 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** or **Splendor (辉月)** | **Void Staff:** Penetration vs Magic Cloak. **Splendor:** Stasis survival + S1 clone escape combo for diving the backline. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Attack Speed (AA threshold), Magic Penetration, Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: CN Server "Omen-Hunt Speed" (Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Omen (凶兆) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Attack Speed +0.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** **+22% Attack Speed**, +42 Magic Attack, **+64 Magic Penetration**, +10% Movement Speed. Hits the early Attack Speed threshold — one knife, one kill.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Sima Yi's timing and Attack Speed feel are critical. The easiest setup:

1. Open **Preparation (备战)** → **Change Build (更换)**
2. Select **Recommended / CN #1 Sima Yi** setup
3. The game auto-syncs optimal Arcana and item order
