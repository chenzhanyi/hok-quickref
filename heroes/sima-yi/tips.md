# Sima Yi Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Behind-the-Target AA (Your Best Dodge)

After any ability, your next AA teleports behind the target. Don't fight the auto-target — use it to:
- Dodge Mai Shiranui's directional burst
- Sidestep Gan & Mo's skillshots
- Confuse enemy skillshot aim

### 2. S2: Silence + Magic Absorption

S2 applies Silence AND converts **30% of Magic Damage taken into HP over 5 seconds**. Time it against burst Mages (Shangguan S3, Zhuge Liang S3, Daji combo) — you'll heal through their entire rotation.

### 3. Energy Management

Energy runs out fast early game. S2's field **refunds Energy after 2 seconds** if you stay inside. Between fights, let S2 refill your bar. Kills/assists permanently increase Energy cap — scale up.

### 4. S1 Clone: Bait Everything

S1 sends a clone forward while your real body stays behind **immune to CC**. Use the clone to:
- Bait Donghuang/Zhang Fei ultimates
- Force enemy Flashes
- If the situation looks bad: just don't auto-attack — you never teleport

### 5. S3: Escape OR Execute

S3 has global range but a landing delay. Two uses:
- **Escape:** Blind-cast S3 to a jungle camp across the map when collapsed on
- **Execute:** Predict enemy backline position and S3 directly onto them

## Signature Combos

### Tower Dive One-Shot: **S1 (clone) → AA → S2 → AA**

1. S1 clone dives under tower (clone ignores tower aggro)
2. AA teleports behind target (first hit)
3. S2 immediately — Silence prevents Flash/escape
4. AA (second hit) — target dead
5. Walk out or S3 away

### Global Gank Execution: **S3 → AA → S2 → AA → S1 → AA**

1. S3 from across map onto enemy backline
2. AA teleport behind (first hit)
3. S2 Silence (second hit)
4. If target survives: S1 clone chase → AA (third hit)

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Enhanced AA doesn't crit.** Build Magic Attack + Attack Speed, not Critical.
- **S2 absorbs Magic Damage only.** Physical burst still hurts. Don't face-tank AD Assassins.
- **S3 can be cancelled by CC during the channel.** Cast from fog of war.
- **Matchup:** Destroys immobile Mages and Marksmen. Struggles vs tanky CC-heavy frontlines that survive his burst.
