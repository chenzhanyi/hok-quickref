# Biron Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Energy Is Everything

30+ Energy = Enhanced skills. Below 30 = weak. Energy decays out of combat. Before rotating: farm minions/jungle camps to **60+ Energy (2 bars).** Enter every fight with Energy ready.

### 2. S2 + Flash: Signature Long-Range Engage

Enhanced S2's knockup AA can be **held for several seconds.** Pre-cast S2 → hold enhanced AA → walk → **Flash** onto the enemy backline → AA knockup. Undodgeable off-screen initiation.

### 3. S1: More Targets = More Healing

Enhanced S1 heals per target hit. When low HP: position so S1 hits the **entire minion wave + enemy Hero.** Go from 10% to 100% HP in one cast. Never retreat with S1 available.

### 4. Level 1-2 Absolute Dominance

Start S1. AA the first wave to build Energy. At level 2 with Energy active: S2 knockup → Enhanced S1 → enemy is dead or forced to recall. 80% of CN server First Bloods happen exactly this way.

### 5. S3: Don't Hold It

S3's cooldown is extremely short (a few seconds late game). Use it to: tank initial burst, steal enemy MS, shield right before CC lands.

## Signature Combos

### Lane Trade: **S2 → AA (knockup) → S1 → S3**

1. Energy ≥ 30. S2 → Enhanced AA knockup
2. S1 point-blank (max damage + heal)
3. S3 shield + damage → enemy dead or forced out

### Bush Ambush One-Shot: **S3 → S1 → S2 → AA**

1. Wait in bush with Energy
2. S3 → S1 (burst + max Energy)
3. S2 chase + Enhanced AA knockup
4. Dead before they can Flash

### Long-Range Backline Delete: **S2 → Flash → AA → S3 → S1**

1. S2 dash → **Flash** mid-dash onto enemy ADC/Mage
2. Enhanced AA knockup
3. S3 shield + damage
4. S1 execute

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Level 1-2 is your absolute peak.** Buy Iron Sword, rush wave, build Energy, get First Blood.
- **Energy decays when out of combat.** Don't AFK between fights — keep hitting things.
- **Matchup:** Destroys melee Fighters in lane. Struggles vs long-range kiters (Mi Yue, Marco).
