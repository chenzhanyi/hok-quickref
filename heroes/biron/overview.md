# Biron（狂鐵） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Lane Bully / Shield Brawler)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 戰車意志 (Chariot's Will)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 狂铁)

> Biron (Kuang Tie in CN) is a dominant lane bully with insane burst and terrifying sustain. His core mechanic is **Energy** — at 30+ Energy, all abilities become Enhanced (double damage, double heal, knockup, massive shield). Enhanced abilities **don't consume Energy.** With Energy, he's the "Lightning King." Without it, he's mediocre.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Energy Charging

Hitting anything (Minions, Monsters, Heroes) builds Energy. At **30+ Energy (1 bar)**: all abilities gain Enhanced effects. Enhanced abilities are stronger but **don't consume Energy.** Energy decays when out of combat — pre-stack to 60+ before rotating.

### S1: Lifesteal God

Enhanced S1 restores **massive HP**, scaling with **number of targets hit.** Minion wave + enemy Hero = full HP restore. Never retreat at low HP — Enhanced S1 into a wave refills instantly.

### S2: Hold the Knockup

Enhanced S2's next AA becomes a **knockup** that can be **held for several seconds.** Pre-cast S2 → hold enhanced AA → Flash → surprise knockup from off-screen.

### S3: Massive Shield + Steal MS

Enhanced S3 grants a massive shield, AoE damage, and **steals enemy Movement Speed.** Extremely short cooldown late game — don't hold it, spam it.
