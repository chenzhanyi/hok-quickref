# Biron Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** S3 grants massive shields — build **high Attack + max CDR.** Semi-tank burst. CN server consensus: **CDR bruiser.**

---

## Recommended Clash Lane Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity mandatory. Biron must melee brawl — CC-lock = can't cast S1 = dead. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core #1. 15% CDR + flat Penetration + Health. Early kill pressure. |
| 3 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Absolute core!** 10% CDR + high Attack. Active DR can be used while CC'd. Combined with S3 shield, tank 5-man focus fire. |
| 4 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Taking hits grants stacking MS (up to 10%) + damage (up to 10%). Perfect for extended brawls. |
| 5 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** or **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** | **Siege Breaker:** execute threshold for one-shotting squishies. **Star Breaker:** 40% Penetration vs tanky teams. |
| 6 | **Phoenix Eye (不死鸟之眼)** or **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | **Phoenix Eye:** amplifies S1 healing at low HP ("medical miracle"). **Sanctuary:** resurrection for final fights. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Zero Attack Speed or Critical needed. **Physical Penetration + Movement Speed.**

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% MS, +3% AS. Max level 2 First Blood potential.
