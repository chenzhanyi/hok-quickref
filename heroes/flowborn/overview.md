# Flowborn（元流之子） — Mage / Tank

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage / Tank (Multi-Weapon Flex)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane / Clash Lane / Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 元流之子 (Child of Primordial Flow)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive

> Flowborn is the game's most unique "multi-class, multi-weapon" Hero. Players can switch weapons in the fountain to change their core abilities, role, and stat bonuses. The two strongest forms are **Tank** (Clash Lane / Roaming) and **Mage** (Mid Lane).

---

## 🛡️ Tank Form (Clash Lane / Support)

Tank Flowborn has massive base HP. Core: shields, CC, and dual-defense buffs for the team.

### Weapon Choice

- **Sword:** MS + Physical Penetration. Use for Clash Lane duels or when team needs extra damage.
- **Shield:** Max HP + Healing. Use for pure tank Support or when team lacks frontliner.
- **Spear:** CDR + Attack. Use when you need frequent CC rotations.

### Abilities

- **S1 (Flow Unity):** Self-buff that auto-links to the **highest-damage nearby ally** — granting them massive bonus Attack or Magic Attack. Always stand near your ADC/Mage when casting.
- **S2 (Grand Entrance):** Unstoppable dash + knockup + massive self-shield. Engage or counter-engage tool.
- **S3 (Decisive Blow):** Large AoE slam + stun. Combo: S2 knockup → S1 buff → S3 stun.

### Core Build: Pure Tank HP Stacking

Blazing Cape → Resistance Boots → Ominous Premonition → Eternal Night / Magic Cloak → Overlord's Platemail → Pure Sky / Berserker's Armor

---

## 🔮 Mage Form (Mid Lane)

Mage Flowborn has extreme ranged poke and zone control. Weapon choice determines playstyle.

### Weapon Choice

- **Primordial Heart:** Max AP burst. Best vs squishy teams.
- **Primordial Staff:** Flat Magic Penetration. Best when enemies rush magic resist.
- **Primordial Plume:** Movement Speed. Best vs mobile/slippery comps.

### Abilities

- **S1:** Long-range AoE blast. Spam for poke and wave clear.
- **S2:** Persistent magic vortex — continuous damage + knockback/slow vs divers.
- **S3:** Channeled meteor bombardment. Combo: S1 slow → S2 zone → S3 full coverage.

### Core Build: Pure AP Burst

Calm Boots → Echo Staff → Savant's Wrath → Mask of Agony / Sunset Flow → Void Staff → Splendor / Sage's Tome
