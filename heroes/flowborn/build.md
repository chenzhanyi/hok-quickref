# Flowborn Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Two completely different builds depending on form. **Choose your form BEFORE the match.** Tank = pure HP stacking. Mage = pure AP burst.

---

## 🛡️ Tank Form Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Blazing Cape (红莲斗篷)** | HP + burn aura. Core for wave clear and melee damage. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Must survive to cast S2 Unstoppable. |
| 3 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Anti-ADC. |
| 4 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense. Eternal Night for burst; Magic Cloak for poke. |
| 5 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** | Massive HP — amplifies S2 shield. In-combat regen. |
| 6 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** or **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | CDR + active DR, or stacking MS + damage. |

### Tank Arcana

| Slot | Rune | Qty |
|------|------|-----|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 |

**Total:** **+1,162 Max HP**, +6% CDR, +4% MS. Level 1 dominance.

## 🔮 Mage Form Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR for S1 spam. *Late: swap to Arcane Boots.* |
| 2 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | +MS + AoE explosion on S1 hit. |
| 3 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP spike. S3 bombardment one-shots. |
| 4 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** or **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** | Multi-hit S2/S3 synergy. |
| 5 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Penetration vs Magic Cloak. |
| 6 | **Splendor (辉月)** or **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** | Stasis or max AP. |

## Quick In-Game Setup

**Choose your form BEFORE the match.** Don't mix Tank and Mage builds. Copy the **CN #1 Flowborn** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
