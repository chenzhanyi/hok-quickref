# Flowborn Tips & Tricks

---

## 🛡️ Tank Form Tips

### 1. Weapon Selection Matters

- **Sword:** Clash Lane duels, need extra damage
- **Shield:** Pure tank Support, team lacks frontline
- **Spear:** Need frequent CC, skill-spam matchups

### 2. S1: Buff Your Carry

S1 auto-links to the **highest-damage nearby ally** — granting them massive bonus Attack/AP. Always position near your ADC or Mage before casting. This is your most impactful ability.

### 3. S2: Unstoppable Engage

S2 grants Unstoppable + knockup + massive shield. Use to: initiate fights, counter-CC (walk through Lady Zhen freeze), or escape over walls.

### 4. Tank Combo: **S2 → S1 → S3**

S2 knockup + shield → S1 buff your carry → S3 AoE stun. Perfect teamfight initiation.

---

## 🔮 Mage Form Tips

### 1. Weapon Selection Matters

- **Primordial Heart:** Max AP. Squishy enemy teams.
- **Primordial Staff:** Flat Pen. Enemies building magic resist.
- **Primordial Plume:** MS + kiting. Mobile/slippery opponents.

### 2. S1: Constant Poke

S1 range is extremely long. Spam on cooldown to chip enemies and clear waves. Never walk into auto-attack range — you're pure artillery.

### 3. S2: Anti-Dive

S2 creates a persistent vortex that knocks back/slows enemies. When Assassins dive you: S2 at your feet → walk into the vortex → they can't reach you.

### 4. Mage Combo: **S1 → S2 → S3**

S1 slow + poke → S2 zone (block escape/trap them) → S3 full meteor coverage.
