# Kongkong Er Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure Magic burst Assassin. Needs **high AP, Magic Penetration, and CDR** for frequent clone swaps. Splendor for the clone-swap cycle.

---

## Recommended Jungle / Mid Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Rune Greatsword (符文大剑)** or **Arcane Boots (秘法之靴)** | Jungle: Blue knife for AP + enhanced AA. Mid: straight to boots. |
| 2 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | Core burst. Mark hits trigger AoE explosion. +MS for roaming. |
| 3 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP spike. Mark detonation now one-shots squishies. |
| 4 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** or **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** | **Mask:** %HP burn vs tanky teams. **Sunset Flow:** Penetration stacking vs squishy teams. |
| 5 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Penetration vs late-game Magic Cloak. |
| 6 | **Splendor (辉月)** | **Core item!** Dive → combo → Stasis → clone swap. The perfect in-and-out cycle. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Magic Penetration, Movement Speed, mild Lifesteal.**

### Setup 1: "Hunt 88-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +16% AS, **+10% MS**. Max gank tempo + near true-damage early.

### Setup 2: "Lifesteal Sustain"

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Greed (贪婪) | ×5 | Magic Lifesteal +1.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×5 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +88 Magic Penetration, +11% AS, +5% MS, **+8% Magic Lifesteal**. Jungle sustain without recalling.
