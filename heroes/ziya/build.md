# Ziya Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs **CDR** (frequent S3, faster XP orbs), **Magic Penetration** (S1 multi-tick synergy), and **AP burst**. Two paths: Burst and Utility CDR.

---

## Recommended Mid Lane Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR for frequent S3 + faster XP orb farming. |
| 2 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | S1's multi-tick endlessly triggers Mask's %HP burn. Core damage item. |
| 3 | **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** | S1's multi-tick **stacks 10 penetration layers instantly.** No need for Void Staff early. |
| 4 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP spike. S2+S3 burst threshold skyrockets. |
| 5 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Penetration vs late-game Magic Cloak. |
| 6 | **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** or **Splendor (辉月)** | **Tome:** max AP + damage amp. **Splendor:** Stasis vs heavy dive. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Magic Penetration, Movement Speed, mild sustain.**

### Setup 1: "Hybrid 88-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×5 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×5 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +8% AS, +7% MS, +173 Max Health, +26 HP/5s.

### Setup 2: "10% CDR Utility" (Coordinated Play)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Compassion (怜悯) | ×10 | Cooldown Reduction +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +24 Magic Penetration, **+10% CDR**, +450 Max Health, +4% MS, +52 HP/5s. More abilities = faster XP orbs = faster level 15.
