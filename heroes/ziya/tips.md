# Ziya Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Race to Level 15 — Collect ALL XP Orbs

Enemies you kill/assist drop XP Orbs. Walk over them. Prioritize wave clear + skirmish participation. Hitting level 15 before the enemy Mage is your #1 objective.

### 2. Confer Godhood: Bless the Right Ally

At level 15: bless ONE ally. They AND you can now level to 25. Always bless:
- **Your hyper-carry ADC** (Hou Yi, Luban No.7, Garo) — 10 extra levels of stats and skill scaling
- **Your fed Jungler** (Luna, Wukong)
- Never bless: the Support, a behind laner, or yourself (auto-self is fine but prioritize carries)

### 3. S1: Defense Shred + Multi-Tick

S1 slows AND **shreds enemy dual defenses.** It also deals multi-tick damage — perfect for stacking Mask of Agony + Sunset Flow. Always S1 before your team follows up.

### 4. S2 → S1: Guaranteed Hit

S2 has a delay. Technique: **S2 first (where they'll be) → S1 immediately.** S1's strong slow makes S2 nearly undodgeable. Reverse the order and S2 misses.

### 5. S3 + Flash: Final Wave Redirect

S3 fires 3 waves. Enemies dodge the first two and think they're safe. **Flash before the 3rd wave** to redirect it onto them. Also: S3 damages turrets — use to siege and defend.

## Signature Combos

### Standard Poke: **S2 → S1 → S3**

1. S2 knockup (predict)
2. S1 slow + defense shred
3. S3 charged through them

### Bush Ambush: **S1 → S2 → S3 + Flash**

1. S1 from bush (slow + shred)
2. S2 knockup (guaranteed because slowed)
3. S3 charge → Flash final wave

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 can clear waves AND damage turrets simultaneously.** Aim through both.
- **XP Orbs persist briefly.** You don't need to collect them mid-fight — grab them after.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in coordinated play with a hyper-carry to bless. Struggles when team has no clear "blessing target."
