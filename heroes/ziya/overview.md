# Ziya（姜子牙） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (XP Accelerator / Siege)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 封神者 (God-Conferrer)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 姜子牙)

> Ziya's rework transformed him from a "utility pick" into the "God-Conferring Mage." Enemies he kills/assists drop **Experience Orbs** — collect them to level faster. At level 15, he can **"Confer Godhood"** on one ally, allowing both Ziya and that ally to **break the level 15 cap and reach level 25.** His S3 can also **damage turrets.**

---

## Core Mechanics

### Experience Orbs: Race to Level 15

Enemies killed near Ziya drop XP Orbs. Collect them to level faster than anyone else. Prioritize farming and skirmishing — hitting level 15 first is your win condition.

### Confer Godhood (Level 15): Who to Bless?

At level 15, bless one ally. They AND Ziya can now level to 25. Priority:
- **#1: Late-game hyper-carry ADC** (Hou Yi, Luban No.7, Garo) — 10 extra levels of stats + skill scaling
- **#2: Jungle hyper-carry** (Luna, Wukong, Han Xin)
- **#3: Scaling Fighter** (Mi Yue, Sikong Zhen) — base-stat-reliant Heroes

Never bless the Support or a behind-early-game Hero.

### S3: Damages Turrets

Ziya's S3 deals damage to structures. Siege and defend towers with S3 from safe range.

### S3 + Flash: Redirect Mid-Channel

S3 fires 3 shockwaves. **Flash before the final wave** to redirect it onto enemies who thought they dodged. Surprise kills from off-screen.
