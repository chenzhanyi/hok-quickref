# Li Bai Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure Physical burst Assassin. Needs **Physical Penetration, Attack, CDR, and Attack Speed (smooth AA mark stacking).**

---

## Recommended Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Standard Assassin jungle item: Attack, Penetration, CDR. |
| 2 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Attack Speed for faster AA mark stacking. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC.* |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core first major item. CDR, Health, flat Penetration. Early gank power spike. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Frost Grip:** S1→AA triggers slow, adds tankiness. **Pure Sky:** active DR for dives, CDR. |
| 5 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | Massive Attack. S3 execute damage on low-HP targets. |
| 6 | **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** | 40% Penetration. Combined with S2 armor shred, even tanks take near-true damage. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Attack Speed (AA smoothness), Physical Penetration, Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Hunt 100-Pen" (CN Standard — 95% of Players)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% Attack Speed, +10% Movement Speed.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Li Bai's AS breakpoints are critical. Missing the threshold makes AA animations too slow. Copy the **CN #1 Li Bai** setup via **Preparation → Recommended** for precision-optimized runes.
