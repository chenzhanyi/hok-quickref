# Li Bai Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Pre-Stack Marks on Jungle Camps

Before ganking, AA jungle camps or minions 2-3 times. S1 dash in (adds 3rd/4th mark), S3 immediately, S1 third cast to retreat. The enemy takes full S3 damage without ever being able to touch you.

### 2. S2 and S3: Two Invulnerability Windows

Both S2 cast animation and S3 are **untargetable**. Use them reactively:
- Zhuge Liang S3 incoming? S2 or S3.
- Daji charm flying at you? S2 or S3.
- Tower shot about to kill you? S2 or S3.
- Li Bai with both up is effectively immortal for ~1.5 seconds.

### 3. S2 Edge = Armor Shred

Enemies touching S2's circle EDGE take bonus damage + **massive Physical Defense reduction**. Don't just use S2 for the dodge — position so the edge clips the enemy before S3 for amplified burst.

### 4. S1 Shadow: Know Where You'll Return

The shadow stays at your FIRST cast position. Always check your shadow before committing. If enemies are camping your shadow, you're dead on return. Sometimes it's better NOT to S3-return — walk away instead.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S1 first, then S2. Take S3 whenever available.** S1 upgrade reduces cooldown significantly — more dashes, more marks, more S3s.

## Signature Combos

### Point-Blank Speed Unlock: **S1 → AA → S1 → AA → S2 → S3 → S1 (return)**

1. S1 dash → stun + AA (1st mark)
2. S1 second dash → stun + AA (2nd mark)
3. S2 edge clip (armor shred + dodge)
4. S3 immediately (4 marks from S1 hits + AAs)
5. S1 third cast → return to shadow

### Pre-Stacked Safe Dive: **AA×2 (on camp) → S1 → S1 → S3 → S1 (return)**

1. AA jungle camp twice (2 marks pre-stacked)
2. S1 dash → S1 second dash (4 marks)
3. S3 burst
4. S1 return
5. Zero risk, full damage

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Attack Speed thresholds matter.** Without enough AS, your AA animations are too slow to stack marks quickly.
- **Watch your shadow.** Smart enemies camp it. If you see them waiting, walk away instead of returning.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile squishies. Struggles vs tanks who can survive his burst and camp his shadow.
