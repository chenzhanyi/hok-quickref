# Li Bai（李白） — Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin (Mobile / Invulnerable Outplay)
- **Lane:** Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 青蓮劍仙 (Blue Lotus Sword Immortal)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 李白)

> Li Bai is an iconic, high-ceiling Assassin known for his unmatched mobility and **invulnerability frames**. His core mechanics: stack 4 marks (Passive) to unlock S3 (untargetable burst), S1 double-dash + return-to-shadow for "hit and run" gameplay, and S2 circle edge for armor shred + dodge. A master Li Bai is untouchable.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Unlock S3

Hitting enemies (AAs + S1) stacks marks. At **4 stacks**, S3 unlocks, grants bonus Attack, and Li Bai becomes **untargetable** during S3's slash animation. S3 must be refreshed within 3 seconds of stacking.

### S1: Double Dash + Shadow Return

Two dashes (each stuns). Third cast: **teleport back to the original shadow position.** This is Li Bai's signature "hit and run" — dive, burst, vanish.

### S2: Circle Edge = Armor Shred + Dodge

S2 draws a circle. **Enemies touching the edge** take bonus damage + massive Physical Defense reduction. Cast animation is also **untargetable** — use to dodge Zhuge Liang S3, Daji charm, tower shots.

### Jungle Camp Stacking

Before ganking: AA jungle camps/minions 2-3 times to pre-stack marks. S1 dash in (3rd-4th mark), S3 immediately. Zero risk, maximum damage.
