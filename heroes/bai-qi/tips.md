# Bai Qi Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S1: Always Hold to Charge

Tap S1 = base damage + healing. **Hold S1 to full charge = 3× damage + 3× healing.** Always charge S1 in teamfights and lane trades. The difference is enormous.

### 2. Fight Inside Crowds (More Hits = More Free S1s)

S1 passive: 20% chance to auto-cast on taking damage. The more enemies/minions hitting you, the more free S1s you get. **Always fight near minion waves or in the middle of enemy teams.**

### 3. S3 → S2: The Perfect CC Chain

S3 Taunts enemies. While they're Taunted: **place S2 at their feet.** When Taunt ends, S2's delay finishes and **pulls them right back.** They never escape.

### 4. Max Energy = +80% Healing

At full Shadow Energy, Bai Qi receives **+80% healing.** S1 charged heals, Eternal Night burst-heal, Blood Rage shield, and even Heal summoner spell are massively amplified. Build Energy before expecting big heals.

### 5. Stack HP — Taunt Duration Scales With It

S3's Taunt duration: every 1000 Bonus HP = +0.2s (max 2.5s). Every HP item is a CC item. Rush high-HP items to maximize your teamfight control.

---

## Signature Combos

### Perfect Engage: **S3 → S2 → S1 (hold) → AA**

1. S3 jump + Taunt the enemy carry (AoE)
2. S2 at their feet during Taunt
3. S1 charge to max → release right as S2 pulls them back
4. AA → they're dead before they regain control

### Lane Trade: **S2 → S1 (charge) → AA**

1. S2 predicts their retreat path (or just zone)
2. S1 full charge through minion wave + enemy
3. Minions hitting you = more free S1 procs = more healing

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Blood Rage shield scales with HP.** At max Energy (+80% healing), Blood Rage's shield is massive — effectively a second health bar.
- **S3 + Flash extends range.** Flash during S3 to surprise-taunt the backline.
- **Matchup:** God-tier as engage tank. Struggles vs True Damage (Lu Bu) that ignores your HP stacking.
