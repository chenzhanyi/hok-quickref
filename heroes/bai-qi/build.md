# Bai Qi Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** S3 Taunt duration scales with **Max HP.** Stack HP + defenses. Two paths: **Clash Lane Smite** (meta) and **Flash Tank.**

---

## Build A: Clash Lane Smite (Recommended)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** | HP stacking per jungle clear. Every HP point = longer S3 Taunt. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Must survive to cast S3. |
| 3 | **Blazing Cape (红莲斗篷)** | HP + burn aura. Burn scales with Max HP. New version also applies mild Grievous Wounds. |
| 4 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Anti-ADC passive. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | **Eternal Night:** burst-heal benefits from +80% healing at max Energy. **Magic Cloak:** vs sustained magic poke. |
| 6 | **Blood Rage (血魔之怒)** | **Tank's divine item!** Massive shield at low HP. At max Energy (+80% healing), the shield is absurdly large. Second life. |
