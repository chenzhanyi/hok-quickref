# Bai Qi（白起） — Tank

## Overview

- **Role:** Tank (Engage / Drain Tank)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 最終兵器 (Ultimate Weapon)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 白起)

> Bai Qi is a quintessential Clash Lane tank (or powerful Support). His core mechanics: taking damage builds **Shadow Energy** — more Energy = more MS + up to **+80% healing received.** His S3 is one of the strongest hard-CC engages in the game: an AoE **Taunt** whose duration scales with **Max HP** (up to 2.5 seconds). He's the team's primary engager, damage sponge, and counter-initiator.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Shadow Energy: More Damage Taken = More Healing

Taking damage or casting abilities builds Shadow Energy. At max Energy: **+MS** and **+80% healing received.** This means S1 healing, Blood Rage shield, and even Heal summoner spell are nearly doubled.

### S1: Hold to Charge (3× Heal at Max)

S1 can be **held to charge.** Full-charge S1 deals **3× damage and 3× healing** compared to tap-cast. Always charge S1 in crowds.

### S1 Passive: 20% Auto-Cast on Damage Taken

When Bai Qi takes damage, there's a **20% chance to auto-cast a free S1.** The more enemies hitting him, the more free S1s he gets. He loves being in the middle of enemy teams and minion waves.

### S3: Taunt Duration = Max HP

S3's Taunt duration scales with **Bonus Max HP** (every 1000 HP = +0.2s, max 2.5s). This is why Bai Qi stacks HP items — more HP = longer Taunt = more teamfight control.

### S2: Delayed Pull (Don't Open With It)

S2 has a long delay. Don't use it raw — enemies walk out. Correct: **S3 Taunt first → S2 at their feet during Taunt → S2 pulls them back as Taunt expires.** Perfect CC chain.
