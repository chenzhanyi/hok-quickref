# Huang Zhong Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs **Critical Rate, Critical Damage, Attack Speed, and Physical Lifesteal**. Current season: "Shadow Blade + Infinity Edge burst."

---

## Recommended Crit-Speed Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early AS for smooth laning and AA kiting. |
| 2 | **Shadow Blade (影刃)** | AS + Crit + MS. On-crit AS boost lets Huang Zhong fire more S3 shots or kite faster on foot. |
| 3 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | **Power spike.** Passive crit stacks + Infinity Edge = every S3 shell is a nuke. |
| 4 | **Blood Weeper (泣血之刃)** | High Physical Lifesteal. In S3 turret mode, hitting multiple enemies heals to full HP instantly. Swap to **Sanction Blade (制裁之刃)** vs heavy heal comps (Cai Yan, Cheng Yaojin). |
| 5 | **Dawn (破晓)** | Marksman ultimate item. AS, Crit, doubled Penetration for ranged. Tanks become paper. |
| 6 | **Sun Bow (逐日之弓)** → Late: swap for **Sage's Sanctuary / Fate's Edge** | Sun Bow: active +Range for S3 AND walking AAs. Late game: rotate resurrection + immunity items. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Capped Critical Damage (250% limit), initial Penetration, early Lifesteal.**

### Setup 1: "3 Peerless + Lifesteal Hybrid" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×3 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×5 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×5 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +10.9% Critical Rate, +10.8% Critical Damage (caps at 250%), +64 Physical Penetration, +8% Physical Lifesteal, +5% MS, +5% AS.
