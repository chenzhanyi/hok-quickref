# Huang Zhong Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Stack Passive to 5 Before Fighting

AA hits grant Attack + Critical Rate (max 5 stacks). Before any fight, AA minions or jungle monsters to 5 stacks. Then S3 — your cannon damage is nearly doubled.

### 2. S1 = Instant S3 Cancel

When an Assassin dives you during S3 turret mode: **tap S1** (NOT S3 again). S1 instantly packs up the turret AND grants Movement Speed. This is the difference between living and dying.

### 3. S2 Mine: Armor Shred Wins Duels

S2 reduces enemy Physical Armor by **25%** AND grants Huang Zhong a shield. In any 1v1, drop S2 at your feet (guaranteed hit) → the armor shred + shield makes you win almost every duel.

### 4. Don't Blindly S3 — Know When to Walk

Late-game Huang Zhong's walking AAs also deal massive damage. Only S3 when:
- Your team is sieging a tower
- Defending high ground
- Enemy CC abilities are on cooldown
- You're in a safe bush (invisible bombardment)

### 5. Bush Turret = Invisible Death

S3 from a bush. As long as the outer edge hits enemies without revealing your position, **your S3 circle is invisible to them**. They take damage without knowing where it's coming from.

---

## Signature Combos

### Lane Duel: **AA×2 → S2 → AA**

1. AA twice (stack passive)
2. Enemy commits → drop S2 at their feet (armor shred + your shield)
3. Continue AA — the combined advantage wins the duel

### Perfect Siege: **AA×5 (on minions) → S2 (bush vision) → S3 → S1 (escape)**

1. Stack 5 passive on minion wave
2. S2 mine in flank bush (anti-gank early warning)
3. S3 turret → bombard tower
4. When enemies collapse: **S1 instant cancel → sprint away**

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3's range is your biggest strength.** Position so enemies are at the EDGE of your circle — you can hit them, they can't hit you.
- **Never S3 in the open without your team.** You're immobile — Lu Bu, Gan & Mo, and Zhong Kui will delete you.
- **Matchup:** God-tier with protection (Zhang Fei, Ming). Struggles vs long-range artillery that out-ranges your turret.
