## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Stack Before Fighting

AA hits stack Attack + Critical Rate (max 5 stacks). **Before teamfighting or using S3, AA minions/monsters to 5 stacks first.** Your S3 damage is dramatically higher with full stacks.

### S1: Instant S3 Cancel (Core Survival)

S1 grants Movement Speed AND **instantly cancels S3's turret mode**. When an Assassin dives you during S3, don't slowly press S3 again — **tap S1** to instantly pack up and sprint away.

### S2: Mine = Armor Shred + Shield

S2 mines provide vision, **reduce enemy Physical Armor by 25%**, and grant Huang Zhong a shield. During duels, drop S2 at your feet or on the enemy — the armor shred + shield makes you win almost any 1v1 standoff.

### S3: Don't Blindly Turret

New players see enemies and immediately S3, becoming a free target for Lu Bu, Gan & Mo, and Zhong Kui. **Late-game Huang Zhong's walking AAs also deal massive damage.** Use S3 primarily for: tower sieges, high-ground defense, or when enemy CC is on cooldown — from a safe bush.

### Bush Turret: Invisible Bombardment

If you S3 from a bush and the outer edge hits enemies without revealing you, **your S3 radius circle is invisible to enemies**. This "invisible turret" creates devastating ambushes.
