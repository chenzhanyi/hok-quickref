# Cirrus（雲中君） — Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin (Aerial Diver / Displacer)
- **Lane:** Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 流雲之翼 (Wings of Flowing Cloud)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 云中君)

> Cirrus received a major rework. He no longer has the "Breath" limitation (no more grounded "walking chicken"). He now has **permanent flight** and a reworked ultimate with a hard **"grab-and-carry" CC mechanic**. At level 4+, he ignores all terrain permanently.

---

## Core Mechanics (Post-Rework)

### Permanent Flight & Terrain Ignore

From level 4 onward, Cirrus **permanently flies** and can pass through all walls. His jungle clear, rotation, and flank speed are among the fastest in the game.

### Tear Stacks (Passive)

Basic attacks stack **Tear (bleed)** on enemies. At 4 stacks, the target takes continuous damage and Cirrus **recovers HP**. Tear damage can **critically strike**.

### Ultimate: Sky Dive + Grab (S3)

Cirrus ascends into the sky (**untargetable**), then dives down to **grab one enemy Hero, carrying them into the air** (similar to Fuzi's rope + Donghuang's suppress). He can **move the grabbed enemy a short distance**. This is the ultimate tower-dive, backline-kidnap, and peel tool.
