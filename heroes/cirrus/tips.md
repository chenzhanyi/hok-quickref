# Cirrus Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Permanent Flight (Level 4+)

From level 4, Cirrus **never lands**. He can fly through all terrain, making him the fastest jungler for rotations and flanking. Rush level 4 as quickly as possible.

### 2. Tear Stacks = Crit Bleed

Basic attacks stack **Tear** (max 4 stacks). At 4 stacks: continuous bleed damage + HP recovery for Cirrus. **Tear damage can crit** — paired with Infinity Edge, every bleed tick becomes a massive critical hit.

### 3. Ultimate: Grab & Carry (S3)

S3 makes Cirrus **untargetable** as he ascends, then dives to **grab one enemy Hero and carry them into the air**. You can **move the grabbed enemy a short distance**. This is the strongest displacement tool in the game — use it to:
- **Kidnap squishies into your team:** Grab → pull toward your allies → instant deletion
- **Tower dive execute:** Grab enemy under tower → carry them OUT of tower range → finish
- **Peel for carries:** Grab diving Assassins off your backline

### 4. S1: Speed Boost & Engage

Hold S1 for continuous Movement Speed + Slow Immunity. Release to swoop forward. Use to close distance instantly. Hitting enemies reduces their damage against you.

### 5. S2: Ranged Feather Volley

Fires feathers in a cone — ranged damage + slow. Use to stack Tear from range before committing.

### 6. Skill Priority

**Max S1 first, then S2. Take ultimate whenever available.**

## Signature Combos

### Long-Range Tower Dive: **S1 → S2 → S3 → AA×4**

1. S1 charge in at max speed
2. S2 ranged slow + stack Tear
3. S3 ascend (untargetable) → grab enemy under tower → carry them OUT
4. Land → basic attack to 4 stacks → Tear bleed finishes them

### Bush Ambush: **AA → S2 → AA → S1 → S3 → AA**

1. Start with auto-attack from bush
2. S2 immediately — 4 Tear stacks applied
3. Enemy Flashes/dashes → S1 pursuit
4. S3 grab to finish or pull them back

### Reverse Kidnap (S3 direction control): **S3 grab → Left Joystick toward team**

After grabbing with S3, immediately pull your left joystick **toward your team/tower**. The grabbed enemy moves with you — drop them into certain death.

## Advanced Tips

- **Pre-level-4 play safe:** Cirrus is vulnerable before permanent flight. Farm efficiently, avoid early skirmishes.
- **Tear crit is your power spike:** Rush Infinity Edge after Shadow Axe. It transforms your bleed from poke to execution.
- **S3 untargetable frames:** Use to dodge key abilities (Daji S2, Angela ultimate, tower shots).
- **Matchup:** Destroys immobile squishies (Lu Ban No.7, Xiao Qiao). Struggles vs instant CC (Zhang Liang S3) that can lock him during S1 swoop.
