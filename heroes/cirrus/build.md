# Cirrus Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Post-rework Cirrus is a Physical Assassin. His Tear bleed damage fully benefits from **Physical Penetration** and **Critical Rate** (bleed CAN crit). High Attack Speed helps stack Tear faster. Build path: **Crit + Penetration**.

---

## Recommended Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Standard Assassin jungle item: Attack, Penetration, CDR. |
| 2 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early Attack Speed for smoother kiting and faster Tear stacking. *Late game: swap to Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴) vs heavy CC.* |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core squishy-killer: CDR, Health, flat Physical Penetration. |
| 4 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | **Cirrus's power-spike item.** Tear bleed damage CAN crit. Infinity Edge transforms every bleed tick into massive critical numbers. |
| 5 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** or **Shadow Blade (影刃)** | **Master's Power:** enhanced AA after abilities for burst. **Shadow Blade:** Attack Speed + crit synergy for sustained Tear stacking. |
| 6 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** or **Fate's Edge (名刀·司命)** | Survival slot. S3 is untargetable but you're vulnerable on landing. Pure Sky's active DR ensures you survive the landing. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Initial Critical Rate (synergy with Infinity Edge), Physical Penetration, Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Crit 100-Pen" (Highest Winrate)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×6 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×4 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×7 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** ~86 Physical Penetration, +25 Physical Attack, **+6.4% Critical Rate**, +4.8% Physical Lifesteal, +7% Movement Speed. The early crit chance creates "faith crits" in early jungle skirmishes.

### Setup 2: "Max Speed Kiting"

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** **+100 Physical Penetration**, +29 Physical Attack, **+10% Attack Speed**, +10% Movement Speed. Maximum aerial glide speed and seamless auto-attack chaining.
