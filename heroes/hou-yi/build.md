# Hou Yi Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Stack **Attack Speed + Critical Rate**. Late-game god. Needs dedicated protection.

---

## Recommended Crit-Speed Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early AS for smoother passive stacking. |
| 2 | **Lightning Dagger (闪电匕首)** or **Shadow Blade (影刃)** | **Lightning Dagger:** chain lightning for fast wave clear. **Shadow Blade:** on-crit AS + MS (CN meta standard). |
| 3 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | Crit damage spike. Hou Yi starts solo-killing here. |
| 4 | **Blood Weeper (泣血之刃)** | 25% Physical Lifesteal. With triple arrows + S1 multi-target, heal to full HP in seconds. |
| 5 | **Dawn (破晓)** | Marksman core. AS, Crit, doubled Penetration. Tank-shredding comes online. |
| 6 | **Sun Bow (逐日之弓)** or **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** / **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | **Sun Bow:** +Range active for safe teamfighting. **Defense:** vs dive-heavy teams. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Critical Rate, Attack Speed thresholds, early Lifesteal (skip early lifesteal item).**

### Setup 1: "5 Reave 5 Hunt" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×3 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×5 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×5 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +13.3% Critical Rate, +7% Critical Damage, +64 Physical Penetration, +5% AS, +5% MS, **+8% Physical Lifesteal**.

### Setup 2: "10 Reave" (Max Sustain — Solo Queue)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×3 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×10 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +13.3% Critical Rate, +7% Critical Damage, +64 Physical Penetration, **+16% Physical Lifesteal**. You start with a mini lifesteal item. Best for solo queue without a Support.
