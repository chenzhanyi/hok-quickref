# Hou Yi（后羿） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Turret / Sustained DPS)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 半神之弓 (Demigod's Bow)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 后羿)

> Hou Yi is the quintessential "stand-and-deliver" Marksman. He has terrifying sustained DPS and a global-range S3 engage/support tool, but zero mobility. His passive splits his AA into 3 arrows at max stacks, and S1 turns that into multi-target carnage. Positioning and team protection are everything.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Triple Arrows (Max Stacks)

AA hits grant AS stacks (max 3). At 3 stacks: **AAs split into 3 arrows**, each triggering on-hit effects. **Always stack to 3 before teamfighting.** Pre-stack on minions/monsters, then maintain stacks by weaving "AA → step → AA."

### S1: Multi-Target Onslaught

S1 makes AAs hit multiple nearby enemies. **Always activate S1 AFTER reaching 3 passive stacks** for maximum arrow count and damage.

### S3: Global Stun (Scales with Distance)

S3 fires a global-range arrow. **The farther it travels, the longer the stun (up to 3.5s).** Use for: cross-map initiation, stealing objectives, or point-blank self-peel when dived.

### S2: Vision Tool

S2 reveals the target area. Use to check bushes, scout Dragon/Baron pit, or slow fleeing enemies.
