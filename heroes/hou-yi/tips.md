# Hou Yi Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Always Stack Passive to 3 Before Fighting

Without 3 passive stacks, Hou Yi's damage is halved. Before any fight: AA minions or jungle monsters 3 times. Then **AA → step → AA → step** to maintain stacks while repositioning.

### 2. S1 AFTER 3 Stacks, Never Before

S1 splits arrows to multiple targets. **Activate S1 ONLY after passive is at 3 stacks.** The combined arrow count (3 passive × S1 multi-target) is your maximum DPS. Opening with S1 at 0 stacks wastes the steroid.

### 3. S3: Point-Blank Self-Peel

When an Assassin (Lanling Wang, Ake) dives you, **S3 their face.** The stun is short at close range but long enough to interrupt their combo. Then Flash away or AA them down with lifesteal.

### 4. S3 Cross-Map: The Longer, The Better

S3 stun scales with travel distance (max 3.5s). Watch the minimap constantly. Fire S3 from bot lane toward mid/top fights. A 3-second stun from across the map wins teamfights before you arrive.

### 5. S2: Free Vision

S2 reveals the target area. Use to: check Dragon/Baron pit, scout bushes before face-checking, or slow fleeing enemies.

## Signature Combos

### Duel/Burst: **AA×3 (stack passive) → S1 → AA spam → S2 (slow) → S3 (finish)**

1. Pre-stack 3 passive on minions
2. S1 + AA spam at max arrows
3. S2 slow when they try to flee
4. S3 to secure the kill

### Self-Peel vs Assassin: **S3 (point-blank) → S2 (slow) → AA + S1**

1. Assassin dives → S3 their face (stun)
2. S2 at your feet (slow + vision)
3. AA + S1 lifesteal through their damage

---

## Advanced Tips

- **AA → Step is mandatory.** Standing still = dead. Weave movement between every 1-2 shots.
- **S3 can steal objectives.** Time it when Dragon/Baron is low.
- **Matchup:** God-tier with protection. Struggles when team has no frontline.
