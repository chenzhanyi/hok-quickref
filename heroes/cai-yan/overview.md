# Cai Yan（蔡文姬） — Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Support (Pure Healer)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 天籟絃音 (Heavenly Strings)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 蔡文姬; nickname: "Nanny")

> Cai Yan is a strategically vital pure-healing Support. Her entire kit scales with **Magic Attack** — more AP = bigger heals. Her signature S2 "Bounce Music" stuns enemies by ricocheting between them, and her S3 provides massive single-target healing + dual-defense buffs. She's the ultimate babysitter for hyper-carries.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Damage = Speed + Heal

When Cai Yan takes damage, she gains **+70% Movement Speed and HP regen for 1 second** (10s cooldown). Intentionally let jungle monsters or minions hit you to trigger this for faster rotations.

### S2: Bounce Music (Signature CC)

Fires a sound wave that bounces between enemies (Minions, Monsters, Heroes), **stunning on each bounce** (max 2 bounces per target). Never aim directly at mobile Heroes — **aim at tanks or minion waves** instead. The ricochet will auto-target the backline.

### S3: Don't Wait for Low HP

S3 provides massive single-target healing AND **huge Physical + Magic Defense buffs**. When an Assassin dives your carry, cast S3 immediately — **even if they're at full HP.** The defense buff negates the enemy's burst window.

### S1: Group Heal + Speed

AoE healing + **+20% Movement Speed** to nearby allies. Use constantly in teamfights.
