# Cai Yan Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S2: Never Aim Directly at Mobile Heroes

S2 bounces between enemies. Direct shots at mobile targets miss. Instead: **aim at the enemy tank or an approaching minion wave.** The bounce auto-targets squishies behind them. This is the #1 Cai Yan skill gap.

### 2. S3: Cast When Carry Is Full HP (Not When Low)

S3 grants massive **Physical + Magic Defense buffs** on top of healing. When an Assassin (Jing, Lanling Wang, Nakoruru) dives your ADC, **cast S3 immediately even if they're at 100% HP.** The defense buff absorbs all the burst. Waiting until they're low means they might die before the heal ticks.

### 3. Passive: Trigger It Intentionally

Taking damage grants **+70% MS + HP regen for 1s** (10s CD). When rotating or escaping, let a jungle monster or minion hit you once — the speed boost is huge.

### 4. AP Scaling = Bigger Heals

Cai Yan's heals scale massively with Magic Attack. A Savant's Wrath makes your S1+S3 nearly double in healing power. Don't build pure tank — you need AP.

### 5. Anti-Heal Awareness (Crucial)

When enemies buy **Sanction Blade (制裁之刃)** or **Nightmare Fang (梦魇之牙)**, your healing is **cut by 50%**. Look for the purple broken-heart icon on allies. When you see it:
- **Kite and stall** — don't burn S1/S3 during Grievous Wounds
- Wait the ~3 seconds for the debuff to expire
- **Then burst-heal** your team back to full

## Signature Combo

### Teamfight Cycle: **S2 → S1 → S3**

1. **S2** at enemy tank/minion wave → ricochet stun the backline
2. **S1** AoE heal + speed boost your team
3. **S3** on your carry when the enemy Assassin engages (defense buff + massive heal)

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S1 grants 20% MS.** Use it to help your team chase or disengage, not just for healing.
- **S2 max bounces = 2 per target.** Position so the wave passes through as many enemies as possible.
- **Matchup:** God-tier with hyper-carries. Struggles vs anti-heal itemization.
