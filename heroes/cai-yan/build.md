# Cai Yan Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Healing scales with **Magic Attack**. More AP = bigger heals. CN server consensus: **high-AP semi-tank** — big heals without getting one-shot.

---

## Recommended Support Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎)** | Shadow Aegis aura: AS + CDR + Mana regen. **Redemption active:** group shield — stacks with heals for double lifesaving. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Cai Yan must survive to heal. Tenacity prevents CC-lock. |
| 3 | **Holy Grail (圣杯)** | **Core sustain item.** 15% CDR + infinite Mana. When Mana is full, converts to %HP regen. Never recall for Mana again. |
| 4 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | **Power spike!** Percentage AP boost makes S1/S3 healing effectively double. The defense buff from S3 also scales harder. |
| 5 | **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | AP + Max HP. Converts AP to dual defenses — with Savant's Wrath, Cai Yan becomes as tanky as a full-tank while healing like a dedicated healer. |
| 6 | **Cocoon Breaker (破茧之衣)** or **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | **Cocoon Breaker:** more AP + damage reduction. **Ominous Premonition:** vs heavy Physical dive. |

## Anti-Heal Counterplay

When enemies build Sanction Blade or Nightmare Fang, your healing is halved. Watch for the **purple broken-heart icon** on allies. During the debuff (~3s): kite and stall. After it expires: burst S1+S3 to full-heal your team.
