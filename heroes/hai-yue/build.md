# Hai Yue Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** High AP scaling. Needs **Magic Penetration + AP + CDR** (Moon Disc regen). S3 shields scale with AP.

---

## Recommended Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR for faster Moon Disc regeneration. |
| 2 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | +MS + AoE explosion on S1 hit. Longer S3 link range from MS. |
| 3 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP spike. S1 True Damage + S3 shields both skyrocket. |
| 4 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Penetration vs late-game Magic Cloak. |
| 5 | **Ardent Dominator (炽热支配者)** or **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | **Ardent Dominator:** auto-cleanse shield at low HP — can save you for S3 cast. **Mask:** sustained burn. |
| 6 | **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** | Max AP + damage amp. S1 chunks 50% HP. S3 becomes unwinnable for the pulled target. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Magic Penetration, Movement Speed, mild sustain.**

### Setup 1: "Hunt 88-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +16% AS, **+10% MS**. Fastest rotations + S3 link positioning.

### Setup 2: "Sustain Hybrid" (Safer Laning)
