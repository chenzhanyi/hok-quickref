# Hai Yue Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Burst, Then Kite (Moon Disc Management)

Moon Discs are limited (3 charges). Dump S1+S2 for burst → kite until discs regenerate → re-engage. Never stay in extended AA trades with 0 discs.

### 2. S1 First (Mark) → S2 (Root)

S2 only roots marked targets. Always: S1 (apply mark) → S2 (guaranteed root). Then S1 again (True Damage on marked target). The 121 combo is your core trade.

### 3. S3: Who to Pull?

- **Winning:** S3 the enemy ADC/Mage. Kill them in the pocket dimension. Your team wins the 4v4 outside.
- **Losing/Objective:** S3 the enemy Jungler (stealing Dragon). Remove them from the pit. Your team steals.
- **Protecting:** S3 the Assassin diving your ADC. They're gone for several seconds.

### 4. S3: Use the Bonus Dash

Inside the pocket dimension, you have a bonus dash. Dash through the arena walls to kite the enemy. You have the advantage — shields + CDR + dash.

### 5. S3: Stall Even If You Can't Kill

Against a fed enemy you can't beat: S3 them anyway. You survive for several seconds inside the dimension. That's several seconds they're NOT in the teamfight. Trade your life for your team's win.

## Signature Combos

### Core Trade: **S1 → S2 → S1 → AA**

1. S1 mark (apply Glass Mark)
2. S2 root (guaranteed on marked target)
3. S1 again (True Damage on marked target)
4. Enhanced AA

### Pull + Kill: **S1 → S2 (root) → S3 → S1 → S2**

1. S1 mark + S2 root (they can't dodge S3 link)
2. S3 pull into pocket dimension
3. Inside: S1 + S2 spam with CDR
4. Bonus dash to kite

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 link can be broken by extreme range.** Root them first with S2 to prevent escape.
- **Moon Disc regeneration speed scales with CDR.** Calm Boots help.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs fed single carries. Struggles vs teams with multiple threats (can only isolate one).
