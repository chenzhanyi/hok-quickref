# Hai Yue（海月） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (1v1 Duelist / Isolation Control)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 水月鏡華 (Water Moon Mirror)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (海月)

> Hai Yue is a strategically powerful control/isolation Mage. Her signature S3 **"Water Moon Illusion"** forcibly pulls an enemy into a **1v1 pocket dimension** — removing them from the teamfight. Inside, she gains massive shields and CDR, turning unfavorable fights around. Her Moon Disc energy system gives her burst windows with downtime between.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Moon Discs (3 Charges)

S1 and S2 consume Moon Discs (max 3). When all 3 are depleted: **cooldown period before they regenerate.** Hai Yue plays in bursts — dump all abilities → kite → re-engage.

### S1: Long-Range Poke + True Damage

S1 fires a long-range projectile. **If the target has a Glass Mark, deals bonus True Damage.** Main poke and wave-clear tool.

### S2: Mark + Root

S2 fires 3 orbs (grants small shield + MS). **First orb roots enemies with a Glass Mark.** Apply marks with S1, then S2 for guaranteed root.

### S3: 1v1 Pocket Dimension (The Signature)

S3 pulls a targeted enemy into an isolated arena. Inside: **Hai Yue gains massive shields + CDR + a bonus dash ability.** Uses: assassinate fed carries (winning games), remove enemy Jungler from objective fights (stealing Dragons), or isolate a diver from your backline.
