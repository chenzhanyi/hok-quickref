# Daji Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure single-target burst. No sustained DPS. One combo = one kill. Needs **max AP, Magic Penetration, and Movement Speed** for bush rotations.

---

## Recommended Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Arcane Boots (秘法之靴)** | Flat Magic Penetration. Combined with passive, enemy squishy MR drops to zero. *Late: swap to Calm Boots for CDR.* |
| 2 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | Core burst. AoE explosion on hit. +7% MS for faster bush rotations. |
| 3 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP spike. After this, 231 deletes any squishy below 70% HP. |
| 4 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Penetration vs late-game Magic Cloak. |
| 5 | **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** | Max AP + percentage damage amp. Every S3 foxfire hits like a truck. |
| 6 | **Splendor (辉月)** or **Ardent Dominator (炽热支配者)** | **Splendor:** Stasis during ~5s post-combo vulnerability window. **Ardent Dominator:** auto-cleanse shield at low HP for slower reactions. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Magic Penetration, Movement Speed, mild sustain.**

## Quick In-Game Setup

Daji's combo is simple but positioning is everything. Copy the **CN #1 Daji** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**. Spend more time in bushes than in lane.
