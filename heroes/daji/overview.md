# Daji（妲己） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Single-Target Burst / Bush Ambusher)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★☆☆☆☆
- **Title:** 魅力之狐 (Charming Fox)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 妲己)

> Daji is the game's most classic pure single-target burst Mage. Her kit is simple and brutal: S2 is an **undodgeable lock-on stun**, S3 fires 5 homing foxfires, and her passive **shreds enemy Magic Defense** per ability hit. Her entire playstyle revolves around bush camping and one-combo deletion. Mid-late game, a fed Daji turns any unwarded bush into a death sentence.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Magic Defense Shred

Each ability hit reduces the target's Magic Defense (max 3 stacks). The more hits in a combo, the closer to True Damage. **This is why combo ORDER matters.**

### S2: Undodgeable Lock-On Stun

S2 is a point-and-click stun — **impossible to dodge without invulnerability frames.** Use to: stop divers (Ma Chao, Guan Yu, Lam), initiate kills, or interrupt channeled abilities.

### S3: Foxfires Split Between Targets

S3 fires 5 foxfires. **If multiple enemies are nearby, they split — drastically reducing single-target damage.** Always ensure your target is ISOLATED (no minions, no nearby allies) before S3.

### Bush Camping = Your Identity

Daji is weak in open teamfights. Her power lies in **bush ambushes.** Clear wave → rotate to enemy jungle or river bushes → wait. One passing squishy = one kill.
