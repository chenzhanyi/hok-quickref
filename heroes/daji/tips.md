# Daji Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Combo Order Matters (Passive Shred)

Passive shreds Magic Defense per hit. Correct order: **S2 → S3 → S1.** S2 stuns. S3's 5 foxfires stack the debuff to max. S1 finishes with near-True Damage. Wrong order (S2 → S1 → S3) does significantly less damage.

### 2. Drag S2 — Don't Auto-Cast

Auto-casting S2 targets the nearest enemy (usually the tank). In teamfights, **drag S2's cast wheel** onto the enemy ADC or Mage. This is the #1 skill gap for Daji players.

### 3. Isolate Before S3

S3's foxfires split between all nearby targets. Before casting S3: ensure your target is **alone** — no minions, no monsters, no other enemy Heroes nearby. A split S3 can't kill.

### 4. Bush Camp: Your True Power

Daji's lane presence is weak. Her strength is **bush ambushes.** Clear mid wave → rotate to enemy Red/Blue buff bush or river bush → wait. Enemy squishy passes = S2 → S3 → S1 → dead. Repeat.

### 5. S1 + Flash: Surprise Finisher

S1 has a brief cast delay. Press S1 → **Flash forward** during the animation. The wave extends to your Flash destination. Surprise kills on fleeing enemies.

## Signature Combos

### Standard Delete: **S2 → S3 → S1**

1. S2 lock-on stun (guaranteed)
2. S3 5 foxfires (stacks passive to max)
3. S1 (near-True Damage finisher)
Optimal for 95% of situations.

### Bush Speed Kill: **S3 → S2 → S1**

1. S3 first (longer range — fires before they react)
2. S2 mid-S3 (stun lands while foxfires travel)
3. S1 finish
Enemies with escape skills (Arli, Li Bai) can't react.

## Advanced Tips

- **S2 interrupts dashes.** Time it vs Lam, Guan Yu, Ma Chao mid-charge.
- **Post-combo, you're vulnerable for ~5 seconds.** Hide in a bush until cooldowns return.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs solo squishies. Struggles vs tanky teams that body-block and survive the combo.
