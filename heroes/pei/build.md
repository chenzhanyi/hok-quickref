# Pei Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pei needs **Physical Penetration, Attack Speed, and the Siege Breaker execute threshold**. He's a hybrid Marksman-Assassin — builds reflect his dual-form playstyle. CN server consensus: **lightning burst + on-hit**.

---

## Recommended Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Standard Assassin jungle item: Attack, CDR, Penetration. |
| 2 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Attack Speed dramatically smooths Human form kiting and jungle clear. *Mid-late: swap to Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴) vs heavy CC.* |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core squishy-killer: CDR, Health, flat Physical Penetration. |
| 4 | **Doomsday (末世)** | **Pei's core item!** Human form's rapid AA and Tiger form's enhanced AA perfectly trigger Doomsday's 8% Current HP on-hit damage. Also provides essential Physical Lifesteal sustain. |
| 5 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** | "Power Strike" passive procs on the first AA after transforming — this single hit chunks 50%+ of squishy HP. Bonus MS on proc for chase/escape. |
| 6 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Siege Breaker:** snowballing — Tiger S1 execute threshold becomes lethal. **Pure Sky:** even games — active DR for survival. **Star Breaker (碎星锤):** vs armor stacking. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Attack Speed, Movement Speed, Physical Penetration.** All three are mandatory.

### Setup 1: "Hunt 100-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% Attack Speed, **+10% Movement Speed**.

### Setup 2: "AS Threshold Mix" (Smoother AA Feel)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×8 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |

| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +100 Physical Penetration, **+13.2% Attack Speed**, +10% Movement Speed, +1% Critical Rate. Hits a smoother AA breakpoint for early-game invades.

### Setup 3: "Reave Sustain" (Solo Jungle)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
