# Pei（裴擒虎） — Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin (Early-Game Jungle Dominator)
- **Lane:** Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 六合虎拳 (Six-Harmony Tiger Fist)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 裴擒虎; nickname: "Tiger")

> Pei is the ultimate early-game jungle dominator. He starts with dual forms at level 1 (Human form = ranged poke; Tiger form = melee burst + execute). No need to wait for level 4 — his entire game plan is **invading enemy jungle at level 1-2**, snowballing economy, and crushing the enemy team before they can scale.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Dual Forms at Level 1

- **Human Form:** Ranged. AA deals bonus Magic Damage (ramps up). S2 grants shield + Attack Speed. Use for: poking, kiting, tower pushing.
- **Tiger Form:** Melee. Enhanced burst. S2 can wall-jump with a second cast. S1 is an **execute** (more damage at lower HP). Use for: gap-close, burst, finishing kills.

### Tiger S2: Wall Jump Double-Bounce

Tiger S2 charges toward a wall. **Press S2 again or pull joystick on wall contact** for a second, longer jump with double damage. This is Pei's signature mobility tool — wall-hop ganks, jungle invades, and escapes.

### Tiger S1: Execute — Never Open With It

Tiger S1 deals bonus damage based on **target's missing HP**. NEVER use it at the start of a combo — it's your finishing move.
