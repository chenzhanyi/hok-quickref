# Pei Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Level 1: Invade or Die Trying

Pei is one of the strongest level-1 Heroes. At match start:
- **If your Mid/Support are strong early:** immediately invade enemy Red/Blue
- **If your team is weak early:** farm your own jungle, but at level 2 (both forms ready) immediately contest river crab or gank side lane

Pei's win condition is **choking the enemy jungler before level 4**. Don't afk farm.

### 2. Tiger S2 Wall-Jump (Signature Mobility)

Tiger S2 charges toward a wall. **Press S2 again or pull joystick on contact** for a second, longer jump. This lets Pei:
- Wall-hop for creative gank angles
- Escape over walls after invading
- Chase fleeing enemies across terrain

Practice the timing — it's Pei's highest-skill mechanic.

### 3. Tiger S1 = Execute, NEVER Opener

Tiger S1 deals damage based on **target's missing HP**. Always use it LAST in your combo — it's an execute, not a poke tool. Opening with S1 wastes most of its damage.

### 4. Push Towers After Ganks

Pei is a Marksman in disguise. After a successful gank, **don't go farm your jungle — push the tower**. Human form S2 grants Attack Speed. Your tower damage is surprisingly high.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take ultimate whenever available.** Human S2 = shield + AS. Tiger S2 = mobility + burst. Level 2 gives more value than any other skill.

## Signature Combos

### Long-Range Ambush: **Human S2 → S3 (transform) → Tiger S2 (wall-jump) → AA → S1**

1. Human S2 shield + AS buff
2. Transform to Tiger (bonus MS + enhanced AA ready)
3. Tiger S2 wall-jump to close distance (double-bounce if possible)
4. Enhanced AA (bite) on landing
5. Tiger S1 execute to finish

### Melee Brawl: **Tiger S2 → AA → S1 → S3 (transform) → Human AA → S1**

1. Tiger S2 jump in → AA
2. S1 if they're low enough
3. Transform back to Human if they start kiting
4. Human AA + S1 ranged poke while walking backward

## Advanced Tips

- **Red Buff is mandatory on Pei.** The slow + true damage burn makes Human form kiting unescapable.
- **Tower pushing is your secondary job.** Human S2 + AS melts towers. Don't just jungle — siege.
- **Late game fall-off:** Pei is an early-mid game hero. If the game goes past 15 minutes, shift to Human form poke and tower pressure.
- **Matchup:** Destroys scaling junglers who need level 4. Struggles vs early-game duelists who can match his invade.
