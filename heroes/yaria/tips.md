# Yaria Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Body-Block CC with Passive (Game-Changing)

Yaria's passive triggers on **stun, airborne, or knockback** — she becomes an invulnerable Deer Spirit that slows nearby enemies. **Intentionally eat enemy CC** before level 4:
- Walk into Zhong Kui's hook
- Face-tank Daji's charm
- Absorb Mozi's cannon
Then transform to deer and escape — you just wasted a critical enemy cooldown for free.

### 2. Deer → Human Reset Trick

In Deer form, **press S1 to instantly return to human form** — this **refreshes ALL human-form cooldowns**. Advanced rotation: dump S1+S2 → eat CC → deer form → S1 (transform back) → immediately dump S1+S2 again. Two full rotations in 2 seconds.

### 3. Proactive Detach (The #1 Skill Gap)

Yaria's S3 shield has a long cooldown (~12s+) if it breaks. But **manually detaching before break = only ~50% cooldown** (~5-6s). **Constantly watch the yellow shield bar on your ally** — jump off before it pops.

### 4. S1 Bush-Check

S1 has two-stage CC (first stage: knockback; second stage: auto-tracking projectile that detects invisibility and stuns at endpoint). When approaching any suspicious bush, throw S1 first — if enemies are inside, the orb will **curve toward them automatically**.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S1 first, then S2. Take ultimate whenever available.** S1 is Yaria's only hard CC and bush-check tool — max it first.

## Signature Combo

### Protect & Counter-Engage: **S1 → S3 (attach) → S2 → Watch Shield → S3 (detach) → S1**

1. S1 knockup enemies near your carry
2. S3 attach (shield applied)
3. S2 auto-attack enemies nearby
4. **Watch shield bar** — detach before it breaks
5. S1 comes off cooldown — knockup again for double peel

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Tower shots and true damage ignore Yaria's shield** — detach before tower diving.
- **Don't attach permanently.** Detach to body-block, bush-check, and reposition.
- **Know when NOT to attach:** If your ally is about to take fatal burst, attaching shares the death. Sometimes staying separate is safer.
- **Matchup:** Excels protecting immobile hyper-carries. Struggles against instant-burst Assassins who kill faster than you can detach.
