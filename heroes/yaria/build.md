# Yaria Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Yaria's S3 true shield scales with **Max Health + Magic Attack**. Since she's attached most of the time, conventional resistances (armor, magic resist) don't apply to the shield. CN server consensus: **pure Max Health stacking**.

---

## Recommended Support Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis (极影)** or **Guard (近卫)** | **Shadow Aegis:** if allies need Attack Speed + CDR (Marco, Gongsun Li, Zhuge Liang). **Guard:** if allies need tankiness. Keep at tier 2 until core items are done. |
| 2 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | Frequent shield refreshes and S1 CC. *Late game: swap to Resistance Boots to avoid getting CC-locked when detached.* |
| 3 | **Frost Breath (凝冰之息)** | **Yaria's soul damage item.** S2 auto-attacks nearby enemies while attached — paired with Frost Breath, this creates **continuous AoE slow** around your ally, creating devastating kiting and chase potential. |
| 4 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** | Highest pure Max Health item in the game. Directly scales S3 shield into a massive HP wall. Out-of-combat %HP regen lets Yaria heal to full without recalling after detaching. |
| 5 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Personal survival when not in Deer form. Prevents getting one-shot by Assassins before passive triggers. |
| 6 | **Wings of Redemption (救赎之翼)** or **Rising Sun (旭日初光)** | Second support item. **Redemption:** group shield after detaching — double insurance. **Rising Sun:** shields lowest ally on ability hit. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Yaria needs **zero Penetration**. Core stats: **Max Health (1000+ HP), CDR, Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Thousand-HP Harmony Tank" (95% of Players)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, Cooldown Reduction +0.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** **+1,162 Max Health**, +23 Physical Defense, +4% Movement Speed, +6% CDR, +10% Attack Speed.

### Setup 2: "Max CDR Aggressive" (Frequent Gank Support)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |
| 🟢 Green | Compassion (怜悯) | ×10 | Cooldown Reduction +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +712 Max Health, +23 Physical Defense, **+10% CDR**, +4% Movement Speed, +10% Attack Speed. Combined with Calm Boots, easily hits the 40% CDR cap for near-constant S1 CC.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Yaria's shield is thin if her build lacks Health. The safest setup:

1. Open **Preparation (备战)** → **Change Build (更换)**
2. Select **Recommended / Pro Player (推荐/大神配置)**
3. Copy the current season's **CN #1 or #2 Yaria** or pro league Support setup
4. The game auto-syncs the optimal loadout
