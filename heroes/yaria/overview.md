# Yaria（瑤） — Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Support (Enchanter / Bodyguard)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 鹿靈守心 (Deer Spirit Guardian)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 瑶)

> Yaria is one of the most popular and controversial soft Supports. Her core strengths lie in her **true-damage-ignoring ultimate shield**, frequent CC, and her unique ability to **body-block hard CC** for her team. The gap between a good Yaria and a passive "attach-and-afk" Yaria is enormous — mastering her passive and shield management is everything.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Deer Spirit (CC Immunity)

When hit by **stun, airborne, or knockback** hard CC, Yaria **dispels the CC and transforms into an invulnerable Deer Spirit**. Enemies near the deer are slowed. Use this to **intentionally body-block hooks and CC** for your jungler/carry, then escape unscathed.

### Deer → Human Reset

In Deer form, press S1 to instantly transform back — **refreshing all human-form skill cooldowns**. Advanced: dump all abilities → eat a hard CC → deer → S1 transform back → immediately cast a second rotation.

### Ultimate: True Shield + Attach

Yaria attaches to an ally, granting a **true shield** (ignores all damage types). The shield scales with **Max Health + Magic Attack**. If the shield breaks, S3 enters a long cooldown (~12s+). But if you **manually detach before it breaks, the cooldown is reduced by 50%** (just a few seconds).

### Proactive Detach (Core Shield Management)

**Watch the yellow shield bar on your ally.** When it's about to break, manually press S3 to jump off. Your S3 will be back up in seconds instead of 12+ seconds. This separates good Yarias from passive ones.
