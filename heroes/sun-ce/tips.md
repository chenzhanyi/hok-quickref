# Sun Ce Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S3 Ghost Ship: Global + Carry Allies

S3 drives a ship across the map. Explodes on terrain/enemy/recast for **AoE knockup**. Allies can **press interact to board** — enabling duo global ganks.

- Best passengers: Gao Jianli, Angela, Liu Shan (AoE burst + CC)
- Ship can be recast early to jump off — the empty ship continues forward

### 2. S2 AA Cancel Weaving

S2 has 3 charges. **Each charge cancels AA animation.** Best rhythm: **S2 → AA → S2 → AA → S2 → AA** for maximum damage output in minimum time.

### 3. Jump Off Early ("Triangle Jump")

When your ship is about to collide, **recast S3 to jump off early**. The ghost ship continues forward and still knocks up enemies, while Sun Ce is already on the ground ready to chain S1 for a second knockup — perfect control chain.

### 4. Enhanced AA Sustain

Every 6 seconds, Sun Ce's next AA is enhanced (bonus damage + **HP restore**). In lane, weave these into trades for constant sustain. Don't waste the enhanced AA on full-HP minions — use it to poke the enemy laner.

### 5. Unyielding Armor Stacking

Taking Physical/True damage OR hitting enemies with abilities grants Physical Defense stacks (max 10). The longer you fight, the tankier you get. Don't be afraid of extended brawls vs Physical damage dealers.

## Signature Combos

### Global Engage: **S3 → Jump Off → S1 → AA → S2×3 (with AA cancels)**

1. S3 from across map toward enemy backline
2. Recast S3 to jump off early (ship still knocks up)
3. S1 immediately — second knockup
4. AA → S2×3 with AA cancels for full burst

### Bush Ambush: **S1 → AA → S2×3 → S3 (close range)**

1. S1 from bush — knockup
2. AA → S2×3 with cancels
3. If they survive or Flash: S3 point-blank → knockup → finish

## Advanced Tips

- **S3's minimap icon is visible to enemies when you're near.** Stealth ganks require fog of war.
- **Pair with Stun summoner spell** — S1 + Stun guarantees the knockup lands.
- **Matchup:** Destroys immobile backliners. Struggles vs hyper-mobile heroes who dodge S1 knockup.
