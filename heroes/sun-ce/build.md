# Sun Ce Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Sun Ce's abilities have massive Physical Attack scaling. Build path: **Penetration burst + semi-tank survivability**. He flexes between Clash Lane and Jungle.

---

## Build A: Clash Lane Burst

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity prevents getting CC-locked after landing from ship. Sun Ce has Unstoppable frames, but this covers the gaps. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core first major item: CDR, Attack, Penetration, Health. |
| 3 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** or **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | **Frost Grip:** S2+AA perfectly triggers AoE slow. **Berserker's Armor:** stacking MS + damage when taking hits. |
| 4 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | Attack + CDR. Active DR ensures Sun Ce survives the initial engage burst. |
| 5 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | Power spike. One combo deletes squishies. Execute passive seals the kill. |
| 6 | **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** or **Fate's Edge (名刀·司命)** / **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | Penetration vs tanky teams. Survival rotation for late-game dives. |

## Build B: Jungle Roamer

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** or **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Tank or burst jungle knife. Easier Mana management + faster damage scaling. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Same as lane build. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core. |
| 4 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | CDR + survival for ganks. |
| 5 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | Burst spike. |
| 6 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** | Enhanced AA after S1/S3 for gank burst. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Physical Penetration, base Attack (early lane dominance), Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Stealth 100-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +45 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% Movement Speed. Level 1 full combo + enhanced AA easily secures First Blood.

### Setup 2: "Hybrid Sustain" (vs Kiting Matchups)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×5 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×5 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +37 Physical Attack, +100 Physical Penetration, +225 Max Health, +26 HP/5s, +7.5% Movement Speed. Better sustain vs Gongsun Li, Mi Yue.
