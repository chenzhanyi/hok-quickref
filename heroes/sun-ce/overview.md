# Sun Ce（孫策） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Engage / Global Roamer)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 光明之海 (Sea of Light)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 孙策)

> Sun Ce is a strategically dominant engage Fighter with global-range support capability. His core tags: **high burst, strong CC, multiple Unstoppable frames**, and his unique **S3 ghost ship (carries allies)**. He can solo-kill squishies with one rotation or transport a teammate across the map for duo-gank mayhem.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Unyielding Armor

Every 6 seconds, Sun Ce's next AA is enhanced (bonus damage + HP restore). Taking Physical/True damage or hitting enemies with abilities grants **Unyielding stacks** (max 10), each granting bonus Physical Defense. He gets tankier as fights go on.

### S3: Ghost Ship (Global + Carry)

Sun Ce drives a ship across the map. On collision with terrain, enemy Hero, or recast, it explodes for AoE knockup. **Allies can press interact to board the ship** — enabling duo global ganks (bring Gao Jianli, Angela, Liu Shan).

### S2 Cancel + AA Weave

S2 has 3 casts. Each cast **perfectly cancels AA animation recovery**. Optimal rhythm: S2 → AA → S2 → AA → S2 → AA.
