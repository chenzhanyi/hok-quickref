# Kaizer（鎧） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Duelist / Bruiser)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 破滅刃鋒 (Blade of Destruction)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 铠)

> Kaizer is a perennially popular, straightforward, and devastating all-round Fighter. His S3 grants a unique **flat damage Block** (reduces every damage tick by a fixed amount), making him nearly immune to multi-hit attacks. He can play "one-shot squishy" full-damage or "unkillable tank" — the latter currently dominates both CN and Global jungle meta.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Solo Duelist

When Kaizer's AA or S2 hits **only one enemy**, damage increases by **+50%**. Always isolate targets — drag jungle camps apart, fight in open space away from minions.

### S3: Demon Armor (Block Mechanic)

S3 grants Attack, Movement Speed, and **flat damage Block**. This means every tick of multi-hit damage (Hou Yi split arrows, Marco bullets, Ying Zheng ultimate, Yunying flurry) deals near-zero damage. **Never save S3 for low HP — activate it as you engage, before taking burst.**

### S1: Lifesteal + Slow

S1 bounces between enemies, slowing them and **restoring %Max HP** while reducing S1 cooldown by 30%. Use constantly for sustain and bush-checking.

### S2: Dash + Knockup

S2 charges forward with knockup. The next AA becomes a **lunge** with a small dash — can cross thin walls to reach jungle camps or minions.
