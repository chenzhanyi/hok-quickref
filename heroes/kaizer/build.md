# Kaizer Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Two distinct styles. **Tank Kaizer** (highest winrate, jungle meta) and **One-Shot Kaizer** (fun/smurf). Tank build leverages S3 Block + HP stacking to become unkillable.

---

## Build A: Tank Kaizer — Jungle Dominator (Recommended)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** | HP stacking per jungle clear + burn aura. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity to ensure S3 activates. |
| 3 | **Blazing Cape (红莲斗篷)** | Double burn aura with Giant's Grip. Tank Kaizer's melee damage is surprisingly high. |
| 4 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Reduces enemy AS on-hit — Marksman counter. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | **Eternal Night:** burst-heal vs magic burst. **Magic Cloak:** sustained magic defense + spell shield. |
| 6 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** or **Blood Rage (血魔之怒)** | Offensive defense. **Berserker's:** stacking MS + damage when hit. **Blood Rage:** massive shield + bonus on-hit at low HP. |

---

## Build B: One-Shot Kaizer — Squishy Deleter

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | Shadow Boots (影忍之足) or Resistance Boots | Lane durability. |
| 2 | Shadow Axe (暗影战斧) | Penetration + CDR. |
| 3 | Master's Power (宗师之力) | **Core.** S2 → AA perfectly triggers Power Strike — one hit deletes. |
| 4 | Infinity Edge (无尽战刃) | Critical damage spike. |
| 5 | Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲) | Offense + defense hybrid. |
| 6 | Fate's Edge (名刀·司命) or Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护) | Survival for squishy dive. |

## Quick Recommendation

For solo queue climbing, **Tank Kaizer is strongly recommended**. Even if your early jungle is behind, mid-late game you simply S3 and stand in the enemy team — unkillable, soaking everything, creating space for your carries.
