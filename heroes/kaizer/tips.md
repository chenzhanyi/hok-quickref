# Kaizer Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Isolate for +50% Damage

Kaizer's passive: AA/S2 on a single target = **+50% damage**. When jungling, pull camps apart. When dueling, fight away from minion waves. This +50% is why Kaizer wins nearly every 1v1.

### 2. S3: Activate BEFORE Taking Damage

S3 grants flat Block — every damage tick reduced by a fixed amount. Multi-hit attacks (Marco, Hou Yi, Ying Zheng) become harmless. **Never save S3 for low HP.** Activate it as you engage.

### 3. S1: Infinite Sustain

S1 restores %Max HP on hit + reduces its own cooldown by 30%. When low, S1 a minion wave or jungle camp. Also works as a bush-check — if it bounces, someone's there.

### 4. S2 Lunge Over Thin Walls

S2's follow-up AA is a lunge with a small dash. Use it to cross thin walls toward jungle camps or minions — great for escapes and creative gank paths.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take ultimate whenever available.** S2 is core burst and CC.

## Signature Combos

### Bush Ambush One-Shot: **S3 → S1 → S2 → AA**

1. S3 from bush (enemy can't react)
2. S1 slow + speed boost
3. S2 knockup
4. Enhanced AA lunge — squishy deleted

### Tank Kaizer Teamfight: **S1 → S2 → S3 → AA → S1 → AA**

1. S1 slow + engage
2. S2 knockup
3. S3 immediately — soak all damage
4. AA weave + S1 refresh through passive CD reduction
5. Stay on the backline, unkillable

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Block hard-counters multi-hit Heroes.** Pick Kaizer into Marco, Hou Yi, Ying Zheng, Yunying.
- **S3's Block is flat, not percentage.** The more ticks an attack has, the more effective Block is.
- **Matchup:** Destroys multi-hit and sustained-damage Heroes. Struggles vs true burst that comes in one big hit (siege mages).
