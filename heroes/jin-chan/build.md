# Jin Chan Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Utility control Mage. Needs **CDR** (frequent S1 curses) and **tankiness** (survive to curse again). CN server consensus: **Semi-tank CDR utility.**

---

## Recommended Semi-Tank Utility Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR for frequent S1. *Many CN players build double boots — add Swift Boots (疾步之靴) for roaming.* |
| 2 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | **Only damage item needed.** S2's multi-hit endlessly triggers Mask's %HP burn. |
| 3 | **Rising Sun (旭日初光)** or **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** | **Rising Sun:** shields ally on S1/S2 hit. **Glacial Storm:** 20% CDR + Physical Defense. |
| 4 | **Frost Breath (凝冰之息)** | S1 (empowered by S3) + Frost Breath = enemies literally can't move inside the curse ring. |
| 5 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense + spell shield. Stacks with passive 35% DR. |
| 6 | **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** or **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** or **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | Penetration if team needs damage; Resurrection if you need survival. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Max HP, CDR, Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Thousand-HP Utility" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, Cooldown Reduction +0.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** **+1,162 Max Health**, +23 Physical Defense, +4% MS, +6% CDR. Combined with passive 35% DR — deceptively tanky.

### Setup 2: "88-Pen CDR Speed" (More Damage)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Compassion (怜悯) | ×10 | Cooldown Reduction +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +24 Magic Penetration, **+10% CDR**, **+10% MS**. More damage + faster roaming.
