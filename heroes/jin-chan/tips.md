# Jin Chan Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S1: Hold It for the Right Target

Don't waste S1 on the enemy tank. Hold it for: Luna, Arli, Marco, Lam, Jing, Shangguan — any mobility Hero. When they engage → S1 → they're stuck. They can't dash out. Your team deletes them.

### 2. S3 FIRST, Then S1 + S2

S3 empowers S1 (adds slow) and S2 (faster hits). **Always: S3 → S1 → S2.** S3 also shares your passive DR with all nearby allies — cast early for team protection.

### 3. S3 Second Cast: Save for Execute

S3's second cast deals %missing HP damage. Don't waste it early. Stack passive "Mantra" charges (max 10) by dealing damage, then S3 recast when enemies are low.

### 4. S2 Auto-Tracks Cursed Targets

If S1 is active on an enemy, S2 **auto-deploys directly on them** and deals full damage instantly. S1 → S2 = guaranteed hit. No aim needed.

### 5. Anti-Burst: S3 When You See It Coming

Passive reduces single-hit damage by 35%. When Lanling Wang's "!" appears, or Shangguan's aerial sounds play: **S3 immediately.** The team DR sharing + your passive = their burst is neutralized. Then S1 them when they engage.

## Signature Combos

### Standard Lockdown: **S3 → S1 → S2 → AA → S3 (recast)**

1. S3 (team DR + empower)
2. S1 lock (anti-mobility + slow)
3. S2 auto-deploy on cursed target
4. AA to stack Mantra charges
5. S3 recast (execute blast)

### Flash Initiation: **S1 + Flash → S3 → S2**

1. S1 the enemy carry → **Flash forward** to extend S1 range
2. S3 + S2 immediately
3. They're cursed, can't Flash out, team kills

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S1 prevents even Flash.** There is NO escape from the curse.
- **S3 DR sharing makes your entire team tanky.** Pop it preemptively in teamfights.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs mobility-reliant comps. Struggles vs tanky frontlines that don't need mobility.
