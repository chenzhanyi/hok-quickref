# Jin Chan（金蟬） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Anti-Mobility / Utility Control)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 渡世行者 (World-Crossing Walker)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (金蝉)

> Jin Chan is a strategically powerful utility control Mage. His signature S1 **"Tightening Curse"** locks a target in place — **preventing ALL mobility skills (dashes, blinks, Flashes).** He's the absolute hard-counter to hyper-mobile Heroes: Luna, Arli, Han Xin, Marco. His passive reduces all incoming single-hit damage by 35%, and S3 can share this DR with allies.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S1: Tightening Curse (The Anti-Mobility Lock)

S1 places a curse ring on a target. For 3 seconds: they **cannot use any mobility skill** (dashes, blinks, Flashes) and **cannot leave the ring's area.** This hard-counters: Luna, Arli, Han Xin, Marco, Lam, Jing, Shangguan, Li Bai — every "fancy" Hero.

### Passive: 35% Single-Hit Damage Reduction

Any single hit exceeding 5% of Jin Chan's Max HP is **reduced by 35%.** This makes him naturally tanky against burst. **When S3 is active, this DR is shared with all nearby allies.**

### S3: Team DR + Empowered Skills

S3 grants team DR sharing AND empowers S1 (adds slow) and S2 (faster attack speed). Second cast: fires a blast dealing **%missing HP damage** — the lower they are, the harder it hits.

### S2 + S1: Auto-Tracking

If S1 is on a target, dropping S2 **auto-deploys directly onto the cursed enemy** and instantly deals full damage. No aiming needed.
