# Yuan Ge（元歌） — Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin (Puppet Master / Complex)
- **Lane:** Jungle / Mid Lane / Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 無間傀儡 (Endless Puppet)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 元歌)

> Yuan Ge is widely considered the highest skill-cap Assassin in the game. He has a unique **Body + Puppet dual-form** with 8 active abilities (4 body + 4 puppet). Mastering him requires extreme hand speed, spatial awareness, and split-second reaction time.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Puppet (S1)

S1 deploys a puppet that can **disguise as a random enemy Hero**. The puppet inherits Yuan Ge's items (except resurrection and certain actives). If the puppet is destroyed, Yuan Ge is **revealed, stunned, and S1 is disabled for ~15 seconds** — catastrophic in teamfights. Always recall the puppet before it dies.

### S4 Body: Invincibility Frame

Yuan Ge's body S4 is a dash with a **brief invincibility + CC-cleanse frame** on cast. With perfect timing, this dodges Zhuge Liang S3, Musashi S3, or tower shots.

### Stealth Mechanic

While controlling the puppet, if Yuan Ge's real body is in a bush, his body remains **invisible on the minimap**. This makes him the best "safe scouting" and "long-range engagement" hero.
