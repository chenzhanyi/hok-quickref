# Yuan Ge Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** All of Yuan Ge's abilities scale with **Bonus Physical Attack (Bonus AD)**. He desperately needs **CDR** to minimize puppet downtime. Pure damage burst is the only viable build. Zero Attack Speed or Critical needed.

---

## Recommended Pure Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | Early CDR rush. More CDR = less puppet downtime = more kill windows. *Late game: swap to Resistance Boots when CDR overflows.* |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core first major item: Attack, Health, CDR, flat Penetration. Amplifies both puppet and body burst. |
| 3 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Yuan Ge's soul item.** Massive AD scaling on all skills. Execute threshold for 2-3 combo skyrockets. This is the "delete squishies" power spike. |
| 4 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** or **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | **Master's Power:** Enhanced AA after abilities for max burst. **Frost Grip:** Slow + CDR. Puppet auto-attacks also trigger Frost Grip's on-hit slow — strong kiting tool for Clash Lane. |
| 5 | **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** | 40% Physical Penetration. Mandatory vs late-game armor stacking (Ominous Premonition, Thornmail). |
| 6 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** or **Fate's Edge (名刀·司命)** | Survival flex. **Pure Sky:** active DR for surviving initial burst when caught out. **Fate's Edge:** fatal-damage immunity for clutch outplays. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Zero Attack Speed or Critical needed. **Pure Physical Penetration + base Attack + Movement Speed** is the only correct setup.

### Setup 1: "Stealth 100-Pen" (95% of Top Players)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, **+10% Movement Speed**. The MS dramatically improves body bush-rotation and kiting efficiency.

### Setup 2: "Clash Lane Sustain"

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×7 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +100 Physical Penetration, **+4.8% Physical Lifesteal**. The lifesteal lets Yuan Ge sustain in lane without recalling, preventing missed minion waves.

## Quick Tip for Beginners

Yuan Ge's skill layout is extremely complex. Before taking him into ranked:
1. Copy the **CN #1 Yuan Ge** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**
2. Go to **Training Mode** and drill the classic **1433223** combo until it's muscle memory
3. Only then try real matches
