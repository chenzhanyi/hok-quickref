# Yuan Ge Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Protect the Puppet at All Costs

If the puppet is destroyed: you're **revealed, stunned, and S1 is disabled for ~15s**. This is usually fatal. When puppet HP is low:
- Press **S4 (body)** to pull the puppet back
- Or press **S1** to recall it immediately
Never let the enemy kill your puppet.

### 2. S4 Invincibility Frame

Yuan Ge's body S4 dash has a **brief invincibility + CC-cleanse** on cast. With frame-perfect timing you can dodge:
- Zhuge Liang S3 (ultimate lock-on)
- Musashi S3 (aerial strike)
- Tower shots
Practice this timing — it's the hallmark of a truly skilled Yuan Ge.

### 3. Disguise Mind Games

When first deployed, the puppet disguises as a random enemy Hero. Walk the puppet casually toward enemy squishies — in lower ranks and Global server, players often don't realize it's a puppet until they're already combo-locked.

### 4. Bush Stealth

While controlling the puppet, if your real body is in a bush, it's completely invisible on the minimap. This lets you:
- Scout the entire enemy jungle safely
- Engage from extreme range while your body stays hidden

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S3. Take ultimate whenever available.** Body S2 (dark projectile barrage) is your main wave-clear and burst tool.

## Signature Combos

All classic Yuan Ge combos derive from one fundamental sequence:

### Core One-Shot Combo: **1 → 4 → 3 → 3 → 2 → 2 → 3**

1. **1** — Deploy puppet
2. **4** — Puppet invincible dash to close distance
3. **3** — Puppet slow (first hit)
4. **3** — Puppet continuous stun (locks enemy in place)
5. **2** — Body swaps position with puppet
6. **2** — Body point-blank fires all 4 projectiles (massive damage)
7. **3** — Body cross-slash execute (finisher)

### Long-Range Turret Dive: **1 → 3 → 3 → 4 → 2 → 3**

1. **1** deploy puppet → **3** slow → **3** stun (locks enemy under tower)
2. **4** — Body teleports in, replacing puppet
3. **2** → **3** — Point-blank burst + execute to finish
4. Walk out or S4 to escape

### Escape / Counter-Engage: **1 → 4 → 2 → 1**

When jumped on:
1. **1** deploy puppet + **4** — puppet pushes forward + knocks enemy back
2. **2** — Swap positions with puppet (create distance)
3. **1** — Recall puppet

## Advanced Tips

- **The classic **1433223** is the foundation.** Drill it in Training Mode until it's muscle memory before taking Yuan Ge into real matches.
- **Puppet applies on-hit effects.** Items like Frost Grip trigger from puppet auto-attacks.
- **Matchup:** Destroys immobile squishies. Struggles vs instant CC that can kill the puppet before you react.
