# Jing Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Jing is a pure burst Assassin pursuing **one-shot kills**. Her ultimate's Unstoppable + DR replaces the need for defensive items. CN server consensus: **full damage full burst**.

---

## Recommended Jungle Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Physical Attack, Physical Penetration, CDR. Standard Assassin jungle item. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity is critical — Jing is vulnerable to CC during engage before she starts swapping. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core first major item. Attack, Penetration, CDR. Perfect for Jing's early-game tempo. |
| 4 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** | Jing's combos weave frequent auto-attacks. The "Power Strike" passive procs from BOTH Jing and her mirror simultaneously — devastating burst on 1A or ultimate AA combos. |
| 5 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Jing's ultimate power spike.** Her damage has execute properties (more damage at lower HP). Stacked with Siege Breaker's +30% vs targets below 50% HP, squishies evaporate on contact. |
| 6 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** or **Fate's Edge (名刀·司命)** / **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | **Pure Sky:** Active DR stacks with S3 swap DR for near-immortality during tower dives. **Fate's Edge / Sage's Sanctuary:** Late-game survival rotation. |

### Late-Game Rotation

Cycle Fate's Edge → Sage's Sanctuary → buy back Pure Sky for the final teamfight.

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Jing desperately needs early **Physical Penetration** and **Movement Speed** for map roaming.

### Setup 1: CN Server "Stealth 100-Pen" (90% of Top Players)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** **+29 Physical Attack, +100 Physical Penetration, +10% Movement Speed.**

### Setup 2: "Dream Crit + Sustain" (Solo Queue Comfort)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×9 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×1 | Critical Rate +1.6% ("buy a dream" — when Flying Raijin crits, it deletes) |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×8 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×2 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +27 Physical Attack, +96.4 Physical Penetration, +8% Movement Speed, +3.2% Physical Lifesteal, +1.6% Critical Rate. The Lifesteal enables health-sustaining jungle clears and early Dragon contests.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Jing is an extremely high-execution hero with precise stat thresholds. The easiest way to get a perfect setup:

1. Open **Preparation (备战)** → **Change Build (更换)**
2. Select **Recommended / Pro Player (推荐/大神配置)**
3. Copy the current season's **CN #1** or **KPL pro jungler** setup
4. The game auto-syncs max-level Arcana and recommended item order
