# Jing Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Mirror Image Mechanics

Jing's S1, S2, and basic attacks create a **mirror image** that copies her actions:
- Mirror inherits a portion of Jing's damage and healing
- **Mirror hits trigger on-hit item effects** (Shadow Axe, Master's Power, etc.)
- When Jing AND mirror both hit the same enemy, S1 and S2 cooldowns reset

### 2. Ultimate: Infinite Swap ("Flying Raijin")

During S3's mirror matrix:
- **Press S3 to swap positions with the mirror**
- During swap: **Unstoppable + 30%~50% Damage Reduction**
- **Hitting an enemy** (by Jing or mirror) instantly resets the swap cooldown
- If nothing is hit, swap has a brief cooldown

### 3. Force Swap with Smite / Restore

If you miss all abilities and can't reset swap naturally, **use Smite or Restore**. These abilities dealing damage/healing also instantly refresh the swap cooldown — lifesaving for escapes and surprise counter-kills.

### 4. Skill Priority

**Max S1 first, then S2. Take ultimate whenever available.** S1 is Jing's core mobility and burst tool — maxing it dramatically improves early-mid game gank efficiency.

## Signature Combos

### Fastest Burst: **S1 → AA → S2 → AA → S3 → AA**

1. S1 dash in + auto-attack
2. S2 slow + auto-attack
3. Abilities refreshed by double-hit
4. S3 root + auto-attack for maximum burst
Best from bush or mid-range engage.

### Long-Range Backline Delete: **S1 → S1 → S3 → S2 → AA → S3 (swap) → AA...**

1. Use S1's two dashes (bounce off minion/frontline as springboard)
2. Close distance to enemy backline instantly
3. S3 root + S2 for double damage + healing
4. Enter "Flying Raijin" swap sequence to finish all targets

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Practice the swap rhythm:** The key to Jing is mastering S3 swap timing. One hit = one swap. Miss = pause.
- **Tower dive with impunity:** S3 swap's DR + Unstoppable lets you dive, kill, and swap out without taking lethal damage.
- **Bush ambush:** Pre-cast abilities to stack mirror before revealing yourself.
- **Matchup:** Jing destroys immobile squishies. She struggles against tanky CC-heavy comps that can lock her down before she starts swapping.
