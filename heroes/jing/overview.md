# Jing（鏡） — Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin (Burst / Outplay)
- **Lane:** Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 破鏡之刃 (Shattered Mirror Blade)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 镜)

> Jing is an extremely high-skill-cap burst Assassin. Her core mechanics revolve around **Mirror Image** (clone duplicates her actions) and her ultimate's **Mirror Swap** (teleport + Unstoppable + DR). When both Jing and her mirror hit the same enemy, Ability 1 & 2 cooldowns reset. In high-rank and pro play, she's a game-deciding jungle carry.

---

## Passive & Core Mechanics

### Mirror Image (Passive)

Casting S1, S2, or basic attacks creates a **mirror image** that duplicates Jing's actions. The mirror inherits a portion of her damage and healing, and **mirror hits also trigger on-hit item effects** (Shadow Axe, Master's Power, etc.).

### Ultimate: Mirror Matrix (S3)

Jing dashes forward, **rooting the first enemy hit for 1 second**. The ultimate creates a mirror matrix on both sides. While inside, press S3 to **swap positions with the mirror** — during the swap, Jing is **Unstoppable with 30%~50% Damage Reduction**. This is her outplay, tower-dive, and CC-dodge tool.

### Cooldown Reset

During ultimate, **any damage dealt by Jing or her mirror to an enemy instantly refreshes the swap cooldown**. If nothing is hit, the swap has a brief cooldown.
