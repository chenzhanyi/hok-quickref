# Pan Gu（盤古） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Utility Jungler / Anti-Carry)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 破曉之神 (God of Dawn)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 盘古)

> Pan Gu is a highly strategic utility Fighter/Jungler. His signature mechanic is the game's unique **Disarm** — preventing enemies from using basic attacks. He's the absolute hard-counter to auto-attack-dependent heroes (Baili Xuance, Sima Yi, Fuzi, Hou Yi, Han Xin).

---

## Core Mechanics

### Pan Gu Axe Form (Core)

Has 10 durability. Basic attacks on enemy Heroes consume durability and apply **Bleed** (deals %Max Health Physical Damage every 0.5s). This is why Pan Gu clears jungle fast and wins early duels.

### Disarm (Signature Anti-Carry Mechanic)

The following apply **Disarm** (enemy cannot basic attack, only move/use abilities):
- S1 second-phase slam
- S2 grab
- S3 ultimate fissures

Disarm completely shuts down auto-attack carries and Assassins.

### Energy Arm Form

When Axe durability runs out or is manually charged. Basic attacks stack marks — at 5 stacks: **stun + bonus damage**. Cannot Disarm in this form. Revert to Axe form ASAP.

### Ultimate: Fissure Scaling

S3 creates ground fissures. **Each nearby ally adds +1 fissure (max 5)**. Never use S3 solo — in teamfights with 5 allies, cover the entire enemy backline with AoE Disarm.
