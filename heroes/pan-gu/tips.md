# Pan Gu Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Disarm = Absolute Counter

Disarm prevents enemies from basic attacking. Pan Gu hard-counters:
- **Baili Xuance, Sima Yi, Fuzi, Hou Yi, Han Xin** — any hero reliant on auto-attacks
- Apply Disarm via S1 (second slam), S2 (grab), or S3 (fissures)
- A Disarmed ADC is completely useless for the duration

### 2. S2 Lifesteal: Absurd Sustain

S2 marks enemies for 3 seconds. **Basic attacking marked targets restores % of Pan Gu's Max Health**. When low HP:
- S2 a Jungle Monster, Minion, or enemy tank
- Auto-attack relentlessly
- HP bar refills dramatically

### 3. S3 Ulimate: Go With Your Team

S3 fissures scale with allies: **+1 fissure per nearby ally (max 5)**. NEVER solo-ult — wait for teamfights. A 5-fissure ultimate blankets the entire enemy backline with AoE Disarm.

### 4. Axe vs Energy Arm Management

- **Axe form:** Bleed + Disarm. Your primary combat form.
- **Energy Arm:** No Disarm. Only use to stack 5 marks → stun → immediately switch back.
- Revert to Axe when safe — don't fight extended battles in Energy Arm.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take ultimate whenever available.** S2 is Pan Gu's sustain, Disarm, and dueling core.

## Signature Combo

### Standard Gank / Long-Range Engage: **S1 → S1 → S2 → AA → S3 → AA**

1. S1 leap → mid-air S1 again (second-phase lock-on slam) — gap-close + first Disarm
2. S2 grab on landing — second Disarm + lifesteal mark
3. Auto-attack to trigger lifesteal
4. When Disarm expires and enemy tries to retaliate/flee, S3 ultimate — third AoE Disarm + slow
5. Auto-attack to finish

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Disarm timing is everything:** Don't chain all your Disarms at once. Space them out to maximize the enemy's downtime.
- **Pre-fight S2 stack:** Mark a Jungle Monster with S2 before ganking — you enter with lifesteal already active.
- **Teamfight positioning:** Stay near your team for maximum S3 fissures. Solo-ulting wastes Pan Gu's strongest teamfight tool.
- **Matchup:** Destroys auto-attackers. Struggles against ability-based mages who don't need basic attacks (Gan & Mo, Angela).
