# Pan Gu Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pan Gu's passive Bleed scales with **Max Health**. He needs Attack Speed for smooth auto-attack weaving and S2 lifesteal triggers. CN server consensus: **semi-tank Attack Speed bruiser** or **tank-jungle heavy HP build**.

---

## Recommended Semi-Tank Attack Speed Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** | Massive Max Health. Bleed synergizes with burn aura. HP directly amplifies passive %HP damage. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity is mandatory — Pan Gu needs to melee auto-attack for S2 lifesteal. Getting CC-locked = death. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core Fighter item: CDR, Attack, flat Penetration. Strong early dueling power. |
| 4 | **Doomsday (末世)** | **Pan Gu's power-spike item!** Axe Bleed + Doomsday's 8% Current HP on-hit = double %HP damage stacking. Melts tanks and Fighters. Attack Speed bonus makes post-Disarm auto-attacks silky smooth. |
| 5 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Physical Defense + Max Health. Taking hits grants up to +10% damage output and +10% Movement Speed — perfect for Pan Gu's skirmishing style. |
| 6 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense. **Eternal Night:** burst-heal on taking magic damage, synergizes with S2 lifesteal for insane survival. **Magic Cloak:** sustained magic poke shield. |

## Quick In-Game Setup

Pan Gu's effectiveness depends heavily on Attack Speed thresholds and HP ratios. The safest setup:

1. Open **Preparation (备战)** → **Change Build (更换)**
2. Select **Recommended / Pro Player (推荐/大神配置)**
3. Copy the **CN #1** or current-season highest-winrate Pan Gu setup
