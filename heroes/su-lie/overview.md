# Su Lie（蘇烈） — Tank / Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Tank / Fighter (CC Monster / Two Lives)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 不屈鐵壁 (Unyielding Iron Wall)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 苏烈)

> Su Lie is a comprehensive CC-heavy Tank/Fighter with a built-in resurrection passive and a devastating AoE ultimate. He can play aggressive Roaming Support or surprise Clash Lane carry. His signature: **"Nuclear Ultimate"** — damage scales with enemies hit, and can one-shot entire teams.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Two Lives (Beacon Resurrection)

When taking fatal damage, Su Lie enters a 4.5s "dying state." Three beacons spawn around him. **Allies stepping on a beacon restores 10% Max HP to both Su Lie and themselves.** All 3 beacons lit = Su Lie revives with 60% HP + AoE slow. **Tell your teammates about the beacons in draft!**

### S3 Ultimate: Nuclear Bomb

S3 channels a ground smash. **The more enemies (Minions, Monsters, Heroes) in range, the higher the AoE damage.** Full-charge S3 + Flash can delete entire teams. Always charge from bushes to hide the yellow circle indicator.

### S1: Hold the Knockup AA

S1 empowers the next 3 AAs; the 3rd knocks up. This empowered state lasts a long time. **Pre-cast S1 and hit air twice — hold the 3rd knockup AA** — then walk into a fight and open with a guaranteed knockup.
