# Su Lie Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Most common and highest winrate: **aggressive Roaming Support**. Needs tankiness for engage + Movement Speed for S3+Flash plays.

---

## Build A: Roaming Support (Recommended)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Wolf Rush (极影·奔狼)** or **Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎)** | Shadow Aegis for CDR + AS. **Wolf Rush:** team speed burst for charging engages. **Redemption:** group shield. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Prevents getting CC-cancelled during S3 charge. |
| 3 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max Health. Reduces enemy AS by 40% on-hit. Counters Marksmen and Assassins. |
| 4 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic Defense + spell shield. Prevents getting poked out while charging S3. |
| 5 | **Blazing Cape (红莲斗篷)** | Physical Defense + Max-HP-scaling burn aura. Supplements Support wave clear and melee damage. |
| 6 | **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | **Third life!** Passive resurrection + Sage's Sanctuary + evolving into Raging Soul = up to 3 lives in one fight. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Attack Speed (smooth S1 3-hit chain), Max Health (passive + tankiness), Movement Speed.**
