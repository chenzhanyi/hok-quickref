# Su Lie Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Teach Your Team About Beacons

Many players (especially in lower ranks) don't know Su Lie's passive. At the start of every match: **"When I die, step on the lights — we both heal and I revive!"** This single sentence doubles your winrate.

### 2. S3 Nuclear Ultimate

S3's damage scales with **number of enemies in range** (Minions, Monsters, Heroes). Full-charge S3 into a minion wave + enemy team = one-shot potential. Always charge from **bushes** — enemies can't see the yellow circle indicator from fog of war. **S3 + Flash** at full charge = undodgeable team-wipe.

### 3. S1: Pre-Stack the Knockup

S1 empowers 3 AAs (3rd = knockup). The empowered state lasts a long time. Before ganking: **S1 → AA air twice → hold the 3rd knockup AA** → walk into lane → guaranteed knockup opener.

### 4. S2: Push Enemies Into Your Team

S2 charges and pushes enemies. Always angle to push them **toward your tower or team**. Use Flash mid-charge to reposition behind them for a reverse push.

### 5. Clash Lane "Nuclear" Build (Off-Meta)

Shadow Boots → Shadow Axe → Frost Grip → Lightning Dagger → Siege Breaker → Fate's Edge. Full-charge S3 + Flash can one-shot squishies from 100% HP.

## Signature Combos

### Bush Nuclear Bomb: **S3 (full charge in bush) + Flash → AA → S1 → S2**

1. Charge S3 from bush (enemies can't see circle)
2. Flash at full charge → unleash into enemy cluster
3. AA → S1 knockup chain → S2 push survivors toward team

### Standard Engage: **S2 + Flash → S1 → AA×3 → S3**

1. S2 charge → Flash mid-charge for angle
2. Push enemy into team/tower
3. S1 → 3-hit knockup chain
4. S3 for cleanup or zone control

## Advanced Tips

- **Beacons also heal allies.** Even if you're going to die, allies can top themselves off by stepping on your lights.
- **S3 damage multiplier:** More targets = more damage. Fight near minion waves or jungle camps.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in clustered teamfights. Struggles vs spread-out poke comps.
