# Xuance Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Xuance is a crit-based Assassin. Needs **Critical Rate, Physical Penetration, and survival flex**. His passive gives massive AS — he doesn't need full AS items.

---

## Recommended Crit Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Standard Assassin jungle item: Attack, Penetration, CDR. |
| 2 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early AS for smooth jungle clear. *Mid-late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC — getting CC'd with passive up = wasted.* |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core early-game item: Health, flat Penetration, 10% CDR. |
| 4 | **Shadow Blade (影刃)** or **Lightning Dagger (闪电匕首)** | Crit + AS. **Shadow Blade** preferred: on-crit MS bonus stacks with S1 for god-tier kiting. |
| 5 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | Crit core. Paired with Shadow Blade, one hook = one kill. |
| 6 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** / **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | **Master's Power:** S1 → AA burst deletes squishies. **Pure Sky:** survival vs focus fire. **Sanctuary:** resurrection for final fights. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Xuance's passive provides massive AS — he doesn't need full AS runes. Core: **Critical Rate, Physical Penetration, sustain (jungle without recalling).**

### Setup 1: "Classic Crit Lifesteal" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×3 | Attack Speed +1.6%, Critical Rate +0.5% |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×7 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +11.2% Critical Rate, +9.8% Attack Speed, +64 Physical Penetration, **+4.8% Physical Lifesteal**, +7% Movement Speed. The lifesteal enables health-sustaining jungle clears and Dragon contests.

### Setup 2: "Pure 100-Pen Speed" (Aggressive Invade)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% Attack Speed, **+10% Movement Speed**. Max Movement Speed for S2 hook positioning and early invades.
