# Xuance Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Hook Accuracy = Everything

Xuance IS his hook. If you miss S2, you're useless for the next ~8 seconds. Practice in Training Mode. Aim for the edge of the hook's return path — it catches the farthest target.

### 2. Don't Throw Immediately After Hooking

New players always S2 → throw instantly. Big mistake. The correct flow:
1. S2 hook
2. S1 pull (reposition)
3. S3 dash behind (damage)
4. AA until they Flash/dash
5. **Now S2 recast — throw them back**

### 3. Berserker Mode: Kill One → Kill All

Passive triggers on kill/assist: **+75% MS, +150% AS**. In teamfights, don't dive the full-HP tank. **Target the lowest-HP enemy first.** One assist = berserker mode = chain-kill their entire team.

### 4. Hook the Biggest Threat for DR

Hooked enemies deal reduced damage to Xuance. When an enemy Assassin dives you, hook them — the DR lets you out-trade and potentially turn the fight.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S1 first, then S2. Take ultimate whenever available.** S1 provides Physical Attack scaling — your main damage steroid.

---

## Signature Combos

### Standard Gank: **S2 → S1 → S3 → AA → S2 (throw)**

1. S2 hook from bush
2. S1 pull toward you
3. S3 dash behind enemy
4. AA for damage
5. S2 recast to throw them back when they Flash

### Tower Reverse Throw: **S2 → S1 → S2 (throw) → S3**

1. S2 hook
2. S1 pull toward your tower
3. **S2 throw** — launches enemy deep into your tower
4. S3 to follow up and finish under tower

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 dash is unstoppable during travel.** Use it to dodge CC at point-blank range.
- **Attack Speed thresholds matter.** Xuance's S1 and passive change his AS breakpoints. Copy pro player builds for optimized thresholds.
- **Matchup:** Destroys immobile squishies he can hook. Struggles vs tanks who don't care about being hooked.
