# Xuance（百裡玄策） — Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin (Hook / Snowball Jungler)
- **Lane:** Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 囂狂之鐮 (Frenzied Sickle)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 百里玄策)

> Xuance is a high-tempo, snowball-heavy cleanup Assassin/Jungler. His core mechanic is his S2 **"Nightmare Hook"** — land the hook and you're a god; miss it and you're a minion. On kill or assist, his passive grants **+75% Movement Speed and +150% Attack Speed**, turning him into an unstoppable "berserker mode" team-wiper.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S2 Hook: Near vs Far Target

S2's hook only binds the **farthest target on its return path**. If a minion and enemy Hero overlap, reposition so the hook's farthest edge catches the Hero.

### S2 Second Cast: Don't Throw Immediately

After hooking, DON'T immediately throw. S1 pull → S3 dash → AA for damage → **only throw (S2 recast) when the enemy Flashes or tries to escape.**

### Passive: Berserker Mode

On kill: +75% MS, +150% AS (half on assist). This is Xuance's team-wipe engine. **Always target low-HP enemies first** — one assist triggers the rampage, then chain-kill everyone.

### Hooked Target Debuff

Xuance deals bonus damage to hooked targets, and they deal reduced damage to him. When dived, hook the Assassin — the DR lets you out-trade them.
