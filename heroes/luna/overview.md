# Luna（露娜） — Fighter / Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter / Assassin (Magic Skirmisher / Infinite Reset)
- **Lane:** Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★★
- **Title:** 月光之女 (Daughter of Moonlight)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 露娜)

> Luna is universally considered the highest skill-ceiling Hero in the game. Her core: apply **Moonlight marks** to enemies via S1, S2, and every 3rd AA. Her S3 is a dash — if it passes through a marked target, **S3 cooldown fully resets.** Properly executed, Luna achieves the legendary **"Infinite Moon Combo"** — dashing endlessly through marked targets. She's also the most CD-dependent Hero: missing one mark = S3 on full cooldown = dead.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Four Sources of Moonlight Marks

1. **S1 (弦月斩):** Line AoE that marks all enemies hit.
2. **S2 (炙热剑芒):** AoE pull + shield + marks all nearby enemies.
3. **S3 (新月突击):** Dash. If it passes through a marked target → **S3 cooldown resets.**
4. **Every 3rd AA:** Applies a Moonlight mark. First AA is a mini-dash toward the target.

### Attack Speed = Luna's Lifeline

Luna relies on the 3rd AA mark. Without sufficient AS, AA animations are too slow and the 3rd hit won't land in time → S3 doesn't reset → dead. **32% AS from runes is the universal standard.**

### Minions/Jungle = Dash Springboards

Don't only mark enemy Heroes. Use S1 or 2nd AA on minions/jungle camps as "stepping stones" — S3 through them for gap-close or escape.

### S3 Hitbox: Wider Than It Looks

S3 refreshes if its **path trajectory touches** any marked enemy — you don't need to land exactly on them. Abuse the wide hitbox.

### Disable Auto-Attack

Enable **"Advanced Free Attack Mode"** (补刀/推塔键) and **disable auto-attack.** Luna needs precise AA counting — auto-attacking randomly breaks the 3rd-mark rhythm.
