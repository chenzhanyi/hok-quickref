# Luna Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. 32% Attack Speed Is Non-Negotiable

Luna's 3rd AA applies a Moonlight mark. Without 32% AS (runes), the 3rd AA animation is too slow and your combo breaks. Never use less than 32% AS runes. Period.

### 2. Disable Auto-Attack

Auto-attack randomly ruins your 3rd-hit count. **Settings → Disable Auto-Attack → Enable Advanced Free Attack Mode.** Count your AAs manually: 1-2-3-mark.

### 3. S3 Path Hitbox: Use It

S3 resets if the **dash path touches** a marked target — not just the destination. You can S3 through a marked minion to reach a distant enemy, or angle through multiple marks for maximum mobility.

### 4. Minions Are Your Escape Route

When collapsed on: S1 the nearest minion wave → S3 through them → S2 nearby jungle camp → S3 through it → you're now half a map away. Minions and camps are Luna's best friends.

### 5. Pre-Stack Your 2nd AA

Before ganking: AA minions twice. Walk in with the 3rd AA (moon mark) ready. S3 in → instant 3rd AA mark → S3 again. This gives you two S3s before the enemy can react.

## Signature Combos

### Basic Safe Poke: **S1 → S3 → S2 → S3 (retreat)**

1. S1 mark (from range)
2. S3 in
3. S2 (shield + mark + pull)
4. S3 out (back to safety)
5. Safest trade — you're gone before they retaliate

### Infinite Moon Combo: **S1 → S3 → AA → S2 → AA → S3 → AA → S3**

1. S1 mark → S3 in
2. AA (count: 1)
3. S2 mark → AA (count: 2)
4. S3 through
5. AA (count: 3 → **moon mark applied**)
6. S3 through the new mark
7. Repeat from step 1

### Springboard Chase: **S1 (minion) → S3 (through minion) → AA → S2 → S3**

1. S1 a minion ahead of the fleeing enemy
2. S3 through the minion (gap-close)
3. AA → S2 enemy (mark)
4. S3 through enemy

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Resistance Boots are mandatory.** Luna's #1 counter is hard CC. Tenacity gives you a chance to S3 out.
- **S2 grants a shield.** Use it to tank burst while you wait for the next S1 cooldown.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile comps. Hard-countered by Suppression (Donghuang, Liang) and instant point-and-click CC.
