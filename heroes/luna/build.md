# Luna Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Semi-tank Magic Bruiser. Must survive in melee while chain-dashing. Needs **Magic Lifesteal, CDR, and tankiness.** CN server consensus: **Semi-tank sustain.**

---

## Recommended Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Rune Greatsword (符文大剑)** | Blue jungle knife: AP stacking + enhanced AA after abilities. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | **Mandatory.** CC is Luna's #1 counter. Tenacity keeps S3 chain alive. |
| 3 | **Devourer's Tome (噬神之书)** | 25% Magic Lifesteal + 10% CDR. Sustain engine. Low HP → S3 through jungle → full HP. |
| 4 | **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | AP + Max HP. Converts AP to dual defenses — Luna becomes a genuine Magic Tank. |
| 5 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** or **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Physical defense. **Ominous Premonition:** anti-ADC. **Berserker's:** stacking MS + damage. |
| 6 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** or **Splendor (辉月)** | **Void Staff:** vs magic resist. **Splendor:** Stasis for dodging fatal CC while waiting for S1. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Luna's AS threshold is the strictest in the game. **32% AS is the universal standard.**

### Setup: "32% AS Moon Combo" (Universal — 100% of Top Players)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1.6%, Critical Rate +0.5% |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** **+32% Attack Speed**, +10% MS, +64 Magic Penetration, +5% Critical Rate. The only setup. Never change the reds or greens.

### Micro-Adjustment: "Sustain Hybrid"

| Change | From | To |
|--------|------|-----|
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) ×3 → Greed (贪婪) ×3 | Sacrifice 3% AS/MS for **+4.8% Magic Lifesteal.** Slightly healthier early jungle clears. |

## Settings (Critical)

- **Disable Auto-Attack**
- **Enable Advanced Free Attack Mode** (补刀/推塔键)
- **AA count: 1-2-3-mark**

## Quick In-Game Setup

Luna is AS-breakpoint dependent. Copy the **CN #1 Luna** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**. Practice "S1 → S3 → S2 → S3" in Training Mode until muscle memory.
