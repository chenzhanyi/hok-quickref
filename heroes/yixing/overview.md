# Yi Xing（弈星） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Zone Control / Strategic)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 天元之弈 (Grandmaster of Go)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 弈星)

> Yi Xing is a strategically dominant zone-control Mage. He has one of the best area-lockdown ultimates in the game ("draw a prison"), strong map control, and a built-in death-immunity passive that makes him deceptively tanky. His chess-piece collision mechanic rewards precise ability sequencing.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Built-In Death Immunity

When Yi Xing takes fatal damage, he becomes **invulnerable (ethereal)** and gains a shield. 120s cooldown. This lets him bait enemy Assassin ultimates, face-check bushes, and position aggressively — he's far tankier than he looks.

### Chess Collision + Enhanced AA

S1 places a piece. S2 places a formation that **pulls S1 pieces toward it**, triggering an explosion. After collision, Yi Xing's next AA becomes a **super-long-range enhanced auto-attack** — always weave this between spells.

### S2 Second Cast: Pull + Knockup

Enemies touching S2's edge OR recasting S2 **pulls enemies toward the center with a brief knockup**. Core CC — interrupts channeled abilities (Mozi S3, Zhong Kui S3).

### S3 Ultimate: Draw a Prison (Resizable)

S3 can be **dragged to any rectangular shape** before casting. Long strip = block jungle paths. Square = trap enemies in Dragon pit.
