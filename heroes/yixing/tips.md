# Yi Xing Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Built-In Death Immunity = Bait Everything

Yi Xing's passive grants invulnerability + shield on fatal damage (120s CD). Use this aggressively:
- Face-check suspicious bushes (if enemies jump you, passive triggers → walk away)
- Bait Assassin ultimates (Lanling Wang, Jing, Sima Yi)
- Position forward in teamfights to absorb key cooldowns

### 2. S2 Second Cast: Pull + Interrupt

S2's second cast pulls enemies toward the center with a brief knockup. This interrupts channeled abilities:
- Mozi S3 (ultimate)
- Zhong Kui S3
- Zhang Liang S3
Also use to yank enemies back when they try to escape your ultimate.

### 3. Always Weave Enhanced AA

After any S1+S2 collision, your next AA becomes a **super-long-range enhanced attack**. Always: S1 → S2 → AA. Don't leave the enhanced AA unused — it's a significant portion of your damage.

### 4. S1 Bush-Check

S1 pieces provide vision. Store multiple charges and constantly toss them into suspicious bushes. If a piece lands and reveals enemies, you've just saved your team from a face-check.

### 5. S3 Resizable — Not Just Square

S3 can be dragged to any shape:
- **Long strip:** block jungle corridors, cut off escape paths
- **Square:** trap enemies in Dragon/Baron pit
- **Wide rectangle:** zone control in open teamfights

## Signature Combos

### Lane Poke: **S2 → S1 → S2 (recast) → AA**

1. S2 formation under enemy
2. S1 piece inside → collision explosion
3. S2 recast → pull enemy toward center + knockup
4. Enhanced AA from max range to finish

### Perfect Teamfight Engage: **S2 → S2 (recast) → S3 → S1 → S1 → AA**

1. S2 → recast pull for initial CC
2. S3 immediately — trap pulled enemies inside
3. S1 spam inside the cage for multiple collision explosions
4. Enhanced AA to clean up

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Passive stacks permanent AP:** Each collision hit grants permanent Magic Attack. Poke constantly in lane to scale.
- **S3 blocks dashes:** The cage walls can't be dashed through (similar to terrain). Enemies with Flash can still escape.
- **Matchup:** Excels vs immobile teamfight comps. Struggles vs hyper-mobile Assassins who can dash out of S3.
