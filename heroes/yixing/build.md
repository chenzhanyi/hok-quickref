# Yi Xing Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Yi Xing is a utility-control Mage. His enhanced AA and multi-hit skills scale with **CDR, Magic Penetration, and on-hit item effects**. CN server consensus: **semi-tank utility** or **burst**.

---

## Recommended Semi-Tank Utility Build (CN Standard)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR rush for frequent S3 cages and S2 pulls. |
| 2 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | S1+S2 collision is AoE multi-hit — perfectly triggers Mask's percentage HP burn repeatedly. |
| 3 | **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** | S1 collisions and S3 cage deal multi-tick damage — **Sunset Flow stacks to max penetration in under 1 second**. Higher value than Void Staff on Yi Xing. |
| 4 | **Frost Breath (凝冰之息)** | Core lockdown item. Collision slow + Frost Breath = enemies can't escape S3 cage. Bonus damage vs controlled targets. |
| 5 | **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | AP + Health + massive dual defenses. Combined with death-immunity passive, Yi Xing becomes as tanky as a Support. Assassins can't kill him. |
| 6 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** or **Splendor (辉月)** | Burst if team needs damage; Stasis for survival. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Magic Penetration, Movement Speed (roaming), mild Attack Speed (smoother enhanced AA).**

### Setup 1: "Hunt 88-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +16% Attack Speed, **+10% Movement Speed**. Fastest rotations after clearing mid wave.

### Setup 2: "Harmony Sustain" (vs Burst Laners)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +88 Magic Penetration, +6% Attack Speed, **+450 Max Health**, +52 HP/5s, +4% Movement Speed. Survive Mai Shiranui, Shangguan all-ins.

---

## Quick In-Game Setup

Yi Xing's enhanced AA damage is significant late game. For optimal feel, mix **5× Hunt + 5× Harmony** in blue runes. In-game, copy the **CN #1 Yi Xing** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
