# Athena Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Pre-Stack S2 ("Hold the Spear")

Before ganking or ambushing: press S2 early. The enhanced AA buff lasts a long time. Walk in → 1st thrust → S2 is already off cooldown → 2nd thrust immediately → S1/S3 → resets S2 → 3rd thrust. Three thrusts in seconds.

### 2. S1/S3 Reset S2 Cooldown

S1 or S3 hitting an enemy **reduces S2 cooldown by 4 seconds**. This is the engine of her thrust chain. Always weave: S2 → thrust → S1 (reset) → S2 → thrust → S3 (reset) → S2 → thrust.

### 3. Shield = CC Immunity

S1 and S3 grant shields. **While the shield holds, Athena is immune to ALL CC.** Time S1/S3 to absorb Daji's charm, Mozi's cannon, or Lu Bu's knockup.

### 4. S3 + Smite = Guaranteed Hit

S3 roots Athena while charging. Enemies can walk out. At the last instant before S3 detonates: **Smite the enemy to slow them** — this ensures S3 hits and resets S2.

### 5. Death = Free Ward

When you die, you become a True God spirit. **Don't go AFK.** Use this time to:
- Scout the enemy jungle
- Position for a safe revive
- Provide vision for your team
- Follow low-HP enemies so your team can track them

### 6. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take S3 whenever available.** S2 is core damage and mobility.

## Signature Combos

### Standard Triple-Thrust: **Pre-S2 → AA → S2 → AA → S1 → S2 → AA**

1. Pre-stack S2 (hold enhanced AA)
2. Thrust in (1st hit)
3. S2 → Thrust (2nd hit)
4. S1 (shield + reset S2)
5. S2 → Thrust (3rd hit — Penetrate triggers)

### Tower Dive Burst: **Pre-S2 → AA → S2 → AA → S3 → Smite → S2 → AA**

1. Pre-S2 thrusts 1+2
2. Enemy retreats under tower → **S3** (tank tower shot with shield + CC immunity)
3. **Smite** just before S3 detonates (guarantees hit)
4. S3 resets S2 → 3rd thrust — Penetrate execute

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Blue Buff is mandatory.** Athena is extremely Mana-hungry and needs the CDR. Protect your Blue at all costs.
- **Don't revive in enemy crowds.** When dead, float to safety before respawning.
- **Matchup:** Destroys squishy comps. Struggles vs tanky CC-heavy teams that break her shield.
