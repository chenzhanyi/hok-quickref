# Athena Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure burst jungle carry. Needs **CDR** (constant S2 resets) and **Critical Rate** (S2 thrusts CAN crit). CN server consensus: **full damage crit-burst**.

---

## Recommended Jungle Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Standard Assassin jungle item: Attack, Penetration, CDR. |
| 2 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** or **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | **Berserker's:** smoother thrust AA animations. **Calm Boots:** faster S2 resets. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs CC.* |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core squishy-killer: CDR, Health, flat Penetration. |
| 4 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** | **Athena's power spike!** Every S2 thrust perfectly triggers "Power Strike" — one thrust chunks 50%+ HP. |
| 5 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | S2 thrusts CAN crit. Infinity Edge makes crit thrusts delete squishies from full HP. |
| 6 | **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** or **Fate's Edge (名刀·司命)** / **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | Penetration vs armor. Survival for late-game teamfights. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Critical Rate (thrust crits), Physical Penetration, Lifesteal (sustain jungle).**

### Setup 1: "Crit Burst" (CN Standard — Recommended)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×3 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×10 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +13.3% Critical Rate, +10.8% Critical Damage, +64 Physical Penetration, +9 Physical Attack, **+16% Physical Lifesteal**. 10× Reave = skip lifesteal item entirely, snowball economy.

### Setup 2: "100-Pen Attack Speed" (Smoother Animations)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×7 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +7% AS, **+7% MS**, +4.8% Physical Lifesteal. Smoother thrust AA animations + better roaming.
