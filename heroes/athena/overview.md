## Core Mechanics

### Death: Become a Moving Ward

Athena doesn't return to base when killed. She becomes a **True God spirit** that can freely roam the map, providing vision and scouting. On resurrection, she revives where she stands. Don't revive in the middle of enemies вҖ” position safely.

### S2: Spear Thrust (Core)

S2 empowers the next AA into a **dash-thrust**. Land 3 thrusts on the same target = **Penetrate** (25% target's missing HP as bonus damage). This is her execute mechanic.

### Shield + CC Immunity

S1 and S3 grant a shield. **While the shield holds, Athena is immune to ALL CC** (Unstoppable). S1 or S3 hitting an enemy **reduces S2's cooldown by 4 seconds** вҖ” this is how she chains thrusts.

### Pre-Stack S2 ("Hold the Spear")

Before ganking, press S2 early. The enhanced AA buff lasts a long time. Walk into the fight with one thrust ready (1st hit), S2 is off cooldown for the 2nd hit, S1/S3 reset S2 for the 3rd hit.
