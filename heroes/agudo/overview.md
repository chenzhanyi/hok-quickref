# Agudo（阿古朵） — Support / Tank Jungler

## Overview

- **Role:** Support / Tank Jungler (Summoner)
- **Lane:** Roaming / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 山林之子 (Child of the Mountain)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 阿古朵)

> Agudo is a uniquely designed summoner-style Tank Jungler. She doesn't auto-attack jungle camps — instead, she uses **Tame** to instantly clear them. Her strength scales with the number of monsters tamed (max 4 per type), and in combat she summons an army of jungle creatures alongside her mount **Qiuqiu (球球)** for coordinated assaults.

---

## Passive & Core Mechanics

### Tame (Damage-Free Instant Clear)

Approach a jungle monster and use the Summoner Spell button (becomes **Tame**). After a channel, the monster is instantly cleared for full rewards. **Any damage taken from Heroes, Minions, or Monsters interrupts the channel.**

### Monster Stacking

Each monster type can be tamed up to **4 times (4 upgrades)**. Agudo's summoned creatures' damage and tankiness scale directly with tame counts.

### Human-Bear Separation (Ultimate)

Agudo's ultimate sends Qiuqiu crashing down with all tamed monsters. Agudo herself becomes a **ranged Marksman** in this state — but loses Qiuqiu's Health bonus, making her **extremely fragile**. Use defensively or when safe.
