# Agudo Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Master Tame (Passive)

Approach jungle monsters and use the Summoner Spell button (becomes **Tame**) to instantly clear them without fighting. **Any damage interrupts the channel** — from Heroes, Minions, or Monsters. Clear safely.

### 2. Double-Camp Taming (Advanced)

Agudo can basic-attack two adjacent camps (e.g., Blue Buff + Lizard, or Red Buff + Three Pigs) to pull their aggro together. Position at the boundary between both camps and cast Tame — **both camps clear simultaneously**. Fastest jungle clear in the game.

### 3. Ultimate Timing (Critical Survival)

When casting Ultimate (S3), Qiuqiu crashes down with all tamed monsters while Agudo becomes a ranged Marksman. **Agudo is extremely fragile without Qiuqiu's Health bonus.** If enemy Assassins (Lanling Wang, Li Bai) are nearby, **do not blindly throw ultimate**. Use it:
- As counter-engage when dived
- For pushing towers when safe
- To burst down an isolated target

### 4. Recall Qiuqiu (Lifesaving)

If you're dived after throwing ultimate, **press S3 again immediately**. Qiuqiu teleports back to you, restoring a large amount of Health. This is your best survival tool.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S1 first, then S2. Take ultimate whenever available.** S1 provides massive AoE healing + Movement Speed — core for team control and tower pushes. S2 is mainly for unstoppable mobility and escape.

## Signature Combos

### Seamless Lockdown: **S2 → S1 → S3 → AA**

1. S2 charge (roll as a ball) into the enemy — knocks up on contact
2. Immediately cast S1 at their feet (summons vines that root + speed boost allies)
3. Predict enemy movement and cast S3 — Qiuqiu crashes down with knockup
4. Stay at safe range and auto-attack as Marksman form

### Emergency Recall: **S3 → (if dived) S3**

1. Throw ultimate for damage/engage
2. If an enemy Assassin jumps on you, **press S3 again immediately**
3. Qiuqiu teleports back, restoring massive Health

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Stack all monster types to 4:** Pigs, Lizards, Wolves, and Birds. Max-stacked ultimate is devastating.
- **Tower pushing:** Agudo + Qiuqiu + tamed army can demolish towers with minimal minion support.
- **Vision control:** Use tamed monsters' aggro to check bushes safely.
- **Matchup:** Agudo dominates immobile compositions. She struggles against hyper-mobile Assassins who can dive her in Marksman form.
