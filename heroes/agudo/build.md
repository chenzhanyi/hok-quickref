# Agudo Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Agudo's summoned creatures (jungle army + Qiuqiu) inherit **her Max Health**. CN server consensus: **full-tank support build**. She's also a "tower-pushing machine" for the team.

---

## Recommended Full-Tank Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** | Agudo's core item. Stacking Max Health per monster kill + burn aura synergizes perfectly with melee brawling. |
| 2 | **Shadow Boots (影忍之足)** or **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Shadow Boots for physical-heavy teams (stacks with S1 healing for absurd durability). Resistance Boots vs heavy CC. |
| 3 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | CDR + Health + Physical Defense. Agudo's auto-attacks (both mounted and separated) frequently proc the AoE slow, making her extremely sticky. |
| 4 | **Blood Rage (血魔之怒)** | Exceptional cost-efficiency. High Max Health = massive shield from active. The bonus on-hit damage also applies to Qiuqiu. Lets Agudo survive and counter-kill during Human-Bear separation. |
| 5 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Ultimate defense vs high-attack-speed Marksmen (Hou Yi, Marco). Massively reduces their damage output. |
| 6 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense slot. Eternal Night vs burst mages (Gan & Mo, Mai Shiranui); Magic Cloak vs sustained magic poke. |

### Late-Game Swaps

- Sell boots for **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** or **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)**
- If enemies lack magic burst, swap magic defense for more Health items

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Agudo does **not need Physical Penetration** — her damage comes from summoned creatures and magic/true-damage passives. Core stats: **Max Health (1000+ HP), CDR, Movement Speed**.

### Setup 1: Universal "Thousand-HP Speed Tank"

Grants over **+1,100 base Health** at level 1. Combined with Giant's Grip, Agudo + Qiuqiu become unkillable before level 4.

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, Cooldown Reduction +0.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** **+1,162 Max Health**, +23 Physical Defense, +4% Movement Speed, +6% CDR, +10% Attack Speed.

### Setup 2: CN High-Rank "Greed Kiting"

Mixes in Magic Lifesteal for sustain when auto-attacking in separated Marksman form.

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, Cooldown Reduction +0.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×7 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |
| 🔵 Blue | Greed (贪婪) | ×3 | Magic Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** Sacrifices ~100 Max Health for **+4.8% Magic Lifesteal** — sustains Agudo's Marksman-form auto-attacks when kiting at range.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Agudo relies heavily on monster stacking. After entering a match:

1. Open **Preparation (备战)** → copy the **CN #1** or highest-winrate pro setup
2. Prioritize stacking **Pigs, Lizards, Wolves, and Birds** to 4 layers each
3. A max-stacked ultimate crushes towers and teamfights
