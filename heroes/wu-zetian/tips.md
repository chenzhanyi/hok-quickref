# Wu Zetian Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Pre-Stack Enhanced S1

While rotating or bush-camping: cast S1 once (hold the stack). When enemies appear: cast S1 again → instant Enhanced knockup. Two casts of anything = Enhanced S1. Most common: S1 → walk → S1 → Enhanced S1.

### 2. S2: Save for Assassins, Never Farm

S2 is your ONLY self-peel. When Lanling Wang, Ake, Lam, or any Assassin dives you: S2 → they're knocked back + slowed → Enhanced S1 knockup → walk away. **Never S2 minions for wave clear.**

### 3. S3: Best as Follow-Up, Not Opener

S3 stuns globally. But alone, it's easily dodged (enemies see the circle). **Cast S3 as follow-up** when your team's initiator (Guiguzi, Zhang Fei, Lian Po) has already engaged. Enemies are CC-locked and can't dodge.

### 4. S3: Global Vision Check

Enemy team disappears for 5+ seconds from minimap → **S3 immediately.** You'll reveal if they're on Dragon, Baron, or ambushing. This alone can save games.

### 5. Enhanced S1 + S2 Cycle

Optimal rotation: **S1 → S1 (Enhanced) → S2 → S1 (Enhanced again).** S2 counts as the second ability. You can chain TWO Enhanced S1s in rapid succession.

## Signature Combos

### Lane Poke: **S1 → S1 (Enhanced) → AA → S2 → S1 (Enhanced)**

1. S1 (stack 1)
2. S1 again → Enhanced (knockup)
3. AA
4. S2 (counts as another ability)
5. S1 → Enhanced again

### Teamfight Chain: **S3 → S1 → S1 (Enhanced) → S2 → S1 (Enhanced)**

1. S3 (after ally engage — global stun)
2. S1 → S1 (Enhanced knockup on stunned targets)
3. S2 → S1 (Enhanced knockup again)
4. Three consecutive CCs

## Advanced Tips

- **Passive Mana regen means no Holy Grail needed.** Skip Mana items — go straight to Echo Staff + damage.
- **Enhanced S1 has longer range than normal S1.** Use this to snipe enemies who think they're safe.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in coordinated play. Struggles vs hyper-mobile Assassins if S2 is on cooldown.
