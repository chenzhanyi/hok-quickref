# Wu Zetian Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs **CDR** (frequent Enhanced S1 + S3), **Magic Penetration**, and **semi-tank survivability** (no mobility). CN server consensus: **CDR burst.**

---

## Recommended Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR rush. More abilities = more Enhanced S1s. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy dive.* |
| 2 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | MS + AoE explosion. Enhanced S1 triggers it perfectly. |
| 3 | **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** | **Core item!** Enhanced S1 spam + S3 multi-tick stacks 10 penetration layers instantly. |
| 4 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP spike. Enhanced S1 + S3 burst threshold skyrockets. |
| 5 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** or **Frost Breath (凝冰之息)** | **Void Staff:** vs magic resist. **Frost Breath:** perma-slow for kiting vs mobile teams. |
| 6 | **Splendor (辉月)** or **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | **Splendor:** Stasis (stacks with S2 for double survival). **Prophet's Hour:** AP + dual defenses. |
