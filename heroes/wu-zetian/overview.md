# Wu Zetian（武則天） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Global Control / Vision)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 女帝 (Empress)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 武则天)

> Wu Zetian is the game's most exclusive Hero (CN server premium) and a uniquely powerful global-control Mage. Her S3 is a **global-range ultimate** that reveals ALL enemies AND deals damage + stuns them, regardless of distance. Properly timed, she can secure objectives, save allies across the map, and chain-CC entire teams.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Out-of-Combat Mana Regen + MS

When out of combat: **+50 MS + 1% Max Mana regen per second.** She almost never needs Mana items (no Holy Grail needed).

### Enhanced S1: Any 2 Abilities = Empowered Shot

Casting any 2 abilities **refreshes S1** and empowers the next S1: larger projectile, longer range, **double damage + knockup.** This is her core damage + CC tool.

### S2: Anti-Dive (Don't Waste It)

S2 knocks back nearby enemies + slows + grants MS. **This is your ONLY self-peel.** Never use S2 to farm or poke. Save it for when an Assassin dives you.

### S3: Global Vision + Stun

S3 reveals ALL enemies globally and damages + stuns them. Three strategic uses:
1. **Vision:** Enemies missing for 5+ seconds → S3 to check Dragon/Baron or reveal ambushes
2. **Cross-map support:** Cast during side-lane fights to contribute damage + stun
3. **Chain CC (best):** Wait for ally initiation (Guiguzi, Zhang Fei, Lian Po) → S3 follow-up = undodgeable team-wide stun
