# Xiao Qiao（小喬） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Burst / Artillery)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 戀之微風 (Breeze of Love)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 小乔)

> Xiao Qiao is a classic high-burst, long-range traditional Mage. She has strong lane poke and late-game "Flash in, delete everyone" potential. Her passive grants **+25% MS for 2 seconds** whenever any ability hits ANY target — Minions, Monsters, Heroes, even hidden enemies. This makes her deceptively mobile and hard to catch.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Hit Anything = Speed Boost

Any ability hitting ANY target grants **+25% MS for 2 seconds.** Use S1 on jungle camps or minions while rotating for free speed. During S3, this MS boost is constantly active.

### S1: Boomerang Fan (Double Hit)

S1's fan deals damage going out AND coming back. Damage decays per target hit (min 60%). For max damage: position so the fan's **endpoint** lands directly on the target (both outbound and return hit at full damage).

### S1 Bush Check: Watch Your Feet

Throw S1 into a suspicious bush. If you suddenly **glow and move faster** — someone's inside. The passive procs on hidden enemies too.

### S2: Predict, Don't React

S2 has a cast delay. Don't aim where they ARE — aim where they'll BE. Best used: from bush (invisible), after S1 slow, or chained with ally CC.

### S3: Flash Ult Engage

S3 has no cast animation and doesn't interrupt movement. **S3 → Flash** into the enemy backline → S2 → S1. Late-game, this deletes squishies before they can react.
