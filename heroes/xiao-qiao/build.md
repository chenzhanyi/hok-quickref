# Xiao Qiao Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure burst. High AP scaling on all abilities. Needs **Magic Penetration + AP + Movement Speed.** Late-game S3+Flash deletes squishies.

---

## Recommended Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Arcane Boots (秘法之靴)** | Flat Magic Penetration + Mana regen. S1 hits hard early. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC.* |
| 2 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | **Core #1!** MS + AoE explosion on hit. S1 poke becomes devastating. MS stacks with passive. |
| 3 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP spike. After this, S2+S1 deletes squishies. |
| 4 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Penetration vs late-game Magic Cloak. |
| 5 | **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** | Massive AP + damage amp. Combined with Savant's Wrath = 1500+ AP. |
| 6 | **Splendor (辉月)** or **Devourer's Tome (噬神之书)** | **Splendor:** Stasis vs Assassin dives. **Devourer's Tome:** Lifesteal + Health for extended fights. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Magic Penetration, Movement Speed, mild sustain.**

### Setup 1: "Harmony 88-Pen" (CN Standard — Safest)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +4% MS, +450 Max Health, +52 HP/5s. Early survival + near true-damage vs squishies.

### Setup 2: "Hunt-Greed Hybrid" (Max Speed + Sustain)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
