# Xiao Qiao Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Passive MS: Free Speed Everywhere

Any ability hit on ANY target = +25% MS for 2s. While rotating: S1 a jungle camp. While escaping: S1 behind you at chasing enemies. While pushing: S1 the minion wave. You're constantly fast.

### 2. S1 Bush Check: Watch Your Feet Glow

S1 into a bush. If you suddenly speed up → enemies inside. The passive triggers on hidden enemies. Never face-check a bush again.

### 3. S2: Predictive, Not Reactive

S2 has a cast delay. Don't aim at moving feet — aim ahead of their path. Best combo: **S1 slow → they walk predictably → S2 where they'll be.** From bush, it's undodgeable.

### 4. S3 → Flash: The Signature Teamfight Play

S3 has no cast animation. **S3 → Flash** into 3+ enemies → S2 → S1. S3 continues hitting during Flash. This deletes entire backlines late-game.

### 5. S3 Defensive: Anti-Assassin Tool

When an Assassin dives you: **S3 immediately → run.** S3's constant MS + damage hits them while you kite. Don't stop to cast S2/S1 — just run and let S3 + passive MS save you.

## Signature Combos

### Bush Ambush Delete: **S2 → S1 → S3**

1. S2 from bush (invisible, undodgeable)
2. S1 point-blank (both hits at max damage)
3. S3 to finish — they die before landing

### Teamfight Engage: **S3 → Flash → S2 → S1**

1. S3 (MS boost active)
2. Flash into enemy backline
3. S2 underneath the squishies
4. S1 through the cluster

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S1's return hit is guaranteed if enemies are running toward you.** Kite backward after throwing.
- **S3 has a max of 4 hits per target.** Don't expect it to kill tanks — it's for squishy deletion.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile squishies. Struggles vs long-range artillery (Gan & Mo) and stealth Assassins.
