# Sakeer Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Healing scales with **Max HP.** Pure tank = more healing + more survivability. Needs **CDR** for frequent S2/S3.

---

## Recommended Full-Tank Support Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎)** or **Guard · Redemption (近卫·救赎)** | Shadow Aegis: team CDR + Mana regen. Guard: team dual defense. **Redemption active:** group shield — stacks with S1 burst heal. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. S2 flight can be CC-interrupted. |
| 3 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Anti-ADC passive. |
| 4 | **Rising Sun (旭日初光)** | **Sakeer's hidden gem!** S1/S2 constantly procs Rising Sun's shield on Sakeer + lowest-HP ally. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense. Eternal Night for burst; Magic Cloak for poke. |
| 6 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** | Highest Max HP in the game. Amplifies all healing. In-combat regen. "Walking Fountain." |

## Quick In-Game Setup

Sakeer's S2 bush-hopping is his signature. Master the S3 → S2 → S1 combo. Copy the **CN #1 Sakeer** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
