# Sakeer Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Hold AA to Heal (Walk While Healing)

When no enemies nearby: **hold the AA button** while walking. You'll continuously heal the lowest-HP ally (or yourself). Zero Mana cost. Use between fights, during rotations, while your ADC farms.

### 2. S2: Bounce Between Bushes (Never Get Caught)

S2 stores 3 charges. In jungle fights: S2 → bush A → S2 → bush B → S2 → bush C. You're untouchable while dropping healing fireflies the entire time.

### 3. S3 + S2: On-Demand Mobility Anywhere

S3 creates a bush. S2 can fly to it. In open lanes with no terrain bushes: **S3 → S2 to your own bush.** Free engage or escape tool anywhere on the map.

### 4. S1: Save for Peel or Big Heal

S1 is your only hard CC (knockup). Don't waste it for minor poke. Save for: knocking an Assassin off your ADC, or the massive burst heal when allies are low.

### 5. S3 → S2 → S1: The Signature Engage/Save

S3 a bush on the enemy backline (or at your ally's feet) → S2 fly to it → S1 slam immediately on landing. Knockup + massive heal + you're now in position.

## Signature Combos

### Long-Range Engage/Save: **S3 → S2 → S1**

1. S3 create a bush at target location
2. S2 fly to your bush (drops healing fireflies en route)
3. S1 slam on landing (knockup + burst heal)
4. Perfect for: engaging, saving a distant ally, or contesting objectives

### Jungle Kiting: **S2 → S2 → S2 → Hold AA**

1. S2 to bush A, S2 to bush B, S2 to bush C
2. Bounce between bushes — enemies chase shadows
3. Hold AA between flights to heal allies

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S1 heal scales with Firefly charges.** Wait for S3's fireflies to accumulate before S1 for maximum burst heal.
- **S2 can be interrupted by CC mid-flight.** Resistance Boots reduce this risk.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in jungle-heavy fights (many bushes). Struggles in open-lane fights without S3 available.
