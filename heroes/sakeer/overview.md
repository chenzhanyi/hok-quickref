# Sakeer（桑啟） — Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Support (Healer / Mobility Enchanter)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 螢火之旅 (Firefly Journey)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (桑启)

> Sakeer is a powerful hybrid healer Support. His core mechanics: **Fireflies** that auto-heal allies or damage enemies, and his S2 that lets him **fly to any bush** (up to 3 charges). He can create his own bush with S3, then fly to it — giving him on-demand mobility anywhere. His healing scales with **Max HP**, so he builds pure tank.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Hold AA to Heal

When no enemies are nearby, **hold the AA button** to continuously heal yourself or the lowest-HP nearby ally. Zero Mana cost. Walk while holding — heal your team between fights.

### S2: Fly to Any Bush (Up to 3 Charges)

S2 lets Sakeer fly to any bush in range. Stores up to 3 charges. During flight: **drops healing fireflies along the path.** In jungle fights, bounce between bushes like a pinball — enemies can't catch you, allies stay healed.

### S1: Ground Slam (Knockup + Burst Heal)

S1 slams the ground. **With Firefly charges:** deals AoE knockup + **massive burst heal to nearby allies.** Save for: peeling Assassins off carries, or combo with S3 for big team heal.

### S3: Create a Bush (15s Duration)

S3 creates a bush that lasts 15 seconds, spawning fireflies every second. **Sakeer can S2-fly to his own bush.** This gives him on-demand mobility anywhere — even in open lanes with no terrain bushes.
