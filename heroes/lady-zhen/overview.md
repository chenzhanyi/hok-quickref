> Lady Zhen is a classic zone-control burst Mage. Her passive **Freeze** mechanic: 3 ability hits on a target = frozen for 1 second + bonus Magic Damage. Her S2 bounces between enemies (up to 6 times), her S3 creates a 5-second damaging water field that ticks every 0.5s. In clustered teamfights, she can freeze entire enemy teams.

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: 3-Stack Freeze

Each ability hit stacks a mark (7s duration). At **3 stacks: frozen for 1 second + bonus Magic Damage.** Same target can only be frozen once per 5 seconds. S3's multi-tick + S2 bounces = instant team-wide freeze.

### S2: Bounce Off Minions, Not Directly

S2 bounces between enemies up to 6 times (max 2 per target). **Never cast S2 directly at the enemy Hero.** Cast S2 on minions near them вҖ” the bounce auto-targets the Hero. Safer, longer range, guaranteed hit.

### S1: Delayed Knockup (Follow-Up, Not Opener)

S1 has a delay and is easily dodged. Never open with S1. Use it: after freeze (guaranteed hit), after ally CC, or at your own feet when an Assassin dives you.

### S3: Passes Through Minions, Stops on Heroes

S3 passes through minions and monsters but **stops on the first enemy Hero hit.** Use this to S3 through the minion wave directly onto the enemy backline.
