# Lady Zhen Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs **CDR** (frequent S2 spam), **Magic Penetration**, and one defensive item (zero mobility). CN server consensus: **burst with survivability.**

---

## Recommended Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | 15% CDR. S2 spam = more poke + more passive stacks. |
| 2 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | S3's multi-tick + S2 bounces endlessly trigger Mask's %HP burn. |
| 3 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | MS + AoE explosion on hit. S2 bounce triggers it from safety. |
| 4 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP spike. S3+S2+S1 combo now deletes squishies. |
| 5 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Penetration vs late-game Magic Cloak. |
| 6 | **Splendor (辉月)** or **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** / **Ardent Dominator (炽热支配者)** | **Splendor:** Stasis vs Assassin dives. **Prophet's Hour:** AP + dual defenses. **Ardent Dominator:** auto-cleanse shield at low HP. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Magic Penetration, Movement Speed, mild sustain.**

### Setup 1: "Harmony 88-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +450 Max Health, +4% MS, +6% AS. Early durability + near true-damage vs squishies.

### Setup 2: "Hunt Max Speed" (Aggressive Roaming)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +88 Magic Penetration, **+10% MS**, +16% AS. Faster rotations + easier skillshot dodging.
