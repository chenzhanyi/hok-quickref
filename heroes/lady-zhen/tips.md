# Lady Zhen Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S2: Bounce Off Minions, Not Enemies Directly

S2 bounces between targets. **Always cast S2 on minions near the enemy,** not the enemy directly. The bounce range is longer than your cast range — you'll hit enemies from safety.

### 2. S1: Never Open With It (Unless Self-Defense)

S1's delay makes it easily dodged. Correct usage:
- **After freeze:** S3 → freeze → S1 guaranteed hit
- **After ally CC:** Chain with your team
- **Self-defense:** S1 at your own feet when an Assassin dives you

### 3. S3 → S2 → S1: The Perfect Chain

S3 creates a multi-tick water field (0.5s per tick) → instant 2+ passive stacks. Immediately S2 → 3 stacks → freeze. Then S1 on the frozen target → guaranteed knockup. This chain CC's for 2+ seconds.

### 4. S3 Passes Through Minions

S3 stops on the **first enemy Hero** but passes through minions/monsters. Enemies hiding behind their wave thinking they're safe? S3 through the wave onto them.

### 5. Positioning: Never Face-Check

Lady Zhen has zero mobility. Never walk into unwarded areas alone. Use S2 on minions from max range. Stay behind your frontline in teamfights.

---

## Signature Combos

### Teamfight AoE Freeze: **S3 → S2 → S1**

1. S3 into the enemy cluster (multi-tick = instant 2+ stacks)
2. S2 (bounces between them → 3 stacks → mass freeze)
3. S1 on the frozen group (guaranteed knockup)

### Lane Poke: **S2 (on minions) → wait for bounce → S1 (if bounce hit)**

1. S2 the approaching minion wave
2. S2 bounces to enemy → 1 stack
3. Wait for next S2 → 2 stacks
4. If they're slowed/out of position → S1 → S3

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S2 can bounce to enemies in bushes.** Use S2 on nearby minions to check bushes safely.
- **Freeze has a 5-second per-target cooldown.** Track it — don't waste abilities on recently frozen enemies.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs grouped, immobile teams. Struggles vs hyper-mobile Assassins who can dash past S3.
