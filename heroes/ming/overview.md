# Ming（明世隱） — Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Support (Tether Buffer / Healer)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 靈魂劫卜 (Soul Diviner)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 明世隐; nickname: "Xiao Ming")

> Ming is a strategically dominant tether-based Support. He functions as a "mobile 7th item" — his Soul Chain connects to allies (granting massive Attack, Defense, Movement Speed) or enemies (dealing continuous damage + defense shred). His ultimate sacrifices his own HP to instantly restore a teammate's HP (or deal True Damage to enemies).

---

## Core Mechanics

### Soul Chain (S1)

Connect to an ally (buff them) or enemy (debuff them). The chain deals continuous damage to enemies it passes through.

### S2: Stance Toggle

- **Purple Chain (Attack/AP buff):** Default. Use 95% of the time — boosts ally damage and jungle clear speed.
- **Yellow Chain (Defense buff):** Switch when your carry is dived by Assassins (Lanling Wang, Wukong) or targeted by Zhuge Liang S3.

### S3: HP Transfer

Sacrifice Ming's HP to heal an ally (or deal True Damage to enemy). **Over the next 10 seconds, Ming recovers 60% of the sacrificed HP.** Combined with Overlord's Platemail, this becomes nearly cost-free.

### Chain Break Conditions

Chain breaks if: distance too far, target enters bush while Ming is outside, or target becomes untargetable. Stay close to your tethered ally.
