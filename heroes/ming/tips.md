# Ming Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Purple Chain by Default, Yellow for Defense

- **Purple (Attack/AP):** Use 95% of the time — fighting, farming, pushing.
- **Yellow (Defense):** Switch ONLY when your carry is dived by burst Assassins (Lanling Wang, Wukong) or targeted by execute ultimates (Zhuge Liang S3).

### 2. S3 + Redemption Shield Combo

S3 has a ~0.75s cast delay before healing. If your carry is critically low, they might die during the delay. **Press S3 AND Redemption (support item shield) simultaneously** — the shield buys 1 second for the heal to land.

### 3. S3 HP Recovery (Nearly Free)

After S3, Ming recovers **60% of sacrificed HP over 10 seconds**. With Overlord's Platemail or Phoenix Eye, this becomes effectively cost-free. Don't be afraid to use S3 — you'll heal back.

### 4. Chain Touch Damage

The chain deals damage to enemies it passes through. When helping Mid clear wave or in early skirmishes, position so the chain drags through enemies and minions for bonus damage.

### 5. Level 1 Decision

- **Jungler strong early (Liu Bei, Han Xin):** Follow jungler for fast clear
- **Mid needs help:** Tether Mid for wave priority, then rotate together
- Never tether the ADC at level 1 — they don't need you yet

## Signature Combo

### Perfect Life-Saving: **S3 + Redemption Shield**

1. Carry drops to critical HP
2. Press S3 → **immediately press Redemption active**
3. Shield absorbs damage during S3's cast delay
4. Massive heal lands → carry lives

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Move faster than your tethered ally.** If you're slower, the chain breaks. Swift Boots are mandatory.
- **Don't stand in the same bush as your ally.** If they enter a bush and you're outside, the chain breaks. Stand next to the bush, not inside.
- **Matchup:** God-tier with hyper-carries. Struggles when team has no single clear carry to tether.
