# Ming Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Ming's chain buff strength and S3 healing have **massive AP scaling**. CN server meta: **semi-tank AP** — big heals, big buffs, not dying to random AoE.

---

## Recommended Semi-Tank AP Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎)** | Team Attack Speed + CDR + Mana regen. Redemption active: group shield for anti-burst. |
| 2 | **Swift Boots (疾步之靴)** | **Mandatory.** Ming MUST move faster than his tethered ally — chain breaks if you fall behind. |
| 3 | **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | AP + massive HP. Passive converts AP to dual defenses — incredible cost-efficiency. |
| 4 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | **Ming's power spike!** Percentage AP boost: chain buff skyrockets, S3 heals from 10% → 100% HP. |
| 5 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** | Massive HP pool for S3 sacrifice. Continuous HP regen negates S3's HP cost. |
| 6 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Flex defense vs Physical or Magic damage. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Max Health (S3 survival buffer), Magic Attack (buff + heal strength), Movement Speed (chain integrity).**

### Setup 1: "Saint Thousand-HP AP" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Saint (圣人) | ×10 | Magic Attack +5.3 |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, Cooldown Reduction +0.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** **+53 Magic Attack**, **+825 Max Health**, +6% CDR, +4% Movement Speed. Strongest level-1 chain buff.

### Setup 2: "Max Speed Chase" (Mobile Marksmen)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, Cooldown Reduction +0.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +10% Movement Speed, +20% Attack Speed, +23 Physical Defense, **+712 Max Health**, +6% CDR. Best for keeping up with hyper-mobile carries (Marco, Arli, Genghis Khan).
