# Kongming（諸葛亮） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Magic Assassin / Snowball)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 絕代智謀 (Peerless Strategist)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 诸葛亮)

> Kongming is a high-burst, high-mobility Magic Assassin with terrifying snowball potential. His core gameplay revolves around **stacking passive Orbs** (5 marks = 5 homing projectiles) and his **S3 Execute** (kills/assists during cast reduce cooldown by 80% + refresh passive). One successful S3 reset can chain-wipe entire teams.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Orb Stacking

Abilities hitting enemies (Heroes, Minions, Monsters) grant marks. At **5 marks**: summon 5 auto-targeting orbs. **Pre-stack marks before fights** by hitting jungle camps with S1/S2 to reach 3-4 marks — then one ability in the teamfight triggers full orbs.

### S1: Point-Blank = Triple Marks

S1 fires 3 orbs. At range, only 1-2 hit. At **point-blank**, all 3 hit simultaneously — instant 3 marks. Always S1 from melee range for maximum stacking speed.

### S2: Double Slow

S2 leaves a slowing field. If an enemy is **hit by S2 twice in quick succession**, they're slowed by **90%** — effectively rooted.

### S3: Execute + Reset

S3 fires a homing nuke. If the target dies during S3's flight/channel: **-80% cooldown + free passive Orbs**. Enemy tanks can body-block S3 — use S2 or Flash to reposition and curve the projectile around blockers.
