# Kongming Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure burst. Needs **high AP** (S3 execute threshold), **Magic Penetration**, and **Lifesteal** (melee skirmishing). Currently thrives in Jungle with occasional Mid Lane appearances.

---

## Recommended Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Rune Greatsword (符文大剑)** | Blue jungle knife: AP stacking + enhanced AA after abilities. |
| 2 | **Arcane Boots (秘法之靴)** | Mana regen + flat Magic Penetration. Early gank damage is near true-damage. |
| 3 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | Core burst. Passive Orbs and S1 both trigger AoE explosion. |
| 4 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP spike. S3 execute threshold skyrockets. |
| 5 | **Devourer's Tome (噬神之书)** | **25% Magic Lifesteal** + Health. Kongming is melee — the lifesteal lets him drain-tank through fights via passive Orbs. |
| 6 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** or **Splendor (辉月)** | **Void Staff:** vs magic resist. **Splendor:** Stasis survival. *During Stasis, passive Orbs still attack and S3 can be pre-cast before activating.* |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Magic Penetration, Magic Lifesteal, Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "88-Pen Hunt Speed" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×7 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Greed (贪婪) | ×3 | Magic Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +10.2% Attack Speed, **+7% Movement Speed**, +4.8% Magic Lifesteal.

### Setup 2: "Max Lifesteal" (Mid Lane Sustain)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
