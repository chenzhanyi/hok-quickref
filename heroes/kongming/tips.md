# Kongming Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Pre-Stack Passive Before Fights

Never start a teamfight at 0 marks. Before engaging: hit jungle camps or minions with S1/S2 to reach **3-4 marks**. One cast in the fight = instant 5-stack Orb activation.

### 2. S1: Point-Blank for Triple Hit

S1 fires 3 orbs. At range: 1-2 hit. **At point-blank: all 3 hit.** Always close distance (S2) before S1 for maximum mark stacking.

### 3. S2 Double Slow = 90% Slow

S2's field slows enemies. **Hit the same enemy with S2 twice** and they're slowed by **90%** — they can barely move. Use consecutive S2 dashes through the same target to lock them in place.

### 4. S3: Curve Around Body-Blockers

Enemy tanks can body-block S3. When locked on:
1. **S2 sideways** to change your angle
2. S3's projectile curves toward the target from your new position
3. The nuke bypasses the tank and hits the low-HP carry

### 5. S3 Reset = Team-Wipe Engine

If the target dies during S3 (your kill OR teammate's), S3 cooldown is reduced by **80%** and passive Orbs refresh. Chain multiple S3 resets in one fight to wipe teams.

## Signature Combos

### Point-Blank Burst: **S2 → S1 → S3**

1. S2 dash into melee range
2. S1 — all 3 orbs hit (instant 3 marks + passive triggers)
3. S3 execute on the now-low target

### Long-Range Chase: **S2 → Flash → S1 → S3**

1. S2 dash → Flash to extend range
2. Point-blank S1 (3 marks → 5-stack Orbs)
3. S3 execute

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 can be cast during Splendor (Stasis).** S3 → immediately Stasis. The projectile still fires while you're invulnerable.
- **Passive orbs auto-attack even when you're CC'd or in Stasis.**
- **Economic advantage is everything.** Kongming with a gold lead is unstoppable. Farm aggressively.
- **Matchup:** Destroys squishy comps. Struggles vs tanky teams that body-block S3 and survive his burst.
