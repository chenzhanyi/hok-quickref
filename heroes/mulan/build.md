# Mulan Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Heavy Form's built-in DR means she doesn't need full tank items. Build: **max CDR + flat Penetration + semi-tank.** One rotation deletes squishies.

---

## Recommended Clash Lane Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Boots (影忍之足)** | **Not Resistance Boots!** Mulan is Unstoppable in Heavy form — she doesn't need Tenacity. Shadow Boots' Physical DR makes her unkillable vs Physical laners. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core #1. CDR, flat Penetration, Health. Early kill pressure. |
| 3 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Taking hits in Heavy form stacks MS + damage. Perfect synergy with her DR tanking. |
| 4 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Core advanced item!** 10% CDR + active 35% DR. **Stacks with Heavy DR for ~80% total.** Tower dive with impunity. |
| 5 | **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** | 40% Penetration. Late-game armor shred. |
| 6 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** or **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** | Magic defense. Cloak vs sustained poke; Eternal Night vs burst. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Zero Attack Speed (except micro-threshold) or Critical needed. **Pure Physical Penetration + Attack + Movement Speed.**

### Setup: "Stealth 100-Pen" (CN Universal Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% Movement Speed. Stealth (not Hunt) because Mulan scales with raw AD, not Attack Speed.

### Pro Micro-Adjustment: "Dream Crit"

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Reason |
|------|------|-----|--------|
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×1 | **1.6% Critical Rate** |
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×9 | Remaining |

Mulan's Light form combo hits frequently. If that one Calamity rune triggers a crit during the silence combo, you can solo-kill at level 1. Fly (KPL pro) and top CN players use this.
