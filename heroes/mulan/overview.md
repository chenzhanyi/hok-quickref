# Mulan（花木蘭） — Assassin / Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin / Fighter (Dual-Form Skirmisher)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 傳說之刃 (Legendary Blade)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 花木兰)

> Mulan is the most iconic dual-form Clash Lane Hero. Light Form: extreme mobility + 5-stack silence. Heavy Form: massive burst + **Unstoppable + 40-50% Damage Reduction.** She has zero sustain, so her survival depends entirely on Heavy Form's DR and perfect combo execution. Timing, bush camping, and DR management separate gods from minions.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Light Form (Assassin Mode)

+40 Movement Speed. AAs and abilities stack marks — **5 stacks = silence + slow + bonus Physical Damage.** This is how she locks enemies down.

### Heavy Form (Juggernaut Mode)

Slow AAs but massive damage. During Heavy S1 and S2, Mulan is **Unstoppable + 40-50% DR.** Use this to face-tank CC and burst.

### Heavy S1: Double Charge

Hitting ANY target (Minion, Monster, Hero) with Heavy S1's first charge **unlocks a second charge immediately.** Also resets ultimate cooldown.

### Heavy AA Cancel

Heavy AAs are slow. **Press S1 immediately after the AA damage number appears** to cancel the recovery animation. Reverse also works: S1 → AA instantly.
