# Mulan Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Fastest Bush Silence: Light S2 → S1 → AA → Heavy

From bush: S2 (gear slow) → S1 dash + AA (instant 5-stack silence). Enemy is silenced — they can't Flash or cast. Immediately S3 to Heavy → S2 push → S1 double-charge delete. Dead before they regain control.

### 2. Heavy Form DR = Face-Tank Everything

Heavy S1 and S2 grant **40-50% DR + Unstoppable.** When Zhang Fei roars, Daji charms, or Lu Bu ults: **S3 to Heavy → S1 charge.** You'll eat the CC and take almost no damage. Never panic — Mulan in Heavy form is the tankiest Hero in the game for those 2-3 seconds.

### 3. Pure Sky + Heavy DR = ~80% DR

Heavy DR (40-50%) + Pure Sky active (35%) = **~80% total Damage Reduction.** You can stand in the enemy fountain and survive. Use this to tower-dive, face-tank 5-man focus fire, or outplay guaranteed-death situations.

### 4. Heavy AA Cancel

Heavy AAs are sluggish. Cancel the recovery: **AA (damage number appears) → immediately S1.** Also works in reverse: **S1 slash → immediately AA.** This doubles your DPS in Heavy form.

### 5. Minion/Monster = Free Second S1 Charge

Heavy S1's second charge unlocks by hitting ANY target. In lane: charge S1 through the minion wave → second charge unlocked → aim it at the enemy laner.

### 6. Skill Priority

**Max S1 first, then S2. Take S3 whenever available.** S1 is primary engage and burst.

## Signature Combos

### Bush Ambush Silence Kill: **Light S2 → S1 → AA → S3 (Heavy) → S2 push → S1 ×2**

1. S2 gear from bush (slow)
2. S1 dash + AA (5-stack silence)
3. S3 to Heavy
4. S2 continuous push (they're silenced, can't escape)
5. S1 double-charge → dead

### Long-Range Chase: **Light S1 (off minion) → S2 → S1 → AA → Heavy burst**

1. S1 first dash off a minion (gap-close)
2. S2 gear throw (slow)
3. S1 second dash through enemy + AA (silence)
4. S3 Heavy → S2 → S1 ×2

## Advanced Tips

- **No boots but Resistance Boots? Wrong.** Mulan's Heavy form is Unstoppable — she doesn't need Tenacity. Build Shadow Boots (影忍之足) for Physical lane dominance.
- **1.6% "Dream Crit" rune:** Swap 1 Mutation for 1 Calamity. That one lucky crit during Light form combo = unexpected level-1 First Blood.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs squishies and skillshot-reliant Heroes. Struggles vs sustain tanks who outlast her burst.
