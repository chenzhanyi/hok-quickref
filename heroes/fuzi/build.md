# Fuzi Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs **Attack Speed** (fast Rage stacking + S2 CD reduction), **CDR**, and **semi-tank** items. Full damage = dead before S2 activates. Semi-tank = unkillable duelist.

---

## Recommended Split-Push Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early AS for Rage stacking. *Mid-late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC.* |
| 2 | **Lightning Dagger (闪电匕首)** or **Doomsday (末世)** | **Lightning Dagger:** faster wave clear + chain lightning. **Doomsday:** %HP on-hit vs tanky teams. |
| 3 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | CDR + Physical Defense. S1/S2 → AA triggers AoE slow. Maximum stickiness. |
| 4 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Stacking MS + damage while fighting. Perfect for extended duels. |
| 5 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Core advanced item!** 35% active DR stacks with S2's 70% = **~90% DR cap.** Tower dive with impunity. |
| 6 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense. Eternal Night vs burst; Magic Cloak vs sustained poke. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Attack Speed (Rage stacking + CD reduction), Movement Speed, Physical Penetration.**

### Setup 1: "Red Moon Max AS" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1.6%, Critical Rate +0.5% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** **+26% Attack Speed**, +10% MS, +5% Critical Rate, +64 Physical Penetration. Smoothest Rage stacking.

### Setup 2: "Tank Hybrid" (vs Lane Bullies)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |

| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |

**Total:** Sacrifices some AS for Max Health, Physical Defense, and **+4.8% Lifesteal.** Safer laning vs Mulan, Kuang Tie.

---

## Split-Push Macro

Fuzi's win condition is NOT teamfighting. Push the side lane opposite the current objective. Force enemies to respond. Create 4v3 advantages for your team. A Fuzi who groups is wasting his kit.
