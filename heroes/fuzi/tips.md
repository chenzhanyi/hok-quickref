# Fuzi Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Pre-Stack Rage Before Every Fight

Fight only when Rage bar is at 3-4 stacks. AA minions or jungle camps before engaging. A Fuzi fighting at 0 Rage is half a Hero. A Fuzi at full Rage with S2 is unkillable.

### 2. S2 Timing: Activate Right Before Damage Lands

S2 lasts only a few seconds. Don't open with it — activate it **right before** the enemy's burst lands. With Rage bar + AA CD reduction, S2 will be back up almost immediately.

### 3. S2 + Pure Sky = Near-Immortal

S2 (70%) + Pure Sky (35%) = **~90% DR** (game cap). Activate both when tower-diving or when 3+ enemies collapse on you. You'll walk out alive.

### 4. S3 + Flash: Undodgeable Bind

S3's short range makes it dodgeable. **S3 → Flash** directly onto the enemy ADC. They're trapped for 5 seconds. They also deal **20% less damage to you** while bound.

### 5. Split-Push Macro: Your True Win Condition

Fuzi doesn't teamfight — he **forces enemies to answer him.** Push the side lane opposite the current objective. When 1 enemy comes: S3 + kill. When 3+ come: S2 + Pure Sky + walk away. Your team gets a free Dragon/tower.

## Signature Combos

### Duel: **AA (pre-stack) → S2 → AA → S1 → AA → S2 → AA**

1. Pre-stack 3-4 Rage on minions
2. S2 (DR) → AA (hit Rage cap)
3. S1 (pull + slow)
4. AA (reduce S2 cooldown)
5. S2 again (permanent DR)

### Long-Range Bind: **S3 + Flash → S2 → AA → S1 → AA**

1. S3 → Flash onto priority target
2. S2 immediately (block their counter-burst)
3. AA until S2 refreshes
4. S1 to keep them close

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S2 reflects AA damage back at attackers.** Enemy ADC kills themselves hitting you.
- **Rage bar = True Damage.** Even full-tank enemies can't ignore Fuzi's sustained DPS.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in 1v1 and split-push. Struggles when team forces you to group 5v5.
