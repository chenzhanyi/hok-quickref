# Fuzi（老夫子） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Split-Push King / Duelist)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 萬古長明 (Eternal Light)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 老夫子)

> Fuzi is the undisputed "lane bully" and "split-push ceiling." He has terrifying Physical DR (up to 70% on S2) and his S3 is a **5-second forced bind** that prevents enemies from escaping. The longer the game goes, the more unkillable and unstoppable he becomes in 1v1. He's not a teamfighter — he's a sidelane nightmare.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Rage Bar (Red Bar): Your Win Condition

Every 5 AAs fills the Rage bar. When full: **+25% MS, +25% AS, True Damage on AAs, and each AA reduces S1/S2 cooldown by 1s.** Always pre-stack 3-4 AAs on minions before fighting.

### S2: 70% Damage Reduction

S2 grants up to **70% DR** AND reflects enemy AA damage + slows them. With full Rage bar + AA CD reduction, late-game Fuzi achieves **near-infinite S2** — permanently 70% DR. This is why he wins every 1v1.

### S2 + Pure Sky = 90% DR Cap

S2 (70%) + Pure Sky active (35%) = **~90% total DR** (the game's cap). Tower dive with impunity. Face-tank 5 enemies.

### S3 + Flash: Long-Range Bind

S3 has short range but **can be Flash-extended.** S3 → Flash onto the enemy ADC. They're bound for **5 seconds** with 20% reduced damage against Fuzi. They can't escape.

### Split-Push Is Your Job

Don't group. Push the farthest lane. 1 enemy comes = S3 + kill. 3+ come = S2 DR + walk away. Your team gets free objectives.
