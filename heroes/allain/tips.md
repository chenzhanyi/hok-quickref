# Allain Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Pre-Stack Rage Before Every Fight

Rage stacks on enemies. Before engaging: AA minions, jungle camps, or even air to build Rage. Enter fights with Rage ready — not at 0 stacks.

### 2. Air-AA to Build S1 Charge

S1's power scales with AA charge count. From bushes: AA air 2-3 times to pre-charge S1. When the enemy walks in: unleash full-charge S1 with 20% missing HP True Damage finisher.

### 3. S2: Always Keep One Charge

S2 stores 2 charges. Never burn both. Use one to engage → keep the second for chase, escape, or dodging skillshots.

### 4. S3: Offensive AND Defensive

S3 is fully untargetable during the leap. Use to: dodge Zhuge Liang S3, Daji combo, Guitarist S3. Also: lock onto a fleeing enemy for the kill, or lock onto a dying ally for the shield save.

### 5. S3 Refreshes S2 Charges

After S3 lands, S2 charges are fully refreshed. Engage cycle: S2 (charge 1) → S1 → S3 → S2 (charge 2) to chase or S2 to escape.

---

## Signature Combos

### Standard Trade: **S2 → AA → S1 → S3 → S2**

1. S2 dash + defense shred
2. AA to max Rage + S1 charge
3. S1 full combo (Unstoppable + True Damage finisher)
4. S3 if needed (chase or dodge)
5. S2 (refreshed from S3) to secure kill or escape

### Long-Range Backline Dive: **S2 → S2 → S3 → AA → S1**

1. S2 two dashes to close distance
2. S3 lock onto enemy backline carry
3. AA + S1 to finish

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S1's final hit at max Rage is the most important damage.** Don't cancel it early by moving.
- **S3 can target allies.** Use to shield your ADC in emergencies.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile squishies. Struggles vs CC that cancels S1 charge.
