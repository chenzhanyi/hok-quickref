# Allain（亞連） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Pursuit Duelist / Mixed Damage)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 追月之影 (Shadow of the Chasing Moon)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (亚连)

> Allain is a dominant pursuit Fighter. His AAs are **4-stage combos (9 total hits)** with Unstoppable frames, dashes, and **triple mixed damage (Physical + Magic + True).** He shreds both squishies and tanks. His S3 is a targeted leap with full untargetability — used offensively to chase or defensively to dodge.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Rage Stacking (Max 16 Stacks)

AAs and abilities on enemy Heroes build Rage. Each stack: +2% DR + 3% Hybrid Penetration. **At max stacks: restores %missing HP.** Always pre-stack on minions/air before engaging.

### S1: Rage-Empowered Finisher

S1 evolves through 4 stages based on Rage. **At max Rage/full charge:** the final hit deals **20% missing HP True Damage** AND grants **Unstoppable/Invulnerability.**

### S2: Defense Shred + Stored Charges

S2 dashes forward, reducing enemy dual defenses by **10-50%** (scales with skill level). **Stores up to 2 charges.** Always keep one charge for chase or escape.

### S3: Untargetable Lock-On

S3 leaps into the air (**fully untargetable**), locks onto an enemy OR ally. Landing: AoE mixed damage + knockup (enemy) or shield (ally). Use to: dodge burst (Zhuge Liang S3, Daji combo), chase fleeing enemies, or shield allies.
