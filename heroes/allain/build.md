# Allain Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Built-in hybrid Penetration from Rage. High AS for fast Rage stacking + on-hit synergy. Two paths: **On-Hit Semi-Tank** (meta, highest winrate) and **Crit Burst** (snowball).

---

## Build A: On-Hit Semi-Tank (Recommended)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Allain must melee — CC stops S1 charge. |
| 2 | **Lightning Dagger (闪电匕首)** | **Core!** High AS + chain lightning. Allain's multi-hit AAs trigger it constantly. |
| 3 | **Blood Rage (血魔之怒)** or **Blazing Cape (红莲斗篷)** | **Blood Rage:** massive shield at low HP + bonus on-hit. **Blazing Cape:** HP + burn aura. |
| 4 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Stacking MS + damage while taking hits. Perfect for Allain's brawl style. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense. |
| 6 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** or **Raging Soul (怒魂)** | Massive HP — amplifies Blood Rage shield and on-hit damage. |
