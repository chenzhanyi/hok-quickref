# Lam Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Lam relies heavily on **Cooldown Reduction (CDR)** to spam Ability 2. He must go into melee range, so full-glass-cannon builds get deleted. CN server consensus: **semi-tank CDR bruiser**.

---

## Recommended Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Attack, Penetration, CDR. Standard Assassin jungle item. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity is essential — getting CC'd mid-dash cancels Lam's entire rhythm. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | CDR + Health + flat Physical Penetration. Core squishy-killer item. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** or **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | **Frost Grip:** Lam's enhanced AAs perfectly trigger the slow, adding stickiness and CDR. **Berserker's Armor:** better for extended brawls — stacking Movement Speed and damage on-hit. |
| 5 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Lam's power-spike item!** Attack + Health + CDR + **active 30% Damage Reduction for 3s**. Lets Lam survive focus fire when diving the backline. |
| 6 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | Late-game must-buy. The percentage bonus damage **stacks multiplicatively with Lam's passive** (+20% True Damage vs Prey < 30% HP). Low-Health targets get deleted instantly. |

### Late-Game Swaps

- Sell jungle item for **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** or **Fate's Edge (名刀·司命)**
- If enemies have heavy magic burst, swap **Berserker's Armor** for **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)**

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Lam's abilities **do not trigger critical hits**. Focus on **high Physical Penetration (100-Pen), Movement Speed, and a touch of Lifesteal** for sustain.

### Setup 1: CN Server "Stealth 100-Pen" (Most Popular)

The go-to for the vast majority of high-ranked CN Lam players. Stealth runes provide high base Attack to raise the execute threshold on every dash.

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% Movement Speed. The Movement Speed massively improves early-game submerged map rotations.

### Setup 2: Jungle Sustain Variant (Beginner-Friendly)

If you struggle with early jungle clear HP or Dragon contests, mix in some Lifesteal runes for smoother sustain.

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×7 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +100 Physical Penetration, +7% Movement Speed, **+4.8% Physical Lifesteal**. Healthy jungle clears, full HP for every gank.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Lam's combos require practice with timing and rhythm. The fastest way to get a working setup:

1. Open **Preparation (备战)** → **Change Build (更换)**
2. Select **Recommended / Pro Player (推荐/大神配置)**
3. Copy the current season's **pro player** or **CN #1** setup
4. In fights, use Minions and Jungle Monsters as **"springboards"** for S2 marks — you'll find Lam surprisingly easy to style on enemies!
