# Lam（瀾）— Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin (Skirmisher / Cleanup)
- **Lane:** Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 鯊之獵刃 (Shark's Hunting Blade)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 澜)

> Lam is a dominant cleanup Assassin. His core mechanics revolve around **Submerge** (diving for mobility) and **Shark Pursuit** (using Ability 2 marks to dash wildly through crowds while dealing damage and healing). He excels at finishing low-Health targets and disrupting enemy formations in the mid-to-late game.

---

## Passive & Core Mechanics

### Passive: Predator's Hunt

Enemies below **30% Health** are marked as **Prey**. Lam deals **+20% True Damage** to Prey targets.

### Ability 1: Submerge (Dive)

Lam dives underwater, gaining Movement Speed. If a **Prey** target is nearby, the speed boost increases dramatically. While submerged:
- **Ability 2** gains an extra dash
- **Ability 3** can be cast **instantly with zero wind-up**

### Ability 2: Shark Pursuit (Core — "Infinite Dash")

Hitting enemies (Heroes, Minions, Jungle Monsters) with Ability 2 grants **Marks** (max 3 stacks). Each basic attack consumes 1 mark, transforming into a **dash-strike** that **reduces Ability 2 cooldown by 1s**. At max CDR with multiple targets nearby, Lam can chain near-infinite dashes.

### Ability 3 / Ultimate: Reverse Drag

Lam throws a dagger and dashes forward, **dragging all enemies in the path to the endpoint**. Use submerged flanking → reverse ultimate to **kidnap enemy squishies** back into your team.
