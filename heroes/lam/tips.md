# Lam Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Master the Submerge (Ability 1)

Lam's dive grants **bonus Movement Speed**, and the speed skyrockets when a **Prey** (enemy < 30% HP) is nearby.

- While submerged: **Ability 2** gains an extra dash
- While submerged: **Ultimate casts instantly** with zero wind-up animation
- Use submerged form to **flank behind enemies**, then reverse-ultimate them into your team

### 2. "Infinite Ability 2" Secret

Ability 2 grants **Marks** (max 3) upon hitting any target — Heroes, Minions, or Jungle Monsters. Each basic attack consumes 1 mark:

- Transforms into a **dash-strike**
- **Reduces Ability 2 cooldown by 1s**

At **max CDR (40%)** with multiple targets, chain: **S2 → AA → S2 → AA → S2 → AA** for near-infinite dashes through crowds.

### 3. Ultimate "Reverse Kidnap" (Ability 3)

Lam's ultimate throws a dagger then dashes, **dragging ALL enemies in the path to the endpoint**.

- Flank with S1 submerged → reverse ultimate → throw enemy squishy into your team
- Can be cast **instantly from submerged state** (no wind-up)
- Use to peel for your backline or isolate a priority target

### 4. Pre-Stack Marks

Before ganking or teamfighting:
- Hit Jungle Monsters with S2 to **pre-stack 3 marks**
- When enemies approach, dash-strike in with auto-attacks → immediately S2 again
- This gives you **double burst** on first contact

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take ultimate whenever available.** S2 is Lam's primary damage and healing source — max it first.

## Signature Combos

### Long-Range Reverse Kidnap: **S1 → S1 (or S3) → S3 → S2 → AA×3**

1. S1 submerge for speed
2. Approach from behind — either S1 again to surface, or **S3 directly (instant from submerged)**
3. Ultimate drags target backward into your team
4. S2 for marks
5. Dash-strike ×3 to finish

### Crowd Chaos / Outplay: **S2 → AA → S1 → AA → S3 → AA**

1. S2 on multiple targets (enemy team + minion wave = full marks)
2. Dash-strike through marks, healing with each hit
3. S1 to reposition or chase
4. Once enemies drop below 30% HP (Prey mark triggers), ultimate to clean up
5. True Damage from passive finishes any survivors

### Tower Dive Execute: **S1 submerge → S3 instant → S2 → AA**

1. Submerge outside enemy vision
2. Instant S3 under tower — no wind-up means no time to react
3. S2 for burst marks
4. Dash-strike execute
5. S1 second cast to escape tower range

## Advanced Tips

- **Minions are your best friend:** In teamfights near lanes, use minion waves as extra S2 mark sources for more dashes and healing.
- **Prey vision:** Enemies below 30% HP are visible to Lam even in bushes — use this to track down low-Health escapees.
- **Don't initiate blindly:** Lam is a **cleanup Assassin**, not an initiator. Wait for your team to engage, then dive the backline once CC is spent.
- **CDR is everything:** Without 40% CDR, your S2 has downtime and the "infinite dash" dream falls apart. Prioritize CDR items.
- **River mobility:** S1 submerged form gets extra speed in the river — use it for fast map rotations between lanes.
