# Wuyan Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S2 Center Hit = Double Damage (Mandatory)

S2's center deals **double damage.** The edge deals normal damage. Always Petrify → S2 center. Without the center hit, Wuyan hits like a wet noodle. With it, she one-shots.

### 2. Petrify → S2 Center → Delete

If the enemy gets Petrify'd (50% chance from any hit): **immediately S2 center.** They can't dodge. This combo deletes squishies from full HP.

### 3. S3: Edge Dealers, Not Huggers

S3's **outer edge deals +40% damage.** When spinning, keep enemies at the edge of your spin radius, not directly on top of you. The damage difference is significant.

### 4. S3 + Flash: Reposition Mid-Spin

You can Flash during S3. Uses: Flash into enemy backline to catch their ADC, Flash to follow their escape, Flash to dodge CC while still spinning.

### 5. Pre-Hold S1 Enhanced AA

S1's enhanced AA can be held for several seconds. Pre-cast S1 → walk into gank range → AA (heavy strike + slow) → if Petrify procs → S2 center → S3.

## Signature Combos

### Bush Ambush One-Shot: **S1 (hold) → AA → S2 → S3**

1. Pre-cast S1, hold enhanced AA
2. AA (heavy strike + slow, chance to Petrify)
3. If Petrify'd: **S2 center** (guaranteed double damage)
4. S3 spin (edge them) to finish

### Teamfight Backline Dive: **S1 → Flash → AA → S2 → S3 + Flash**

1. S1 dash → Flash to close remaining distance
2. AA heavy strike
3. S2 center
4. S3 spin → Flash to stay on the ADC if they Flash away

## Advanced Tips

- **Petrify has an 8-second per-target cooldown.** Track it mentally — after petrifying someone, switch targets.
- **Pure damage Wuyan is viable in Jungle.** One-hammer deletion snowballs hard.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile squishies. Struggles vs long-range kiters who never let you S2.
