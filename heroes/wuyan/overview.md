# Wuyan（鍾無豔） — Fighter / Tank

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter / Tank (Burst Bruiser)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 野蠻之錘 (Barbarian Hammer)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 钟无艳)

> Wuyan wears a tank's skin but has terrifying burst damage. All her abilities have a **50% chance to Petrify** (stun) enemies for 0.75s. Her S2 deals **double damage** at the center impact point. A Petrify → S2 center-hit → S3 spin can one-shot squishies from full HP. She's nicknamed the "One-Hammer Wonder."

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: 50% Petrify on ANY Damage

Every damage instance (AAs, abilities, S3 ticks) has a **50% chance to Petrify** for 0.75s. Same target can only be petrified once per 8 seconds.

### S2: Center = Double Damage

S2 is Wuyan's core burst. **Only the center impact deals double damage.** Petrify → S2 center-hit is the deletion combo. Without the center hit, damage is mediocre.

### S3: Outer Edge = +40% Damage

S3's spin deals **+40% damage at the outer edge.** When spinning, keep enemies at the edge, not directly on top of you. S3 also grants a constant shield.

### S3 + Flash (Big Flash)

You can **Flash during S3's spin.** Use to: reposition onto the enemy backline, chase fleeing enemies, or dodge skillshots while still dealing damage.

### S1: Hold the Enhanced AA

S1 dashes + the next AA becomes an enhanced heavy strike with slow. This AA can be **held for several seconds.** Pre-cast S1 before ganking → hold the heavy strike → AA when in range.
