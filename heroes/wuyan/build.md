# Wuyan Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Two paths. **Full Burst** (Jungle/Clash — one-hammer deletion) and **Semi-Tank Bruiser** (Clash Lane high-ELO — consistent).

---

## Build A: One-Hammer Burst (Jungle / Clash)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** or **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Jungle knife. Tank for safer clears; burst for faster kills. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Wuyan needs to reach melee range. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core. CDR + flat Penetration + Health. |
| 4 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Burst build's soul!** Massive AD. S2 center double-damage + Siege Breaker <50% HP bonus = one-shot. |
| 5 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** | S1 → enhanced AA triggers "Power Strike." Massive burst + MS. |
| 6 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** or **Fate's Edge (名刀·司命)** | Survival. Pure Sky's active DR during S3 spin keeps you alive. |

---

## Build B: Semi-Tank Bruiser (Clash Lane — High-ELO)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | Resistance Boots | Tenacity. |
| 2 | Shadow Axe | Core. |
| 3 | Frost Grip (冰痕之握) | S1 AA triggers AoE slow. CDR + Defense. |
| 4 | Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲) | Stacking MS + damage while fighting. |
| 5 | Siege Breaker (破军) | Late-game damage spike. |
| 6 | Eternal Night (永夜守护) or Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷) | Magic defense. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Zero Attack Speed or Critical needed. **Pure Physical Penetration + Attack + Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Stealth 100-Pen" (85%+ Usage — CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +45 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% MS. Max S2 center-hit damage.

### Setup 2: "Harmony Sustain" (vs Lane Bullies)
