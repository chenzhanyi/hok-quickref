# Gan & Mo Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Permanent AP Stacking = Win Condition

Every kill or assist = **+15 permanent AP** (max 20 stacks). This is equivalent to a free Savant's Wrath at max stacks. Roam constantly, even just to tag assists. A stacked Gan & Mo one-shots squishies from full HP.

### 2. S1 → S2 Guaranteed Center-Hit

S1's pushback distance **perfectly aligns with S2 (short sword) convergence.** When dived by Assassins:
- **S1 push them away → immediately S2**
- The enemy lands exactly at the S2 intersection point
- All 4 quad-swords (if S4-empowered) hit center-mass
- Most Assassins die instantly to this counter

### 3. S4 Quad-Sword Burst

S4 refreshes S2/S3 + empowers the next cast to **4 swords**. Max burst sequence: **S3 → S4 → S3** (8 swords total in rapid succession). Late-game, a center-hit quad-S3 deletes squishies from 100% HP.

### 4. Choose the Right Sword Distance

- **Enemy is close/mid-range:** Use S2 (short sword)
- **Enemy is far:** Use S3 (long sword)
- Wrong sword choice = swords don't converge on the enemy = zero damage

### 5. Skill Priority

**S2 and S3 level together.** Max S1 last. Take S4 whenever available.

## Signature Combos

### Anti-Assassin Counter: **S1 → S4 → S2**

1. Assassin dives you
2. S1 push them back (perfect S2 distance)
3. S4 empower
4. S2 quad-sword → guaranteed center-hit → assassin deleted

### Long-Range One-Shot: **S3 → S4 → S3**

1. S3 long sword to scout/poke
2. S4 empower + refresh
3. S3 quad-sword at max range → squishy HP bar disappears

### Standard Poke: **S3 → S2**

1. S3 long sword to test range
2. If enemy walks closer, S2 short sword
3. Constant sword pressure in lane

## Advanced Tips

- **Enable "camera follows skill direction" in settings.** Gan & Mo's swords go off-screen at max range.
- **S1 also shreds enemy Magic Defense.** Use it offensively when safe — S1 → S2 combo kills faster.
- **Matchup:** Destroys immobile backliners. Struggles vs hyper-mobile Assassins who can juke the convergence point.
