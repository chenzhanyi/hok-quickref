# Gan & Mo Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure burst. High AP + high Magic Penetration. No utility needed — the goal is "one sword = one trip back to fountain."

---

## Recommended Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR for frequent S2/S3 spam. More swords = more kill pressure. |
| 2 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | Core burst item. Sword convergence at range perfectly triggers Echo's AoE explosion. Also excellent for bush-checking. |
| 3 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | **Power spike.** Percentage AP boost amplifies both base AP AND the permanent AP from passive stacks. Mid-game quad-swords start one-shotting. |
| 4 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Magic Penetration. Mandatory when enemies build Magic Cloak/Eternal Night. |
| 5 | **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** | Highest base AP in the game. Bonus damage scaling based on total AP. |
| 6 | **Splendor (辉月)** or **Ardent Dominator (炽热支配者)** | **Splendor:** Stasis for dodging fatal damage when S1 is on cooldown. Fast players can sell→buy Sage's Sanctuary during Stasis. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Magic Penetration (true-damage feel), Movement Speed (roam for assists), mild sustain.**

### Setup 1: "Harmony 88-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +4% Movement Speed, +450 Max Health, +52 HP/5s, +6% Attack Speed. Early durability + near true-damage vs squishies.

### Setup 2: "Hunt Roam Speed" (Aggressive Assist Hunter)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×7 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Greed (贪婪) | ×3 | Magic Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, **+7% Movement Speed**, +4.8% Magic Lifesteal, +13% Attack Speed. Faster rotations for passive stacking.
