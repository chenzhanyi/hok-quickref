# Gan & Mo（幹將莫邪） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Artillery Burst)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★★
- **Title:** 淬命雙劍 (Tempered Twin Swords)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 干将莫邪)

> Gan & Mo is a classic long-range artillery burst Mage. Their S2/S3 are curved flying swords that converge at a **fixed intersection point**, demanding extreme precision and prediction. Their passive permanently stacks Magic Attack on kills/assists (max 20 stacks = +300 free AP), making them an unstoppable late-game carry.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Permanent AP Stacking

Every **kill or assist** permanently grants **+15 Magic Attack** (max 20 stacks = +300 AP, equivalent to half a Savant's Wrath). **Constantly roam for assists** — this passive is your win condition.

### S2/S3: Short & Long Swords

- **S2:** Shorter-range convergence (close targets)
- **S3:** Longer-range convergence (distant targets)
- The **intersection point** is where maximum damage occurs. Estimate enemy distance and choose the right sword.

### S1: Push + Perfect S2 Combo

S1 pushes enemies back and retreats Gan & Mo. **The push distance perfectly aligns with S2's (short sword) convergence point!** When an Assassin dives you: S1 → S2 (guaranteed center-hit).

### S4 Ultimate: Quad Swords

S4 refreshes S2/S3 cooldowns and empowers the next cast to fire **four swords instead of two**. Maximum burst: S2/S3 → S4 → S2/S3 (quad sword).
