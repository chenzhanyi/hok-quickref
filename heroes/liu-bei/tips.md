# Liu Bei Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Point-Blank or Nothing

Liu Bei's AA fires a spread. At range: scratch damage. At melee: all pellets hit for maximum damage. Always S2 gap-close or bush ambush to point-blank before firing. Never poke from range.

### 2. Pre-Stack S1 at Level 1 (Mandatory)

S1 lasts 5s, cooldown ~6s. At 0:26 before jungle spawns: cast S1. Camps spawn → 2 enhanced AAs → S1 off cooldown → 2 more enhanced AAs. Fastest level 1 clear in the game. Master this.

### 3. S3: Walk Through CC

S3 cleanses CC + grants a massive shield. **While shielded, Liu Bei is IMMUNE to all CC.** When the enemy has Daji, Mozi, or heavy CC: pop S3 and walk straight through their abilities onto the backline. They can't stop you.

### 4. S3 Shield = Damage → More Shield

S3 converts damage dealt into additional shield. The more you attack, the tankier you get. Never retreat when S3 is active — keep shooting.

### 5. S2 Wall Stun

S2 push + stun. **If the enemy hits a wall, stun duration increases.** In jungle corridors or near towers, angle S2 to slam enemies into terrain.

## Signature Combos

### Bush Ambush Delete: **Pre-S1 → AA×2 → S2 → S1 → AA×2 → S3**

1. Pre-stack S1 from bush
2. Enemy walks past → 2 enhanced AAs at point-blank
3. S2 push into wall (extended stun)
4. S1 (off cooldown) → 2 more enhanced AAs
5. S3 if needed for CC immunity + shield

### Aggressive Dive: **S2 → S3 → S1 → AA×2**

1. S2 dash into enemy backline
2. S3 immediately (cleanse any CC + shield)
3. S1 → 2 enhanced AAs at point-blank
4. Keep firing — shield grows with damage dealt

## Advanced Tips

- **Liu Bei is a Dragon/Baron shredder.** He solos objectives faster than almost anyone. Control the map through objectives.
- **Center pellets can crit.** The middle two pellets trigger critical hits and on-hit effects.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile squishies and objective-focused games. Struggles vs hyper-mobile kiting comps.
