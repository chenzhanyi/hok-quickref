# Liu Bei Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Center pellets can crit and trigger on-hit effects. Build path: **Crit + On-Hit Penetration** or **Semi-Tank Bruiser** depending on enemy comp. This is the highest winrate burst build.

---

## Recommended Jungle Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Standard Jungler item: Attack, Penetration, CDR. |
| 2 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Attack Speed for smoother AA spraying. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC (stacks with S3 for double immunity).* |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core Fighter item: flat Penetration, Health, CDR. |
| 4 | **Doomsday (末世)** | **Liu Bei's power spike!** Multiple pellets per AA perfectly trigger 8% Current HP on-hit. Melts tanks. Built-in Lifesteal. |
| 5 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** | S1 spam → constant "Power Strike" procs. Enhanced AA after every S1. Massive burst + MS. |
| 6 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** or **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Berserker's:** Defense + stacking damage/MS. **Siege Breaker:** execute for snowballing. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Physical Penetration, Movement Speed, mild Attack Speed + Lifesteal.**

### Setup 1: "Hunt 100-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% AS, +10% MS.
