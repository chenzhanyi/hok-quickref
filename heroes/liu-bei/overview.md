# Liu Bei（劉備） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Melee Burst Jungler)
- **Lane:** Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 仁德義槍 (Benevolent Gun)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 刘备)

> Liu Bei is a uniquely designed close-range burst Fighter/Jungler. Despite wielding a shotgun, he plays like a melee Assassin — **the closer he is, the more pellets hit, the higher the damage.** His S3 grants a massive shield that makes him **CC-immune while it holds** and converts damage dealt into more shield. Get in their face and unload.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Point-Blank = Maximum Damage

Liu Bei's AA fires a spread of pellets. At range: 1-2 hit (scratch damage). At melee range: **all pellets hit** (devastating damage). Always play like an Assassin — flank, bush camp, or S2 gap-close to point-blank before firing.

### S3: CC-Immune Shield (Your Entire Identity)

S3 cleanses CC and grants a massive shield. **While the shield holds, Liu Bei is immune to ALL CC.** Damage dealt converts to shield. This is what makes him unpeelable — pop S3 and walk through enemy CC to delete the backline.

### Pre-Stack S1 (Level 1 Cheat Code)

S1 buff lasts 5 seconds, its cooldown is ~6 seconds. **At 0:26 before jungle spawns: cast S1 once.** When camps spawn: 2 enhanced AAs → S1 off cooldown → 2 more enhanced AAs. This gives you the fastest level 1 jungle clear in the game.

### S2: Stun on Terrain Collision

S2 charges and knocks back. If the enemy hits terrain, **stun duration increases.** Position to push enemies into walls.
