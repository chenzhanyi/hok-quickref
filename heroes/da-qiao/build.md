# Da Qiao Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs max **CDR** (frequent S2/S4), **survivability** (can't get one-shot before casting), and **utility**. Full support build.

---

## Recommended Support Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎)** | Shadow Aegis aura: team AS/CDR/Mana regen. **Redemption active:** group shield — pop during S2 channel to protect allies waiting for the teleport. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Da Qiao must survive to cast S2/S4. Tenacity prevents CC-lock into death. |
| 3 | **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** | **20% CDR** + Physical Defense. Da Qiao's core item — her abilities are her entire value. |
| 4 | **Rising Sun (旭日初光)** or **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | **Rising Sun:** shields lowest ally on hit. **Ominous Premonition:** anti-ADC/Assassin defense. |
| 5 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic Defense + spell shield. Prevents getting deleted by Magic Assassins. |
| 6 | **Frost Breath (凝冰之息)** or **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | **Frost Breath:** S1/S3 multi-hit triggers AoE slow. **Prophet's Hour:** AP + dual defenses for mixed survival. |

Core needs: **CDR, Max Health (survival), Movement Speed.**

## Quick In-Game Setup

Da Qiao depends heavily on teammate coordination. In solo queue, set quick-chat to **"Teleport to me"** or **"Watch my elevator"** — pinging before S2 activates dramatically improves team synergy. For builds, copy the **CN #1 Da Qiao** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
