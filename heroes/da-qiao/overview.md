# Da Qiao（大喬） — Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Support (Strategic / Global Teleport)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 滄海之曜 (Light of the Sea)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 大乔)

> Da Qiao is a strategically dominant, game-deciding utility Support. Her unique **S2 "Elevator"** (teleport allies to base for full heal) and **S3/S4 global teleport** make her a staple in high-rank and pro play. She doesn't require mechanics — she requires **macro awareness and placement genius**.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S2: Elevator (Teleport to Base)

Summons a circle. After 4 seconds, all allies inside are **teleported to base and fully healed**. **Never place S2 under fighting allies** — they'll be accidentally sent home. Place it **to the side, in bushes, or on the tank's retreat path.**

### S4: Global Rally (Teleport to Fight)

Summons a large circle. **All allies across the map can tap to teleport directly to it.** Also grants Attack Speed to allies inside. Use for: team-wide ganks, objective contests, split-push escapes, and tower sieges.

### S2 → S4 Combo (Full Heal + Instant Return)

1. Place **S2** in safe spot
2. 1-2 seconds before S2 activates, cast **S4** on the battlefield
3. Team takes S2 home (full heal) → immediately **tap S4 to teleport back**
4. Entire team returns to the fight at full HP in under 5 seconds

### S1: Knockback + S3: Silence

- **S1:** Tidal wave knocks enemies back — interrupts Mozi S3, Dun Shan shield, Zhong Kui S3.
- **S3:** Continuous **Silence** zone. Drop on Gan & Mo, Shangguan, or diving Assassins to prevent them from casting.
