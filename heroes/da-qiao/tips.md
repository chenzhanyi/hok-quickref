# Da Qiao Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S2 Placement: NEVER Under Fighting Allies

This is the #1 Da Qiao mistake. S2 under fighting allies = they get sent home mid-fight ("actor elevator"). **Always place S2 to the side, in bushes, or on the tank's retreat path.** Let allies CHOOSE to step in.

### 2. S2 → S4 Combo: Full HP Team Returns

1. Place **S2** in a safe spot behind your team
2. ~2 seconds in, cast **S4** where the fight is happening
3. Team takes S2 home → full HP + Mana → **immediately tap S4**
4. Entire team is back in the fight at full resources within 5 seconds
5. This is Da Qiao's most game-breaking mechanic

### 3. S3 Silence: Shut Down Casters

S3 creates a **continuous Silence zone**. Drop it on:
- Gan & Mo (can't cast swords)
- Shangguan (can't dash)
- Diving Assassins (can't combo)
The Silence prevents ALL ability casts while they stand in it.

### 4. S1: Interrupt Channeled Abilities

S1's knockback wave interrupts:
- Mozi S3 (ultimate)
- Dun Shan S1 (shield)
- Zhong Kui S3 (ultimate)
- Zhang Liang S3 (ultimate)

### 5. S4: Not Just for Fighting

S4's global teleport has multiple strategic uses:
- **Split-push escape:** Push sidelane → S4 to join team when enemies respond
- **Objective contest:** Teleport whole team to Dragon/Baron instantly
- **Tower siege:** S4 at enemy tower + Attack Speed buff = fast demolition

---

## Signature Combo

### Full Team Reset: **S2 → S4**

1. Team is low after extended fight
2. Place S2 behind retreat path
3. Cast S4 on the objective/enemy position
4. Team heals to full → teleports back → wins fight with resource advantage

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S4 grants Attack Speed.** Use it when sieging towers — the AS buff speeds up demolition significantly.
- **Coordinate S2 timing.** In solo queue, quick-chat "Retreat" or "Gather" before S2 activates so teammates know to step in.
- **Da Qiao + Fuzi/Milady is unfair.** Global split-push + instant team rotations = unanswerable map pressure.
