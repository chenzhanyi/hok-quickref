# Liang（張良） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Suppression / Anti-Carry)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane / Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 言靈之書 (Book of Word Spirits)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 张良)

> Liang is a strategically dominant "utility" Mage. His S3 is **Suppression** — the strongest CC in the game, uncleansable by anything except Da Qiao's S2 (teleport to base). He's the ultimate "anti-fancy" Hero: Li Bai, Lam, Luna, Diaochan, and Arli all fear his point-and-click shutdown. His passive deals True Damage on consecutive hits, letting him shred even tanks.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S3: Absolute Suppression

S3 **Suppresses** a target — uncleansable by Purify, Unstoppable, or any CC removal (only Da Qiao's S2 can break it). **During S3: DO NOT move the left joystick or cast other abilities** — either will cancel S3 instantly. Take your hand off the screen.

### S2: Vision + Continuous Damage

S2's magic field provides **bush vision** AND deals multi-tick damage. Use to: check bushes before face-checking, zone enemies, and stack passive True Damage.

### Passive: True Damage on Consecutive Hits

Abilities and AAs that hit within a short window trigger **True Damage**. S2's multi-tick + S1 + S3 chain = constant True Damage procs.

### Defensive > Offensive S3

Don't Flash+S3 into the enemy team — you'll die. Play with your ADC/Mage. When the enemy Assassin dives them: **S3.** They're suppressed and die to your carries.
