# Liang Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Liang is a utility Mage. His job is CC, not damage. Two paths: **Mid Semi-Tank Utility** (recommended) and **Roaming Support**.

---

## Build A: Mid Semi-Tank Utility (Recommended)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Swift Boots (疾步之靴)** | Clear wave fast → roam fast. S3 ganks win side lanes. |
| 2 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | S2's multi-tick endlessly triggers Mask's %HP burn. |
| 3 | **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** | 20% CDR + Physical Defense. Survive while channeling S3. |
| 4 | **Rising Sun (旭日初光)** or **Holy Grail (圣杯)** | **Rising Sun:** shields ally on hit. **Holy Grail:** infinite Mana for constant S2 spam. |
| 5 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense vs burst Mages. |
| 6 | **Frost Breath (凝冰之息)** | S2 + Frost Breath = perma-slow zone. Enemies can't escape. |

---

## Build B: Roaming Support (Bodyguard)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎) | Team CDR + Mana regen. Redemption active for ally saves. |
| 2 | Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴) | Tenacity to survive CC and cast S3. |
| 3 | Glacial Storm (极寒风暴) | 20% CDR. |
| 4 | Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装) | Massive HP + regen. |
| 5 | Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷) | Magic defense. |
| 6 | Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆) | Physical Defense + anti-ADC. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Movement Speed (roaming), CDR, Magic Penetration.**

### Setup 1: "Harmony 88-Pen" (CN Standard — Utility)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +4% MS, +450 Max Health, +52 HP/5s.

### Setup 2: "10% CDR Speed" (Max Control)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Compassion (怜悯) | ×10 | Cooldown Reduction +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +24 Magic Penetration, **+10% CDR**, **+10% MS**. S3 available almost every fight. *Note: slower wave clear without Mind's Eye.*
