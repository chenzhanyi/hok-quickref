# Liang Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S3: Don't Move — Take Your Hand Off

S3 Suppresses a target. **Any joystick movement or ability cast cancels S3.** When you S3 someone: take your left thumb off the joystick. Become a statue. This is the #1 Liang mistake — self-canceling S3.

### 2. S3: Defensive, Not Offensive

Don't Flash+S3 into 5 enemies. You'll die instantly. Correct: **stay next to your ADC/Mage.** When the enemy Assassin (Jing, Lam, Lanling Wang, Li Bai) dives them → S3. They're suppressed and your carries kill them for free.

### 3. S2: Bush Vision

S2 provides bush vision. Never face-check a bush — throw S2 in first. If enemies are inside, the damage numbers reveal them. Also great for Dragon/Baron pit vision.

### 4. S2 + S1 + S3: Continuous True Damage

S2 multi-tick → S1 stun → S3 chain = constant passive True Damage procs. Even tanks melt if they stand in your S2 + get S3'd.

### 5. Rotate After Wave Clear

Liang clears waves extremely fast (S2 + S1). As soon as wave is clear: **roam with Swift Boots to side lanes.** S3 is a guaranteed kill if you arrive while the enemy is extended.

## Signature Combos

### Bush Ambush: **S2 → S1 → S3**

1. S2 field under the enemy (they can't see it from bush)
2. S1 wall (stun + push into S2 field)
3. S3 immediately — they're locked in the S2 field taking constant True Damage

### Flash Engage (Risky — only with team): **S2 → Flash → S3**

1. S2 at your feet or forward
2. Flash into range
3. S3 the enemy carry
4. Your team deletes them

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 is uncleansable by Purify or Unstoppable.** Only Da Qiao's S2 (base teleport) can save the target.
- **S1 can block paths.** Use S1 walls to zone enemies in jungle corridors.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs hyper-mobile carries. Struggles vs long-range poke that outranges S3.
