# Erin Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Erin is a **magic-damage attack-speed Marksman**. She converts Magic Attack → Critical Rate and deals 100% Magic Damage. Standard physical marksman items are **useless** on her. Build path: **Magic Attack Speed**.

---

## Recommended Core Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early Attack Speed for faster energy stacking and smoother slide-steps. *Late game: swap to Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴) vs heavy CC.* |
| 2 | **Golden Sword (金色圣剑)** | **Erin's #1 core item.** Perfect for AA-based magic champions. At max stacks grants massive Attack Speed + bonus Magic Damage on-hit. |
| 3 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage-based Magic Attack steroid. Since Erin's crit rate scales with Magic Attack, this item **doubles her damage and crit rate simultaneously**. |
| 4 | **Shadow Blade (影刃)** | Yes — a physical crit-speed item on a magic marksman. Works because Erin's Magic Attack conversion gives her high crit rate, and Shadow Blade's on-crit Attack Speed/Movement Speed makes her an **uncatchable slide-step machine**. |
| 5 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | Magic Penetration. Essential mid-late game when enemies build Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷) or other magic resist. |
| 6 | **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** or **Splendor (辉月)** | **Defensive flex slot.** Prophet's Hour gives dual defenses + Health scaling with Magic Attack. vs burst assassins (Lanling Wang, Jing), swap to Splendor (Stasis active). |

### Late-Game Swaps

- Sell **Berserker's Boots** for **Resistance Boots** (vs CC) or **Arcane Boots** (vs tanks)
- If enemies have no magic resist, swap **Void Staff** for **Sage's Tome (贤者天书)** for raw AP
- **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** or **Fate's Edge (名刀·司命)** for final teamfight survival

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Erin needs **Magic Lifesteal (sustain), Attack Speed, and Magic Penetration**. Never use physical marksman runes on her.

### Setup 1: CN Server "16% Lifesteal Speed" (Most Popular)

Built-in 16% Magic Lifesteal means you can skip the lifesteal item entirely and go full damage.

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1.6%, Critical Rate +0.5% |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Greed (贪婪) | ×10 | Magic Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +26% Attack Speed, +5% Critical Rate, +64 Magic Penetration, **+16% Magic Lifesteal**.

### Setup 2: "Movement Speed Kiting" (Smoother Slides)

Mix in Hunt runes for Movement Speed if 10× Greed feels too slow in early laning.

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1.6%, Critical Rate +0.5% |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Greed (贪婪) | ×5 | Magic Lifesteal +1.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×5 | Attack Speed +1%, Movement Speed +1% |

**Total:** +31% Attack Speed, +5% Critical Rate, +64 Magic Penetration, +8% Magic Lifesteal, **+5% Movement Speed**.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Like Ying, Erin's build is completely different from standard marksmen. Using the wrong runes = starting with dead stats. The safest method:

1. Open **Preparation (备战)** → **Change Build (更换)**
2. Select **Recommended / Pro Player (推荐/大神配置)**
3. Copy the **CN #1** or current-season **highest-winrate pro** setup
4. The game auto-syncs high-level Arcana
