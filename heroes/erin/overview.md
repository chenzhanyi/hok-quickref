# Erin（艾琳） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Magic Damage)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 精靈之舞 (Dance of the Spirits)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 艾琳)

> Erin is a **magic-damage Marksman** — all of her basic attacks and abilities deal **Magic Damage**. She converts Magic Attack into Critical Rate and relies on stacking energy through basic attacks to trigger rapid slide-step dashes for kiting.

---

## Passive & Core Mechanics

### Energy Slide-Step (Kiting Core)

Erin has **no traditional attack-move lock**. Every 3 basic attacks, she gains max energy and performs a **slide-step dash** while empowering her next basic attack.

| Mechanic | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Energy stacking** | Each basic attack grants 1 energy stack (max 3) |
| **Full energy** | Triggers a slide-step dash + empowers next basic attack |
| **Stat conversion** | Every 100 Magic Attack → +1% Critical Rate |
| **Damage type** | ALL damage converted to Magic Damage |

### Ultimate: Meteor Shower (Core Burst)

Ability 2 and basic attacks stack **Laurel Leaf marks** (max 12/15). Activating ultimate grants Movement Speed and auto-fires homing shots at the **lowest-Health enemy nearby**.

> ⚠ **Cannot basic attack during ultimate!** Only move + Ability 1 / Ability 2.

### Abilities

- **Ability 1 (S1):** Fires a leaf. On hit/max range → expands into a **ring**. Enemies touching the ring edge are **heavily slowed**. Use for: bush-checking, zoning, escape denial.
- **Ability 2 (S2):** **Instantly maxes energy** (triggers slide-step) and grants Attack Speed. **Max this first.**
- **Ultimate (S3):** Consumes Laurel Leaf stacks. Auto-fires at lowest-Health enemy nearby. +Movement Speed.
