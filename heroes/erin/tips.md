# Erin Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Master the Energy Slide-Step

Erin has **no attack-move lock animation** — every 3 basic attacks, her max energy triggers a **slide-step dash** with an empowered follow-up attack.

- In lane or teamfights, think like a dancer: **AA ×3 → slide → reposition**
- Use the slide-step to **kite melee champions** — they should never touch you
- The empowered attack after sliding has **extended range** — use it for safe poke

### 2. Ultimate Tech

- **Ability 2 works DURING ultimate!** If enemies try to escape or dive you while ult is active, press S2 to **trigger a slide-step mid-ult** — repositioning while adding more Laurel Leaf stacks.
- Stack Laurel Leaves **before** fights by poking with basic attacks and S2.
- Activate ultimate when enemies are grouped or a low-Health target is in range. The auto-targeting prioritizes the **lowest Health enemy**.

### 3. Ability 1: Bush-Check & Zone Control

- S1 expands into a ring on hit/max range. Enemies touching the edge are **heavily slowed**.
- Use S1 to **check bushes** before face-checking
- Drop S1 behind fleeing enemies to **block their escape path**
- Use defensively when getting chased — the ring slow creates distance
- In teamfights, place S1 at choke points to split the enemy team

### 4. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take ultimate whenever available.**

- S2 provides instant energy refill + Attack Speed steroid. Lower cooldown = more slides = more survivability.
- S1 only adds minor base damage per rank.

## Signature Combos

### Safe Lane Poke: **AA×3 → Empowered AA → S2 → AA**

1. Stack 3 autos on minions
2. Slide-step toward the enemy marksman
3. Land the long-range empowered AA
4. If they try to trade back, immediately S2 for another slide-step away
5. **Result:** Free damage, zero risk

### Full Ultimate Execute: **S2 → AA → S1 → AA×3 → S3 → S2**

1. S2 to close distance and max energy
2. Empowered AA for poke
3. S1 at enemy's feet to slow
4. Keep auto-attacking to stack Laurel Leaves
5. Activate S3 — full meteor barrage
6. S2 mid-ult for final reposition/cleanup

### Desperation Kite: **S1 → AA×3 slide away → S2 slide away → S3**

When getting collapsed on:
1. S1 behind you to slow pursuers
2. Basic attack retreat — third hit slide-steps you farther
3. S2 for instant second slide-step
4. S3 for Movement Speed boost + damage while escaping

## Advanced Tips

- **Turret sieging:** Slide-step's empowered AA has long range — use it to safely chip towers.
- **Teamfight positioning:** Stay at max range. Slide-step laterally, not forward. Your job is constant DPS, not diving.
- **Matchup knowledge:** Erin destroys immobile tanks/bruisers who can't close gaps. She struggles against long-range mages (Gan & Mo, Angela) and hyper-mobile assassins (Lam, Prince of Lanling).
- **Don't build Physical items.** Erin deals 100% Magic Damage. Standard marksman items are useless on her.
- **Critical Rate from Magic Attack:** The more Magic Attack you build, the more you crit. This is why high-AP items are her core.
