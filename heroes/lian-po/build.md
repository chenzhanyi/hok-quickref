# Lian Po Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Abilities scale with **Max HP** (more HP = more damage). Two paths: **Clash Lane Bruiser** and **Roaming Tank.**

---

## Build A: Clash Lane Bruiser

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** | HP stacking + burn aura. Synergizes with Max HP scaling. |
| 2 | **Shadow Boots (影忍之足)** or **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Shadow Boots vs Physical laners. Resistance Boots vs CC-heavy (though Lian Po is Unstoppable). |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | **Only damage item needed.** CDR + flat Penetration + Health. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | S1→AA triggers AoE slow. CDR for more S1-S2-S1 cycles. |
| 5 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Stacking MS + damage while fighting. Stacks with Battle Spirit. |
| 6 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense. |

---

## Build B: Roaming Tank

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | Guard · Redemption (近卫·救赎) or Shadow Aegis · Star Spring (极影·星泉) | Team aura + active. |
| 2 | Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴) | Tenacity. |
| 3 | Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆) | Physical Defense + anti-ADC. |
| 4 | Eternal Night (永夜守护) | Burst-heal vs magic. |
| 5 | Blazing Cape (红莲斗篷) | Burn aura + HP scaling damage. |
| 6 | Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护) or Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装) | Resurrection or massive HP. |

## Quick In-Game Setup

Lian Po is a "teamfight Hero." Mid-late game, clear your lane then rotate. One good S1+Flash onto the enemy backline wins games. Copy the **CN #1 Lian Po** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
