# Lian Po（廉頗） — Tank

## Overview

- **Role:** Tank (Unstoppable CC / Flex Pick)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Roaming / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 正義爆轟 (Righteous Bombardment)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 廉颇)

> Lian Po's rework transformed him from a damage sponge into an **Unstoppable CC machine.** All abilities (S1, S2, S3) grant **Unstoppable (霸体)** — he ignores Wang Zhaojun freeze, Daji charm, Zhang Fei roar. His Battle Spirit (战意) grants up to **20% DR + 30% AS** when maxed. His S2 refreshes S1 if it hits anything. He flexes Clash Lane, Jungle, and Roaming.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Battle Spirit: 20% DR + 30% AS

Entering combat builds Battle Spirit. At max: **20% DR + 30% AS.** Always engage with Spirit pre-stacked from minions or jungle camps.

### Full Unstoppable: Ignore All CC

S1, S2, and S3 all grant **Unstoppable.** You can walk through Daji charm, Wang Zhaojun freeze, Zhang Fei roar, and Mozi S3 like they don't exist.

### S2 Refreshes S1 (Core Mobility)

If S2 hits any enemy (Minion, Monster, Hero): **S1's cooldown instantly resets.** This gives Lian Po **S1 → S2 → S1** double-dash + double-knockup. Core combo: S1 knockup → S2 pull + damage → S1 knockup again.

### S3 + S1: Reposition Mid-Ultimate

S3 has 3 phases. During phases 1 and 2: **you can press S1 to dash and reposition.** This prevents enemies from Flashing out of the final phase 3 knockup.
