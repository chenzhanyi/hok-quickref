# Mi Yue Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Magic Attack Speed. S2 chain empowers AAs — needs AS for maximum empowered-hit DPS. Semi-tank with sustain. CN server consensus: **Magic AS + Tank hybrid.**

---

## Recommended Clash Lane / Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** or **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | **Berserker's:** AS for empowered AA DPS. **Resistance Boots:** vs CC-heavy laners (Mulan, Sun Ce). |
| 2 | **Golden Sword (金色圣剑)** | **Core #1!** AS + MS + on-hit Magic Damage. Perfect synergy with S2-empowered AAs. |
| 3 | **Devourer's Tome (噬神之书)** | Massive Magic Lifesteal + HP. Combined with ravens, full-HP after one jungle camp. Never recall. |
| 4 | **Lightning Dagger (闪电匕首)** or **Shadow Blade (影刃)** | **Lightning Dagger:** AS + MS + chain lightning for jungle clear. **Shadow Blade:** on-crit AS + MS for kiting. |
| 5 | **Cocoon Breaker (破茧之衣)** | **New-season AP Tank item.** AP + massive HP. Passive converts AP to DR + Magic Penetration. Mi Yue becomes a genuine drain-tank. |
| 6 | **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** or **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** / **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Full defense flex based on enemy comp. Prophet's Hour: AP + dual defenses for balanced survival. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Attack Speed (AA DPS), Magic Penetration, Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "32% AS Speed" (CN Standard — Highest Winrate)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1.6%, Critical Rate +0.5% |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** **+32% Attack Speed** (max possible from runes), +64 Magic Penetration, +10% MS, +5% Critical Rate. Level 1 lane dominance — nobody out-trades 32% AS Mi Yue.

### Setup 2: "Lifesteal Sustain" (Newcomer Friendly)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1.6%, Critical Rate +0.5% |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Greed (贪婪) | ×5 | Magic Lifesteal +1.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×5 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +27% Attack Speed, +64 Magic Penetration, +5% MS, **+8% Magic Lifesteal**. Better early sustain before Devourer's Tome.
