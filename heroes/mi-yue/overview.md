# Mi Yue（羋月） — Fighter / Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter / Mage (Sustained Duelist / Split-Pusher)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 永恆之月 (Eternal Moon)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 芈月)

> Mi Yue is a sustained-damage Magic Fighter who combines continuous poke, dominant laning, high DR, and invulnerability. She's an unmatched split-pusher in Clash Lane and a "health-sustaining" Jungler who never recalls. Her S2 chain steals Attack/Magic Attack/Movement Speed from enemies — the longer she's connected, the stronger she gets and the weaker they become.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Raven Servants (Damage + Healing)

Hitting enemies with abilities summons ravens (max 6). Ravens auto-attack nearby enemies and **restore Mi Yue's HP.** Always maintain 6 ravens before fighting.

### S2: Chain (Win Condition)

S2 chains to an enemy, **stealing their Physical Attack, Magic Attack, and Movement Speed** per tick while empowering Mi Yue's AAs. A connected Mi Yue wins any 1v1. A disconnected Mi Yue loses. **Land S2 or don't fight.**

### S3: Invulnerability (2 Seconds)

S3 makes Mi Yue **completely immune to damage and CC for 2 seconds.** Use to: tower dive, bait enemy ultimates (Donghuang, Zhang Fei, Daji), dodge fatal burst.

### S1: Crow Teleport + Bait

S1 sends a crow forward. Recast to teleport to its position. Use direction fake: send crow LEFT, walk RIGHT. When enemies chase, teleport away. Or: send crow TOWARD enemy backline, teleport in for flank engage.
