# Mi Yue Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Never Fight Without 6 Ravens

Ravens = damage + healing. Before any fight: use abilities on nearby minions/jungle camps to reach 6 ravens. A Mi Yue with 0 ravens is half a Hero.

### 2. S2 Chain: Land It or Don't Fight

S2 steals Attack, Magic Attack, and MS from the enemy. A connected Mi Yue out-stats everyone. **If S2 misses: disengage immediately.** Wait for cooldown (~8s), then try again.

### 3. Pre-Buff Before Fighting

Before trading in lane: S2 a nearby jungle camp or minion to max out your stolen stats. Walk into the fight with full buffs + 6 ravens. The enemy laner can't contest you.

### 4. S1 Direction Fake (Signature Outplay)

S1 sends a crow in one direction. Recast to teleport. Mind game:
- Send crow LEFT, walk RIGHT
- Enemy chases you right → recast S1 → teleport left → safe
- Send crow toward enemy backline, walk away → recast to flank

### 5. S3: 2 Seconds of God Mode

S3 grants complete immunity. Use proactively:
- Tower dive → S3 to drop tower aggro
- Bait Donghuang/Zhang Fei/Daji ultimates
- Dodge fatal burst mid-fight
- Not just an escape — use it to stay in the fight longer

## Signature Combos

### Standard Duel: **S2 (chain) → AA → S1 → AA → S3 (if needed)**

1. Land S2 chain
2. AA while chain drains stats
3. S1 toward enemy's escape path → teleport
4. Continue AA with empowered stats
5. S3 if they try to burst you

### Long-Range Flank: **S1 (toward backline) → S1 (teleport) → S2 → AA → S3 (escape)**

1. S1 crow toward enemy ADC/Mage
2. Recast S1 → teleport directly onto them
3. S2 chain immediately
4. AA them down
5. S3 to walk out or tower-dive finish

## Advanced Tips

- **Jungle Mi Yue never recalls.** Ravens + Devourer's Tome = full HP after one jungle camp.
- **Split-push monster.** Mi Yue wins 1v1 against almost anyone with S2 connected. Force enemies to send 2+ to stop you.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in 1v1. Struggles vs hyper-mobile Heroes who break S2 chain (S1 teleport, S2 dash, etc.).
