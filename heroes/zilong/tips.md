# Zilong Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Never Flee at Low HP

Passive grants up to ~30%+ DR at critical HP. **You're deceptively tanky when low.** Enemies overcommit thinking you're dead → passive kicks in → S2 heals + S3 knockup → reverse kill. Don't panic at low HP — it's your power window.

### 2. S3: Center Knockup or Nothing

S3 only knocks up at the **inner circle center.** The outer ring deals damage with zero CC. Always aim the center on your target. From bushes or with S1 repositioning first.

### 3. S1 → AA Slow: Guaranteed S3 Setup

S1 → AA slows by 25%. The slow makes S3's center-hit nearly undodgeable. Always: **S1 → AA (slow) → S3 (guaranteed center knockup).**

### 4. S2: Heal Off Anything

S2 heals per hit — works on minions, monsters, Heroes. When low between fights: S2 a jungle camp. Full HP without recalling.

### 5. Pure Sky + Passive = Near Immunity

Pure Sky's active DR (35%) stacks with passive DR (up to 30%+) = **~65%+ total DR at low HP.** You're nearly unkillable for those few seconds.

---

## Signature Combos

### Standard Burst: **S3 → AA → S2 → S1 → AA**

1. S3 center knockup
2. AA (S3's bonus magic damage mark procs)
3. S2 full damage (triggers S3 mark)
4. S1 chase → AA (slow)

### Long-Range Engage: **S1 → AA → S3 → S2 → AA**

1. S1 gap-close → AA slow
2. S3 center knockup (guaranteed because slowed)
3. S2 + AA finish

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 from bushes is undodgeable.** Enemies can't react to what they can't see coming.
- **Track your HP percentage.** The lower you are, the tankier. But don't get one-shot — know when to use S2 heal.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile squishies. Struggles vs True Damage (Lu Bu) that ignores DR.
