# Zilong Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Semi-tank bruiser. Built-in DR means he doesn't need full tank. Needs **Physical Penetration + CDR + Attack.** CN server consensus: **semi-tank burst.**

---

## Recommended Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Attack, Penetration, CDR. Standard Assassin jungle item. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Zilong dives into melee — CC-lock = can't S2 heal = dead. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core #1. CDR + flat Penetration + Health. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** or **Master's Power (宗师之力)** | **Frost Grip:** S1→AA slow + S1→AA Ice Grip slow = double slow. CDR + Defense. **Master's Power:** max burst for snowballing. |
| 5 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | Massive AD. S3 mark damage + Siege Breaker <50% HP bonus. One combo deletes squishies. |
| 6 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** or **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** / **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | **Pure Sky:** active 35% DR + passive 30%+ DR = ~65%+ total DR. Near-immune at low HP. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Zero Critical or Attack Speed needed. **Physical Penetration + Attack + Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Stealth 100-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% MS. Max early gank burst.

Zilong's passive DR makes him deceptively tanky. Don't fear low HP. Copy the **CN #1 Zhao Yun** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
