# Zilong（趙雲） — Assassin / Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin / Fighter (Dive Bruiser / Juggernaut)
- **Lane:** Jungle / Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 蒼天翔龍 (Heavenly Dragon)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 赵云)

> Zilong (Zhao Yun in CN) is a high-burst, high-mobility Assassin/Fighter with a unique passive: **the lower his HP, the more Damage Reduction he gains** (up to ~30%+ at critical HP). This makes him deceptively tanky and a master of low-HP comeback kills. His S3 is a long-range leap with a center knockup — land the center hit and enemies are dead before they land.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Low HP = High DR

Every 3% HP lost = +1% DR. At critical HP, Zilong has **~30%+ Damage Reduction.** Never flee at low HP — you're tankier than you look. This is the "low HP god mode" that enables impossible comeback kills.

### S3: Center = Knockup (Edge = Nothing)

S3's **inner circle knocks up.** The outer ring only deals damage with no CC. Always aim so the **center hits the target.** Pre-cast from bushes or use S1 to reposition before S3.

### S1: Enhanced AA Slows

After S1, the next AA becomes a sweeping strike with bonus damage + **25% slow.** Use S1 → AA to stick to enemies.

### S2: Sustain

S2's multi-hit deals damage and **restores HP** per hit (works on minions/monsters). This is Zilong's jungle sustain and comeback tool.
