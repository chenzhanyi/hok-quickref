# Cao Cao（曹操） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Lifesteal Bruiser / Jungler)
- **Lane:** Jungle / Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 鮮血梟雄 (Blood Warlord)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 曹操)

> Cao Cao's recent rework added a **Sword Aura (剑气) mechanic**: abilities charge his weapon, and enhanced AAs shoot ranged sword beams (up to 3 consecutive slashes). This transformed him from a short-range melee into a mid-range skirmisher. His S3 is one of the best turnaround skills in the game — **on activation, cleanses all CC, heals based on missing HP, and every subsequent hit drains massive life.**

---

## Core Mechanics

### Sword Aura (Post-Rework)

Every ability charges a sword aura. Enhanced AAs have **longer range + faster speed.** Always weave AAs between abilities — never dump all skills at once.

### S3: The Turnaround God

S3 **instantly cleanses all CC** and heals based on missing HP. For the duration, ALL damage dealt to enemies converts to HP recovery. **Never cast S3 at full HP** — save it for when you're CC'd and low. The enemy thinks you're dead → S3 → full HP in 2 seconds.

### S1: Triple Dash (Third = Knockup)

S1 has 3 dashes. First two: movement + damage. **Third dash: knockup.** Pre-cast the first two from a bush or while rotating, then use the third with Flash for surprise long-range initiation.

### S2: Slow + AA CD Reduction

S2 fires a ranged slowing wave. **Each AA reduces S2 cooldown by 1 second.** S2 → AA spam → S2 again in the same trade.
