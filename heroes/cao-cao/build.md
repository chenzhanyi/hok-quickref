# Cao Cao Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Currently strongest as **Jungler** (viable Clash Lane). Needs semi-tank stats to survive long enough for S3 turnaround. CN server consensus: **tank-jungle bruiser.**

---

## Recommended Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** or **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | **Giant's Grip (meta):** HP stacking + burn aura — more HP = harder to burst before S3. **Greedy Bite:** burst for snowballing. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity stacks with S3 passive → near Tenacity cap. CC is irrelevant. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core Fighter item: CDR, flat Penetration, Health. Early kill pressure. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | CDR + Physical Defense. Enhanced AAs perfectly trigger AoE slow. Maximum stickiness for Sword Aura AAs. |
| 5 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Taking hits (which Cao Cao does) grants stacking MS + damage output. Perfect for extended S3 fights. |
| 6 | **Phoenix Eye (不死鸟之眼)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Phoenix Eye:** signature magic resist — amplifies S3 healing at low HP ("medical miracle"). **Pure Sky:** active DR vs full-AD teams. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Movement Speed, Physical Penetration, mild Attack Speed (smoother Sword Aura AAs).**

### Setup 1: "Hunt 100-Pen" (CN Jungle Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% AS, **+10% MS**. Max jungle tempo + kiting.

### Setup 2: "Hybrid Tenacity" (Clash Lane)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×9 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×1 | Critical Rate +1.6% ("turnaround crit" dream) |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×5 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×5 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +37 Physical Attack, +96.4 Physical Penetration, +5% AS, +10% MS, +1.6% Critical Rate. Heavier-hitting S3 turnaround blows.
