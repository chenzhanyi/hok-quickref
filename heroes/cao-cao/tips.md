# Cao Cao Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S3: Never Cast at Full HP

S3 cleanses CC + heals based on **missing HP.** The lower you are, the bigger the heal. Save S3 for when you're CC'd and about to die. The enemy commits everything → S3 → you're at full HP and they have no cooldowns. This is Cao Cao's entire identity.

### 2. Weave AAs Between Abilities

Sword Aura enhanced AAs are fast and long-range. Never dump all skills at once: **S1 → AA → S1 → AA → S2 → AA → S1 → AA.** Missing the AAs means missing 40% of your damage.

### 3. S1 Pre-Cast for Surprise Knockup

S1 has 3 dashes. The third one knocks up. Pre-cast the first two from a bush or while rotating, then **the third dash + Flash** directly onto the enemy backline. Surprise knockup from a screen away.

### 4. S2 AA CD Reduction

S2's cooldown is reduced by **1 second per AA hit.** In extended fights: S2 → AA spam → S2 again. You can cast S2 twice in one trade if you keep auto-attacking.

### 5. Tenacity Stacking

Cao Cao's S3 grants bonus Tenacity. With Resistance Boots, he **approaches the Tenacity cap** — CC from Wang Zhaojun, Mozi, etc. lasts a fraction of a second. He walks through control like a terminator.

## Signature Combos

### Bush Ambush: **S1 (pre-cast 2) → S1 (knockup) → AA → S2 → AA → S1 → AA**

1. Pre-cast 2 S1 dashes from bush
2. Third S1 knocks up the enemy
3. AA → S2 slow → AA
4. S1 (cooldown reset from AAs) → AA
5. S3 saved for when they try to burst back

### Low-HP God Mode: **S3 → AA → S1 → AA → S2 → AA → S1 → AA**

1. Enemy dumps everything, you're at 10% HP and CC'd
2. **S3** (cleanse + big heal)
3. AA → S1 dash → AA → S2 → AA → S1 → AA
4. You're now at 80% HP, they're dead

## Advanced Tips

- **Phoenix Eye (不死鸟之眼) is Cao Cao's signature magic resist item.** Its passive amplifies healing at low HP — stacks with S3 for "medical miracle" levels of sustain.
- **S3's heal scales with damage dealt.** The more aggressive you play during S3, the more HP you recover.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs CC-heavy comps (S3 cleanse + Tenacity). Struggles vs burst that kills before S3 activates.
