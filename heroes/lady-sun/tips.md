# Lady Sun Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S2 Before EVERY Cannon

S2 shreds Defense + marks the target. **Always S2 before firing S1's enhanced shot.** The damage difference is night and day. S1 cannon without S2 = half damage.

### 2. Pre-Stack S1 ("Hold the Gun")

S1's enhanced AA lasts several seconds. Before ambushing from bush: S1 roll → hold the shot → enemy walks past → AA (cannon #1) → S1 is already off cooldown → S2 → S1 (cannon #2). Two cannons, one dead squishy.

### 3. S1 Radar: Never Face-Check Bushes

S1 grants a burst of MS when enemies are nearby. When approaching suspicious bushes: S1 roll near them. If you suddenly speed up → someone's inside → retreat or S2 the bush.

### 4. S3: Long-Range Execute, Not Melee

S3 has long range but a slow cast animation. **Never S3 during close-range duels** — you'll get deleted during the animation. Use S3 for: long-range cleanup on fleeing enemies, pre-fight poke on enemy backline, or finishing after S1+S2 combo.

### 5. AA Constantly to Refresh S1

Every AA reduces S1 cooldown by 0.5s. After the cannon shot: AA minions, monsters, enemies — anything. S1 comes back faster → more rolls → more cannons.

## Signature Combos

### Bush Ambush One-Shot: **S1 (hold) → AA → S2 → S1 → AA → S3**

1. Pre-stack S1 (hold cannon)
2. AA (first cannon)
3. S2 shred + mark
4. S1 (fresh cooldown) → AA (second cannon)
5. S3 execute at range

### Standard Poke: **S2 → S1 → AA**

1. S2 shred
2. S1 roll sideways (never straight forward)
3. Enhanced AA cannon
4. Walk away or AA to refresh S1

## Advanced Tips

- **Never roll forward into enemies.** Always roll sideways or diagonally.
- **Critical Damage cap is 250% in CN server.** 3 Peerless runes + Infinity Edge = perfect cap.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile squishies. Struggles vs multiple divers.
