# Lady Sun Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure burst. One cannon = one kill. Needs **Critical Rate, Critical Damage, and Physical Penetration.** Attack Speed is secondary — she's not a turret ADC, she's a burst Assassin-Marksman.

---

## Recommended Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early AS for smoother AA→S1 cooldown reduction. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs CC, or Fate's Edge.* |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Early-game core. CDR + flat Penetration. Strong at 4-8 minute gank window. |
| 3 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | **Power spike #1!** Crit Rate + Crit Damage. Without this, S1 cannon is just a firecracker. |
| 4 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** | **Power spike #2!** "Power Strike" procs on S1's enhanced AA — one cannon deletes 70%+ HP. |
| 5 | **Dawn (破晓)** | Marksman capstone. Penetration + Crit + AS. Combined with S2 shred, even full tanks evaporate. |
| 6 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** / **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | **Berserker's:** Defense + stacking MS/damage. **Pure Sky:** 50% DR vs burst. **Magic Cloak:** vs heavy magic. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Critical Rate, Critical Damage (cap at 250%), Physical Penetration, Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "3 Peerless Crit Burst" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×3 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** ~12% Critical Rate, +36 Physical Penetration (+64 with green), +10% MS. 3 Peerless + Infinity Edge = perfect 250% Crit Damage cap.

### Setup 2: "Max Lifesteal" (No Lifesteal Item Needed)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×3 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×8 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×2 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** **+12.8% Physical Lifesteal**, +11.2% Crit Rate, +64 Physical Pen, +2% MS/AS. Skip lifesteal item — go straight to burst.
