# Lady Sun（孫尚香） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Burst / Roll-Cannon)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 千金重弩 (Heavy Crossbow Heiress)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 孙尚香; nickname: "Xiang Xiang")

> Lady Sun is a classic "burst-roll Marksman." She's not a sustained AA turret — she's an **Assassin-Marksman** who "holds her gun" (pre-stacks S1) and one-shots squishies with a single enhanced cannon shot. Her S1 roll + S2 armor shred + Infinity Edge + Master's Power combo can delete carries from full HP.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S1 Roll: AA Reduces Cooldown

Every AA reduces S1 cooldown by 0.5 seconds. After rolling and firing, **AA constantly** to bring S1 back faster. The S1 → AA → S1 cycle is her DPS engine.

### S1 Roll: Built-In Radar

When rolling near an enemy Hero, Lady Sun gains a **burst of Movement Speed.** Use this to check bushes: roll near a suspicious bush → if you suddenly speed up, someone's inside.

### S2: ALWAYS Before the Cannon

S2 shreds Physical Defense AND marks the target for bonus AA damage. **Never fire S1's enhanced cannon without S2 first.** The sequence is always: **S2 (shred) → S1 cannon (burst).**

### Pre-Stack S1 ("Hold the Gun")

S1's enhanced AA lasts several seconds. Before ambushing: S1 roll (hold the shot) → enemy approaches → AA (enhanced cannon) → S1 is already off cooldown → S1 again → second enhanced AA. Two cannons in rapid succession.
