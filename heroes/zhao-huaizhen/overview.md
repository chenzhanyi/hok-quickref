# Zhao Huaizhen（趙懷真） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Tai Chi Counter / Tank Bruiser)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 自在之心 (Heart of Ease)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (赵怀真)

> Zhao Huaizhen is a uniquely designed Tai Chi-style Fighter/Tank with a four-stage AA combo and the ability to **absorb and redirect enemy projectiles** via his S3. His S1 can **forcefully change enemy facing direction** — redirecting channeled abilities. He excels at counter-engage, peel, and sustained brawling.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Four-Stage AA Combo (Energy System)

Zhao Huaizhen's AAs follow a 4-hit cycle. **Don't blindly spam AA** — use each stage intentionally:
- **1st Hit (Palm):** Bonus damage to minions/monsters. **Pause briefly after the 1st hit to reset the cycle** — fastest jungle/wave clear.
- **2nd Hit (Fist):** Rapid strikes. Best for stacking on-hit effects.
- **3rd Hit (Elbow):** **Restores HP** (more healing at lower HP). Sustain tool.
- **4th Hit (Shoulder):** Dash + high damage. **Pre-stack 3 AAs on air, hold the 4th as a gap-close.**

### S1: Redirect Enemy Facing

S1 pulls AND **changes the enemy's facing direction.** Mulan charging Heavy S1? Ying Zheng ult channel? Jiang Ziya S3? **S1 them → their ability fires backward.**

### S2: Counter-Shield

S2 is a charged shield. During charge: **immune to Physical CC.** If hit by hard CC (stun, knockup): **automatically triggers a counter-knockback + heal.** Pre-cast S2 when expecting Daji/Fuzi ambushes.

### S3: Projectile Absorption

S3 creates a vortex that **absorbs ALL incoming projectiles** (Hou Yi bird, Zhuge Liang S3, Marco S1, tower shots). The more damage absorbed, the stronger S3's second-cast pushback burst. **Unstoppable during channel.**
