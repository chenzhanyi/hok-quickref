# Zhao Huaizhen Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Semi-tank bruiser. Needs **CDR** (S1/S2/S3 uptime), **Max HP + defenses**, and one damage item for kill threat.

---

## Recommended Jungle / Clash Lane Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** or **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Jungle: HP stacking. Clash: straight to damage. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Must survive to cast S2/S3. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Only damage item needed. CDR + Penetration. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | AA spam perfectly triggers AoE slow. CDR + Defense. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense. Eternal Night for burst; Magic Cloak for poke. |
| 6 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Berserker's:** stacking MS + damage. **Pure Sky:** active DR. |

## Quick In-Game Setup

Zhao Huaizhen's S1 redirect mechanic is more valuable than damage. Prioritize CDR + tankiness. Copy the **CN #1 Zhao Huaizhen** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
