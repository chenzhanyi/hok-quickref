# Zhao Huaizhen Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. AA Cycle: Pause After 1st Hit for Fastest Clear

The 1st AA (palm) deals bonus damage to minions/monsters. **AA once → brief pause → AA again = repeat the 1st hit.** This doubles jungle/wave clear speed. Don't mindlessly spam AA.

### 2. Hold 4th AA as Gap-Close

The 4th AA is a dash. Pre-stack: AA air 3 times → walk into gank range → 4th AA dashes onto the enemy. Free engage tool.

### 3. S1: Redirect Enemy Ultimates

S1 changes enemy facing direction. When Mulan charges Heavy S1, Ying Zheng S3, or Jiang Ziya S3: **S1 them.** Their ultimate fires in the wrong direction. Game-changing counterplay.

### 4. S2: Pre-Cast When Ambush Is Likely

S2's counter triggers on hard CC. Walking past a bush Daji might be in? S2 first. Daji's charm triggers your auto-counter → she's knocked back + you're healed. You just turned her ambush into your kill.

### 5. S3: Absorb Everything, Return Harder

S3 absorbs all projectiles. The more damage absorbed, the bigger the return burst. Use when: enemy ADC is firing, Zhuge Liang S3 is incoming, or tower shots are flying. **S3 second cast = massive AoE burst.**

## Signature Combos

### Standard Engage: **S1 → S2 → AA×4 → S3 → S3 (return)**

1. S1 pull + redirect
2. S2 gap-close + counter ready
3. AA 4-hit combo (3rd hit heals)
4. S3 absorb return fire
5. S3 second cast — burst

### Backline Protection: **S2 → S1 → S3**

1. Assassin dives your ADC
2. S2 at ADC's feet (counter-knockback)
3. S1 push them away
4. S3 absorb their follow-up projectiles

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 absorbs even tower shots.** Tower dive → S3 the tower bullet → S3 return kills the defender.
- **1st-hit AA reset works on all non-Hero targets.** Use on turrets too.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs projectile-reliant Heroes. Struggles vs melee auto-attackers who don't use projectiles.
