# Chang'E Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Full Mana = Invincible, Empty Mana = Dead

Chang'E converts 60% of damage to Mana. At full Mana, she's absurdly tanky. At zero Mana, she dies to a sneeze. **Never engage below 50% Mana.**

### 2. S2 Mana Drain: Your Engine

S2's Mana Vortices drain Mana from enemies and restore Chang'E's. Prioritize Minion waves and Jungle Monsters as "Mana batteries." Against manaless heroes, the drain is **doubled** — Chang'E hard-counters energy-based champions.

### 3. Recall → Fly Back

Chang'E's unique recall ability lets her teleport back to lane within a time window. Use it to base for Mana mid-fight and return instantly. Best tempo hero in the game.

### 4. Jungle Chang'E (Preferred Role)

CN server now prefers Chang'E as a **Jungler**:
- Level 1: solo two camps simultaneously
- Blue Buff = permanent full Mana = permanent max damage + tankiness
- Aggressive early invader — few junglers can duel a full-Mana Chang'E

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take ultimate whenever available.** S2 is core damage and Mana sustain. S1 is a long-range snare — use from bushes for guaranteed hits.

## Signature Combos

### Bush Ambush One-Shot: **S1 → S2 → S3**

1. S1 snare from bush (point-blank = undodgeable)
2. S2 Mana Vortex at enemy's feet for drain + damage
3. S3 ultimate — unload full Mana barrage → instant deletion of squishies

### Melee Brawl: **S2 → AA → S1 → S3**

1. Drop S2 at your feet vs diving Fighter
2. Enhanced AA slow
3. Point-blank S1 (guaranteed snare)
4. S3 ultimate at close range for maximum damage
5. **Caution:** S3 drains Mana rapidly. Stop firing if you can't secure the kill.

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S1 from bushes only:** S1 has a slow projectile — skilled players dodge it easily. Cast from fog of war for guaranteed hits.
- **Blue Buff is mandatory:** Never give Blue Buff to your Mid Laner as Jungle Chang'E. You need it more than anyone.
- **Garo is your hard counter:** Garo's passive deals double crit damage to shielded targets. Chang'E's Mana bar gets deleted instantly. If enemy picks Garo, swap heroes.
- **Matchup:** Destroys energy-based and manaless champions. Struggles vs long-range poke that chips Mana from safety (Gan & Mo).
