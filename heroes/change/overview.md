# Chang'E（嫦娥） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Mana Tank / Burst)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 寒月公主 (Moon Princess)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 嫦娥)

> Chang'E is a uniquely designed Mana-Tank Mage. Her core mechanic is **Mana-as-Health**: 60% of damage taken is absorbed by Mana instead of HP. More Mana = more damage + more tankiness. At full Mana she's nearly unkillable; at zero Mana she's the squishiest hero in the game.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Mana Shield (Blue = HP)

Chang'E converts **60% of incoming damage to Mana loss**. At full Mana, she has the strongest effective HP in the game. At zero Mana, she's paper. **Never fight with low Mana.**

### S2: Mana Drain + Recovery (Core)

S2 summons two Mana Vortices dealing continuous damage. **Hitting enemies (especially Minions and Jungle Monsters) drains their Mana and restores Chang'E's.** Against manaless heroes (Marco, Musashi), the drain is doubled!

### Recall → Fly Back (Return-to-Lane)

Chang'E's unique recall skill lets her **teleport back to her recall point** within a time window. Best lane sustain and tempo in the game.

### Jungle Chang'E

Currently preferred over Mid Lane in CN server. Insane clear speed (can solo two camps at level 1). Blue Buff = permanent full Mana = permanent invincibility. Aggressive early invader.

## Counters & Synergies

**Hard counter: Garo (伽罗).** Garo's basic attacks deal double crit damage to shielded targets — instantly deletes Chang'E's Mana bar. If the enemy picks Garo, avoid picking Chang'E.
