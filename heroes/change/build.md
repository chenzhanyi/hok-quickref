# Chang'E Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Mana = damage + tankiness. Stack **Max Mana and Magic Penetration**. No traditional tank items needed — your Mana bar IS your tank item. CN server consensus: **Mana-stacking burst Mage**.

---

## Recommended Mid / Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Rune Greatsword (符文大剑)** or **Evolution Crystal (进化水晶)** | Jungle: Blue jungle knife for Mana sustain. Mid: Evolution Crystal for early laning — sell later. |
| 2 | **Arcane Boots (秘法之靴)** | Magic Penetration + Mana regen. Mana regen = HP regen for Chang'E. Core boots. |
| 3 | **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | **Chang'E's #1 core item!** Massive HP + Mana. Passive converts Magic Attack to Physical & Magic Defense 1:1 — transforms Chang'E into a genuine Mana Tank. |
| 4 | **Spellblade Staff (巫术法杖)** | Chang'E casts abilities constantly. Spellblade's enhanced AA after casting massively boosts burst and tower-pushing speed. |
| 5 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage Magic Attack steroid. Indirectly amplifies both damage AND tankiness (via Prophet's Hour conversion). |
| 6 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** or **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** | Penetration vs Magic Cloak. **Flex alternative: Ardent Dominator (炽热支配者)** — Mana + auto-cleanse shield at low HP, perfect synergy with Chang'E's kit. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Zero CDR needed. Core stats: **Magic Penetration, Movement Speed (Chang'E has the slowest base MS at 350), initial Magic Attack.**

### Setup 1: CN Server "88-Pen Hunt Speed" (Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +16% Attack Speed, **+10% Movement Speed**. Mandatory 10× Hunt to compensate for Chang'E's 350 base MS (slowest in the game).

## Critical Warning: Garo

Garo's passive deals **double crit damage to shielded targets**. Chang'E's entire Mana bar counts as a shield — Garo deletes it instantly. If the enemy picks Garo, **do not pick Chang'E**. If already locked in, focus Garo first in every teamfight.
