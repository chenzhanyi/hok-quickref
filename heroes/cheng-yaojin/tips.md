# Cheng Yaojin Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Keep HP at 30-40% — Never Full

Passive grants massive Physical Attack at low HP. At 30% HP, you effectively have a free Siege Breaker. **Never trade at full HP** — you're half as strong. Intentionally take some damage before engaging.

### 2. S3 Timing: Wait for the Right Moment

Ideal S3: HP at 20-30%, enemy burst is on cooldown, and you DON'T have the Grievous Wounds icon. If you see the purple broken-heart (anti-heal), wait for it to expire or create distance, THEN S3.

### 3. Proxy Waves Behind Enemy Towers

Mid-late game: S1 into the space between enemy T2 and T3 towers. Kill the incoming minion wave BEFORE it reaches your team's push. The enemy team can't siege without minions. This single tactic wins games.

### 4. AA to Refresh S1

S1 cooldown is reduced by 1 second per AA hit. When escaping: **AA minions/jungle camps while running.** S1 refreshes mid-chase → jump over a wall → safe. Never just run — always AA something while fleeing.

### 5. Draw 2-3 Enemies, Then Your Team Wins

Your entire macro purpose: push the farthest lane from the current objective. When 2-3 enemies come to stop you: **you've already won.** Your team now has a 4v2 or 4v3 advantage. Don't feel pressured to escape — sometimes dying slowly is a win if your team takes Baron.

## Core Trading Pattern

### **S2 → AA → S1 → AA**

1. S2 spin (two hits) + AA
2. Enemy tries to disengage → S1 jump + slow
3. Continue AA (each hit reduces S1 CD)
4. S3 saved for when you're low

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Grievous Wounds is your #1 counter.** Sanction Blade and Nightmare Fang cut healing by 50%. If enemies build these, wait for the debuff to expire before S3.
- **You scale harder than most Fighters late-game.** The longer the game goes, the harder you are to kill.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs teams without anti-heal. Struggles when enemies rush Sanction Blade + Nightmare Fang early.
