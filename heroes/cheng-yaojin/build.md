# Cheng Yaojin Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure tank with **max HP + CDR.** Needs CDR for frequent S3, and HP for passive scaling. Split-push immortal build.

---

## Recommended Split-Push Tank Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Blazing Cape (红莲斗篷)** | **Absolute core #1!** Physical Defense + Max HP. Burn aura solves slow wave clear and constantly refreshes S1 CD via AA reduction. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity to survive CC and cast S3. |
| 3 | **Phoenix Eye (不死鸟之眼)** | **Cheng Yaojin's soul item!** Passive amplifies all healing at low HP. S3 becomes a full-HP restore instead of partial. |
| 4 | **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** | 20% CDR + Physical Defense. S3 uptime dramatically increases. Frost shockwave on burst helps escape ganks. |
| 5 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** | Massive HP + in-combat regen. Combined with S3 + Phoenix Eye, you heal faster than enemies can damage you. |
| 6 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** or **Blood Rage (血魔之怒)** | **Berserker's:** stacking MS + damage while being focused. **Blood Rage:** massive shield at low HP — perfect synergy with passive. |
