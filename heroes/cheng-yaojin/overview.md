# Cheng Yaojin（程咬金） — Tank / Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Tank / Fighter (Immortal Split-Pusher)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 熱血神將 (Fervent General)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 程咬金)

> Cheng Yaojin is the classic "unkillable" split-push Tank. His passive grants massive Physical Attack at low HP (up to a free Siege Breaker's worth). His S3 restores huge chunks of HP. His entire game plan: push side lanes, invade enemy jungle, proxy minion waves, and force 2-3 enemies to chase him — creating 4v3 advantages for his team.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Low HP = High Damage

Gains Physical Attack per 1% HP lost. At ~20-30% HP, he has the equivalent of a free Siege Breaker. **Never fight at full HP.** Keep HP at 30-40% for maximum damage output.

### S3: Don't Panic-Cast

Ideal S3 timing: **HP at 20-30% AND enemy burst abilities are on cooldown.** If you have Grievous Wounds (Sanction Blade / Nightmare Fang icon), wait for it to expire or create distance, then S3.

### S1: Save for Escape

S1 is his only mobility. **AA reduces S1 cooldown by 1 second per hit.** When being chased: AA minions/enemies while walking → S1 cooldown refreshes → jump over a wall to escape.

### Split-Push Is Your Entire Identity

Don't play 5v5 teamfights. Your job: push the farthest lane, proxy waves behind towers, invade enemy jungle. Force 2-3 enemies to deal with you. When they come: S1 away or S3 tank through it. Your team gets free objectives.
