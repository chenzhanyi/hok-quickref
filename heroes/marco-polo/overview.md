# Marco Polo（馬可波羅） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (True Damage / Mobile ADC)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 遠遊之槍 (Wandering Gun)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 马可波罗)

> Marco Polo is a uniquely designed Marksman. He's one of the only ADCs with **two mobility skills and zero hard CC**. His damage output depends entirely on **Attack Speed thresholds** and his passive **True Damage**. He's the tank-shredder supreme and one of the hardest carries to catch.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: True Damage (Tank Killer)

Hits stack marks. At **10 stacks** (enemy shield icon breaks), subsequent Physical Damage becomes **True Damage** that CAN crit. This is why Marco melts tanks that other ADCs can't touch.

### Attack Speed Thresholds

S1 and S3 **bullet count scales with Attack Speed**. Key thresholds: **0% / 80% / 130% / 180%**. Higher AS = more bullets = exponential damage. Reaching 180% AS is priority #1.

### S2: Built-In Radar (Anti-Gank)

S2's passive: when an enemy Hero is nearby, a **glowing gear circle** appears at Marco's feet, granting bonus damage + MS. This is the best anti-bush-ambush tool in the game. If the circle lights up near a bush, someone's there — S1 the bush or retreat.

### S3: Never Initiate With It

New Marcos die by S2 → S3 into 5 enemies. S3's correct usage:
1. **Stack 10 marks first** (S1 or AAs), THEN S3 in for True Damage carnage
2. **Cleanup:** S3 to finish low-HP survivors
3. **During S3, DON'T touch the left joystick** — it cancels the ultimate. But you CAN Flash, use Pure Sky, or Stun during it.
