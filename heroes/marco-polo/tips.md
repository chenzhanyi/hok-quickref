# Marco Polo Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Stack 10 Marks BEFORE S3

Marco's #1 mistake: S2+S3 into enemies at 0 stacks. Correct: **S1 (stack marks) → wait for shield-break icon → S2+S3 in for True Damage.** The difference is 5× the damage.

### 2. S2 Radar = Never Get Ganked

S2's passive: glowing gear circle at your feet = enemy nearby. When approaching any bush:
- Circle lights up? **S1 the bush.** Someone's there.
- Circle stays dark? Safe to pass.
This single mechanic makes Marco nearly ungankable.

### 3. S3: Never Touch Left Joystick

During S3, **moving the left joystick cancels the ultimate instantly.** Keep your left thumb OFF the joystick. You CAN: Flash, activate Pure Sky, or cast Stun during S3.

### 4. Attack Speed Thresholds Are Everything

S1/S3 bullet count scales with AS. Every % matters:
- **80%:** Baseline
- **130%:** Mid-game spike
- **180%:** Maximum bullets — full damage potential
Missing a threshold by even 1% costs multiple bullets.

### 5. Semi-Tank, Not Full Damage

Marco has True Damage. He doesn't need Dawn (破晓). Stack AS to 180%, then build defense. The longer he survives, the more True Damage he outputs.

## Signature Combos

### Lane Poke: **S1 → S2 → AA**

1. S1 at max range — stack marks
2. S2 reposition (lateral or forward based on enemy response)
3. AA to finish stacking or maintain pressure

### Perfect Teamfight: **S1 (stack 10) → S2+S3 → Pure Sky → Cleanup**

1. S1 from safe range — stack to 10 marks on priority target
2. Shield-break icon appears (True Damage unlocked)
3. S2 dash in → S3 immediately
4. **Pure Sky during S3** (DR + unstoppable)
5. Enemy team evaporates to True Damage

## Advanced Tips

- **S1 reveals stealth.** Sweep bushes or track Lanling Wang/Ake with S1.
- **S2's circle also grants bonus damage + MS.** When it lights up, you're stronger.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs tanky comps (True Damage ignores armor). Struggles vs hyper-mobile divers who CC-cancel S3.
