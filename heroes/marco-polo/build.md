# Marco Polo Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Stack **180% Attack Speed** first (S1/S3 bullet count threshold), then build **semi-tank**. True Damage means he doesn't need Dawn or full damage. Survivability = more True Damage uptime.

---

## Recommended Semi-Tank Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early AS for thresholds. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC — prevents S3 cancellation.* |
| 2 | **Doomsday (末世)** | **Marco's soul item!** Every S1/S3 bullet triggers 8% Current HP on-hit. First major power spike. |
| 3 | **Lightning Dagger (闪电匕首)** or **Shadow Blade (影刃)** | **Lightning Dagger:** chain lightning on-hit, fast wave clear, AS + Crit. **Shadow Blade:** on-crit AS + MS, stronger S3 burst. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | Physical Defense + CDR. AA/S1 perfectly triggers AoE slow — massively improves S1 accuracy. |
| 5 | **Dawn (破晓)** or **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | **Dawn:** max AS + Penetration vs heavy armor. **Infinity Edge:** Crit True Damage on S3 — squishy deleter. |
| 6 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic Defense. Prevents getting poked out or one-shot by Magic Assassins. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Max Attack Speed** (hit 180% threshold ASAP), **Physical Penetration, mild Lifesteal.**

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1.6%, Critical Rate +0.5% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×7 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +23% Attack Speed, +5% Critical Rate, +64 Physical Penetration, +7% MS, **+4.8% Physical Lifesteal**. Fastest route to 130% and 180% thresholds.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Marco is extremely sensitive to AS — even 1% below a threshold costs multiple bullets. Copy the **CN #1 Marco Polo** setup via **Preparation → Recommended** for precision-optimized thresholds.
