# Yang Jian（楊戩） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Execute / Tenacity Brawler)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 根源之目 (Eye of Origin)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 杨戬)

> Yang Jian is a well-rounded Clash Lane/Jungle Fighter with **execute, Tenacity, sustain, True Damage, and mobility**. His ceiling-to-floor gap is enormous — everything depends on S1 (dog) accuracy and his ability to outplay through S3 lifesteal and passive Tenacity in teamfights.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S1: Divine Dog (Soul Skill)

S1 marks an enemy. Second cast: dash to them. **If the dash KILLS the target (Minion, Monster, or Hero), S1's cooldown fully resets.** Use minions as springboards to gap-close and surprise enemies.

### S2: True Damage Trigger

S2 sweeps forward. **Stuns if enemy HP% ≤ your HP%** (otherwise slows). After hitting, Yang Jian's AAs deal **bonus True Damage for 5 seconds.** Always S2 → AA.

### Passive: Tenacity

Each nearby enemy Hero grants Tenacity (max 30%). With Resistance Boots (35%), Yang Jian reaches the **~60% Tenacity cap** — CC barely affects him in teamfights.

### S3: Triple Laser Lifesteal

Three laser waves. Each wave hitting enemies restores HP. Third wave fires 3 beams simultaneously. **Fight near minion waves or jungle camps** — more targets = more healing. One S3 in a minion wave can go from 10% to 100% HP.
