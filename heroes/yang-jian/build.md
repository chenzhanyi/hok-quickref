# Yang Jian Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Must go **semi-tank bruiser**. Built-in True Damage + Tenacity means he doesn't need full damage. Needs **CDR, Penetration, and survivability**. CN server consensus: CDR bruiser with execute spike.

---

## Recommended Clash Lane / Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | **Yang Jian's perfect boots.** Passive Tenacity (max 30%) + boots (35%) = ~60% Tenacity. CC means nothing. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core Fighter item: 10% CDR, Health, flat Penetration. Early squishy-killing power. |
| 3 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | S2 → AA perfectly triggers AoE slow + bonus damage. CDR + Physical Defense. Keeps enemies locked in True Damage range. |
| 4 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** or **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | **Pure Sky:** active DR for diving + 10% CDR. **Berserker's:** stacking MS + damage when focused — better for extended kiting. |
| 5 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Power spike!** S1 dash execute scales with target's missing HP (16%). Siege Breaker's +30% vs <50% HP = guaranteed kill on any dogged squishy. |
| 6 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** or **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** | Magic defense. Magic Cloak vs sustained poke; Eternal Night vs burst. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Attack Speed (smooth S2→AA), Physical Penetration, Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Hunt 100-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% Attack Speed, +10% Movement Speed. Smoothest S2→AA weaving.

### Setup 2: "Hybrid Sustain" (Clash Lane)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×7 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, +100 Physical Penetration, +7% MS, +7% AS, **+4.8% Physical Lifesteal**. Better sustain for lane trades and jungle clear.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Yang Jian's S1 accuracy is everything. Increase S1 wheel sensitivity in settings for faster lock-on. Copy the **CN #1 Yang Jian** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
