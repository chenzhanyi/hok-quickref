# Yang Jian Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S1: Minion Springboard

Don't always try to dog the enemy directly. Dog a low-HP minion → S1 dash to kill it (S1 resets!) → you're now in the enemy's face with S1 ready again. This is Yang Jian's most fundamental engage trick.

### 2. S2 → AA: True Damage Window

S2 grants **5 seconds of True Damage on AAs**. Always S2 first → then AA. Never AA before S2. The True Damage is what makes Yang Jian win extended trades.

### 3. S3 Lifesteal: Fight Near Targets

S3's lasers heal per target hit. Never S3 in empty space. Always fight near:
- Minion waves (massive healing)
- Jungle camps
- Multiple enemy Heroes

One S3 through a full minion wave takes you from near-dead to full HP.

### 4. Near-CC Immunity

Passive Tenacity (max 30%) + Resistance Boots (35%) = **~60% Tenacity**. In teamfights with 3+ enemies nearby, CC lasts mere fractions of a second. You can practically ignore most crowd control.

### 5. S2 Stun Condition

S2 stuns ONLY if enemy HP% ≤ your HP%. If you're lower HP, it ALWAYS stuns (counter-play). If you're full HP, it only slows unless the enemy is also full. In close-range counter-engages when you're low, S2 guarantees a stun.

### 6. Skill Priority

Clash Lane: **Max S2, then S1.** Jungle: **Max S1, then S2.** Take S3 whenever available.

## Signature Combos

### Backline Delete: **S1 → S1 (dash) → S2 → AA → S3×3 → AA**

1. S1 dog enemy carry
2. S1 dash in
3. S2 stun/slow + True Damage
4. AA weave
5. S3 three waves (lifesteal through fight)
6. AA finish

### Low-HP Counter-Kill: **S2 → AA → S3 → S1 → S1 (execute)**

1. Enemy chases you at low HP
2. S2 — guaranteed stun (their HP% > yours)
3. AA with True Damage
4. S3 lasers — heal back up
5. S1 dog → S1 dash execute

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S1 resets on ANY kill with the dash.** Minions, monsters, Heroes — all work.
- **Third S3 wave fires 3 beams.** Angle yourself so all 3 hit different targets for maximum healing.
- **Matchup:** Destroys squishy backlines and sustained fighters. Struggles vs burst that kills before S3 lifesteal kicks in.
