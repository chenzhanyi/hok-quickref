## Core Mechanics

### Ignite Passive (Don't Forget Auto-Attacks!)

S1 and S3 ignite enemies on hit, revealing them. **Always follow up with an auto-attack on ignited targets** вҖ” that AA deals massive bonus Magic Damage + slow. This is the secret to early first-blood kills.

### S2: Speed Boost + Manual Cancel

S2 grants explosive Movement Speed. After 5 seconds, it auto-ends with a roll + knockback. But you can **press S2 again to cancel early** вҖ” this refunds unused bomb charge time, letting you use S2 purely for a free speed boost.

### S2 + S1 = Guaranteed Hit

While S2 is active, S1 becomes a **lock-on bomb**. If an enemy is in range, S1 **always hits** and **always knocks back on the first hit**. vs diving Assassins (Lam, Jing): S2 вҶ’ run away вҶ’ S1 to knock them back.
