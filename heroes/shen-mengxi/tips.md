# Shen Mengxi Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Always AA Ignited Targets

S1 and S3 ignite enemies. Auto-attacking ignited targets deals **massive bonus Magic Damage + slow**. This is your early-game kill secret. In level 1-3 skirmishes, S1 → AA can secure first blood.

### 2. S2 Manual Cancel

S2 grants Movement Speed for 5 seconds, then auto-rolls. **Press S2 again to cancel early** — this refunds unused bomb charge time. Use S2 purely as a free speed boost between lanes.

### 3. S2 + S1 = Guaranteed Knockback

While S2 is active, S1 becomes lock-on and **always knocks back on first hit**. vs diving Assassins:
- S2 away from them
- S1 behind you (lock-on knockback)
- Perfectly cancels their engage

### 4. S2 + S3 Double Displacement

If you cast S3 (ultimate charge) while S2's timer is about to expire, Shen Mengxi **rolls forward at the same moment the bomb drops** — creating extreme-range bombardment while simultaneously creating distance from divers.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S1 first, then S2. Take ultimate whenever available.** S1 is core wave-clear and poke — max it first.

## Signature Combos

### Level 2 Gank: **S2 → S1 → AA → AA → S2 (roll/knockback)**

1. S2 speed into side lane
2. S1 lock-on knockback + ignite
3. AA ×2 for bonus ignite damage
4. S2 ends → roll directionally to knock fleeing enemy back toward your laner

### Late-Game Artillery: **S3 → S3 → S2 → S1**

1. Pre-fight: charge S3 from fog of war → bomb enemy backline
2. Second S3 when teamfight starts → enemy ADC/Mage now at 30% HP
3. S2 in → S1 lock-on finish remaining targets

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Vision control:** Ignited enemies are revealed. Use S1 to check bushes — if the bomb auto-targets, someone's there.
- **Never face-check:** Shen Mengxi is squishy. Always S1 bushes before walking past them.
- **Ultimate is global pressure:** Even if you can't kill, S3 from across the map forces enemies to recall.
- **Matchup:** Excels against immobile backlines. Struggles vs hyper-mobile Assassins who dodge S3.
