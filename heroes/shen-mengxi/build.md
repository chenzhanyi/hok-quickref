# Shen Mengxi Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Shen Mengxi is an artillery bombardment Mage with built-in mobility. He doesn't need defensive items for kiting. Build: **max Magic Attack + Magic Penetration**. Two S3 bombs should force enemy carries to recall or die.

---

## Recommended Artillery Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Arcane Boots (秘法之靴)** | Flat Magic Penetration maximizes early S1/S3 base damage. Mana regen sustains constant bomb spam. |
| 2 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | **Core poke item.** S3's massive AoE perfectly triggers Mask's percentage HP burn on multiple targets. One ultimate = massive team-wide damage. |
| 3 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | Magic Attack + 7% Movement Speed. On-hit explosion stacks with S3 for double AoE burst. |
| 4 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage Magic Attack steroid. Mid-game power spike — ultimate damage effectively doubles. |
| 5 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Magic Penetration. Mandatory late-game vs Magic Cloak and magic resist stacking. |
| 6 | **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** or **Splendor (辉月)** | **Sage's Tome:** Max Magic Attack + damage amp for two-bomb deleting full-HP squishies. **Splendor:** Stasis survival vs heavy dive (Lanling Wang, Ake, Han Xin). |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Magic Penetration (near true-damage feel), Movement Speed + Attack Speed (smoother S2-S1-AA combo).**

### Setup 1: CN Server "88-Pen Hunt" (Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +16% Attack Speed, **+10% Movement Speed**. Combined with S2's built-in speed, Shen Mengxi hits ~26% bonus MS — among the fastest rotators in the game.

### Setup 2: "Harmony Hybrid" (Safer Laning)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×5 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×5 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +88 Magic Penetration, +13% Attack Speed, +7% Movement Speed, **+225 Max Health, +26 HP/5s**. Better sustain vs heavy poke matchups (Baili Shouyue mid, Mozi).
