# Butterfly Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** **Crit Rate = Crit Damage.** Stack Crit Rate items/runes to maximize backstab burst. Pure burst Assassin.

---

## Recommended Jungle Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Attack, Penetration, CDR. Standard Assassin jungle item. |
| 2 | **Shadow Boots (影忍之足)** or **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Shadow Boots vs Physical-heavy; Resistance Boots vs CC. *Early alternative: Calm Boots for faster S3.* |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core. CDR + flat Penetration + Health. |
| 4 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | **Butterfly's soul item!** Crit Rate → Crit Damage conversion makes this the biggest power spike. |
| 5 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** | S2/S3 → AA triggers "Power Strike." Devastating single-hit burst. |
| 6 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Siege Breaker:** execute for chain-resets. **Pure Sky:** active DR for survival. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Butterfly's backstab auto-crits — she doesn't need Calamity (Crit Rate). She needs **Peerless (Crit Damage)** because Crit Rate converts to Crit Damage.

### Setup 1: "Peerless 100-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×10 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +25 Physical Attack, +64 Physical Penetration, +7% Crit Rate (= +3.5% Crit Damage via conversion), +36% Crit Damage, +10% MS.

### Setup 2: "Sustain Jungler" (Healthy Clears)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×10 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×7 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** Retains max burst + MS, adds **+4.8% Physical Lifesteal** for healthier jungle clears.

## Golden Rule

**Never initiate.** Wait. Watch. When the first enemy drops below 50% HP: S3 in → delete → reset → repeat. Patience = pentakills.
