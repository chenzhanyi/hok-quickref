# Butterfly Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Always Attack from Behind

Backstab = **guaranteed crit.** Front attack = **zero crit.** Always S2 through enemies, or wait for them to flee before attacking. Position is everything.

### 2. Cleanup Only — Never Initiate

Butterfly is pure glass. If you engage first, you die instantly. Wait at the edge of fights. When the first enemy drops low: **S3 → S2 through → AA → S1 → kill → S3 → next target.** Patience is your best weapon.

### 3. S2 = Auto-Backstab

S2 dashes through enemies. Land behind them = instant backstab position. Never dash short — always go THROUGH.

### 4. Kill/Assist = Full Reset

One kill = all cooldowns back. Immediately **S3 (stealth) → find next low-HP target → S2 → kill → repeat.** Chain-reset is how Butterfly pentakills.

### 5. S3 Approach: Angle, Not Straight

Enemies see the red flash indicator when you're near in stealth. Don't approach from the front — come from side lanes, jungle paths, or when they're busy fighting your team.

## Signature Combos

### Single Target Delete: **S3 → S2 → AA → S1 → AA**

1. S3 stealth → approach from behind/side
2. S2 dash through enemy (auto-backstab position)
3. AA (guaranteed crit)
4. S1 (can hit twice on backstab)
5. AA finish

### Teamfight Chain Reset: **S3 → kill → S3 → kill → S3 → kill**

1. Wait at edge — enemy drops low
2. S3 → S2 → kill → **all cooldowns reset**
3. Immediately S3 again → find next low target → repeat
4. Clean up the entire enemy team

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Crit Rate runes/items = more Crit Damage for Butterfly.** 1% Crit Rate = 0.5% Crit Damage. Stack Peerless + Infinity Edge.
- **Don't waste S3 on full-HP enemies.** You need the reset from a kill. Target the lowest HP enemy first.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs squishy teams with poor peel. Struggles vs tanky CC-heavy comps.
