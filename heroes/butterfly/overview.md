## Core Mechanics

### Backstab = Guaranteed Crit

All damage from behind (AAs, abilities) ALWAYS crits. Front attacks NEVER crit. Always position behind enemies вҖ” use S2 to dash through them, or attack when they're fleeing.

### Crit Rate вҶ’ Crit Damage Conversion

Butterfly's base Crit Damage is 125% (others: 200%). But **every 1% Crit Rate = +0.5% Crit Damage.** Stacking Crit Rate items/runes directly boosts her backstab damage.

### Kill/Assist = Full Reset

Scoring a kill or assist **resets ALL cooldowns.** Chain: S3 вҶ’ kill вҶ’ S3 вҶ’ kill вҶ’ S3. This is how Butterfly wipes teams.

### S2: Dash Through вҶ’ Auto-Backstab

S2 dashes through enemies. Land behind them = guaranteed backstab on your next AA/ability. Always S2 through, never S2 short.

### S3: Stealth Engage + Warning

S3 grants 3s stealth + MS. But **enemies see a red flash indicator** when you're near. Approach from unexpected angles or when they're distracted.
