# Zhuangzi（莊周） — Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Support / Tank (Anti-CC Specialist)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 逍遙幻夢 (Dreaming Wanderer)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 庄周; nickname: "Fish")

> Zhuangzi is the game's premier **CC-cleansing** Support. Every 6 seconds, he auto-cleanses himself and gains DR + MS. His S3 is an **AoE team cleanse + shield.** He's the ultimate counter to CC-heavy team comps — when enemies lock in Wang Zhaojun, Zhang Fei, Bai Qi, Dharma, or Sun Ce, Zhuangzi single-handedly negates their entire engage.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Auto-Cleanse Every 6 Seconds

Every 6s, Zhuangzi enters a dream state: cleanses all CC, gains 15% DR + 15% MS for 2 seconds. Watch for the "translucent" visual cue — during this window, you can face-tank CC abilities for free.

### S2: Stacking Damage (Up to 5 Layers)

S2's damage stacks per hit (max 5 layers). Both the active cast AND the automatic 6s passive cast stack layers. Stick to enemies and S2 on cooldown — a 5-stack S2 melts squishies.

### S3: Reactive, NOT Predictive

S3 is an AoE team cleanse + shield. **Never cast it preemptively.** The correct timing: enemy CC lands on your team → S3 immediately. Lu Bu S3 knocks up your ADC? S3. Bai Qi taunts your team? S3. Zhang Fei roars? S3.

### S1: Self-Cleanse + Slow

S1 is a ranged slow AND **grants a self-cleanse on cast.** When your passive is on cooldown and a CC is incoming: S1 to break free.
