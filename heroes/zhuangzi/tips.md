# Zhuangzi Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. NEVER Build Resistance Boots

Zhuangzi has built-in CC immunity every 6 seconds + S1 self-cleanse + S3 team cleanse. **Resistance Boots are completely wasted on him.** Always build Shadow Boots (影忍之足) for Physical AA damage reduction.

### 2. S3: Reactive Only — Never Predict

S3 cleanses CC for the entire team. The #1 mistake: casting S3 before CC lands. Correct: enemy CC connects → S3 immediately. Lu Bu knocks up your ADC? S3. Bai Qi taunts your team? S3.

### 3. S2: Level 1 Double-Stack Trick

At level 1 in the fountain: learn S2. It auto-casts once. When you reach lane, S2's auto-cast timer and your active cast will sync — you can instantly apply 2 stacks of S2 damage on first contact.

### 4. Passive Window: Free Face-Tank

Every 6 seconds, Zhuangzi enters a translucent dream state (15% DR + 15% MS + CC immunity for 2s). Watch for this visual cue. During this window: walk into enemy CC abilities intentionally — you're immune, and you've wasted their cooldowns.

### 5. S1: Emergency Self-Cleanse

When your 6s passive is on cooldown and a CC is flying at you: S1. It grants a self-cleanse on cast. This gives Zhuangzi effectively two personal CC-immune windows.

### 6. Pick Zhuangzi Into CC-Heavy Drafts

Zhuangzi is a counter-pick Hero. Pick him when enemies have: Bai Qi, Wang Zhaojun, Zhang Fei, Dharma, Sun Ce, Guiguzi, Xi Shi, Mozi. If the enemy has zero hard CC, Zhuangzi is half a Hero.

---

## Teamfight Flow

**S1 (slow) → Walk into enemy CC (passive immune) → S2 spam (stack damage) → Watch your carries → S3 (team cleanse when enemy CC lands)**

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S2's damage stacks infinitely if you keep casting.** Stick to enemies and never stop pressing S2.
- **Zhuangzi can split-push surprisingly well.** With passive DR + S2 damage, he wins extended 1v1s against most non-ADC Heroes.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs CC-heavy comps. Struggles when enemy has zero CC (no cleanse value).
