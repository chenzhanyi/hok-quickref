# Zhuangzi Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure tank with CDR. Never Resistance Boots. Needs CDR for frequent S3 team cleanses.

---

## Recommended Support Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis / Guard (极影 / 近卫)** | Shadow Aegis: team AS + CDR + Mana regen. Guard: team dual defense. Pick based on team needs. |
| 2 | **Shadow Boots (影忍之足)** | **Never Resistance Boots!** Zhuangzi has built-in CC immunity. Shadow Boots reduces Physical AA damage. |
| 3 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Reduces enemy AS on-hit — ADC killer. |
| 4 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** or **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** | **Magic Cloak:** vs poke Mages. **Eternal Night:** burst-heal vs Magic Assassins. |
| 5 | **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** | 20% CDR. S3 available every teamfight. Frost shockwave helps peel. |
| 6 | **Redemption active** or **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** | **Redemption:** group shield for carries. **Overlord's:** massive HP + regen — never recall. |

Core: **Max Health, CDR, Movement Speed.**

| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, Cooldown Reduction +0.6% |
