# Donghuang Taiyi（東皇太一） — Tank / Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Tank / Support (Suppression / HP Trader)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 噬滅日蝕 (Devouring Eclipse)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 东皇太一)

> Donghuang Taiyi is a strategically intimidating Roaming Tank. His core mechanic is his S3 **Suppression** — an uncleansable, Unstoppable-ignoring lockdown that **links his HP with the target** (any damage one takes is mirrored to the other). He is the ultimate counter to hyper-mobile, multi-dash, high-burst Heroes (Luna, Jing, Diaochan, Li Bai).

---

## Core Mechanics

### Energy Orbs + HP Recovery

S1 summons up to 3 rotating orbs. Orbs touching enemies deal Magic Damage + **restore Donghuang's HP**. The more enemies nearby, the more healing. In early jungle skirmishes, **walk clockwise around enemies** (orbs rotate counter-clockwise) to double the contact frequency and healing.

### S3: Contract Suppression (Ultimate Lockdown)

S3 is one of the strongest CC abilities in the game — **2.2s Suppression**. During the link, **any damage taken by either party is mirrored to the other**. Cannot be cleansed, cannot be blocked by Unstoppable. Donghuang MUST have more HP than the target to survive the trade.

### Tower Kill Exploit

If Donghuang suppresses an enemy under their own tower, **every tower shot Donghuang takes is mirrored to the enemy**. This lets Donghuang "borrow" the enemy tower to kill them. S2 → Flash → S3 under tower = guaranteed kill on squishy defenders.

### Pre-S3 S2 Combo

S2 throws all orbs for slow/stun. Casting S3 **instantly refills all 3 orbs**. Optimal sequence: **S2 (throw orbs for CC) → Flash → S3 (suppress)** — you get free S2 damage + 3 fresh orbs.
