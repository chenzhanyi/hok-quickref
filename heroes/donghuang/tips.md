# Donghuang Taiyi Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Clockwise Walking = Double Healing

Orbs rotate **counter-clockwise**. Walk **clockwise** around enemies to make orbs contact them faster — doubles your healing and damage output. This is why Donghuang wins early jungle 1v2s.

### 2. S3: HP Trade — You Must Be Tankier

S3 links HP between Donghuang and the target. If you have MORE HP, you win the trade. If the enemy has MORE HP, you die. **Never suppress a tank with more HP than you.** Always target squishy carries.

### 3. Tower Kill Exploit

When an enemy is under their tower: **S2 → Flash → S3** on them. Every tower shot you take = free damage to the enemy. The tower kills them for you.

### 4. S2 Before S3 = Free Damage

S2 throws all orbs. S3 **instantly refills 3 orbs**. Always sequence: **S2 → S3**, not S3 → S2. You get the S2 CC/damage PLUS full orbs during suppression.

### 5. Ping Before Engaging

Donghuang has zero burst. If you Flash+S3 but your team doesn't follow up, you're trading 1-for-1 at best. **Spam "Attack" pings before going in.** In solo queue, make sure your carries are close enough to capitalize.

## Signature Combos

### Long-Range Pickoff: **S2 → Flash → S3**

1. Charge 3 orbs
2. S2 throw orbs toward enemy (slow/stun)
3. **Flash → S3** directly onto the carry
4. Team follows up and deletes them during the 2.2s suppression

### Bush Ambush: **S3 → S2 → S1 → AA**

1. Wait in bush with 3 orbs
2. S3 as enemy walks past
3. S2 after suppression ends (slow + stun)
4. S1 → AA while walking clockwise for max orb hits

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 cannot be cleansed nor blocked by Unstoppable.** It overrides EVERYTHING except death.
- **Pick into hyper-mobile Heroes.** Donghuang is the ultimate Luna, Jing, Diaochan, Li Bai counter.
- **Don't pick into poke comps.** Donghuang struggles against long-range teams that never let him get close.
