# Donghuang Taiyi Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Stack **maximum HP** — more HP = you win S3 HP trades. Needs **Movement Speed** (to reach targets) and adequate **defenses**. Pure HP tank.

---

## Recommended Full-Tank Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎)** or **Guard · Redemption (近卫·救赎)** | Start Knowledge Gem. Upgrade mid-game. **Redemption active:** group shield — pop it during S3 when you're about to die; the shield absorbs mirrored damage. |
| 2 | **Swift Boots (疾步之靴)** | Donghuang is slow. Swift Boots for catching targets. *Late game: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC.* |
| 3 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** | **Donghuang's #1 core item!** Highest base HP in the game. Win every S3 HP trade. In-combat + out-of-combat regen = never recall. |
| 4 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Reduces enemy AS/MS on-hit — survival after S3 ends. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | **Eternal Night:** burst-heal on magic damage — perfect S3 synergy. **Magic Cloak:** sustained magic defense + spell shield. |
| 6 | **Blazing Cape (红莲斗篷)** or **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | Blazing Cape: burn aura for melee damage. Sanctuary: resurrection for final fights. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Max HP (S3 win condition), CDR, Movement Speed.** Zero offensive stats needed.

### Setup 1: "Thousand-HP Tank" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, Cooldown Reduction +0.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** **+1,162 Max Health**, +23 Physical Defense, +4% MS, +6% CDR, +10% AS. Level 1 invade dominance.

### Setup 2: "Max Speed Chase"

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |

**Total:** +737 Max Health, +23 Physical Defense, **+10% MS**, +6% CDR, +20% AS. Sacrifices raw HP for ability to actually reach mobile targets.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Donghuang is heavily team-reliant. **Ping before engaging.** The enemy team will often try to bait your S3 — don't waste it on tanks. Save it for the hyper-carry.
