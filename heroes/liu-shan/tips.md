# Liu Shan Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Tower = Health Pack

After a fight with low HP: go to the nearest enemy tower with minions. S1 → S2 → S3 on the tower. You'll heal to full HP while damaging and stunning the tower. Never recall — just find a tower.

### 2. Pre-Stack S1 (Double Knockup)

S1's enhanced AA knockup lasts a long time. **Pre-cast S1 before engaging** → hold the knockup → AA (first knockup) → S1 is already off cooldown → cast again → AA (second knockup). Two knockups in 2 seconds.

### 3. S2: Instant Undodgeable Stun (Bodyguard Mode)

S2 has zero cast delay. When an Assassin (Lanling Wang, Ake, Jing) dives your ADC: **S2 immediately.** It's undodgeable at close range. Then S1 → S3 for chain CC. The Assassin dies without landing a hit.

### 4. S3 + S1/S2 Mid-Spin

Post-rework: **you can cast S1 and S2 during S3 without canceling the spin.** Optimal: S3 (spin) → S1 (knockup during spin) → S2 (stun during spin). Seamless CC chain.

### 5. Tower Dive With Impunity

With minions or the "Interfere" summoner spell: S1 + S2 on the tower to stun it. The tower can't attack. Your ADC kills the enemy under tower safely. Repeat for every tower on the map.

## Signature Combo

### Perfect CC Chain: **S1 (pre-stack) → AA → S2 → S3 → S1 (mid-spin) → S2 (mid-spin)**

1. Pre-cast S1, hold knockup
2. AA (first knockup)
3. S2 (instant stun before they land)
4. S3 spin
5. S1 mid-spin (second knockup)
6. S2 mid-spin (second stun)
7. Enemy is CC'd for 4+ seconds

## Advanced Tips

- **S1 + S2 on a tower = tower stunned for 2+ seconds.** ADC destroys it for free.
- **Sustain off towers.** You never need to recall. Always push after winning fights.
- **Matchup:** God-tier with dive ADCs. Struggles vs heavy poke that kites before you can S1 gap-close.
