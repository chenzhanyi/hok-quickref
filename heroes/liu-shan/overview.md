# Liu Shan（劉禪） — Support / Tank

## Overview

- **Role:** Support / Tank (Tower Destroyer / CC Chain)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 暴走機關 (Rampage Machine)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 刘禅)

> Liu Shan is known as the "Tower Destroyer" and "Dive Perpetual-Motion Machine." All his abilities deal equal damage to turrets AND **stun turrets**, while **healing Liu Shan** when hitting structures. He can keep a tower permanently disabled while his ADC destroys it. Post-rework, he can now **cast S1/S2 during S3's spin** without interrupting it.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Tower = Health Pack

Abilities deal damage to turrets, heal Liu Shan, AND stun turrets. After a fight: walk to the nearest enemy tower with minions → cast abilities on it → full HP without recalling. This is his "perpetual motion" sustain.

### S1: Pre-Stack the Knockup

S1 grants a shield + MS + enhanced AA (knockup). The enhanced AA lasts a long time. **Pre-cast S1 before ganking** → hold the knockup → engage → S1 is off cooldown → cast S1 again for double knockup.

### S3 + S1/S2 (Post-Rework)

**You can now cast S1 and S2 DURING S3's spin.** S3 continues spinning while the knockup/stun fires. This massively improves CC chain fluidity.

### S2: Instant Stun (Bodyguard Mode)

S2 has zero cast delay — it's an instant stun. When an Assassin (Lanling Wang, Ake, Jing) dives your ADC: S2 their face immediately. Undodgeable. Then S1 knockup chain. Dead Assassin.
