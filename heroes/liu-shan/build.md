# Liu Shan Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs **CDR** (constant CC), **Movement Speed** (gap-close for S1), and **full tank** (dives towers). Pure tank Support.

---

## Recommended Full-Tank Support Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎)** | Shadow Aegis aura: team AS + CDR + Mana regen. Redemption active: group shield. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Must survive CC to chain his own CC. |
| 3 | **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** | **Core item!** 20% CDR for constant S1/S2/S3. Frost shockwave on burst helps stick to targets. |
| 4 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Reduces enemy AS on-hit — ADC counter. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense. Eternal Night vs burst; Magic Cloak vs sustained poke. |
| 6 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** or **Rising Sun (旭日初光)** | **Overlord's:** massive HP + regen. **Rising Sun:** shields ally on ability hit — perfect synergy with frequent CC. |
