# Dian Wei Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** S2 gives +200% AS — zero Attack Speed items needed. **S3 makes ALL damage deal 3% Max HP True Damage** — on-hit and reflect effects become deadly. Semi-tank is meta. CN server consensus: tank items with S3 synergy.

---

## Recommended Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** | HP stacking + burn aura. Burn triggers S3's 3% Max HP True Damage. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Dian Wei needs to survive CC to S1-cleanse. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | CDR + flat Penetration + Health. Only damage item needed. |
| 4 | **Thornmail (反伤刺甲)** | **Dian Wei's soul item!** Reflect damage triggers S3's 3% Max HP True Damage per tick. Enemy ADC kills themselves auto-attacking you. |
| 5 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** or **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | **Master's Power:** S1→AA burst for deleting squishies. **Berserker's:** stacking MS + damage. |
| 6 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense. Prevents getting kited and poked out by Mages. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Zero Attack Speed needed (S2 gives +200%). **Pure Critical Rate + Penetration + Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Calamity Stealth Burst" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×10 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** **+16% Critical Rate**, +16 Physical Attack, +64 Physical Penetration, **+10% MS**. With S2's +200% AS, 16% crit triggers constantly. Never Hunt — AS is wasted.

### Setup 2: "Peerless Crit Cap" (Max Burst)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×3 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +13.3% Crit Rate, +10.8% Crit Damage, +64 Physical Penetration, +10% MS. Crits hit harder at 250% cap.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Dian Wei is immobile and needs MS. 10× Stealth (隐匿) is mandatory — never use Hunt or Harmony. Copy the **CN #1 Dian Wei** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
