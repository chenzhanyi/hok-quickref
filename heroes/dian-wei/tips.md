# Dian Wei Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S1: Save for CC Cleanse, NEVER for Speed

S1 cleanses CC AND gives 2 Rage stacks. If you waste S1 for movement speed, you'll get CC'd and die. Always: **get CC'd → S1 cleanse → surprise, you're stronger now and they have no CC left.**

### 2. S2: Guarantee the Hit

S2's +200% AS + 25-50% Lifesteal ONLY triggers on hit. If S2 misses: you're dead. Use S3 slow or Smite first, then S2 directly on top of the enemy. Never S2 from range.

### 3. S3 + Thornmail = Absurd True Damage

S3 makes ALL damage deal 3% Max HP True Damage for 5 seconds. This includes:
- Your AAs
- **Thornmail's reflect damage** (enemies kill themselves hitting you)
- **Blazing Cape's burn aura**
- Even Smite damage

Thornmail + S3 is Dian Wei's signature combo. The enemy ADC kills themselves by auto-attacking you.

### 4. Stack Passive Early, Scale Forever

Every CC-cleanse/kill/assist = +1 permanent Rage stack. Prioritize early skirmishes to stack up. A 6-stack Dian Wei at 20 minutes is a raid boss.

### 5. Don't Build Attack Speed (S2 Gives 200%)

S2 already gives +200% AS. Building more AS is completely wasted. Focus on: **Attack Damage, Critical Rate, tankiness.**

## Signature Combos

### Standard Engage: **S3 → S2 → AA spam → S1 (save for CC)**

1. S3 jump + slow
2. S2 on the slowed target (guaranteed hit)
3. AA with +200% AS + 25-50% Lifesteal + 3% Max HP True Damage
4. S1 saved — only use if CC'd

### Counter-CC Reverse-Kill: **Get CC'd → S1 (cleanse) → S3 → S2 → AA**

1. Enemy CC's you
2. S1 cleanse → +2 Rage stacks
3. S3 jump on them
4. S2 → AA them to death

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Thornmail is Dian Wei's real damage item.** S3 + Thornmail reflect = the enemy ADC dies in 3-4 auto-attacks against you.
- **10× Stealth (not Hunt) is mandatory.** Dian Wei doesn't need AS from runes. Stealth gives more AD + MS.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs Physical auto-attackers. Struggles vs kite-heavy Magic teams.
