# Dian Wei（典韋） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Late-Game Melee Shredder)
- **Lane:** Jungle / Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 狂戰士 (Berserker)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 典韦)

> Dian Wei is the quintessential late-game melee meat-grinder. His passive stacks permanent Physical Attack per kill/assist/CC-cleanse (max 6 stacks). At 6 stacks, his AA range increases and his damage becomes absurd — even with full tank items, he hits like a truck. His S3 makes ALL damage dealt (AAs, counter-damage from items, burn auras) deal 3% Max HP True Damage for 5 seconds.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Rage Stacks (Permanent Scaling)

Gain 1 stack per CC-cleanse/kill/assist (max 6). Each stack grants **Physical Attack + AA range.** At 6 stacks, Dian Wei hits as hard as a full-damage Assassin even with tank items.

### S1: CC Cleanse (Don't Waste It)

S1 cleanses CC AND grants 2 Rage stacks. **NEVER use S1 for movement speed.** Save it for when the enemy CC's you → S1 cleanse → they've wasted their CC and you're now stronger.

### S2: Must Hit Target

S2 grants **+200% Attack Speed + 25-50% Physical Lifesteal** — but ONLY if it hits an enemy. If S2 misses: no AS, no lifesteal, dead Dian Wei. Use S3 slow or Smite first to guarantee S2 lands.

### S3: True Damage Engine

S3 deals initial damage + slow, then for **5 seconds, ALL damage Dian Wei deals (AAs, Thornmail reflect, Blazing Cape burn) deals additional 3% Max HP True Damage.** This is why tank Dian Wei still shreds everyone.
