# Fang（李元芳） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Burst / Tower Destroyer)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 王都密探 (Royal City Agent)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 李元芳)

> Fang is a uniquely explosive Marksman/Jungler. He has devastating single-target burst, a mobility skill with invulnerability frames, and a passive that periodically reveals nearby enemies — making him nearly impossible to ambush. He's a "tempo master" who excels at taking towers and controlling objectives.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Radar Ears (Best Anti-Gank Tool)

Fang periodically detects nearby enemy Heroes and **reveals them for 1 second**. When his ears flash above his head, someone's in that bush — even invisible enemies (Lanling Wang, Ake). The strongest anti-ambush passive in the game.

### S1: Mark + AA Stack → Detonate

S1 places a mark on an enemy or **tower**. AAs stack the mark (max 4 stacks). At 4 stacks: **massive burst detonation**. S1 also grants **bonus Attack Speed + Attack Range**. Can be used on towers!

### S2: Invulnerability Roll (Your Lifeline)

S2 rolls forward. **During the roll, Fang is completely invulnerable.** Use to dodge: Zhuge Liang S3, tower shots, Daji's charm. This is his only escape — never waste it.

### S3: Ultimate + Fast S1 Stacking

S3 throws a spinning blade that deals multi-hit damage. **S3 hits also stack S1 marks.** Drop S3 under an S1-marked enemy for near-instant 4-stack detonation.
