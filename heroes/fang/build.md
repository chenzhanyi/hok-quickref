# Fang Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Fang's damage is mostly **skill burst + enhanced AAs**. Pure crit-speed (Shadow Blade) isn't optimal. He prefers **Physical Penetration + burst** items. Build path: Penetration bruiser-Marksman.

---

## Recommended Farm Lane / Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early AS to stack S1 marks faster. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core item. CDR (faster S1/S2), Health, flat Penetration. Perfect for Fang's skill-burst style. |
| 3 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | S1 detonation and AAs CAN crit. Infinity Edge is the mid-game burst spike. |
| 4 | **Blood Weeper (泣血之刃)** | Physical Lifesteal for sustain. Never recall — heal off jungle camps and waves. |
| 5 | **Dawn (破晓)** or **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** | **Dawn:** full Marksman Penetration. **Star Breaker:** cheaper Penetration for faster tempo. |
| 6 | **Sun Bow (逐日之弓)** or **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** / **Fate's Edge (名刀·司命)** | **Sun Bow:** +Range to compensate for Fang's short AA range late game. **Survival items:** vs heavy dive. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Physical Penetration (early burst), Movement Speed, mild AS + Crit.**

### Setup 1: "Stealth-Hunt 100-Pen Hybrid" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×9 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×1 | Critical Rate +1.6% ("dream crit") |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×7 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×3 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** ~100 Physical Penetration, +10% MS, +3-5% AS, +1.6% Crit. Max kiting + burst.

### Setup 2: "Reave Sustain" (Jungle / Safe Farm)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×5 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×5 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +5% AS, +5% MS, **+8% Physical Lifesteal**, high Penetration. Healthy jungle clears + lane sustain.
