# Fang Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Passive Ears: Never Get Ganked

Fang's passive periodically reveals nearby enemies for 1s. When his ears flash: **someone's in that bush.** This hard-counters Lanling Wang, Ake, and bush campers. Trust your ears — if they flash, don't face-check.

### 2. S3 + S1 = Instant Detonation

S3's multi-hit blade ALSO stacks S1 marks. Combo: **S1 mark → S3 under enemy → 1-2 AAs → instant 4-stack detonation.** This is Fang's fastest burst — S3 does the stacking work for you.

### 3. S2: Invulnerability Frames — Save It

S2's roll grants complete invulnerability. Use to dodge:
- Zhuge Liang S3 (timed right)
- Tower shots (dive → kill → S2 to immune the tower bullet)
- Daji charm, Mozi cannon
Never waste S2 for mobility when enemies are nearby.

### 4. S1 Works on Towers

S1's mark can be placed on **enemy turrets**. When the enemy ADC recalls or roams: S1 the tower → AA×4 → detonate. Fang melts towers faster than almost any other Marksman.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S1 first, then S2. Take S3 whenever available.** S1 is your primary damage and tower-taking tool.

## Signature Combos

### Lane Burst / Bush Ambush: **S1 → S3 → AA×2 → S2 (if needed)**

1. S1 mark the enemy
2. S3 directly under them (slows + stacks S1 marks)
3. 2 quick AAs → 4 stacks → detonation
4. S2 to chase or escape

### Counter-Engage vs Assassin: **S2 (dodge) → S3 → S1 → AA**

1. Enemy Assassin dives → S2 roll (invulnerability through their burst)
2. S3 behind you as you roll (slows them)
3. S1 mark → AA to detonate
4. They're now low HP and you're at safe distance

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S1 grants Attack Speed + Range.** Always AA after casting S1 — the bonus is significant.
- **S3 also slows.** Use it to trap enemies or zone them away from objectives.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile squishies and bush-reliant Assassins. Struggles vs long-range poke that out-ranges his S1.
