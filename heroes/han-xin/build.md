# Han Xin Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure burst Assassin. Needs **Critical Rate, Physical Penetration, and Lifesteal** (never recall — sustain off jungle). CN server consensus: **Crit Burst with 16% Lifesteal runes.**

---

## Recommended Jungle Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Standard Assassin jungle item: Attack, Penetration, CDR. |
| 2 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | AS + passive's 50% AS = absurdly fast jungle clear. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots or Fate's Edge.* |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core. CDR, flat Penetration, Health. Early gank power spike. |
| 4 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | **Power spike!** S2's enhanced AA CAN crit. With Infinity Edge, one crit sweep deletes 70% HP. |
| 5 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** | S2 → enhanced AA perfectly triggers "Power Strike." Constant ability casts = constant procs. |
| 6 | **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** / **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Star Breaker:** 40% Penetration vs armor. **Siege Breaker:** execute vs squishy teams. **Pure Sky:** active DR for survival. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Critical Rate, Critical Damage (cap at 250%), Lifesteal (sustain), Physical Penetration.**

### Setup 1: "10× Reave Crit" (CN Standard — Highest Winrate)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×3 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×10 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +9.2 Physical Attack, +64 Physical Pen, +13.3% Crit Rate, +10.8% Crit Damage, **+16% Physical Lifesteal**. Never recall — one jungle camp = full HP.

### Setup 2: "Speed Hybrid" (Aggressive Tempo)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×3 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×7 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×3 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +13.3% Crit Rate, +10.8% Crit Damage, +64 Physical Pen, +11.2% Lifesteal, **+3% MS + 3% AS**. Smoother AA feel + faster map rotations.

## Golden Rule

**Farm > Ganks.** Han Xin with a 2,000-gold lead is unstoppable. Han Xin with equal gold is useless. Prioritize economy.
