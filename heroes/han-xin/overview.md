# Han Xin（韓信） — Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin (Hyper-Mobile Jungler / Split-Pusher)
- **Lane:** Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 國士無雙 (Unmatched Strategist)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 韩信; nickname: "Han Jump-Jump")

> Han Xin is the iconic high-mobility Jungler with three long-range dashes. His core gameplay isn't teamfighting — it's **economic oppression, jungle invasion, and split-push pressure.** If Han Xin has equal gold to the enemy Jungler, he's already losing. He must be 2,000 gold ahead, stealing every buff, and forcing enemies to chase his ghost across the map.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: AS Boost + Knockup

Hitting anything with an ability grants **+50% Attack Speed.** Every 4th AA knocks up. Never dump all abilities at once — weave AAs between skills for maximum AS uptime.

### S2: Backward Dash (Muscle Memory Required)

S2 dashes **opposite to the joystick direction.** Joystick back = dash forward. Practice in Training Mode until it's instinct — beginners constantly jump into enemies by mistake.

### S3: Unstoppable + 30% DR

S3 grants **Unstoppable + 30% DR** during the channel. When Li Yuanfang marks you, Zhuge Liang locks S3, or Daji throws her charm: S3 immediately. You'll immune the CC and reduce the damage.

### Economy > Everything

Han Xin's win condition: maintain a **2,000+ gold lead** over the enemy Jungler. Farm everything — your jungle, enemy jungle, all three lanes. Only gank when it's free. If you're even in gold, you're behind.
