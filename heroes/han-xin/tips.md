# Han Xin Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Economy First, Kills Second

Han Xin MUST be ahead in gold. Farm your jungle → invade enemy jungle → clear side waves → take both Dragons. If you're equal in gold, you can't one-shot and you die instantly. A 2,000-gold lead Han Xin can 1v3.

### 2. S2: Muscle Memory Is Everything

S2 dashes **opposite to the joystick.** Pull joystick backward → dash forward. Drill this in Training Mode. One wrong S2 direction = you jump into 5 enemies and die.

### 3. S3: Reactive Counter, Not Engage

S3 grants Unstoppable + 30% DR. Don't open with it — save it as a counter:
- Li Yuanfang S1 mark → S3 (immune detonation)
- Zhuge Liang S3 → S3 (immune lock-on)
- Daji charm → S3 (immune CC)
- Enemy burst → S3 (30% DR)

### 4. Late Game = Split-Push Menace

Han Xin is too squishy for 5v5 teamfights. Late game: **push the farthest side lane.** One enemy comes? Kill them. Three come? You escape with 3 dashes. Meanwhile, your team takes Baron or their towers for free. This is Han Xin's true win condition.

### 5. Weave AAs Between Abilities

Passive grants +50% AS after ability hits. Never S1 → S1 → S2 → S3 in one burst. Always: S1 → AA → S1 → AA → S2 → AA. The AS makes your clear speed and DPS dramatically higher.

## Signature Combos

### Longest-Range Knockup: **S2 → S1 → AA → S3 → S1**

1. S2 backward = forward dash (close distance)
2. S1 first cast (knockup)
3. AA with +50% AS
4. S3 (Unstoppable + DR + damage)
5. S1 second cast (escape over wall or chase)

### Bush Ambush: **S1 → AA → S3 → S2 → AA → S1**

1. S1 from bush (knockup)
2. AA → S3 (tank return damage)
3. S2 backward dash (chase their Flash)
4. AA (enhanced from S2)
5. S1 second cast (finish or escape)

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S2's enhanced AA can crit.** Pair with Infinity Edge + Master's Power for devastating single-hit burst.
- **16% Lifesteal (10× Reave) is standard.** Never recall — heal off jungle camps.
- **Matchup:** God-tier with a gold lead. Struggles when behind and enemies group.
