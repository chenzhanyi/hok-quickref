# Milady Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Robot HP and damage scale entirely with **Magic Attack**. More AP = tankier, stronger robots. Needs **CDR** for frequent S2 robot summons. Pure AP + max CDR.

---

## Recommended Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | MANDATORY first item. CDR for frequent S2 summons. More robots = more pressure. |
| 2 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | AP + Health + 5% CDR. S1's multi-tick damage perfectly triggers Mask's percentage HP burn repeatedly. |
| 3 | **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** or **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | **Sunset Flow:** vs tanky teams — S1 and robots stack penetration instantly. **Echo Staff:** vs squishy teams — burst + Movement Speed. |
| 4 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | **Milady's power spike!** Robots' HP and damage scale with AP. Savant's Wrath percentage boost makes robots hit like Super Minions. |
| 5 | **Frost Breath (凝冰之息)** | S1 and robot auto-attacks constantly trigger the slow. Once enemies are stuck, robots get several extra hits — and S3 execute damage skyrockets. |
| 6 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** or **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** | **Void Staff:** Penetration vs Magic Cloak. **Sage's Tome:** raw AP to max out robot stats vs teams without magic resist. |

## Core Mindset

Milady's win condition is **towers, not kills**. When the enemy Mid roams:
1. Ping retreat for your team
2. Push Mid T1 with robots + S3
3. Enemy jungle is now exposed
4. Repeat for Mid T2, then side lanes
