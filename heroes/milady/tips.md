# Milady Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Your Job = Destroy Towers

Milady is NOT a teamfight mage. When the enemy Mid Laner roams, don't follow — **ping retreat for your team and take their Mid T1 tower instead**. One lost tower = enemy jungle permanently exposed.

### 2. Turret Dive Without Minions

Pre-summon 4 robots (S2 ×2). Walk into tower range. **Cast S3 on the turret itself** (yes, it works on structures). Berserk robots + S3 explosion can 100→0 a full-HP tower with zero minion support.

### 3. S1 Split Timing

S1's aircraft splits on recast. **Split exactly above the enemy's head** for max damage + slow. Don't autopilot — wait for the right moment.

### 4. S3 Self-Defense vs Assassins

Milady has zero mobility. When an Assassin dives you (Lanling Wang, Li Bai, Lam):
- **S3 their face** — stun + mark
- **S2 at your feet** — spawn robots
- **S1 split above them**
- Berserk robots + your full combo often kills the Assassin before they kill you

### 5. Bush-Check with S2

Robots provide vision. When approaching suspicious bushes, drop S2 inside. If the robots start attacking, someone's there.

## Signature Combos

### Bush Ambush One-Shot: **S2 → S3 → S1 → S2 → AA**

1. Pre-summon robots with S2
2. S3 stun + mark
3. S1 split directly above target
4. S2 again for more robots
5. AA to help finish
6. Berserk robot swarm tears the target apart in ~2 seconds

### Ranged Poke: **S1 → S1 (split) → S2**

1. S1 at max range
2. Split above enemy for slow
3. S2 robots walk forward to harass while enemy is slowed

## Advanced Tips

- **Robot HP and damage scale with Magic Attack.** More AP = tankier, harder-hitting robots. This is why Savant's Wrath is her power spike.
- **Don't teamfight blindly.** Your strength is map pressure. Trade towers for dragons if needed.
- **Matchup:** Destroys roaming Mid Laners who leave lane. Struggles vs long-range artillery Mages who outrange her S1.
