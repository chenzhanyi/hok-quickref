# Milady（米萊狄） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Siege / Summoner)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 築城者 (Fortress Builder)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 米莱狄)

> Milady is a uniquely designed "siege" Mage. Her signature mechanic is endlessly spawning **Mechanical Servants (小机器人)** that can **destroy turrets without a minion wave** and overwhelm teamfights with their sheer numbers. Her damage and push speed scale directly with how many robots are alive.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Robot Factory

Whenever Milady or her robots **last-hit any unit** (Minions, Monsters, Heroes, summons), a new robot spawns. **CS'ing (getting the last hit) is absolutely critical.**

### S1: Split at the Right Moment

S1's aircraft splits into two on recast. **Split it exactly when it's above the enemy** for maximum damage + slow. Splitting too early or too late cuts damage significantly.

### S3 Ultimate: Swarm Signal

S3 stuns and marks a target. ALL nearby robots go berserk — **+100% Movement Speed and Attack Speed**. The final explosion deals magnetic execute damage based on %HP the target lost during the mark. **S3 can target turrets!**
