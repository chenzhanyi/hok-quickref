# Wukong Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Pre-Stack Your Staff (Most Important Mechanic)

Enhanced AAs last several seconds. Before ganking: **S2 into empty air, hold the bonk.** Wait for S2 to nearly come off cooldown, then engage: **AA (held) → S2 → AA → S1 → AA → S3 → AA.** This gives you 4 bonks instead of 3 — the difference between a kill and the enemy escaping at 10% HP.

### 2. S2 Body Technique: Springboard to Backline

S2 bounces off anything it touches for a second, longer jump. In teamfights: **S2 off the enemy tank's face** to bypass them entirely and land directly on the ADC/Mage. Enemy frontline is your launchpad.

### 3. S1: Negate Key Abilities

S1's shield triggers when you're hit by an enemy ability. With good timing, negate: Daji charm, Zhuge Liang S3, tower shots. This is Wukong's only defensive tool — don't waste it for movement speed.

### 4. S3 Mark Stacking

S3 applies 3 marks. AAs on marked targets deal **bonus Physical Damage per mark consumed.** Always S3 → AA chain after. Don't S3 and then run away — you're wasting your biggest damage amp.

### 5. Enable Avatar Lock / Minion-Tower Button

Wukong's enhanced AA auto-targets the nearest enemy. Without avatar lock, you'll bonk the tank instead of the carry. **Settings → Avatar Lock ON.** Also use minion/tower button to avoid accidentally jumping to enemies.

## Signature Combos

### Bush Ambush: **AA (held) → S3 → AA → S1 → AA → S2 → AA**

1. Hold pre-stacked bonk
2. S3 mark + knockup → AA
3. S1 (negate counter-CC) → AA
4. S2 (chase or escape) → AA
5. 4 bonks, squishy deleted

### Springboard Backline Delete: **S2 (off tank) → AA → S3 → AA → S1 → AA**

1. S2 bounce off enemy frontline
2. AA on landing (on the backline)
3. S3 → AA
4. S1 → AA
5. ADC/Mage dead before they can react

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S2 can bounce off corpses.** In teamfights, dead minions or Heroes are valid springboards.
- **S2 can execute low-HP jungle monsters** (post-rework). Faster farming.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs squishy teams. Struggles vs tanky CC-heavy comps that survive his burst.
