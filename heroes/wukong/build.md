# Wukong Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Born with 20% Crit Rate but only 150% Crit Damage (others: 200%). **Infinity Edge is non-negotiable.** Every build starts there. After that: more Crit + Attack Speed + sustain.

---

## Recommended Jungle Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Standard Assassin jungle item: Attack, Penetration, CDR. |
| 2 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | AS smooths out bonk animation. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC.* |
| 3 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | **ABSOLUTE CORE #1.** Fixes Wukong's 150% → 200%+ Crit Damage. Without this, Wukong is half a Hero. |
| 4 | **Shadow Blade (影刃)** or **Master's Power (宗师之力)** | **Shadow Blade (meta):** AS + MS on crit for smoother combos. **Master's Power:** max per-bonk burst. |
| 5 | **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** or **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Star Breaker:** 40% Penetration vs armor stacking. **Siege Breaker:** snowballing vs squishy teams. |
| 6 | **Blood Weeper (泣血之刃)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Blood Weeper:** Lifesteal if runes lack it. **Pure Sky:** active DR for teamfight survival. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Critical Damage (Peerless) to reach the 250% cap, Physical Penetration, Lifesteal (sustain jungle).**

### Setup 1: "Peerless 100% Lifesteal" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×10 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×10 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +7% Critical Rate, +36% Critical Damage, +64 Physical Penetration, +9 Physical Attack, **+16% Physical Lifesteal**. Skip lifesteal item — rush Infinity Edge directly. Fastest power spike.

### Setup 2: "Hunt Hybrid" (Body Technique Feel)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×10 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×7 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×3 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +36% Critical Damage, +64 Physical Penetration, +11.2% Physical Lifesteal, **+3% MS + 3% AS**. Lighter movement feel for S2 body technique.
