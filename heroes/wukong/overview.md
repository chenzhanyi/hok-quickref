# Wukong（孫悟空） — Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin (Crit Burst Jungler)
- **Lane:** Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 齊天大聖 (Great Sage Equal to Heaven)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 孙悟空; nickname: "Monkey")

> Wukong is the quintessential crit-burst Assassin. He's born with **20% free Critical Rate** but only **150% base Critical Damage** (vs 200% for others). Infinity Edge is mandatory to fix this. Every ability empowers his next AA into a devastating "staff slam." His S2 can bounce off any unit (including corpses) for a double-jump — mastering this "body technique" separates gods from button-mashers.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Built-In 20% Crit / 150% Crit Damage

Wukong starts with 20% Crit Rate but reduced Crit Damage (150% vs 200%). **Infinity Edge is absolutely mandatory** — it fixes his crit damage and makes his kit functional.

### S2: Body Technique (Double Jump)

S2 dashes forward. **If it contacts ANY unit (Minion, Monster, Hero, corpse), Wukong bounces for a second, longer jump** with bonus MS. Use enemy frontline as springboards to reach the backline.

### S1: Invincibility Frame + Shield

S1 grants a brief window where **if an enemy ability hits you, it's negated + you gain a massive shield.** Time it against Daji's charm, Zhuge Liang S3, or tower shots.

### Pre-Stack Your Staff ("Hold the Bonk")

After using any ability, the enhanced AA lasts several seconds. Before ganking: **cast S2 into empty air, hold the enhanced AA**, wait for S2 cooldown to nearly reset, then engage. This lets you: AA (held bonk) → S2 → AA → S1 → AA → S3 → AA — one extra bonk per combo.
