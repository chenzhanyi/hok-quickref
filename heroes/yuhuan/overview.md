# Yang Yuhuan（楊玉環） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Utility / Skirmisher)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 霓裳風華 (Radiant Beauty)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 杨玉环)

> Yang Yuhuan is an extremely high-ceiling, versatile Mage. Her unique mechanic is dual Stance: **Green (healing)** and **Blue (damage)**. Her ultimate grants **invulnerability** (untargetable). Thanks to her exceptional kiting, she's not only a stable Mid Laner — she's also become a **high-rank and pro-play Jungler** in the current meta.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Stance Toggle (Passive)

- **Blue Stance (惊鸿):** Abilities deal bonus AoE Magic Damage. **Use this 95% of the time** — farming, rotating, poking, teamfighting.
- **Green Stance (清平):** Abilities heal nearby allies + self. **Only switch when your team needs healing** after fights, or you're critically low and need sustain from Minions/Monsters.

### S1 + Enhanced AA

After S1, the next AA deals bonus damage + **reduces all cooldowns by 1 second**. Always weave S1→AA between spells.

### S2: Edge Timing

S2's stun ONLY triggers on the **outer ring edge**. Enemies too close or too far are not stunned. Constantly adjust positioning to keep enemies on the ring.

### S3: Invulnerability

S3 makes Yang Yuhuan **untargetable**. Use to: dodge Zhuge Liang S3, Musashi S3, tower shots; dive towers for kills; or give teammates massive healing during engages.
