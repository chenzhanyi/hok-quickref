# Yang Yuhuan Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Blue Stance 95% of the Time

Blue (damage) is your default. Only switch to Green (healing) when:
- Your team is low HP after a fight
- You're critically low and need to heal off Minions/Monsters
Never fight in Green stance — you lose all your damage.

### 2. S1 → AA Is Mandatory

After every S1, the next AA deals bonus damage + **reduces all cooldowns by 1 second**. Always S1 → AA between spells. This is where 40%+ of your damage comes from. Never skip the AA.

### 3. S2 Edge = Stun, Not the Middle

S2's stun ONLY triggers on the **outer ring**. Enemies inside the ring (close to you) are NOT stunned. Constantly reposition to keep enemies on the outer edge.

### 4. S3 Invulnerability: Offensive AND Defensive

S3 makes you untargetable. Use it:
- **Defensively:** Dodge Zhuge Liang S3, Musashi S3, tower shots
- **Offensively:** Dive towers, follow your divers (Jing, Lam) with massive healing + damage

### 5. Follow Your Frontline

Yang Yuhuan is NOT a solo-carry. Stay behind your tank/engager. Use S2 to chain CC after their initiation. Use S1→AA to pressure from behind them.

---

## Signature Combos

### Standard Poke: **S1 → AA → S2 → AA → S1 → AA**

1. S1 → AA (first cooldown reduction)
2. S2 edge stun → AA
3. S1 (refreshed from AA reductions) → AA
Two S1 casts + two CD reductions in one rotation.

### Tower Dive / Aggressive: **S2 → S1 → AA → S3 → AA**

1. S2 edge stun under tower
2. S1 → AA burst
3. S3 untargetable — soak tower shots + deal damage
4. AA after landing to finish

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 heals allies too.** When your diver goes in, S3 on top of them for massive burst healing.
- **Green stance S1 heals from Minions/Monsters.** If you're low and no ally is nearby, switch Green and S1 a minion wave.
- **CDR is everything.** More CDR = more S1→AA rotations = more damage + healing.
- **Matchup:** Excels in coordinated team play. Struggles when your team has no frontline.
