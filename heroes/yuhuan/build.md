# Yang Yuhuan Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs **CDR, Magic Attack (scales both damage AND healing), and Movement Speed**. Two viable paths: Mid Lane burst-kiting and Jungle semi-tank.

---

## Build A: Mid Lane Burst-Kite

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR rush. More S1→AA rotations. |
| 2 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** or **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | **Mask:** S1 spam perfectly triggers percentage HP burn. **Echo:** instant burst + Movement Speed. |
| 3 | **Spellblade Staff (巫术法杖)** | **Mid Yang Yuhuan's soul item!** "Power Strike" stacks with S1's enhanced AA — one auto-attack chunks 50% of squishy HP. |
| 4 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP boost. Damage AND healing spike simultaneously. |
| 5 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | Penetration vs late-game magic resist. |
| 6 | **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** or **Splendor (辉月)** | **Sunset Flow:** S1 multi-hit stacks penetration instantly. **Splendor:** double invulnerability with S3 — nearly unkillable. |

---

## Build B: High-Rank Jungle Semi-Tank

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Rune Greatsword (符文大剑)** | Blue jungle knife for AP stacking. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity vs jungle invades. |
| 3 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | Core damage source. Tankiness + sustained burn. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | Replaces Spellblade Staff. CDR + Health + Physical Defense. Enhanced AA slow for stickiness. |
| 5 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** or **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Tank stats. Berserker's for stacking damage; Ominous Premonition for anti-Marksman. |
| 6 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** or **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Magic defense or late-game AP for bigger heals. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Movement Speed (S2 positioning), Magic Penetration, smooth AA feel.**

### Setup 1: "Hunt 88-Pen" (Mid Lane Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +88 Magic Penetration, +16% Attack Speed, **+10% Movement Speed**. Smoothest S2 positioning feel.

### Setup 2: "Harmony Sustain" (Jungle / Safe Lane)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +88 Magic Penetration, +6% Attack Speed, +4% Movement Speed, **+450 Max Health**, +52 HP/5s. More forgiving vs burst laners.
