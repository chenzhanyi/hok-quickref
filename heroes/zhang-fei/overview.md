# Zhang Fei（張飛） — Tank / Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Tank / Support (Shield / Anti-Dive)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 禁血狂獸 (Forbidden Blood Beast)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 张飞)

> Zhang Fei is the most versatile, consistent T0 Tank/Support across all metas. He has massive ally shields, hard CC engage, and a game-changing S3 transformation that grants him **40% Max HP as a shield** while becoming a massive disruption tank. He's the gold standard of protective Supports.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Fury (Rage Bar)

Build Fury through: AAs, S1/S2 hits, and shielding allies with S2. S3 (ultimate) can only be cast at full Fury. **Never enter a teamfight without full Fury.** Farm Fury on jungle camps or minions between fights.

### S2: Shield All Nearby Allies

S2 dashes and grants a massive shield to all allies in range. This is your primary Fury-building tool AND your core protection ability. Spam it on cooldown during rotations.

### S1: Knockback (Human Form) / Slam (Beast Form)

Human: knocks back enemies (interrupts Guan Yu charge, Marco S3, Li Xin). Beast: AoE slam + slow.

### S3: Transform + 40% Max HP Shield

Roar forward. **Knocks back enemies to the sides, stuns enemies at the end.** Zhang Fei gains a **40% Max HP shield** and transforms into Beast Form with enhanced abilities. Use to: counter-engage dives, split enemy formations, or push enemies into walls/your team.
