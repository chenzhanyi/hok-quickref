# Zhang Fei Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Never Fight Without Full Fury

S3 requires full Fury. Between fights: **S2 shield allies, S1 jungle camps, AA anything.** Entering a fight without S3 means you're half a Hero. Farm Fury proactively.

### 2. S2 Shield: Spam Constantly

S2 shields all nearby allies AND builds Fury. During rotations, S2 onto teammates on cooldown. Before fights, make sure your carries have shields pre-applied.

### 3. S3: Counter-Engage > Engage

Zhang Fei's S3 is stronger as a **reactive tool**:
- Enemy Assassin dives your ADC? **S3 them away.**
- Enemy team engages? **S3 to split their frontline from backline.**
- Enemy clustered in a narrow jungle path? **S3 to stun multiple.**

S3 → Flash can redirect the roar direction mid-cast for surprise angles.

### 4. S1: Interrupt Everything

Human-form S1 knocks enemies back. Use to interrupt:
- Guan Yu's charge
- Marco Polo's S3
- Li Xin's S1 charge
- Zhong Kui's S3

### 5. Beast Form: Stay in Their Face

After transforming, you're a massive tank with 40% Max HP shield. Don't stand behind your carries — **jump onto the enemy backline** with S2 and harass their ADC/Mage. Soak damage, disrupt positioning.

---

## Signature Play: S3 Counter-Engage

1. Enemy Assassin (Lanling Wang, Jing) dives your ADC
2. **S3 directly at them** — knockback + stun
3. Beast Form: **S2 jump onto the Assassin** (slow)
4. **S1 slam** to continue the CC chain
5. Your ADC kills them for free

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 + Flash mid-cast changes the roar direction.** Surprise enemies with off-angle stuns.
- **S2 can cross terrain.** Use to escape or reposition over walls.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs dive comps. Universal pick — fits any team.
