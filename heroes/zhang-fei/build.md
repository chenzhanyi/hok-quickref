# Zhang Fei Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Max HP + CDR + team utility. Soak damage, shield allies, control fights. Universal tank Support build.

---

## Recommended Full-Tank Support Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎)** or **Guard · Redemption (近卫·救赎)** | **Shadow Aegis:** team AS + CDR. **Guard:** team dual defenses. **Redemption active:** group shield — stacks with S2 for double-shield lifesaving. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity to survive first wave of CC and cast S3. |
| 3 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Reduces enemy AS/MS on-hit — counters ADCs and Physical Assassins. Best cost-efficiency tank item. |
| 4 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic Defense + spell shield. Build earlier vs burst Mages (Gan & Mo, Mai Shiranui). |
| 5 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** | Massive HP for S3's 40% Max HP shield. Continuous regen — never recall. |
| 6 | **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** or **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | **Glacial Storm:** 20% CDR for more S2 shields + S3 uptime. **Sanctuary:** resurrection. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Max Health, CDR, Movement Speed.** Universal for all hard-tank Supports.

### "Thousand-HP Tank" (Universal Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, Cooldown Reduction +0.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** **+1,162 Max Health**, +23 Physical Defense, +6% CDR, +4% MS, +10% AS, +52 HP/5s. Level 1 dominance — you're a tank from minute zero.
