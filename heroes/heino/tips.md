# Heino Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Leave a Safe Remnant Before Fighting

S3 rewinds Heino to his last Time Remnant (S2 cast or fountain exit). **Before every engagement: S2 in a safe bush → that's your escape.** Fight → S3 → instantly back to safety. Master this cycle and you're unkillable.

### 2. S3 Resurrects Turrets

If a turret was destroyed within the last ~5 seconds, casting S3 near its rubble **resurrects it.** This is game-changing for high-ground defense. Never give up a lost tower — Heino brings it back.

### 3. Ranged: Mark Everything, Then Spam

Ranged S1 marks enemies. Once marked, subsequent S1 casts **auto-seek all marked targets.** Mark the enemy frontline AND backline, then spam S1 from safety — every projectile homes in. No aiming needed.

### 4. Melee: Fight in Crowds (More Marks = More Heal)

Melee S1 heals per marked enemy hit. Before going melee: mark enemies with ranged S1. Then S2 toward them → melee S1. 3 marked enemies = massive HP restore per swing.

### 5. Watch Your Remnant Icon

A pointer/icon shows where S3 will take you. Check it constantly. If the remnant is in a dangerous spot (enemy is camping it), don't S3 — walk away instead.

## Signature Combos

### Ranged Poke Cycle: **S1 → S1 → S1 → S1...**

1. S1 marks the enemy frontline (through minions)
2. S1 marks the backline
3. Spam S1 from behind your team — all projectiles auto-track
4. Enemies can't dodge

### Melee Sustain: **S1 (ranged mark) → S2 (engage) → S1 (melee sweep) → S3 (escape)**

1. Ranged S1 marks 2+ enemies
2. S2 toward them (melee form + dual defense)
3. Melee S1 sweep — massive heal
4. S3 back to remnant when low

### Tower Resurrection: **Stand near destroyed tower → S3**

1. Turret destroyed <5 seconds ago
2. Stand near its rubble
3. S3 → turret resurrects

## Advanced Tips

- **Duoliya is Heino's best ally.** At level 15, her S3 refreshes Heino's S3, triggering global Time Freeze.
- **S2 can be recast quickly.** Don't accidentally switch forms mid-fight unless intended.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in coordinated play with Duoliya. Struggles vs teams that camp his remnant.
