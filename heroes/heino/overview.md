# Heino（海諾） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Time Manipulator / Dual-Form Flex)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane / Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 命運之引 (Guide of Fate)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (海诺)

> Heino is an extremely high-ceiling Mage/Fighter with a unique **Time Rewind (S3)** and **ranged/melee dual-form** system. His S3 can teleport him back to a past position AND **resurrect destroyed allied turrets.** In ranged form, he spams homing projectiles. In melee form, he becomes a sustain tank. He's also part of the game-breaking **"Heino + Duoliya" combo** — freezing time globally.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Dual Form: Ranged ↔ Melee (via S2)

- **Ranged (no enemy near S2):** Mage mode. S1 marks enemies — subsequent S1 casts **auto-track all marked targets.**
- **Melee (S2 toward enemy):** Bruiser mode. Gains dual defenses. S1 becomes a 360° sweep that **heals massively per marked enemy hit.**

### S3: Time Rewind + Turret Resurrection

S3 teleports Heino back to his **Time Remnant** (position of last form switch or fountain exit), restoring HP. If cast near a **destroyed turret (within 5 seconds of its death):** the turret **resurrects!**

### Mark System: Tag Then Spam

Ranged S1 marks enemies. Subsequent S1 casts auto-seek ALL marked targets. Mark the entire enemy team once, then spam S1 from safety — every projectile homes in.

### Heino + Duoliya: Time Freeze (Level 15)

When Duoliya (ally) reaches level 15 and ults Heino: it refreshes Heino's S3, triggering **"Time Fracture."** Heino freezes EVERYTHING (allies, enemies, turrets, objectives) — only Heino can move — for several seconds.
