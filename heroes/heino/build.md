# Heino Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Two viable paths. **Semi-Tank Bruiser** (highest winrate — unkillable sustain) and **Pure AP Burst** (ranged poke monster).

---

## Build A: Semi-Tank Bruiser (Recommended)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR rush. Heino desperately needs 40% CDR for S2/S3 cycling. |
| 2 | **Blazing Cape (红莲斗篷)** | HP + burn aura. Melee form brawling synergy. |
| 3 | **Holy Grail (圣杯)** | Infinite Mana + CDR. Heino spams S1 constantly. Mana-hungry. |
| 4 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Anti-ADC. |
| 5 | **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | AP + Max HP + converts AP to dual defenses. Perfect hybrid item. |
| 6 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense. Eternal Night for burst; Magic Cloak for poke. |

---

## Build B: Pure AP Burst (Mid Lane)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | Calm Boots (冷静之靴) | CDR. |
| 2 | Holy Grail (圣杯) | Infinite Mana + CDR. |
| 3 | Echo Staff (回响之杖) | S1 tracking hits trigger AoE explosion. |
| 4 | Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒) | AP spike. Homing S1s chunk 50% HP. |
| 5 | Mask of Agony (痛苦面具) or Sunset Flow (日暮之流) | S1 multi-hit synergy. |
| 6 | Void Staff (虚无法杖) | 45% Penetration. |
