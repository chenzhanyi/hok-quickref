# Dyadia Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S2 Phase 1 Only: Safe Free Gold

You don't HAVE to Phase 2 every time. Apply S2 to the enemy ADC/Support → walk away. Free 5 Gold stolen with zero risk. Repeat on cooldown. Over 5 minutes, you've created a significant economic gap.

### 2. S2 → S1 → Enhanced AA: The Hit-and-Run

1. S2 Phase 1 on enemy
2. S2 Phase 2 → fly to them (root)
3. Enhanced AA (dash backward)
4. S1 on your ADC → Phase 2 → fly back to safety
5. You've rooted the enemy, dealt damage, stolen Gold, and returned to your ADC safely

### 3. S3: Combine Both Marks

Optimal S3: have S1 on an ally AND S2 on an enemy. S3 heals your ally (burst) AND extends the enemy's root + collects Gold. Double value.

### 4. S2 Phase 2: Only When Safe

Flying to an enemy puts you in melee range. Only Phase 2 when: the enemy is isolated, your team is with you, or you have S1 on an ally to fly back immediately after.

### 5. S1 Phase 2: Emergency Save

When your ADC is about to die: S1 Phase 2 → fly to them → massive burst heal + MS boost. This can save them from execute ults (Zhuge Liang S3, Kongming S3).

## Signature Combos

### Gold Steal Harass: **S2 (Phase 1 only) → repeat**

1. S2 enemy ADC. Wait 5 seconds. 5 Gold stolen.
2. Repeat on cooldown.
3. By 5 minutes, your ADC is 300+ Gold ahead.

### Full Teamfight Cycle: **S2 → S1 → S3 → S2 Phase 2 → Enhanced AA → S1 Phase 2**

1. S2 Phase 1 on enemy frontline
2. S1 Phase 1 on your ADC
3. S3 (burst-heals ADC + extends S2 mark)
4. S2 Phase 2 (root enemy + double Gold)
5. Enhanced AA dash back
6. S1 Phase 2 (fly to ADC safely)

## Advanced Tips

- **Gold stolen is REAL.** It directly reduces the enemy's gold. This is the only economic warfare mechanic in the game.
- **S2 can be cast on minions for Phase 2 mobility.** Use S2 on a minion → Phase 2 → free dash.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in extended laning phases. Struggles vs heavy dive that kills before Phase 2 completes.
