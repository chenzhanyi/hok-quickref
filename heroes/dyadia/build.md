# Dyadia Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Healing scales with **Magic Attack.** Needs **CDR** (frequent S1/S2 marks) and **tankiness** (survive S2 Phase 2 dives). CN server consensus: **semi-tank utility AP.**

---

## Recommended Support Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Star Spring (极影·星泉)** | Shadow Aegis aura: team CDR + Mana regen. **Star Spring active:** burst-heal — combo with S1 Phase 2 for massive saves. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. S2 Phase 2 flight can be CC-interrupted. |
| 3 | **Holy Grail (圣杯)** | 15% CDR + infinite Mana. Dyadia spams S1/S2 constantly — Mana-hungry. |
| 4 | **Rising Sun (旭日初光)** or **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** / **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | **Rising Sun:** shields ally on ability hit. **Tank items:** vs dive-heavy teams. |
| 5 | **Frost Breath (凝冰之息)** | S2 Phase 1 becomes a **5-second long-range slow zone.** Enemies can't escape your Phase 2. |
| 6 | **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | AP + Max HP + converts AP to dual defenses. Amplifies healing + tankiness simultaneously. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Max HP, CDR, Movement Speed.** Optional: Magic Penetration for aggressive S2 damage.

### Setup 1: "Thousand-HP Utility" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, Cooldown Reduction +0.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** **+1,162 Max Health**, +23 Physical Defense, +4% MS, **+6% CDR**, +10% AS. Survives aggressive S2 dives.
