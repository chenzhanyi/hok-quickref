# Dyadia（少司緣） — Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Support (Economy / Utility Enchanter)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 聆願之祝 (Listener's Blessing)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (少司缘)

> Dyadia is a uniquely tactical utility Support. Her core mechanic: **two-phase abilities (apply mark → reactivate to fly to target).** S1 marks allies (heals, then fly to them for massive heal). S2 marks enemies (**steals Gold per second**, then fly to them for a root + double Gold payout). She's the only Hero in the game who can directly manipulate the economic gap between teams.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S1: Good Bond (Ally)

- **Phase 1:** Apply "Good Bond" to an ally — continuous healing over time.
- **Phase 2 (reactivate):** Fly to that ally, burst-heal them, and grant +MS to both.

### S2: Evil Bond (Enemy — The Gold Stealer)

- **Phase 1:** Apply "Evil Bond" to an enemy — continuous Magic Damage + **steals 1 Gold per second** (max 5). Free economic advantage!
- **Phase 2 (reactivate):** Fly to that enemy, **root them**, and **doubles the stolen Gold.** If the enemy dies while marked, ALL stolen Gold drops.

### S3: Karmic Melody (Massive AoE)

S3 sends a wave that: burst-heals allies with Good Bond marks, and **extends root duration + instantly collects all stolen Gold** from enemies with Evil Bond marks.

### Enhanced AA After Phase 2

Successfully completing a Phase 2 (flying to ally or enemy) grants an **enhanced AA with extended range + self-dash.** Use to: escape after a risky S2 dive, or gap-close after S1 flight.
