# Dr Bian Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Semi-tank Mage. Must survive long enough to stack 5 marks. Needs **Attack Speed** (AA mark stacking), **Magic Penetration**, and **tanky stats**. CN server consensus: **Semi-tank AP bruiser.**

---

## Recommended Semi-Tank Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity to survive CC and cast S2/S3. *Early alternative: Calm Boots for CDR, swap later.* |
| 2 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | **Core damage item!** Poison's 5-second duration endlessly triggers Mask's %HP burn. Doubles poison tick damage. |
| 3 | **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | AP + Max HP. Converts AP to dual defenses — Dr Bian becomes a genuine Mage-Tank. |
| 4 | **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** | Poison's multi-tick damage **stacks 10 layers in under 1 second** — near true-damage without Void Staff. |
| 5 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP boost. Amplifies both poison damage AND ally healing (S2/S3 scale with AP). |
| 6 | **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** or **Ardent Dominator (炽热支配者)** / **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** | **Glacial Storm:** 20% CDR + Physical Defense vs Assassins. **Ardent Dominator:** auto-cleanse shield at low HP. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Attack Speed (AA mark stacking), Magic Penetration, Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Hunt 88-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, **+16% Attack Speed**, **+10% MS**. Smoothest AA weaving for fast 5-stack.

### Setup 2: "Omen-Harmony Hybrid" (Safer Laning)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Omen (凶兆) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Attack Speed +0.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +64 Magic Penetration, **+12% Attack Speed**, **+450 Max Health**, +4% MS. Better sustain vs burst laners.
