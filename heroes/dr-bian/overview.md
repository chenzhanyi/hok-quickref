## Core Mechanics

### Mark Stacks: 5 Is Everything

Damage and healing scale with mark count. **5 stacks is dramatically stronger than 4 stacks.** In teamfights: stack marks to 5 on priority targets BEFORE casting S3.

### S3: Detonate + Refresh

S3 detonates all marks nearby, dealing burst damage/healing based on stack count. **S3 also refreshes the duration of all marks.** Use S3 as: execute on 5-stacked enemies, massive team heal, or mark refresher mid-fight.

### S2: Spam Constantly

S2 can be cast without a target. **Spam S2 while rotating** вҖ” this keeps 5 white marks on yourself and nearby allies at all times. You enter every fight pre-buffed with healing already ticking.

### S1: Zone Control + Stack

S1's bottles can be stored (max 3). Don't dump all 3 in one spot вҖ” spread them in a line to block retreat paths, zone enemies, or fast-clear waves.
