# Dr Bian Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Stack to 5 Before S3

S3's burst scales with mark count. 5 stacks = massive damage/healing. 4 stacks = underwhelming. **Never S3 at <5 stacks unless it's an emergency.** Weave AAs between abilities to reach 5 faster.

### 2. Spam S2 While Rotating

S2 works without a target. Walking between lanes or through jungle? **Spam S2 constantly.** This keeps 5 healing marks on yourself AND nearby allies. You enter every fight with healing already ticking.

### 3. S1: Zone, Don't Dump

S1 bottles stack up to 3. Don't throw all 3 in one spot. Place them **in a line** across enemy retreat paths or choke points. Enemies must walk through poison or go around — either way, you win.

### 4. S3 Double Duty

S3 detonates marks AND refreshes their duration. Two uses:
- **Burst:** 5-stacked enemies → S3 → execute
- **Sustain:** 5-stacked allies → S3 → massive team heal + mark refresh

### 5. AA Between Everything

Dr Bian's AAs apply marks. The difference between 4 and 5 stacks is enormous. **Always weave AAs:** S1 → AA → S2 → AA. This guarantees 4+ stacks on contact.

---

## Signature Combos

### Lane Trade: **S1 → AA → S2 → AA**

1. S1 bottle (slow + 1 mark)
2. AA (2 marks)
3. S2 (3 marks)
4. AA (4-5 marks)
5. Most laners can't out-trade a 5-stack Dr Bian

### Teamfight: **S1 + S2 (AoE) → AA weave → S3 (detonate)**

1. S1 bottles + S2 into enemy cluster
2. AA to push key targets to 5 marks
3. S3 detonate — AoE burst + ally heal + mark refresh

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Attack Speed is mandatory.** AA is your fastest mark stacker. Without AS, you're too slow.
- **Semi-tank build only.** Pure AP Dr Bian dies before stacking to 5. Prophet's Hour + tank items keep you alive.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in extended fights. Struggles vs burst that kills before you stack.
