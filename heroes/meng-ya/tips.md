# Meng Ya Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Master the Fury Gauge

Meng Ya's energy bar (Fury) is everything:
- **Full Fury = Frenzy State:** S1 bullets split + penetrate, S2 enhanced, **excess Fury converts to HP recovery**
- **Low Fury = low damage.** Always pre-stack Fury on Minions or Jungle Monsters before trading
- **Attack Speed → CDR:** Every 5% Attack Speed = 1% CDR. This is how Meng Ya naturally caps cooldowns

### 2. S1: Free Movement While Firing

S1 (Heavy Cannon Barrage) slows Meng Ya by 10%, but he can **freely move and adjust aim direction**. Press S1 again to **cancel the barrage at any time** — essential for flexible retreats or switching to auto-attacks.

### 3. S2: Ricochet Off Walls

S2 fires a shell that **splits into ricochet projectiles upon hitting a wall**. When an enemy is low HP hiding behind a tower or in a bush corner, don't aim at them — **aim at the wall behind them** to blanket the area with ricochet damage.

### 4. Ultimate: Global Cross-Map Support

S3's radar range expands the longer you channel. Once allies engage enemies within radar, Meng Ya **auto-fires missiles across the map**. Also grants +50% Fury generation during ultimate.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S1 first, then S2. Take ultimate whenever available.** S1 is Meng Ya's primary damage, wave-clear, and Fury-stacking tool.

## Signature Combos

### Teamfight Barrage: **S3 (channel) → S2 → S1 → AA**

1. Channel S3 from long range before the fight starts
2. S2 into the enemy cluster to slow + stack Fury to max
3. S1 — walk and spray while weaving enhanced auto-attacks
4. Let S3 auto-missiles rain down throughout

### Tower Dive Execute: **S2 (wall ricochet) → S1 → S3**

1. S2 ricochet off the tower wall to chip the defending enemy
2. Enter tower with full Fury
3. S1 spray with high HP recovery + burst
4. S3 for the finishing missiles
5. Walk out healthy

## Advanced Tips

- **Pre-fight Fury stacking:** Before any fight, auto Minions or Jungle Monsters to build Fury to at least 50%. Never engage from 0 Fury.
- **S1 cancel micro:** Get used to tapping S1 again to cancel — holding it too long makes you an easy target.
- **Wall geometry matters:** Learn wall angles for S2 ricochets. The difference between a direct hit and a wall-bounce kill is massive.
- **Build semi-tank:** Berserker's Armor + Pure Sky makes Meng Ya surprisingly durable while spraying.
- **Matchup:** Meng Ya bullies short-range laners. He struggles against long-range poke mages and hyper-mobile assassins who can flank his S1 spray angle.
