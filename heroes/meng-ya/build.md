# Meng Ya Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Meng Ya's ability damage scales purely with Physical Attack and Critical Rate (crits double Fury generation). He needs to stand and spray, so **semi-tank crit build** with one high-value defensive item is optimal.

---

## Recommended Burst-Crit Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early Attack Speed converts to CDR. *Late game: swap to Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴) — Meng Ya is vulnerable to CC during S1 spray.* |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core bruiser-style item. Attack, Health, CDR, and the Cripple slow procs perfectly with S1 spray for stickiness. |
| 3 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | **Meng Ya's #1 power-spike item!** Every S1 bullet can crit. Infinity Edge makes the spray devastating. |
| 4 | **Shadow Blade (影刃)** | Critical Rate + Movement Speed. The on-crit Attack Speed boost converts directly to CDR — S1 and S2 come back almost instantly. |
| 5 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | **Meng Ya's soul defensive item.** He tanks hits while spraying; Berserker's Armor provides up to +10% Movement Speed and +10% total damage output when stacked. Perfect synergy with his HP recovery mechanic. |
| 6 | **Dawn (破晓)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Dawn:** max penetration vs tanky teams. **Pure Sky:** active DR for survival vs fed Assassins — lets Meng Ya survive dives and keep spraying. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Meng Ya needs **Physical Penetration, Critical Rate (fast Fury stacking + S1 crit heal), and Movement Speed**. He does NOT need Lifesteal runes — full Fury already provides high HP recovery.

### Setup 1: CN Server "Calamity 100-Pen Speed" (Top Player Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×10 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +16% Critical Rate, +16 Physical Attack, +64 Physical Penetration, **+10% Movement Speed**.

### Setup 2: "Crit Damage + AS Threshold" (Smoother Early Game)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×3 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×7 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×3 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +13.3% Critical Rate, +10.8% Critical Damage, +64 Physical Penetration, +10% Movement Speed, +3% Attack Speed (converts to early CDR).

## Quick In-Game Setup

Meng Ya does NOT use standard Marksman Lifesteal runes — they waste stats. The safest method:

1. Open **Preparation (备战)** → **Change Build (更换)**
2. Select **Recommended / Pro Player (推荐/大神配置)**
3. Copy the **CN #1** or current-season pro setup
4. The game auto-syncs the optimal loadout
