# Meng Ya（蒙犽） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Ability-Based Artillery)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 烈炮小子 (Fierce Cannon Youth)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 蒙犽)

> Meng Ya is a uniquely designed artillery Marksman. He doesn't rely on Attack Speed — instead, he uses abilities and his **Fury Gauge (炽怒值)** to dominate. At full Fury, his S1 and S2 gain enhanced properties and excess Fury converts to HP recovery. He also has a global-range ultimate for cross-map support. Unlike most Marksmen, he scales extremely well with semi-tank builds.

---

## Passive & Core Mechanics

### Fury Gauge (Energy Bar)

At **full Fury**, Meng Ya enters **Frenzy State**:
- S1 bullets split and penetrate
- S2 gains enhanced effects
- **Excess Fury converts to HP recovery**

Without Fury, Meng Ya's damage is low. **Always pre-stack Fury on Minions or Jungle Monsters before fighting.**

### Attack Speed → CDR Conversion

Every **5% Attack Speed = 1% Cooldown Reduction**. Meng Ya naturally caps CDR through Attack Speed items — no need for dedicated CDR purchases.

### Ultimate: Global Bombardment (S3)

Channel S3 to expand radar range. Whenever allies fight within radar, Meng Ya auto-fires missiles across the map. +50% Fury generation during ultimate.
