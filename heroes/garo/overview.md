# Garo（伽羅） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Long-Range Artillery ADC)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 破魔之箭 (Arrow of Demon-Breaking)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 伽罗)

> Garo has the longest auto-attack range and the strongest late-game sustained damage among Marksmen. Her signature strength is **critical-hit slow** (crits apply a 30%~50% slow) combined with extreme range. She's notoriously immobile though — positioning is everything. Survive to late game and you're the enemy tank's nightmare.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S1: Long Bow Toggle (Critical Skill)

- **Long Bow ON:** +150 Range. Auto-attacks deal continuous damage. **Crits apply 30%~50% slow.** Costs 50 Mana per shot. Attack Speed reduced.
- **Short Bow OFF:** Normal range + speed. **Hitting enemies restores Mana.**
- **Mana management:** Clear waves with Short Bow to regen. Switch to Long Bow to poke, engage, or teamfight.

### S3 Ultimate: Open → Close Instantly

S3 creates a circle that boosts ally Critical Rate. But the REAL use: **Open → immediately close.** Closing the circle grants allies +3 seconds of **Movement Speed + Physical Attack burst**. Use this for chase or disengage, not for standing in the circle.

### S2: Wave Clear + Silence

S2 fires an arrow that explodes after a brief delay. Two damage ticks with a **0.5s Silence** in between. Perfect for: fast wave clear, interrupting channeled abilities (Mozi, Dun Shan).
