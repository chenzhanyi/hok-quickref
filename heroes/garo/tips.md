# Garo Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Long Bow / Short Bow Management

- **Farming / safe state:** Short Bow OFF — restores Mana per hit
- **Poking / teamfighting:** Long Bow ON — +150 range + crit slow
- Never run out of Mana with Long Bow on — you'll be useless. Switch back to Short Bow to regen between fights.

### 2. S3: Open → Close Immediately

S3's REAL value is NOT standing in the circle. It's the burst when you close it:
- **Open S3 → Close S3** = all nearby allies gain +3s Movement Speed + Physical Attack
- Use for chase, disengage, or repositioning
- Don't stand still in your circle — you'll die

### 3. S2 for Wave Clear + Silence

S2's two damage ticks with a 0.5s Silence between them. Use to:
- Fast wave clear (line through entire minion wave)
- Interrupt Mozi, Dun Shan, Zhong Kui ultimates
- Bush-check from long range

### 4. Positioning > Everything

Garo has zero mobility. You are the #1 target for enemy Assassins. Always:
- Stay behind your tank/support
- Never face-check bushes
- If you don't die, the enemy tanks die instead

## Signature Combo

### Max-Range Kite: **S2 → S1 (ON) → AA×n → S3 (open→close) → AA×n**

1. S2 from range (slow + silence)
2. S1 ON → auto-attack with bonus range
3. Crits apply 30%~50% slow — enemy can't move
4. If they try to engage: S3 open→close for MS burst
5. Continue kiting with MS advantage

## Advanced Tips

- **Level 1-3 is Garo's weakest phase.** Farm safely, don't trade.
- **Garo hard-counters Chang'E.** Garo's passive deals double crit damage to shielded targets — Chang'E's Mana bar evaporates.
- **Duo queue recommended.** Pair with Zhang Fei, Taiyi Zhenren, or Ming Shiyin for bodyguard protection. Your winrate doubles.
- **Matchup:** Destroys immobile tanks and short-range Marksmen. Struggles vs hyper-mobile Assassins (Lanling Wang, Jing, Sima Yi).
