# Garo Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Garo relies entirely on **Critical Rate** to trigger S1's slow. Needs high Attack Speed to offset Long Bow's AS penalty. Build: **Attack Speed + Crit**.

---

## Recommended Crit-Speed Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early Attack Speed to offset Long Bow penalty. |
| 2 | **Shadow Blade (影刃)** | **Garo's soul item!** Critical Rate + Attack Speed + Movement Speed. On-crit AS/MS boost creates a perfect feedback loop with Garo's crit slow — enemies literally can't move. |
| 3 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | Crit standard. Maxes out Critical Damage. After this, Garo's slow becomes genuinely threatening. |
| 4 | **Dawn (破晓)** | Marksman core. Physical Penetration, Attack Speed, bonus AA damage. Garo's "online" moment — tanks melt in seconds. |
| 5 | **Sun Bow (逐日之弓)** | **Range amplifier.** Active: +125 Range + 20% MS for 5s. Stacked with S1 Long Bow = the longest auto-attack range possible. Hit enemies from outside their screen. *Late: upgrade to Sun Abyss (迅疾·日渊).* |
| 6 | **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Survival flex. Alternate resurrection with Fate's Edge. Magic Cloak vs heavy magic poke. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Critical Rate (early slow procs), Physical Lifesteal (skip lifesteal item), Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "3 Peerless + 16% Lifesteal" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×3 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×10 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +11.4% Critical Rate, +64 Physical Penetration, +9 Physical Attack, +10.8% Critical Damage, **+16% Physical Lifesteal**. Skip lifesteal item entirely — go straight to Dawn + Sun Bow.

### Setup 2: "Movement Speed Kiting" (Safer Positioning)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×3 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×5 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×5 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +11.4% Critical Rate, +64 Physical Penetration, +5% Attack Speed, **+5% Movement Speed**, +8% Physical Lifesteal. Smoother early positioning. May need a small Lifesteal component mid-game.
