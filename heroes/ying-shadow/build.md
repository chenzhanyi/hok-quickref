# Ying (Shadow) Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Semi-tank bruiser. Fury gives built-in Penetration + AS — she doesn't need full damage. **The longer she survives, the more damage she deals.** CN server consensus: **semi-tank sustain.**

---

## Recommended Jungle / Clash Lane Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** or **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Jungle: HP stacking + burn. Clash: straight to Shadow Axe. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. S3 prevents death, but CC stops AAs — no AAs = no Fury = no damage. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core damage item. CDR + flat Penetration + Health. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | S1/S2 → AA perfectly triggers AoE slow. CDR + Defense. Maximum stickiness. |
| 5 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | **Ying's signature item!** Taking hits grants MS + damage output. Stacks with Fury — she gets faster AND stronger as fights progress. |
| 6 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Siege Breaker:** execute burst during S3 chain-kills. **Pure Sky:** active DR — stacks with S3 for near-immortality. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Physical Penetration, Movement Speed, mild Lifesteal.**

### Setup 1: "Stealth 100-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% MS. Max S1 ranged poke damage.
