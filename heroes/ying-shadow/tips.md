# Ying (Shadow) Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S1 Ranged First: Stack Fury from Safety

Before committing: S1 → ranged AA poke from the edge. Build Fury stacks (+AS + Penetration) safely. Once Fury is high AND enemy is softened: S2 in for melee flurry.

### 2. S3: Cast at ~30% HP, Not 5%

Don't save S3 for near-death — burst can one-shot you before you press it. Cast S3 at **~30% HP** or when you **predict** the enemy is about to unload burst/CC. The HP restore + DR + Death Immunity will outlast their cooldowns.

### 3. S3 Kill/Assist = Refresh

Scoring a kill or assist during S3 **refreshes the duration.** In teamfights: S3 → kill one target → duration refreshed → kill the next → refreshed → clean up the entire team. Chain-reset immortality.

### 4. AA Minions/Jungle to Reduce Cooldowns

AAs reduce ability cooldowns. Between fights: AA jungle camps or minion waves to bring S3 back faster. Never stand idle.

### 5. S2 Engage Only When Fury Is High

S2 switches to melee flurry (max DPS). But at 0 Fury, melee is risky. Always pre-stack Fury with S1 ranged AAs before S2 diving in.

## Signature Combos

### Standard: **S1 → AA×4 → S2 → AA → S3 → AA**

1. S1 (ranged mode + MS)
2. AA ×4 from range (stack Fury + slow enemy)
3. S2 dash in (switch to melee flurry)
4. AA melee burst
5. S3 when focused (Death Immunity + chain kills)

### Bush Ambush: **S2 → AA → S1 → AA → S3**

1. S2 from bush (instant melee flurry)
2. AA burst
3. S1 (switch to ranged if they Flash — slow + chase)
4. AA ranged
5. S3 if needed

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3's revive works even if you take fatal damage at the last frame.** Trust the mechanic — keep fighting.
- **Fury stacks give BOTH AS and Penetration.** Late-game high-Fury Ying shreds tanks like paper.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in extended fights. Struggles vs burst that one-shots before S3 can activate.
