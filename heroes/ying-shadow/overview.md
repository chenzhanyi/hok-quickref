# Ying (Shadow)（影） — Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin (Berserker / Immortal Duelist)
- **Lane:** Jungle / Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 黯羽東君 (Dark Feather Lord)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (影)

> Ying (Shadow) is a uniquely designed melee Berserker/Assassin. Her core: **ranged/melee AA mode switching**, a **Fury stacking** system that grants AS + Penetration the more she fights, and her S3 — **Death Immunity (Unyielding).** While S3 is active, she CANNOT die. And if she scores a kill/assist during S3, the duration refreshes. She revives with HP even if S3 expires while taking fatal damage.

---

## Core Mechanics

### AA Mode Switching: Ranged Poke ↔ Melee Flurry

- **S1 active:** AAs become **ranged feather throws** with +MS. Use to poke, kite, and stack Fury from safety.
- **S2/Melee:** AAs become **high-speed dual-blade flurries.** Maximum DPS for all-in fights.

### Fury Stacking: Hit and Get Hit

Taking damage or hitting enemies builds Fury. Higher Fury = **more Attack Speed + Physical Penetration.** Ying gets stronger as fights go on — never retreat at high Fury.

### S3: Death Immunity (The Berserker's Soul)

S3 grants: HP restore, +MS, and **CANNOT DIE** for the duration. If you score a kill/assist during S3: **duration refreshes.** If S3 expires while you're taking fatal damage: you **revive with a portion of HP.** Don't save S3 for 10% HP — cast it at ~30% or when you predict burst incoming.
