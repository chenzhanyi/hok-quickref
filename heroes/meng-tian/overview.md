### Defensive Stance (Ability 2)

Entering Defensive Stance grants Meng Tian **20% Damage Reduction** from the front and **Unstoppable** during charged thrusts. Use this to face-tank burst abilities, CC, and tower shots head-on.

### Soldier's Momentum (Passive)

Charging or taking damage builds **Momentum**. A full-Momentum charged thrust deals **bonus Physical Damage + healing**.
