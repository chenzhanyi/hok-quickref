# Meng Tian Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Two distinct paths — **Semi-Tank Fortress** (high-rank/competitive, highest winrate) and **Full Damage One-Shot** (squishy nightmare, timing-dependent).

---

## Build 1: Semi-Tank Fortress (Recommended)

Balances defense and damage. With ultimate's DR stacking, Meng Tian becomes nearly unkillable while still threatening backline.

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Boots (影忍之足)** or **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** | Clash Lane: Shadow Boots for physical lane dominance. Jungle: Giant's Grip for Health stacking. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core damage item: Physical Penetration, Health, and essential CDR. |
| 3 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | Charged thrusts perfectly proc AoE slow + bonus damage. Maximum stickiness. |
| 4 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Taking hits while charging stacks damage and Movement Speed — perfect synergy with Meng Tian's playstyle. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense. Eternal Night vs burst; Magic Cloak vs sustained poke. |
| 6 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | Active DR stacks with S2 frontal DR + Square Formation for near-immunity to all frontal damage. |

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|

| 4 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | Critical damage core. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Meng Tian needs **Physical Penetration** and **Max Health** for early lane survival.

### Setup 1: Semi-Tank 100-Pen (Most Recommended)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

## Quick In-Game Setup

Meng Tian's formation-switching and engage timing have a steep learning curve. The easiest setup:

1. Open **Preparation (备战)** → **Change Build (更换)**
2. Select **Recommended / Pro Player (推荐/大神配置)**
3. Copy the **CN #1** or current-season pro setup
