# Meng Tian Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Defensive Stance Mastery (S2)

Entering Defensive Stance grants **20% front-facing Damage Reduction**. During charged thrusts, Meng Tian is **Unstoppable**. Face-tank Li Yuanfang's ultimate, Daji's CC, or any burst damage head-on.

- While in Defensive Stance, tap S1 to **adjust thrust direction** and micro-dash forward — critical for kiting and chasing.

### 2. Charge-Flash (Charged Thrust + Flash)

While charging a thrust in Defensive Stance, **release the auto-attack and immediately Flash**. This extends the thrust range massively — an unexpected "turn-back spear" that deletes backline squishies.

### 3. Ultimate Formation Toggle (S3)

After summoning soldiers, press S3 again to toggle:

- **Square Formation:** Soldiers surround you — maximum frontal defense. Best for tower dives and absorbing burst.
- **Wedge Formation:** Soldiers line up — maximum damage coverage. Best in narrow terrain (Dragon pit, jungle fights).

### 4. Reverse S1 Knockback

After summoning soldiers, S1's knockup area expands. Use S2 → Flash behind enemy → S1 to knock them backward into your tower or team — similar to Dharma or Su Lie plays.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S1 first, then S2. Take ultimate whenever available.** S1 is your primary engage, poke, and micro-adjustment tool in Defensive Stance.

## Signature Combos

### Standard Engage / One-Shot: **S3 → S1 → S2 → AA**

1. S3 summon soldiers
2. S1 dash forward + knockup
3. S2 cancel S1 animation + enter Defensive Stance
4. AA — full-Momentum charged thrust for massive burst

### Infinite Push / Kite: **S2 → AA → S1 → AA → S1 → AA**

In Defensive Stance, each charged AA massively reduces S1 cooldown. Cycle AA + S1 (micro-adjust) + AA for continuous Unstoppable thrusts in teamfights.

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Front-facing is everything:** Meng Tian's DR only applies from the front. Never show your back in a fight.
- **Tower diving:** Square Formation + Defensive Stance DR makes you nearly immune to tower shots.
- **Flash engages:** Charge-Flash is your most unexpected kill tool — practice the timing.
- **Matchup:** Dominates immobile mages and marksmen. Struggles against hyper-mobile assassins who can flank behind him.
