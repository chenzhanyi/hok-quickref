# Yao Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs high **CDR** for frequent S3 Star Bleed erasure, **Physical Penetration** for burst, and **semi-tank durability** to survive long enough to rewind. CN server consensus: **semi-tank jungle bruiser**.

---

## Recommended Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Physical Attack, CDR, Physical Penetration. Core Assassin jungle item. |
| 2 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | Early CDR to cap. *Late game: swap to Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴) once CDR overflows.* |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core squishy-killer: 15% CDR, Health, flat Penetration. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** or **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | **Frost Grip:** Enhanced AA dash perfectly triggers AoE slow + on-hit damage. **Berserker's Armor:** stacking MS and damage for extended kiting. |
| 5 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | Yao has multiple burst abilities. Siege Breaker massively amplifies late-game cleanup — Enhanced S1 + Enhanced AA one-shots squishies. |
| 6 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | **Eternal Night:** Synergizes perfectly with Yao's Star Bleed — taking burst → heal back → S3 erases bleed → "more HP than before." |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

No extreme Attack Speed or Critical needed. **High initial Physical Penetration (100-Pen), Movement Speed, and a touch of Lifesteal** are standard.

### Setup 1: CN Server "Stealth 100-Pen" (Classic Burst)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% Movement Speed.

### Setup 2: "Dream Crit + Sustain" (Newcomer Friendly)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×9 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×1 | Critical Rate +1.6% ("dream crit" on enhanced AA) |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×7 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** ~96 Physical Penetration, +4.8% Physical Lifesteal, +1.6% Critical Rate, high Movement Speed. The Lifesteal solves early jungle sustain issues.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Yao's ability → auto-attack weaving rhythm takes practice. The easiest setup:

1. Open **Preparation (备战)** → **Change Build (更换)**
2. Select **Recommended / Pro Player (推荐/大神配置)**
3. Copy the **CN #1** or current-season pro Yao setup
