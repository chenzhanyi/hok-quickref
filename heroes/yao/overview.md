# Yao（曜） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Skirmisher / Outplay)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 星辰之子 (Child of the Stars)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 曜)

> Yao is a flashy, high-mobility skirmisher Fighter/Assassin. His core mechanic is **Star Power** (max 3 stacks; each ability grants 1 stack; at 3 stacks, his next ability is **enhanced** and his next basic attack becomes a **charging dash**). His other signature mechanic is **Star Bleed** — 40% of damage taken becomes a 2-second bleed; casting ultimate **erases the bleed entirely** and rewinds his position.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Star Power (Energy Bar)

Each ability grants 1 stack of Star Power (max 3). At 3 stacks:
- Next ability becomes **Enhanced** (stronger version)
- Next basic attack becomes a **charging dash** (gap-close through thin walls)

### Ultimate: Star Bleed Erasure (Core Tankiness)

When Yao takes damage, **40% becomes a grey "Star Bleed" on his HP bar** over 2 seconds. Casting S3 **rewinds Yao to his position 2 seconds ago** and **completely erases the Star Bleed damage**. This is why semi-tank Yao can dive in and out of teamfights seemingly immortal.

### Golden Arrow Indicator

A **golden arrow** at Yao's feet always points to where S3 will rewind him. Constantly check this during fights to avoid rewinding into the enemy team.
