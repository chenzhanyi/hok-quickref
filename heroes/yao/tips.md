# Yao Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Star Bleed Erasure (Immortality Mechanic)

Yao converts **40% of damage taken into a 2-second grey bleed (Star Bleed)** on his HP bar. Casting S3 **rewinds to his position 2 seconds ago** and **completely erases the bleed**. Master this timing — it's why semi-tank Yao survives fights other Fighters can't.

### 2. Golden Arrow Awareness

A **golden arrow indicator** at Yao's feet always shows where S3 will rewind to. Check it constantly during fights. Rewinding into the enemy team = death; rewinding to safety = immortality.

### 3. Pre-Stack Star Power

Yao can stack Star Power by casting abilities into empty air. **Always keep 2 stacks** before ganking or teamfighting. 2-stack Yao has his strongest burst — one more ability triggers Enhanced immediately.

### 4. Enhanced Auto-Attack Wall Hop

The enhanced basic attack after using an Enhanced ability is a **charging dash that crosses thin walls**. Use it for creative gank angles and escapes.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S1 first, then S2. Take ultimate whenever available.** S1 is Yao's primary damage source — maxing it significantly boosts burst.

## Signature Combos (from 2 Stacks)

### Long-Range Backline Delete: **S2 → S2 → AA → S1 → S3 → S3 → AA**

1. Start at 2 stacks. Double S2 (two curved dashes) close distance to enemy backline
2. Enhanced AA to stick
3. S1 for full Physical burst
4. Double S3 to rewind back to safety, erasing bleed
5. Enhanced AA to finish

### Bush Ambush Burst: **S1 → S1 → AA → S2 → S3 → AA**

1. 2 stacks, waiting in bush
2. Enemy enters → double S1 (two whirlwind slashes) + Enhanced AA
3. Instant deletion of squishy targets
4. S2 to chase, S3 to rewind out

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 is both offensive and defensive:** Don't always save it for escape. S3 also deals damage at the rewind destination — use it to re-engage when enemies chase your rewind path.
- **Watch the clock:** S3 rewinds exactly 2 seconds. Time your engagements so the rewind point is safe.
- **CDR is mandatory:** Without max CDR, Star Bleed expires before S3 comes back up — and you die.
- **Matchup:** Excels against burst-heavy teams (Star Bleed negates their damage). Struggles against sustained DPS that outlasts his S3 window.
