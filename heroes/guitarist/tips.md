# Guitarist Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S3 + Flash: S3 FIRST, Then Flash

Never Flash → S3. Always: **S3 (MS boost + music) → Flash into backline.** S3's damage is already ticking when you land. The S3+Flash combo is Guitarist's entire identity.

### 2. Don't Initiate Blindly

Yes, you have DR during S3. But you're still a Mage. If you're the first one in, Donghuang or Liang will Suppress you, or burst will delete you before DR stacks. **Wait for your tank to absorb first wave of CC, then S3+Flash from the side.**

### 3. S3 + Splendor (Stasis): Damage While Invincible

S3 → S3 damage is ticking → press **Splendor (Stasis).** You're golden and invincible, but **S3's music still deals damage.** The enemy wastes everything on your golden form. This is the ultimate mind-game.

### 4. S1 Bounce: Poke Through Minions

Enemy hiding behind their wave? S1 the front minion. The bounce hits them. Free, safe, undodgeable poke.

### 5. Enhanced AA: Always Weave It

Every 4th hit is a penetrating energy chord. High AP scaling, can crit. Always: S3 → AA → S2 → AA → S1 → AA. Don't leave the enhanced AA unused.

## Signature Combo

### Nuclear Dive: **S3 → Flash → S2 → S1 → AA**

1. **S3** (music starts, MS boost)
2. **Flash** into enemy backline (S3 already ticking)
3. **S2** point-blank (massive slow + heal)
4. **S1** (bounces through clustered enemies)
5. **Enhanced AA** to finish

### Lane Poke: **S1 → S2 → AA**

1. S1 the minion wave (bounces to enemy)
2. S2 if they approach (heal + slow)
3. Enhanced AA

## Advanced Tips

- **S3's DR stacks per enemy.** 1v1 = nearly no DR. 1v5 = massive DR. Always dive when enemies are grouped.
- **Flash is mandatory.** No Flash = half a Hero. Late game: if Flash is on CD, temporarily buy Wolf Rush (奔狼徽章) for the MS active.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs grouped comps. Struggles vs teams that spread out and Suppression.
