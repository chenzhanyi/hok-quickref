# Guitarist（高漸離） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Melee Dive / AoE Teamfight Monster)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 叛逆吟遊 (Rebellious Minstrel)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 高渐离)

> Guitarist (Gao Jianli in CN) is a devastating AoE melee-dive Mage. He doesn't poke from range like Gan & Mo or Xiao Qiao — his romantic core is **"S3 + Flash into 5 enemies and drop a nuclear rock concert on their faces."** The more enemies around him during S3, the tankier he becomes (up to 15% DR per enemy).

---

## Core Mechanics

### S3: More Enemies = More Tanky

S3 grants massive MS + AoE damage. **Each nearby enemy Hero grants bonus Damage Reduction (up to 15% per enemy).** In a 1v1, he's squishy. S3+Flash into 5 enemies? He's a full tank.

### Enhanced AA: Every 4th Hit

Every 4th AA/cast becomes a penetrating energy chord with massive AP scaling. This AA CAN crit. Always weave it between abilities — it's a significant damage source.

### S1: Bounce Off Minions

S1 bounces between enemies. When the enemy laner hides behind minions: S1 the front minion → it bounces to them. Free, safe poke.

### S3 + Flash (The ONLY Engage)

**Never Flash THEN S3.** Always **S3 first (MS boost + music starts) → Flash into the backline.** The S3 damage is already ticking when you land. Follow with S2 (slow + heal) → S1 → Enhanced AA.
