# Guitarist Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Melee-dive Mage. Needs **Lifesteal** (survive in crowds), **Magic Penetration**, and **semi-tank** items. Full AP gets deleted on engage. CN server consensus: **Lifesteal semi-tank.**

---

## Recommended Semi-Tank Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Mandatory. Getting CC'd during S3 = death. Tenacity keeps the music playing. |
| 2 | **Devourer's Tome (噬神之书)** | **Guitarist's soul item!** 25% Magic Lifesteal. S3 in a crowd = HP bar dances from 10% to 100% instantly. |
| 3 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** or **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | **Mask:** S3's multi-tick endlessly triggers %HP burn for sustained fights. **Echo:** instant burst for one-shot dives. |
| 4 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP spike. S3's damage becomes truly nuclear. |
| 5 | **Cocoon Breaker (破茧之衣)** | **2026 rework recommendation!** AP + Max HP. Passive grants Magic Penetration AND **close-range DR.** Stacks with S3's DR — you become a genuine Mage-Tank. |
| 6 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** or **Splendor (辉月)** | **Void Staff:** vs Magic Cloak. **Splendor:** Stasis during S3 — you're invincible while S3 still deals damage. |
