# Geya Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** AAs have reduced damage per hit — **on-hit items are weaker.** Prioritize **Crit + Physical Penetration + Attack.** CN server consensus: Crit burst.

---

## Recommended Crit Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early AS for smooth tap-AA rhythm. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | CDR + flat Penetration. S1/S2/S3 all benefit. Strong early spike. |
| 3 | **Shadow Blade (影刃)** | On-crit AS + MS. Combined with tap-AA speed boosts, Geya becomes untouchable. |
| 4 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | Crit damage spike. Enhanced lances now devastate. |
| 5 | **Dawn (破晓)** | 40% Penetration. Marksman capstone. |
| 6 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Defense. **Berserker's:** stacking MS + damage — more drift speed. **Magic Cloak:** vs heavy magic. |

Core: **Critical Rate, Physical Penetration, Attack Speed + Lifesteal.**

| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×3 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |

**Total:** +11.2% Crit Rate, +82 Physical Penetration, +5% MS/AS, **+8% Lifesteal**.
