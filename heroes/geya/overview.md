# Geya（戈婭） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Mobility Kiter / Drift ADC)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 沙海飛舟 (Sand Ship)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (戈娅)

> Geya is a revolutionary Marksman who rides a hover-sand-skimmer. She has the game's unique **"move-and-shoot with drift inertia"** mechanic. Her AAs can be held (continuous fire, slower) or tapped (burst fire with speed boost). Mastering tap-fire rhythm is the key to her unmatched kiting. Her S3 creates a massive **black hole** that pulls enemies inward.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Tap vs Hold (The Core Skill)

- **Hold AA:** Continuous fire but **slower movement.** Only use when safe (free-farming, behind frontline).
- **Tap AA:** **Burst fire + speed boost.** The CN meta: constantly tap AA for the speed boost while kiting. Never hold AA in fights.

### Enhanced AA (6th Shot)

Every 6th AA becomes a **piercing lance** with bonus range + massive speed boost. **Pre-stack AAs on air** before engaging — walk in with the enhanced lance ready.

### S2: Reverse Dash (Important!)

S2 dashes **backward** while firing forward. To go forward: pull joystick backward + S2. This is Geya's only mobility — master the reverse input.

### S3: Black Hole (Pull + Debuff)

S3 creates a massive sand vortex that **pulls enemies toward the center**, reduces their MS, and **reduces their Physical Attack.** Use to: trap backline, zone objectives, clear waves, block jungle paths.
