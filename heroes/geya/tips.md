# Geya Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Tap AA, Don't Hold (Most Important)

Holding AA = continuous fire but slower movement. **Tapping AA = burst fire + speed boost.** In fights: tap, tap, tap. Each tap gives a mini-dash speed boost. The difference in survivability is night and day.

### 2. Pre-Stack Enhanced AA

Every 6th AA is a piercing lance. Before engaging: tap AA air 5 times → hold the 6th enhanced lance → walk into range → fire. Free long-range poke.

### 3. S2: Reverse Dash (Muscle Memory Required)

S2 dashes **opposite to joystick direction.** Joystick back = dash forward. Drill this in Training Mode until instinctive.

### 4. S3: Multiple Uses

S3's black hole:
- **Teamfight:** Drop on enemy backline → they're pulled together → team AoE deletes them.
- **Wave clear:** S3 a cannon wave to insta-clear and rotate.
- **Zone:** Block jungle corridors, Dragon/Baron pit entrances.
- **Self-peel:** S3 at your feet when dived → enemy is pulled away.

### 5. S3 + S1 + Tap AA = Maximum Kite

S3 pulls enemies → S1 accelerates you while spraying sand (slow) → tap AA for speed boosts while they're trapped in the vortex. They can't reach you.

## Signature Combos

### Safe Poke: **Tap AA×5 → Enhanced AA → S2 (retreat)**

1. Pre-stack 5 tap-AAs from range
2. Enhanced lance → long-range snipe
3. S2 backward → safe

### Teamfight: **S3 → S1 → Tap AA → S2**

1. S3 black hole on enemy cluster
2. S1 accelerate + sand spray (slow)
3. Tap AA cycle (speed boost + damage)
4. S2 to reposition or escape

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Geya's AA damage is reduced per hit.** On-hit builds (Lightning Dagger, Doomsday) are weaker on her. Prioritize Crit + Penetration.
- **S2 can cross thin walls.** Use to escape jungle ganks.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile melee comps. Struggles vs long-range poke that out-ranges her tap-AA.
