# Zhou Yu Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Fire zones deal multi-tick sustained damage. Needs **CDR** (constant fire zones), **Mask of Agony** (percentage burn synergy), and **semi-tank** survivability (must stay alive in the fire to spread it).

---

## Recommended Semi-Tank Zone Control Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR rush. More fire zone charges. |
| 2 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | **Zhou Yu's soul item!** Fire zones' multi-tick damage endlessly triggers Mask's %HP burn. Melts tanks. |
| 3 | **Frost Breath (凝冰之息)** | Fire zones + Frost Breath = **permanent AoE slow** across the entire battlefield. Enemies can't escape the flames. |
| 4 | **Devourer's Tome (噬神之书)** or **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | **Devourer's Tome:** massive spell vamp from burning multiple enemies. **Prophet's Hour:** AP + dual defenses vs burst. |
| 5 | **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** | Fire zones' multi-tick damage **stacks 10 layers of Sunset Flow penetration in under 1 second** — near true-damage output. |
| 6 | **Splendor (辉月)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Splendor:** Stasis for dodging fatal dives. **Pure Sky:** active DR + CC cleanse (new-season CN meta). |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Magic Penetration, Movement Speed (positioning for fire spreading), mild sustain.**

### Setup 1: "Harmony 88-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +450 Max Health, +4% MS, +6% AS. Early durability for aggressive fire-spreading.
