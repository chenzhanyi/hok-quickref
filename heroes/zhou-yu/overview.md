# Zhou Yu（周瑜） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Zone Control / Siege)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 鐵血都督 (Iron-Blooded Commander)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 周瑜)

> Zhou Yu is a zone-control Mage who excels at **area denial, tower destruction, and sustained teamfight control.** His S2 fire zones + S1 wind can blanket the entire battlefield in flames. His passive ignite and S2 fire zones **deal damage to turrets** — he's a "ruthless tower-pushing machine."

---

## Core Mechanics

### S2 + S1: Fire Spreading (Core Combo)

S2 drops a fire zone. S1 (wind) **blows the fire outward, creating 4-5 additional fire zones** in the wind's direction. This is how Zhou Yu covers entire screens in flames.

### S1: Self-Peel Knockback

S1 knocks back nearby enemies. When an Assassin dives you: S1 in their direction to push them away. Combos with S2+S3 for a point-blank stun counter-kill.

### S3: AoE Stun (on Fire)

S3 deals frontal cone damage. **If it overlaps any S2 fire zone, it triggers "Fire Wind" — 1-second AoE stun.** Always combo: S2 → S1 (spread) → S3 (stun).

### Passive: Ignite (Works on Towers!)

Abilities and AAs stack 4 ignites. At 4 stacks: target takes continuous Magic Damage + slow. **This works on turrets!** Drop S2 under a tower and watch its HP melt — even without minions.
