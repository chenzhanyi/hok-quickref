# Zhou Yu Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Tower Melting: Your Hidden Superpower

Zhou Yu's S2 fire zones AND passive ignite **deal damage to turrets.** When minions crash into a tower: S2 under the tower → S1 to spread more fire. The tower loses 30-50% HP while you stand safely out of range. Even without minions, fire zone edges can chip towers. Zhou Yu can end games through pure siege pressure.

### 2. S2 + S1 + S3: The Perfect Teamfight Combo

1. **S2** drop fire zone
2. **S1** wind → spread 4-5 fire zones across the battlefield
3. **S3** over the flames → triggers **AoE 1-second stun** on everyone standing in fire
4. Team cleans up

### 3. S1 Self-Peel → Counter-Kill

When an Assassin (Han Xin, Lam) dives you: **S2 at your feet → S1 toward them** (knocks them back) → **S3 immediately** — the fire at your feet triggers the stun. The Assassin is now stunned, standing in fire, and you walk away or finish them.

### 4. Fire Spreading Through Bushes

S1's wind spreads fire into bushes. Use this to:
- Check bushes (fire reveals enemies)
- Block enemy jungle paths from fog of war
- Zone enemies off buffs without revealing yourself

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take S3 whenever available.** S2 increases damage + max fire zone charges.

## Signature Combos

### Zone Control + AoE Stun: **S2 → S1 → S3**

1. S2 fire zone at your feet/front
2. S1 wind forward → 4-5 fire zones blanket the area
3. S3 over the flames → AoE 1s stun
4. Enemies are stunned inside a sea of fire

### Siege Pressure: **S2 (under tower) → S1 (spread)**

1. Minions crash tower
2. S2 under tower + S1 to spread fire
3. Tower burns. Repeat.

### Self-Defense Counter: **S2 (at feet) → S1 (toward enemy) → S3**

1. Assassin jumps on you
2. S2 at your feet
3. S1 knocks them back
4. S3 stuns them using the fire at your feet

## Advanced Tips

- **S2 charges stack.** You can store multiple fire zones. Drop two S2s before S1 for double the fire spread.
- **Fire zones persist.** Enemies must walk around them or take damage. Use this to block jungle chokepoints.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile comps and siege scenarios. Struggles vs hyper-mobile Assassins who bypass fire zones.
