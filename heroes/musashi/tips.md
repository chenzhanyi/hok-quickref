# Musashi Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Momentum Management: Know Your Stacks

- **2 Stacks:** Dash AA — mobility, gap-close, dodge
- **1 Stack:** Execute AA — 50%+ HP = double slash; <50% HP = %missing HP execute

Time your enhanced AAs based on enemy HP. Use 2-stack to engage, then 1-stack to execute.

### 2. Every Enhanced AA Reduces Cooldowns

Each Momentum AA reduces S1 + S2 by 1 second. Constant weaving: **S2 → AA → S1 → AA → S2 → AA.** This is how Musashi achieves near-infinite ability cycling.

### 3. S1: Save for Projectile Destruction

S1 destroys ALL projectiles. Don't waste it for damage. Save it for: Zhuge Liang S3, Hou Yi bird, Daji charm, Marco S1. A well-timed S1 negates an entire enemy ultimate.

### 4. S3: 100% Anti-Heal (Your Win Condition)

S3 blocks ALL healing for 5 seconds. Priority targets for S3:
- The enemy healer (Cai Yan)
- The lifesteal carry (Cao Cao, Mi Yue, Cheng Yaojin)
- The ADC with Blood Weeper

Don't S3 the tank — S3 the sustain core and watch them die without their healing crutch.

### 5. Pre-Stack Momentum Before Fighting

Hit jungle camps or minions to build 1-2 stacks before ganking. Walk in with Momentum ready — immediate enhanced AA on contact.

## Signature Combos

### Standard Gank: **S2 → AA (2-stack) → S1 → AA (1-stack) → S3 → AA → S2 → AA**

1. S2 + 2-stack AA (dash in)
2. S1 + 1-stack AA (execute if <50%)
3. S3 lock + AA
4. S2 + AA (S2 refreshed from AA CD reduction)

### S3 Initiation: **S3 → AA (2-stack) → S2 → AA → S1 → AA**

1. S3 from bush or close range
2. 2-stack AA (dash to stick)
3. S2 through enemy + AA
4. S1 + AA finish

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 is Unstoppable during the cast.** Use to ignore CC when engaging.
- **1-stack execute scales with missing HP.** The lower they are, the harder it hits.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs sustain comps. Struggles vs burst that kills before enhanced AA cycles.
