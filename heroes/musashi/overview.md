# Musashi（宮本武藏） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Anti-Heal Duelist)
- **Lane:** Jungle / Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 劍聖 (Sword Saint)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 宫本武藏)

> Musashi was reworked into a tempo-based Fighter/Assassin centered on **"Momentum" stacks** (max 2) and his S3 **"Duel to the Death"** — a 5-second **100% Grievous Wounds** (all healing blocked). He's the ultimate counter to sustain/lifesteal Heroes (Cao Cao, Cai Yan, Cheng Yaojin, Mi Yue, Erin).

---

## Core Mechanics

### Momentum (Stacks)

Max 2 stacks. Each enhanced AA consumes 1 stack:
- **2 Stacks (Dash Slash):** Forward dash + damage. Use to gap-close, chase, or dodge.
- **1 Stack (Execute Slash):** >50% HP target = double slash (main damage). <50% HP target = vertical slash with **%missing HP execute damage.**

Every enhanced AA reduces S1 and S2 cooldowns by 1 second.

### S1: Destroys All Projectiles

S1's sword wave **destroys ALL incoming projectiles** — Zhuge Liang S3, Marco Polo bullets, Hou Yi bird, Daji charm. Save S1 for critical projectile negation in teamfights.

### S3: 100% Anti-Heal (5 Seconds)

S3 locks onto a target (Unstoppable during cast). The target enters **"Duel to the Death"** — **ALL healing is blocked for 5 seconds.** This hard-counters sustain Heroes. Time S3 on the enemy healer or lifesteal carry at the start of every fight.
