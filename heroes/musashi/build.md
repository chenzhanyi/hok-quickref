# Musashi Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Semi-tank bruiser. Needs CDR (enhanced AA cycling), Physical Penetration, and survivability (must stay in melee range). Full damage gets deleted.

---

## Recommended Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Attack, Penetration, CDR. Standard Assassin jungle item. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Musashi dives into melee — CC-lock = death. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core Fighter item: CDR, flat Penetration, Health. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | **Core synergy!** Enhanced AAs perfectly trigger AoE slow + bonus damage. CDR + Physical Defense. Maximum stickiness. |
| 5 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Berserker's:** stacking MS + damage while fighting. **Pure Sky:** active DR vs burst. |
| 6 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Eternal Night:** magic defense + burst-heal. **Siege Breaker:** execute damage — stacks with 1-stack's %missing HP slash. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Physical Penetration, Movement Speed, mild Lifesteal.**

### Setup 1: "Stealth-Reave 100-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×7 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +7% MS, **+4.8% Physical Lifesteal**.

### Setup 2: "Dream Crit" (Early Burst)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×9 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×1 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +37 Physical Attack, +96.4 Physical Penetration, +10% MS, **+1.6% Critical Rate**. That one lucky enhanced AA crit at <50% HP deletes.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Musashi's S3 anti-heal is his identity. Always S3 the sustain carry. Copy the **CN #1 Musashi** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
