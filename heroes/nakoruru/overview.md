# Nakoruru（娜可露露） — Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin (Burst / Tank Shredder)
- **Lane:** Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 鷹之守護 (Eagle's Guardian)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 娜可露露)

> Nakoruru is a classic high-burst, stat-check pure Assassin. She has one of the highest **%Max HP Physical Damage** values in the game — making her both a "squishy nightmare" AND a "tank shredder." Her entire burst revolves around her S1 mark: land it, then trigger it with other damage for devastating %HP explosions.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S1: %Max HP Mark (Core Damage)

S1 marks an enemy. When Nakoruru's AA or other abilities hit a marked target, it triggers **%Max HP Physical Damage**. This is why she destroys tanks — the more HP they have, the harder she hits.

### S2: Lifesteal Dash

S2 dashes forward. **Hitting enemy Heroes restores massive HP** (Minions/Monsters also heal, but only 30% effectiveness). At low HP, S2 through an enemy or jungle camp for emergency sustain.

### S3: Eagle Flight (Two-Stage)

Stage 1: Massive Movement Speed (flying). **You can cast S1 while flying!** Stage 2: Slam down dealing AoE damage + **reduces enemy damage output by 30%**. This DR is why Nakoruru can dive teams and survive.

### Economy > Everything

Nakoruru is extremely item-dependent. Before core items (Shadow Axe + Siege Breaker): farm jungle, take Dragons, clear waves. Don't force ganks. After core items: one-shot anyone.
