# Nakoruru Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Scales purely with **Physical Attack (Bonus AD)**. Zero Attack Speed or Critical needed. Pure **Physical Attack + Penetration + CDR.**

---

## Recommended Jungle Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Standard Assassin jungle item: Attack, Penetration, CDR. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Mandatory. Nakoruru dives into melee — getting CC-locked on landing = instant death. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core first major item. Health, CDR, flat Penetration. Early game power spike. |
| 4 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Nakoruru's soul item!** Massive Bonus AD scaling on S1's %HP damage. +30% vs <50% HP: perfect execute synergy. |
| 5 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** or **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | **Pure Sky:** active DR stacks with S3's 30% DR for ~70% total — dive 5 enemies and survive. **Berserker's:** stacking MS + damage for extended fights. |
| 6 | **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** | 40% Penetration. With S1's %HP damage, even full-tank enemies evaporate. *Late: sell boots for Fate's Edge or Sage's Sanctuary.* |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Zero Attack Speed or Critical needed. **Pure Physical Penetration + Attack + Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Stealth 100-Pen" (95% of Players)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, **+10% Movement Speed**. Maximum skill burst + gank tempo.

## Golden Rule

**Farm > Ganks pre-core items.** Nakoruru with Shadow Axe + Siege Breaker one-shots everyone. Without them, she's half a Hero. Prioritize economy.
