# Nakoruru Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Cast S1 While Flying (Crucial Mechanic)

Nakoruru can cast S1 **during S3's flight**. The correct sequence: **S3 (fly) → S1 (mark while airborne) → S3 slam down (triggers mark).** This is vastly faster than landing first then S1. The mark detonates instantly on landing.

### 2. Economy Is Everything

Nakoruru is a stat-check Assassin. Without core items (Shadow Axe + Siege Breaker), you're half a Hero. Priority: farm jungle → clear waves → take Dragons → only gank when it's free. Never force ganks pre-core items.

### 3. S3 Damage Reduction = Survival

S3's slam reduces enemy damage output by **30%**. Combined with Pure Sky active, Nakoruru has ~70% effective DR during dives. You can land in the middle of 3 enemies and walk out.

### 4. S2 Lifesteal: Hit Multiple Targets

S2 heals per enemy Hero hit. In teamfights, angle S2 to pass through 2-3 enemies for massive burst healing. Even jungle camps provide emergency sustain.

### 5. Weave Enhanced AAs Between Abilities

After every ability, Nakoruru's next AA is enhanced. For maximum damage: **S1 → AA → S2 → AA → S3 → AA**. Don't just dump abilities — the AAs add significant damage.

## Signature Combos

### Standard Airborne Execute: **S3 (fly) → S1 (airborne) → S3 (slam) → AA → S2 → AA**

1. S3 takeoff
2. S1 mark while flying
3. S3 slam (triggers mark + 30% DR)
4. Enhanced AA
5. S2 through enemy
6. Enhanced AA — dead

### Melee Brawl: **S1 → AA → S2 → AA → S3 → S3**

1. S1 mark → AA
2. S2 dash through → AA
3. S3 takeoff + immediate slam
4. For when enemies are already on top of you

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 slam also slows.** Use it to trap enemies for your team.
- **Farm > kills pre-core items.** Nakoruru with a gold lead is unstoppable. Without gold, she's nothing.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs tanks (%HP damage) and squishies (burst). Struggles vs CC-heavy comps that cancel S3 or lock her on landing.
