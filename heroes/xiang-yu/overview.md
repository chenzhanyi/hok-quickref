# Xiang Yu（項羽） — Tank

## Overview

- **Role:** Tank (Hard Engage / Burst Bruiser)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 霸王 (Hegemon-King)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 项羽)

> Xiang Yu is a perennial Tank/Fighter with extreme durability, massive teamfight CC, and terrifying low-HP burst. His passive grants **40% DR for 6 seconds when HP drops below 30%** (90s CD). His ultimate deals **up to double damage at low HP.** With the right build, he can one-shot squishies while facetanking entire teams.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: 40% DR at Low HP

When HP <30%, gains **40% Damage Reduction for 6 seconds** (90s CD). Don't retreat when low — this is your strongest moment. Combine with S3's bonus damage for impossible turnaround kills.

### S1: Unstoppable Push

S1 charges forward, pushing enemies and **knocking up at the end.** During the charge: **Unstoppable.** Use to: disrupt enemy formations, push carries into your team, peel for your ADC, or S1+Flash for surprise engages.

### S3: More Damage at Lower HP

S3 deals massive frontal damage + stun. **The lower Xiang Yu's HP, the higher the damage (up to +100%).** Never cast S3 at full HP — save it for when you're low and the passive DR is active.

### S1 + Flash (Signature Engage)

Cast S1, then **Flash during the charge.** The push repositions to your Flash destination. Surprise-kidnap the enemy backline from off-screen.
