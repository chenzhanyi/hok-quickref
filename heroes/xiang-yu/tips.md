# Xiang Yu Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Never Retreat at Low HP

Passive grants 40% DR for 6 seconds when HP <30%. **This is your power window.** The enemy commits everything thinking you're dead → passive kicks in → you take half damage → S3 deals double damage → you win. Never panic at low HP.

### 2. S3: Save for Low HP (Double Damage)

S3 deals up to **+100% damage** the lower your HP is. Never cast S3 at full HP — it hits like a wet noodle. Cast at <30% HP with passive DR active: it one-shots squishies.

### 3. S1 + Flash: The Signature Engage

S1 charges forward pushing enemies. **Flash during the charge** to reposition the push anywhere. Surprise-kidnap enemy carries from off-screen into your team. The most feared Xiang Yu mechanic.

### 4. Clash Lane (Burst) vs Roaming (Tank)

Xiang Yu flexes two playstyles:
- **Clash Lane Burst:** Max S2 first. S2 grants two enhanced AAs (first heals, second guaranteed crit). Build semi-damage.
- **Roaming Tank:** Max S1 first for lower CD. Pure tank build. More pushes, more peels.

### 5. S1: Peel for Your ADC

S1 is as powerful defensively as offensively. When an Assassin dives your ADC: S1 them away. The push + Unstoppable means you can't be stopped from saving your carry.

## Signature Combos

### Clash Lane Burst: **S2 → AA → S1 → AA → S3**

1. S2 (grants two enhanced AAs)
2. AA (first enhanced — heals you)
3. S1 push + knockup
4. AA (second enhanced — guaranteed crit)
5. S3 at low HP → double damage execute

### Roaming Engage: **S1 + Flash → S3 → S2**

1. S1 charge → Flash mid-charge onto backline
2. S3 immediately (AoE stun + damage)
3. S2 to reduce enemy damage output
4. Team cleans up

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Passive has a 90s cooldown.** Track it. Don't play aggressive when it's down.
- **S3's stun is AoE.** Aim to hit multiple enemies — the stun + damage swings teamfights.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile backlines. Struggles vs hyper-mobile Heroes who dodge S1.
