# Xiang Yu Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Two viable paths. **Clash Lane Burst** (semi-damage, insane low-HP S3 one-shots) and **Roaming Tank** (pure defense, CC machine).

---

## Build A: Clash Lane Burst (Recommended — Highest Winrate)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Boots (影忍之足)** | 15% AA DR + passive DR = absurd Physical tankiness in lane. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** or **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | **Shadow Axe:** Penetration + CDR for burst. **Frost Grip:** S2 enhanced AAs perfectly trigger AoE slow. |
| 3 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Taking hits (which Xiang Yu does) grants stacking MS + damage — perfect for low-HP counter-engages. |
| 4 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Burst build's soul!** Massive AD amplifies S3's double-damage at low HP. Half-HP squishies get deleted. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Eternal Night:** burst-heal vs magic. **Pure Sky:** active DR — stacks with passive for ~70-80% total DR. |
| 6 | Flex defensive item | Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷) vs heavy magic, or Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆) vs ADCs. |

---

## Build B: Roaming Tank (Support)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Guard · Redemption (近卫·救赎)** | Team dual defense aura + group shield active. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity to survive CC and cast S1. |
| 3 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Anti-ADC passive. |
| 4 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic Defense + spell shield. |
| 5 | **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** | 20% CDR for frequent S1 engages. |
| 6 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** or **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | Massive HP + regen, or resurrection. |
