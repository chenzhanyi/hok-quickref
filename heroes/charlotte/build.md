# Charlotte Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Charlotte has an absolute requirement for 40% CDR. Semi-tank bruiser is mandatory.

---

## Recommended Clash Lane Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | Early CDR rush. Swap to Resistance Boots later. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Standard Fighter item: Attack, Health, CDR, Penetration. |
| 3 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | Enhanced AAs perfectly trigger AoE slow + on-hit damage. |
| 4 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | Active 35% DR stacks with innate DR for ~70% total. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** | Magic defense vs burst mages. |
| 6 | **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** | Percentage Penetration vs tanky teams. |
