# Charlotte Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Follow-Up Skill System
After any ability hits an enemy, other abilities become **green "Follow-Up" variants**. Hitting three abilities/follow-ups grants 3 marks, unlocking **Seven-Star Radiant Sword** with execute damage, 35% DR, and Unstoppable.

### 2. Attack Speed Cripple
Follow-Up Skills and Seven-Star Radiant Sword apply **Cripple** stacks: 7%~15% Attack Speed reduction per stack (max 7). Late-game, completely disables auto-attack carries.

### 3. Damage Immunity Stacking
Combine Seven-Star (35% DR) + Ultimate (35% DR) + Pure Sky active (35% DR) for up to **~70% Damage Reduction**.

### 4. Pre-Stack Before Fighting
Whiff S1 or S2 into empty air to pre-stack 1-2 green marks. When the fight starts, you're only 1 ability away from Seven-Star Radiant Sword.

### 5. S1 Is Your Combo Engine
S1 must hit a target to grant movement speed and unlock Follow-Up chains. Always ensure S1 connects.

### 6. Skill Priority
**Max S2 first, then S1.** S2 is core mobility, damage, healing, and passive-stacking.

## Signature Combos

### Standard Double Seven-Star: S1→S2→S3→AA→S1→S2→AA
Pre-stack marks. S1 to open. S2 dash in. S3 knockup + DR. Seven-Star proc. S1→S2 again. Second Seven-Star.

### Long-Range Initiation: S1→Flash→S2→S3→AA
S1 hits distant target. Flash to close gap. S2 into backline. S3 knockup. Seven-Star execute.

---

## Advanced Tips
- **CDR is everything:** Must reach 40% CDR to chain abilities seamlessly.
- **Turret diving:** Seven-Star's 35% DR lets you tank 1-2 turret shots.
- **Teamfight role:** Wait for tank engage, then dive backline during chaos.
- **Matchup:** Destroys auto-attack duelists. Struggles vs burst mages with CC.
