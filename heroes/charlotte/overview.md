# Charlotte（夏洛特） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Skirmisher / Lane Bully)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 玫瑰劍士 (Rose Swordsman)
- **Region:** Global (SNK crossover; CN server name: 夏洛特)

> Charlotte is an SNK-crossover lane-dominant Fighter. She relies on chaining abilities to empower her basic attacks, culminating in her signature burst: **Seven-Star Radiant Sword (七星光芒剑)**. Her "Follow-Up Skill" mechanic turns consecutive casts into enhanced green variants, building stacks toward a locked-on execute strike. With built-in Attack Speed reduction, damage immunity frames, and relentless gap-close, she bullies auto-attackers and duels with impunity.

---

## Passive & Core Mechanics

### Follow-Up Skills & Seven-Star Radiant Sword
After any ability hits an enemy, other abilities transform into **green "Follow-Up" variants** with bonus effects. Landing three abilities or follow-ups stacks 3 marks, granting a **Seven-Star Radiant Sword** — an empowered basic attack with locked-on execute damage, 35% Damage Reduction, and Unstoppable.

### Attack Speed Cripple
Follow-Up Skills and Seven-Star Radiant Sword apply **Cripple** stacks on hit, each reducing enemy Attack Speed by **7%~15%** (max 7 stacks).

### Damage Immunity Windows
- Seven-Star Radiant Sword: **35% DR + Unstoppable**
- Ultimate (S3): **35% DR + Unstoppable**
- Stack with Pure Sky active for up to **70% DR**

### Abilities
- **Ability 1 (S1):** Ranged thrust. Must hit a target to enable Follow-Up chains.
- **Ability 2 (S2):** Dash + damage + healing. Max this first.
- **Ultimate (S3):** AoE knockup + 35% DR + Unstoppable.
