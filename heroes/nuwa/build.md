# Nuwa Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Nuwa is a late-game artillery Mage. Needs **high AP, CDR, and Magic Penetration**. S2 matrix HP/damage scales with AP. Build: **pure AP burst**.

---

## Recommended Artillery Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR for frequent S2 matrix stacking. Essential early tempo. |
| 2 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | Movement Speed for positioning. On-hit explosion synergizes with S1/S4's long-range poke. |
| 3 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | **Nuwa's first power spike!** Percentage AP boost makes S4 and matrix explosions devastating. |
| 4 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** or **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** | **Mask:** percentage HP burn vs tanky teams. **Sunset Flow:** multi-hit penetration stacking for frequent teamfights. |
| 5 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Magic Penetration. Mandatory vs late-game Magic Cloak and magic resist. |
| 6 | **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** | Ultimate item. Massive AP + percentage damage amp. Paired with Savant's Wrath, S4 deletes squishies from full HP. |

### Survival Flex

If enemy has heavy dive (Lanling Wang, Ake, Jing), swap Mask/Sunset Flow for **Splendor (辉月)**.

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Magic Penetration, CDR, Movement Speed (compensate for low mobility).**

### Setup 1: "Hunt 88-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +10% Movement Speed, +16% Attack Speed. Smoothest positioning + level-4 S4 kill threshold.

## Pro Tip

Nuwa's vision expands with levels. Enable **three-finger camera drag** in settings. When aiming S4, drag the minimap to see the target area — blind-firing misses far less with visual confirmation.
