# Nuwa Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S2 Matrix Timing (Maximum Burst)

S2 matrices flash before expiring. If S1 or S4 hits the matrix at that moment, it **still explodes**. Pre-place matrices from bushes, wait for enemies to walk past, then S1 → trigger explosion for double damage.

### 2. S4: Global Laser (No Range Limit)

S4 has **zero range limit**. Constantly watch the minimap. When you see low-HP enemies recalling or escaping, S4 them. Also use to: steal Dragons, clear distant minion waves, finish teamfights from Mid Lane.

### 3. S1 Cross Intersection = Max Damage

S1's cross-shape explosion deals **maximum damage at the intersection point**. Aim the cross center directly on enemies or minion waves.

### 4. S3 Teleport Safety

S3 grants Unstoppable during channel, but **Suppression (Donghuang, Zhang Liang)** and **Daqiao's elevator** can interrupt it. Use three-finger camera drag to check the landing spot — never teleport into a bush blind.

### 5. Vision Scaling Wins Games

Every level = more vision + range. Play safe early. After level 12, you outrange nearly everyone. Late-game Nuwa is a god.

## Signature Combos

### Standard Burst: **S2 → S1 → S4**

1. S2 matrix behind or at enemy's feet
2. S1 pushes enemy into matrix → triggers explosion
3. S4 laser through both enemy and matrix → chain explosion → instant deletion

### Global Snipe: **Minimap Scan → S4**

1. Watch minimap for low-HP enemies
2. Three-finger drag to aim
3. S4 laser across the map → kill secure

### Flank Engage: **S3 (teleport) → S2 → S1 → S4**

1. S3 to flank or bush near enemy backline
2. Landing stuns → S2 block escape path
3. S1 push into matrix → S4 laser → team-wipe setup

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Matrix chain explosions:** Stack multiple S2 matrices together. One S1 can trigger all of them simultaneously.
- **S3 passive shield:** When taking damage, S3 grants a shield + slow nearby enemies + MS boost. It's your built-in escape tool.
- **Matchup:** Dominates immobile comps. Struggles vs hyper-mobile Assassins who can dodge S1 and flank past S2.
