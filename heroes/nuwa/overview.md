# Nuwa（女媧） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Artillery / Zone Control)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 至高創世 (Supreme Creator)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 女娲)

> Nuwa is a long-range artillery Mage with **global map presence**. Her unique scaling: every level increases her vision range, ability range, and AA range. Late-game, she controls the entire battlefield from beyond the enemy's sight. Her S2 matrix blocks create chain explosions, her S3 teleports across the map, and her S4 is a global-range laser with no distance limit.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Vision Scaling

Every level grants +3% vision + ability range + AA range. Late-game Nuwa out-ranges everyone.

### S1: Radiate (Push + Burst)

Pushes enemies back, then explodes in a cross shape. The cross intersection deals maximum damage. Use to: peel divers, push enemies into S2 matrices.

### S2: Create (Matrix Block)

Creates a rectangular block that blocks movement. Lasts 4 seconds. **Other abilities hitting the matrix trigger an explosion.** The matrix flashes before expiring — explosions still trigger at that moment.

### S3: Teleport (Global Rotate)

Long-range teleport. Passive: taking damage grants a shield + AoE slow + MS boost. Landing stuns enemies. Use for: cross-map ganks, escape, flanking.

### S4: Annihilate (Global Laser)

Pure damage laser with **zero range limit**. Can snipe low-HP enemies across the map, steal objectives, or clear distant waves.
