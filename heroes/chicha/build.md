# Chicha Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Magic burst Assassin. Needs **high AP, Magic Penetration, and Lifesteal/DR** for S3 dives. CN server consensus: **burst with survivability.**

---

## Recommended Jungle / Mid Lane Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Arcane Boots (秘法之靴)** or **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | **Arcane:** flat Penetration for early kills. **Calm:** CDR for frequent S1 mark stacking. |
| 2 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | Core burst. S1 mark hit triggers AoE explosion. +MS for roaming. |
| 3 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP spike. Mark detonation damage skyrockets. |
| 4 | **Devourer's Tome (噬神之书)** | 25% Magic Lifesteal + HP. S3 AoE in crowds = instant full HP. |
| 5 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Penetration vs late-game Magic Cloak. |
| 6 | **Splendor (辉月)** or **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | **Splendor:** S3 in → combo → Stasis → S2 out. **Prophet's Hour:** AP + dual defenses for safer dives. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Magic Penetration, Movement Speed, Magic Lifesteal.**

### Setup 1: "Hunt 88-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +16% AS, **+10% MS**. Max gank tempo.
