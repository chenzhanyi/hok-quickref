# Chicha Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Stack Marks Before Detonating

S1 and AAs apply Gu marks. **Never detonate at 1-2 marks.** Stack to max (usually 4-5) with S1 → AA weave, then S2/S3 for maximum %HP Magic Damage.

### 2. S3: Offensive Dive AND Defensive Dodge

S3 grants brief untargetability/DR. Use it:
- **Offensive:** S3 onto the enemy backline to bypass tanks
- **Defensive:** S3 to dodge Musashi S3, Zhuge Liang S3, or tower shots
- **Escape:** S3 away when collapsed on

### 3. S2: Save for Disengage

S2 is mobility/cleanse/shield. Don't burn it for poke damage. Save it for: retreating after S3 dive, cleansing CC, or chasing the last hit.

### 4. Minion Wave = Free Marks

S1 pierces/bounces through minions. Mark the front minion → the effect reaches the enemy behind. Free, safe mark stacking.

### 5. S3 + Splendor (Stasis) Cycle

S3 in → full combo → Splendor (Stasis) → S2 cooldown refreshes → S2 out. The classic Assassin in-and-out cycle.

---

## Signature Combos

### Long-Range Solo Kill: **S1 → AA → S1 → AA → S3 → S2**

1. S1 mark + AA (2-3 marks)
2. S1 again + AA (4-5 marks)
3. S3 dive (detonate all marks + AoE burst)
4. S2 to finish or escape

### Teamfight Backline Delete: **S2 → S1 → AA → S3 → S1**

1. S2 dash to flank angle
2. S1 mark the backline carry + AA
3. S3 dive (untargetability through enemy CC)
4. S1 again (marks refreshed)

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Mark duration is generous.** You can stack on one target, then switch to another before detonating.
- **S3 landing zone can be aimed during the channel.** Track enemy movement.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile squishies. Struggles vs instant point-and-click CC (Donghuang, Liang).
