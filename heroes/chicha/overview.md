# Chicha（蚩奼） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Magic Assassin / Skirmisher)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle / Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 五兵之主 (Master of Five Weapons)
- **Region:** CN server (蚩奼)

> Chicha is a mysterious Miaojiang-inspired Magic Assassin/Fighter. Her kit revolves around stacking **Gu Poison marks** on enemies via S1 and AAs, then detonating them with S2 or S3 for devastating burst. Her S3 is a dive tool with untargetability/DR, letting her bypass enemy frontlines and delete squishies.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Gu Poison Marks: Stack Then Detonate

S1 and AAs apply Gu marks (stackable). The more marks, the higher the detonation damage from S2/S3 — scaling to **%Max HP Magic Damage** at high stacks.

### S3: Untargetable/DR Dive

S3 grants brief untargetability or high DR, then Chicha slams down in a target area for massive AoE Magic burst. Use to: dodge enemy CC/ultimates, bypass frontline to reach backline, or escape collapse situations.

### S2: Mobility + Disengage

S2 typically includes a dash, cleanse, or shield. **Don't burn S2 purely for damage.** It's your only escape tool. Save it for: retreating after a failed assassination, cleansing CC mid-fight, or gap-closing for the kill.

### Minion/Jungle Bouncing

S1 can pierce or bounce through minions and monsters. Use waves as springboards — mark the front minion, the effect travels to the enemy behind them.
