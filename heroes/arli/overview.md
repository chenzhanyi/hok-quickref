# Arli（公孫離） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Agile Outplay ADC)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★★
- **Title:** 幻舞玲瓏 (Illusory Dance)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 公孙离; nickname: "A Li")

> Arli is widely considered the **highest skill-ceiling Marksman** in the game. Her core mechanic is her **Paper Umbrella** — when the umbrella is deployed, she gains bonus Movement Speed and reduced attack interval. Every ability cast and umbrella recall triggers a **double-arrow** enhanced auto-attack. Mastering umbrella management is the difference between a godlike Arli and a feeding one.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Maple Seal Explosions (Core Damage)

Attacks and abilities stack **Maple Seals**. At 4 stacks: explosion dealing AoE Magic Damage + **reduces all cooldowns by 1 second**. Stack seals as fast as possible.

### No-Umbrella State = Strongest Form

When the umbrella is deployed (not in hand): **+50 Movement Speed, -20% attack interval**. The umbrella-deployed state is her maximum DPS window.

### S2: Projectile Destruction

S2 destroys incoming projectiles (Zhuge Liang S3, Hou Yi bird, Marco S1, **tower shots**). The umbrella's rotation path is random — check its position before recasting.

### Always Keep One Escape

Never use all three S2 recasts at once. Always hold one umbrella recall to escape when an Assassin dives you.
