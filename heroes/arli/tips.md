# Arli Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. No-Umbrella State = Maximum DPS

When the umbrella is deployed: **+50 MS, -20% attack interval**. This is your strongest form. Throw umbrella → auto-attack relentlessly → recall only when you need to escape or chase.

### 2. Stack Maple Seals to 4

Every 4 stacks = AoE explosion + **-1s all cooldowns**. Always target the same enemy until the seal pops. The explosion is Magic Damage — it chunks even tanky targets.

### 3. S2 Destroys Projectiles (and Tower Shots!)

S2 destroys ALL incoming projectiles, including **tower shots**. When tower-diving or getting tower-aggro: S2 to delete the tower bullet.

### 4. Always Hold One Escape

Never expend S1, S2, AND S3 umbrella recalls. Always keep one available to escape when an Assassin jumps on you.

### 5. S3: Wall Slam

S3 pushes enemies back. In jungle corridors or near towers, angle S3 to **push enemies into walls** (stun) or into tower range.

### 6. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take ultimate whenever available.** S2 upgrade reduces cooldown + increases base damage — it's both offense and defense.

## Signature Combos

### Safe Lane Poke: **S1 → AA → S2 → AA → S1 (recall)**

1. S1 forward → AA (double-arrow + seal stack)
2. S2 to block counter-attack + AA
3. S1 recall to original position
4. Zero risk, half enemy HP gone in 1 second

### Long-Range Chase: **S3 → S3 (recall) → S1 → AA → S2 → AA**

1. S3 throw umbrella forward + knockback
2. S3 recall to teleport to umbrella position (half-screen gap-close)
3. S1 forward for further chase
4. AA → S2 → AA to finish

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S2's umbrella position is random.** Always glance at where it's spinning before recasting.
- **Arli is pure glass.** One CC and you're dead. Your only defense is umbrella mobility.
- **Tower diving with S2:** S2 deletes tower shots. Dive → kill → S2 the tower bullet → walk out.
- **Matchup:** Destroys immobile Marksmen. Struggles vs point-and-click CC (Daji, Zhang Liang).
