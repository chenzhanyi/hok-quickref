# Arli Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Arli desperately needs **Attack Speed** (hit AS thresholds to stack 4 seals quickly) and **on-hit effects** (double-arrows trigger on-hit twice). She's pure glass — mid-late game needs one defensive item.

---

## Recommended On-Hit Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Mandatory early AS. *Mid-late: sell for Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴) vs heavy CC.* |
| 2 | **Doomsday (末世)** | **Arli's #1 core item!** Every double-arrow perfectly triggers 8% Current HP on-hit. Damage doubles. Built-in Lifesteal for sustain. |
| 3 | **Shadow Blade (影刃)** | Critical Rate + Movement Speed. On-crit AS boost pushes Arli to maximum attack speed in no-umbrella state. |
| 4 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | Critical Damage spike. Three arrows delete squishies. |
| 5 | **Dawn (破晓)** | Marksman core. 40% Physical Penetration + AA damage amp. Tank-shredding comes online. |
| 6 | **Sun Bow (逐日之弓)** or **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** / **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | **Sun Bow:** super-range + MS for confident players — hit enemies from outside their screen. **Defensive item:** vs heavy dive/assassin teams. Arli's tiny HP pool needs the safety net. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Attack Speed (AS threshold), Physical Penetration, mild Lifesteal.**

### Setup 1: "Red Moon AS" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1.6%, Critical Rate +0.5% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×7 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +23% Attack Speed, +5% Critical Rate, +64 Physical Penetration, +4.8% Physical Lifesteal, +7% Movement Speed.

### Setup 2: "Dream Crit" (CN High-Rank)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×7 | Attack Speed +1.6%, Critical Rate +0.5% |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×3 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×7 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +18.2% Attack Speed, **+8.3% Critical Rate**, +64 Physical Penetration, +4.8% Physical Lifesteal. Sacrifices ~5% AS for significantly higher crit chance. Early crit → seal explosion → easy solo kills.

Arli is extremely execution-heavy. Copy the **CN #1** or **KPL pro Marksman** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**. Their loadouts are optimized for current-patch AS breakpoints.
