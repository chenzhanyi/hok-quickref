# Li Xin Tips & Tricks

---

## Pre-Level 4: Survive

Li Xin is extremely weak before level 4. Use S2 to last-hit from range. Hitting enemy Heroes with S2 grants **bonus EXP**. Don't trade — just farm to 4.

---

## ☀️ Light Form Tips

### 1. Weave Auto-Attacks After EVERY Ability

Light Form's soul: after each ability, enhanced AA gains range + damage AND **reduces all cooldowns by 1 second**. S3→AA→S1→AA→S2→AA is mandatory rhythm.

### 2. Charge = Unstoppable + 20% DR

S1 (charge) and S3 (ultimate AoE beam) grant **Unstoppable + 20% Damage Reduction while charging**. Use reactively to face-tank:
- Daji's charm
- Mozi's cannon
- Lu Bu's ultimate knockup

### 3. Bush Ambush One-Shot

**S3 (full charge from bush) → AA → S1 → AA → S2 → AA**
Fully charged S3 from fog of war can delete squishies before they see you.

---

## 🌙 Dark Form Tips

### 1. S1 = Uncatchable

Hold S1 to sprint continuously — **immune to ALL CC** and **restores massive HP**. With S1 up, even 3-4 enemies can't catch you. Never get ganked again.

### 2. S2 Mark → Shred

S2 applies slow + **Rend mark**. Auto-attacking marked targets deals **bonus Physical Damage** and heals you. Combo: S2 → AA×n until target dead.

### 3. Never Group — Be the Pressure

Dark Form's job is NOT teamfighting. Constantly:
- Steal enemy jungle camps
- Proxy minion waves behind towers
- Split-push towers while your team takes objectives
- If 1 enemy comes → kill them. If 2-3 come → S1 sprint away → your team gets a 4v2 advantage.

---

## Form Selection Guide

| Situation | Pick |
|-----------|------|
| Enemy has many squishies, few dashes | **Light Form** — AoE bombardment |
| Enemy has tanks, split-push comp (Daqiao, Liu Bang) | **Dark Form** — infinite sidelane pressure |
| You want to teamfight | **Light Form** |
| You want to never group | **Dark Form** |
