# Li Xin Build & Items

> **Critical:** Light Form and Dark Form have completely different builds. You must decide BEFORE the match. Mixing items = both forms are useless.

---

## ☀️ Build A: Light Form — Pure Damage Burst

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | Stack CDR fast for frequent beams and Unstoppable charges. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core first item: Penetration, CDR, Health. |
| 3 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | Light Form's execute power spike. Skills deal massive damage — Siege Breaker deletes targets below 50% HP instantly. |
| 4 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** or **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | Enhanced AA after abilities. Master's Power for burst; Frost Grip for stickiness + CDR. |
| 5 | **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** | Percentage Penetration vs late-game armor stacking. |
| 6 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | Active DR stacks with charge passive for ~40% total DR. *Late game: sell boots for Fate's Edge or Sage's Sanctuary.* |

**Arcana:** Mutation×10 + Eagle Eye×10 + Stealth×10 = **+100 Pen, +29 Attack, +10% MS.**

---

## 🌙 Build B: Dark Form — On-Hit Split-Push (Most Popular)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Boots of Fortitude (影忍之足)** or **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Fortitude for tough lanes; Berserker's for snowballing. |
| 2 | **Lightning Dagger (闪电匕首)** | Dark Form's early farming core. Attack Speed + chain lightning makes split-pushing absurdly fast. |
| 3 | **Doomsday (末世)** | **#1 power spike.** Dark Form's double-hit AA perfectly triggers 8% Current HP on-hit damage. Melts towers and tanks. |
| 4 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Taking hits grants MS + damage output. Perfect for running and dueling. |
| 5 | **Blood Rage (血魔之怒)** or **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | Blood Rage: bonus on-hit damage at low HP + massive shield active — turns losing duels into wins. Frost Grip: slow + CDR for stickiness. |
| 6 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense vs burst mages. |

**Arcana:** Red Moon×10 + Eagle Eye×10 + Hunt×7 + Reave×3 = **+23% AS, +64 Pen, +7% MS, +4.8% Lifesteal.**

---

## Decision Guide

| Factor | Light Form | Dark Form |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Role | Teamfighter / Burst | Split-pusher / Pressure |
| Teamfight contribution | AoE backline delete | Minimal (don't group) |
| Catch potential | Low (immobile while charging) | Almost ungankable (S1 immune) |
| Tower damage | Low | Extremely high |
| Fun factor | One-shot montages | "Why won't this guy die" |

---

> 💡 **Never mix forms mid-match.** If you picked Light Form runes and try to play Dark, your build is useless. Decide at draft based on team comps.
