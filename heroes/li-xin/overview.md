# Li Xin（李信） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Dual-Form — Ranged Burst / Melee Split-Pusher)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 謀世之戰 (War of Strategy)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 李信)

> Li Xin has a uniquely split identity. Pre-level 4 he's a weak Human Form. At level 4, he permanently chooses between two completely different forms:
> - **Light Form (光信):** Ranged artillery Fighter. Long-range sword beams, Unstoppable while charging, massive AoE burst.
> - **Dark Form (暗信):** Melee split-pusher. High Attack Speed, CC immunity + sprint, relentless sidelane pressure.
> 
> You must decide your form BEFORE the match starts — their builds are completely different.

---

## Pre-Level 4: Human Form

Very weak. Use S2 (Powerful Slash) to last-hit from range. Hitting enemy Heroes with S2 grants **bonus EXP**. Combined with passive (hits grant bonus EXP), safely reach level 4 ASAP.

---

## ☀️ Light Form (Ranged Burst)

- **Weave auto-attacks after every ability** — enhanced AA reduces all cooldowns by 1s
- **S1 and S3 charging grants Unstoppable + 20% DR** — face-tank Daji, Mozi, even Lu Bu's ultimate
- **Bush ambush: S3 (full charge) → AA → S1 → AA → S2 → AA**

## 🌙 Dark Form (Split-Push Menace)

- **S1 sprint: CC immune + massive healing.** Never get caught — 3-4 enemies can't catch you
- **S2 marks enemies for bonus AA damage + self-heal**
- **Never group.** Steal jungle, proxy waves, take towers. Draw pressure for your team.
