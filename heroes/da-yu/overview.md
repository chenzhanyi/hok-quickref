# Da Yu（大禹） — Tank

## Overview

- **Role:** Tank (Terrain Control / Frontline)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 鼎鎮山河 (Stabilizer of Rivers & Mountains)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (大禹)

> Da Yu is a strategically powerful Clash Lane / Roaming Tank whose kit revolves around **flood control and terrain manipulation.** He can create temporary walls to block enemies, has a stacking passive that grants defenses and culminates in a devastating **%Max HP True Damage** enhanced AA, and his S3 channels an Unstoppable tidal wave with massive DR.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Unyielding Water Force

Taking damage or casting abilities builds Water Force. Higher Force = **higher Physical & Magic Defense.** At max Force, the next AA consumes all stacks for a massive AoE slam dealing **%Max HP True Damage.** Pre-stack Force before engaging.

### Terrain Wall: Strategic Blockade

Da Yu can create a temporary "earth dam" or "water wall" (similar to Nuwa's matrix or Yi Xing's cage). Use to: block escape paths of immobile carries, isolate enemy Assassins from your backline, or create chokepoints in jungle corridors.

### S3: Unstoppable + High DR During Channel

S3 channels a massive tidal wave. During the channel: **Unstoppable + high DR.** Use reactively — when Daji, Lady Zhen, or Mozi fires CC, cast S3 to ignore it and counter-engage.

### Pre-Stack Force Before Ganking

Before roaming or bush-camping: use S1/S2 on jungle camps or into empty air to build Force close to max. When the fight starts, your first AA is the True Damage slam.
