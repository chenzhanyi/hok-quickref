# Da Yu Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Pre-Stack Water Force Before Fighting

Force grants defenses and culminates in a **%Max HP True Damage AA.** Before engaging: S1/S2 jungle camps or empty air to build Force to near-max. Enter the fight with the True Damage slam ready.

### 2. Terrain Wall: Block, Don't Just Place

The wall is temporary but game-changing. Uses:
- Block enemy retreat paths (they must Flash or die)
- Isolate enemy Assassins from your backline
- Create chokepoints in jungle corridors
- Protect your ADC by walling between them and divers

### 3. S3: Reactive, Not Proactive

S3 grants Unstoppable + high DR during channel. Don't open with it — save for: Daji charm, Lady Zhen freeze, Mozi S2 stun, or any telegraphed CC. **Cast S3 as the CC flies at you** — you'll ignore it and counter-engage.

### 4. Max Force AA = Your Biggest Damage

The Force-consuming AA deals **%Max HP True Damage.** Don't waste it on minions. Always target enemy Heroes (preferably tanks — %HP shreds them).

### 5. S1 + S2 for Continuous CC

S1 is primary damage and CC. S2 creates terrain or slows. Chain: S2 (block escape) → S1 (knockup/stun into the wall) → Force AA → S3 (if needed).

---

## Signature Combos

### Long-Range Engage: **S2 → S1 → AA (Force) → S3**

1. S2 terrain wall (blocks escape path)
2. S1 knockup/stun (enemy hits wall = extended CC)
3. AA with max Force (True Damage slam)
4. S3 tidal wave (cleanup or counter-CC)

### Backline Protection: **S1 → S2 → S3 → AA**

1. Assassin dives your ADC
2. S1 knock them up at your ADC's feet
3. S2 wall between Assassin and ADC
4. S3 if more enemies follow
5. AA Force slam to finish the isolated Assassin

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S2 wall can be placed diagonally.** This maximizes the blocking surface area.
- **Force stacks decay slowly out of combat.** Keep hitting things to maintain stacks.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile comps. Struggles vs hyper-mobile Heroes who ignore terrain (perma-flight Cirrus, long-dash Heroes).
