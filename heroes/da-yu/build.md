# Da Yu Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Damage and shields scale with **Max HP.** Pure tank = high damage + high durability. Stack HP + CDR + defenses.

---

## Recommended Clash Lane / Roaming Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Blazing Cape (红莲斗篷)** | **Core #1.** HP + burn aura. Da Yu relies on melee brawling — burn synergizes with his close-range style. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Must survive CC to cast terrain wall and S3. |
| 3 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Anti-ADC passive — reduces enemy AS on-hit. |
| 4 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** | Magic defense. Burst-heal benefits from Da Yu's innate DR from S3. |
| 5 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** | Highest pure HP in the game. Amplifies Force AA's %Max HP True Damage. In-combat regen keeps Force stacks active. |
| 6 | **Frost Assault (寒霜袭侵)** or **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | **Frost Assault:** AA slow for stickiness. **Sanctuary:** resurrection — on revive, Force resets to full. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Max HP, CDR, Movement Speed.** Universal tank runes shared with Zhang Fei, Lian Po, Niu Mo.

### Setup 1: "Thousand-HP Tank" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, Cooldown Reduction +0.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** **+1,162 Max Health**, +23 Physical Defense, +6% CDR, +4% MS, +10% AS. Level 1 dominance — Force AA hits hard from minute zero.

### Setup 2: "Hunt Speed Skirmish" (Clash Lane Duelist)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7 |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, CDR +0.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +712 Max Health, +23 Physical Defense, +6% CDR, **+10% MS**, +20% AS. Faster Force AA animation + better kiting.
