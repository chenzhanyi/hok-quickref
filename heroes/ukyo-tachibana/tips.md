# Ukyo Tachibana Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Master Edge Spacing

Ukyo is NOT a face-tank. Always keep **~one Flash distance** from enemies. Passive AA and S2 only apply their full effects at the blade's EDGE. Learn this spacing — it's the difference between a god Ukyo and a dead one.

### 2. Bush Ambush = One-Shot

From bush:
1. **Passive AA** (edge slow)
2. **S2** (guaranteed tip stun because they're already slowed)
3. **S3** (full slash damage)
4. **S1 ×2** (finish or escape)
This deletes squishies from 100% HP.

### 3. S1: Use Minions as Springboards

S1 first cast on a nearby minion/monster (unlocks second cast) → S1 second cast into enemy. This gives you double dash distance with no cooldown penalty.

### 4. S3 Flash Redirect

S3 roots you in place, but **you can Flash during S3**. Use this to:
- Chase fleeing enemies
- Redirect slashes onto a priority target
- Escape while still dealing damage

### 5. Give Blue to Your Mage

Ukyo has zero Mana costs. After the first Blue for jungle clear, donate all subsequent Blues to your Mid. This team-level macro is a huge advantage.

### 6. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take S3 whenever available.** S2 is core burst and CC — each rank reduces cooldown and increases damage.

## Signature Combos

### Mid-Range Perfect Control: **Passive AA → S2 → S3 → S1 → S1**

1. Passive AA at edge (slow)
2. S2 at tip (stun — guaranteed because of slow)
3. S3 full slash damage + lifesteal
4. S1 ×2 to chase or disengage

### Long-Range Gap-Close: **S1 (off minion) → AA → S2 → S3 → S1 (retreat)**

1. S1 backward off a minion
2. S1 second cast toward enemy (gap closed)
3. AA → S2 → S3 burst
4. Second S1 to retreat to safety

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Power window is early-mid game (pre-12 min).** Ukyo falls off late when tanks have full armor. End games fast.
- **S3 heals even on non-Heroes.** Use jungle camps as health packs between fights.
- **Matchup:** Destroys squishy comps in early skirmishes. Struggles vs late-game hyper-tanks.
