# Ukyo Tachibana Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Skills have massive **Physical Attack (AD) scaling** and needs **CDR**. Full burst or semi-tank depending on role. Zero Mana = no Mana items needed.

---

## Recommended Jungle / Clash Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** or skip | Jungle: Red knife for burst. Clash: skip to boots. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Ukyo is squishy. Tenacity prevents CC-lock death. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | **Absolute core #1!** 15% CDR, Attack, Health, flat Penetration. Perfect for Ukyo's poke rhythm. |
| 4 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Power spike.** High AD scaling + Siege Breaker's +30% vs <50% HP = S1 second cast and S3 become execute-level finishers. |
| 5 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** or **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | **Master's Power:** enhanced AA burst + MS. **Frost Grip:** CDR + Defense + slow for stickiness and survivability. |
| 6 | **Fate's Edge (名刀·司命)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | Survival. Fate's Edge: fatal-damage immunity. Pure Sky: active DR. S3's lifesteal during immunity/DR can reverse-kill. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Zero Attack Speed or Critical needed. **Pure Physical Penetration + Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Stealth 100-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +45 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% Movement Speed. Every slash hurts.

### Setup 2: "Jungle Sustain"

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×7 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, +100 Physical Penetration, +7% MS, +7% AS, **+4.8% Physical Lifesteal**. Healthy jungle clears for full-HP ganks.
