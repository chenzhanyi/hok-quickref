# Ukyo Tachibana（橘右京） — Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin (Spacing Burst / Manaless)
- **Lane:** Jungle / Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 神夢一刀 (Divine Single Slash)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 橘右京)

> Ukyo Tachibana is a manaless Assassin/Fighter who blends burst and skirmishing. His entire kit revolves around **spacing** — hitting enemies with the **edge** of his abilities for bonus damage and CC. His passive enhanced AA and S2 both deal +50% damage and stun/slow enemies at maximum range.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Edge Spacing = Everything

Ukyo is NOT a face-tank. Always stay **~one Flash distance away** from enemies:
- **Passive AA:** +50% damage + slow at the blade's edge
- **S2:** Stuns only at the **tip** (edge). Mid-range = just damage, no CC.

### S1: Double Dash (Unlock Second Cast)

S1 jumps backward. **Hitting any enemy (Minion, Monster, Hero) unlocks a second cast.** Use S1 off minions as a springboard to engage, then S1 again to chase or escape.

### S3 Ultimate: Flashable + Lifesteal

S3 is a continuous slash with HP recovery (half vs non-Heroes). **You can Flash during S3** to redirect the slashes. The lifesteal lets you turn losing fights.

### Manaless: Give Blue to Your Mage

Ukyo has zero Mana. In Jungle, after the first Blue Buff, **give subsequent Blues to your Mid Mage** (Diaochan, Luna). This elevates your entire team.
