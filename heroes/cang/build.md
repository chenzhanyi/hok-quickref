# Cang Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Semi-tank bruiser. S3 grants built-in DR — stack CDR + Penetration + Defense. CN server consensus: **CDR bruiser.**

---

## Recommended Jungle / Clash Lane Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Jungle: Attack + Penetration + CDR. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Must survive CC to cast S3. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core #1. CDR + flat Penetration + Health. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | S1/S2 → enhanced AA perfectly triggers AoE slow. CDR + Defense. |
| 5 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Core survival!** Active 35% DR stacks with S3's %DR = 60%+ total. |
| 6 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** or **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Berserker's:** stacking MS + damage while fighting. **Siege Breaker:** execute for snowballing. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Physical Penetration, Movement Speed, mild Attack Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Stealth 100-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% MS. Max early burst.

### Setup 2: "Hunt-Reave Hybrid" (Smooth AA Feel)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×7 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +9 Physical Attack, +100 Physical Penetration, +7% AS, +7% MS, **+4.8% Lifesteal.** Smoother enhanced AA animations.
