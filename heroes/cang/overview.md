# Cang（蒼） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Fighter / Jungler Hybrid)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 蒼狼末裔 (Descendant of the Wolf)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (苍)

> Cang is a dominant hybrid Fighter-Marksman built around **Rage control, AoE execute, and armor-shred engages.** His S3 grants Unstoppable + massive %DR during activation, making him deceptively tanky during dives. He excels at cleanup and sustained brawling in teamfights.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Rage Mechanic

AAs and abilities build Rage. At full Rage: next AA becomes an **enhanced long-range strike** with bonus damage + dash + heal. Never break the AA rhythm — the enhanced AA is your biggest damage spike.

### S3: Unstoppable + %DR (Face-Tank Everything)

S3 activation grants **Unstoppable + high %DR for several seconds.** When the enemy Assassin dives you or CC flies at you: S3 their face. You'll eat the burst and counter-kill.

### Minion/Monster = Springboard

S1/S2 can use minions and monsters as "stepping stones" for second-phase dashes. Gap-close by dashing to a minion first, then enhanced AA onto the enemy behind it.

### S2: Kite Tool

S2 slows enemies and creates distance. Against melee stat-checkers (Fuzi, Dian Wei): S2 kite → wait for S1 cooldown → re-engage. Never stand and trade.
