# Cang Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Don't Break the AA Rhythm (Rage Stacking)

Rage builds from AAs and abilities. At full Rage: enhanced AA with bonus damage + dash + heal. Always maintain AA rhythm between abilities — breaking the chain resets Rage buildup.

### 2. S3: Face-Tank, Not Flee

S3 grants Unstoppable + massive %DR for several seconds. When an Assassin dives you: **S3 immediately.** The DR makes you nearly unkillable. Combined with Pure Sky's active DR, you can reach 60%+ total DR.

### 3. Minion/Monster = Gap-Close Springboard

S1/S2 can use minions and monsters as dash targets. If the enemy is far: dash to a nearby minion first → enhanced AA lunges onto the enemy behind it. Two-phase gap-close.

### 4. S2: Kite Melee Stat-Checkers

Fuzi, Dian Wei, and other melee monsters out-stat you in melee range. Use S2 to slow them and create distance. Wait for S1 cooldown. Re-engage when you have abilities up. Never trade AAs with a stat-checker.

### 5. Pure Sky + S3 = Near-Immune

S3's %DR + Pure Sky active (35%) = up to **60%+ total DR.** Pop both when diving the backline. You'll tank everything and walk out.

## Signature Combos

### Long-Range Engage: **S2 → AA (enhanced) → S1 → S3 → AA**

1. S2 slow from range
2. Enhanced AA dash onto target
3. S1 CC + damage
4. S3 (Unstoppable + DR + AoE)
5. AA finish

### Counter-Engage: **S3 → S1 → AA → S2 → AA**

1. Enemy dives → S3 face-tank (DR + Unstoppable)
2. S1 knockup + damage
3. Enhanced AA
4. S2 kite away or chase
5. AA finish

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 can be cast while CC'd** (if it has a cleanse mechanic). Check patch notes.
- **Enhanced AA has a dash component.** Use to cross thin walls by targeting jungle camps on the other side.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs burst-reliant Assassins (DR negates their combo). Struggles vs sustained True Damage.
