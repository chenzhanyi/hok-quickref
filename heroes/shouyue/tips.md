# Shouyue Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Reverse Flick Snipe (Undodgeable S2)

While charging S2, the red laser telegraphs your aim. Pro technique:
1. Aim **away from the enemy** (toward a wall or behind you)
2. Wait for the aim line to fully narrow
3. **Flick toward the enemy at the last instant** and release
Zero reaction time for the enemy.

### 2. Point-Blank S2 = 100% Guaranteed Hit

When an Assassin (Han Xin, Li Bai) dives you, **don't aim — just tap S2**. At point-blank range it's guaranteed. Follow with: **S2 → AA → S3 (backward leap)** to instantly counter-kill most squishy divers.

### 3. AA More, Snipe Less

The reworked Shouyue has incredibly strong AAs. When enemies are in AA range, **use your AAs**. A full-AA Shouyue in a teamfight contributes 10× more damage than one scoped in missing shots. Use S2 for: pre-fight poke, post-fight cleanup.

### 4. Vision Ward Placement

Never drop all 3 wards at your feet. Place them at:
- River bushes (gank early warning)
- Dragon/Baron pit (objective vision)
- Enemy jungle crossroads (track jungler pathing)

Wards reveal invisible Heroes — Lanling Wang and Ake can't gank you.

### 5. Stand Still Before Sniping

Passive: standing still stacks Physical Penetration per second (max 5 stacks). Before a big snipe, stop moving for 5 seconds — your shot hits significantly harder.

## Signature Combos

### Anti-Dive Counter-Kill: **S2 (instant) → AA → S3**

1. Assassin jumps on you
2. Tap S2 instantly (don't aim) — 100% hit
3. AA once
4. S3 backward leap — deals damage + slow + creates distance
5. Most squishy Assassins die to this

### Long-Range Execute: **Stand Still 5s → Reverse Flick S2**

1. Stand still for max Penetration stacks
2. Aim backward (hide laser)
3. Flick toward low-HP fleeing enemy
4. Release at full charge

## Advanced Tips

- **Camouflage gives MS near walls.** Hug walls when rotating for faster map movement.
- **S3 can jump over thin walls.** Use to escape jungle ganks or reposition in fights.
- **Matchup:** Destroys immobile comps. Struggles vs multiple divers who can close distance past your wards.
