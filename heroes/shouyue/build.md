# Shouyue Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Shouyue's AAs can't crit. Instead, **Critical Rate converts directly to Physical Attack**. Two viable paths: **AA Speed** (highest winrate) and **Pure Snipe** (situational).

---

## Build A: AA Speed (Recommended — Highest Winrate)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early AS for smooth AA laning. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | CDR, Health, flat Physical Penetration. |
| 3 | **Doomsday (末世)** | Core AA item. AS + 8% Current HP on-hit. Shreds tanks. |
| 4 | **Dawn (破晓)** | Marksman core. AS, dual Penetration, AA damage amp. Massive power spike. |
| 5 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | Shouyue doesn't benefit from the crit, but **Infinity Edge's high Physical Attack + passive converts crit rate to raw AD** — massive stat boost. |
| 6 | **Sun Bow (逐日之弓)** or **Fate's Edge (名刀·司命)** | **Sun Bow:** +Range active for safer AA. **Fate's Edge:** anti-burst survival. |

---

## Build B: Pure Snipe (Situational / Fun)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | Calm Boots (冷静之靴) | CDR for frequent S2. |
| 2 | Shadow Axe (暗影战斧) | Flat Penetration + CDR. |
| 3 | Star Breaker (碎星锤) | 40% Penetration. |
| 4 | Infinity Edge (无尽战刃) | Raw AD conversion. |
| 5 | Infinity Edge (无尽战刃) | Stack raw AD (or Siege Breaker for execute). |
| 6 | Siege Breaker (破军) | Execute vs low-HP + massive AD. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Shouyue's unique mechanic: **Critical Rate runes convert to Physical Attack at level 1**. Calamity runes are more efficient than Mutation.

### Setup 1: "Calamity Conversion AA" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×10 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×7 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** 16% Crit → **~40 bonus Physical Attack at level 1**, +64 Physical Penetration, +7% AS, +7% MS, +4.8% Lifesteal. More AD than Mutation runes.

### Setup 2: "Stealth 100-Pen" (Pure Snipe)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +45 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% Movement Speed. Every S2 hits like a truck.

---

## Quick Tip

Shouyue is a "team morale" hero — missing snipes tilts your teammates. If you're not confident in your aim, **use the AA Speed build and auto-attack more**. You'll contribute far more and climb faster.
