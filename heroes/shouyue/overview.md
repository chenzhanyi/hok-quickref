# Shouyue（百裡守約） — Assassin / Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin / Marksman (Long-Range Sniper)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 靜謐之眼 (Eye of Silence)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 百里守约)

> Shouyue is a uniquely polarizing Marksman with the longest straight-line snipe range in the game. His AAs cannot crit — instead, **Critical Rate converts directly to Physical Attack**. The latest rework dramatically improved his AA mechanics, splitting the playerbase into two styles: "Charged Snipe" and "AA Speed." The AA Speed build currently has the highest winrate.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Camouflage + Penetration Stacking

Moving along walls/towers/bushes grants **Camouflage + massive Movement Speed**. Standing still stacks **Physical Penetration** per second (max 5 stacks). Before sniping: stand still for max pen.

### S1: Vision Wins Games

Place 3 vision wards. Never drop all at your feet — place at: river bushes, Dragon/Baron pit, enemy jungle crossroads. These reveal invisible Heroes (Lanling Wang, Ake).

### S2: Reverse Flick Snipe

While aiming S2, the red laser is visible to enemies. Pro technique: **aim backward first**, then at the last millisecond **flick toward the enemy** — they have zero time to dodge.

### Point-Blank S2 = 100% Hit

If an Assassin dives you, don't aim. **Tap S2 instantly at point-blank** → guaranteed hit. Combo: S2 (instant) → AA → S3 (backward leap).

### Don't Be a "Peripheral Sniper"

Many Shouyue players spend entire fights scoped in, turning teamfights into 4v5. **The reworked Shouyue's AAs deal massive damage.** When enemies are in AA range, USE your AAs. Save S2 for pre-fight poke and post-fight cleanup.
