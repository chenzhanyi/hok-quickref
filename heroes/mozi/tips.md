# Mozi Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S2 from Bush = Undodgeable

S2's red laser line is visible to enemies. Cast from bushes and it's invisible. Always S2 from fog of war.

### 2. S2 Cross-Map: Watch the Minimap

S2 has extreme range. When you see enemy icons retreating on the minimap: pre-aim their path and fire. Cross-map stuns win games.

### 3. S3 + Flash: The Signature Engage

S3 cage can be repositioned with Flash. **Cast S3 → Flash into enemy backline.** The cage moves with you. Unavoidable multi-man stun.

### 4. Anti-Dive: S1 → AA → S2

When an Assassin dives: S1 dash → immediate AA (knockback punch) → S2 at point-blank (100% guaranteed stun). They're CC-locked for 2+ seconds. Easy kill for your team.

### 5. 4th AA Knockup: Always Track It

Every 4th AA knocks enemies back. Before teamfights, AA minions/monsters to 3 stacks. Hold the 4th knockup AA for the enemy diver or to peel for your ADC.

### 6. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take S3 whenever available.** S2 is core poke and CC.

## Signature Combos

### Ranged CC Chain: **S2 → S1 → AA**

1. S2 long-range stun (1s)
2. S1 dash forward
3. AA (4th punch knockup — if stacked)
4. Enemy is CC'd for 2+ seconds

### Teamfight S3+Flash: **S1 → S3 → Flash**

1. S1 dash toward enemies
2. S3 cage
3. **Flash into their backline** — cage stuns everyone

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S2's damage puddle persists.** Enemies walking through it take continuous damage + proc item effects (Mask, Frost Breath, Sunset Flow).
- **Mozi's shield stacks.** Spam S1/S2 even when no enemies are nearby if you need extra durability.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile comps. Struggles vs hyper-mobile Heroes who dash out of S3 cage.
