# Mozi（墨子） — Fighter / Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter / Support (Artillery CC / Flex Pick)
- **Lane:** Roaming / Clash Lane / Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 和平守望 (Peacekeeper)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 墨子)

> Mozi is a versatile CC-heavy Mage-Tank who flexes between Mid, Support, and Clash Lane. His S2 is a devastating long-range cannon that stuns for 1 second, his S3 is an AoE continuous stun cage (up to 3.3s), and every 4th AA knocks enemies back. He's one of the most flexible Heroes in the game.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Shield + Knockup AA

Every ability grants a stackable 3s shield. Every **4th AA becomes a "cannon punch"** that knocks enemies back. Weave AAs between abilities.

### S2: Long-Range Stun (Signature)

Fires a cannon that stuns for **1 second** on hit and leaves a damage puddle. Use from bushes (enemies can't see the red laser line). Also fire at minimap enemy icons for cross-screen picks.

### S3: AoE Continuous Stun Cage

Creates a cage that stuns enemies touching it (up to 3.3s). **S3 + Flash** (Flash during S3) lets you reposition the cage onto the enemy backline.

### S1: Dash + Knockback

Short dash. Next AA becomes the knockback punch. Use S1 → AA to peel Assassins off your carries, then S2 for guaranteed point-blank stun.
