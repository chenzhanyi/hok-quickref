# Mozi Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Two viable paths. **Mid Lane AP Poke** (Mask of Agony synergy) and **Support Tank CC** (max CDR). Both leverage S2's puddle for item effects.

---

## Build A: Mid Lane AP Poke (Recommended)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR for constant S2 spam. Core early tempo. |
| 2 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | S2's damage puddle endlessly triggers Mask's %HP burn. Poke king. |
| 3 | **Frost Breath (凝冰之息)** | S2 puddle + slow = enemies stuck in damage. Bonus damage on controlled targets. |
| 4 | **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** | S2 puddle + S3 cage multi-tick = instant 10-stack penetration. |
| 5 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | AP spike. Mozi's shield scales with AP — damage AND tankiness. |
| 6 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** or **Splendor (辉月)** | Penetration vs magic resist. Splendor for Stasis survival. |

---

## Build B: Support Tank CC

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎)** or **Shadow Aegis · Star Spring (极影·星泉)** | Team CDR + Mana regen. Redemption/Star Spring for ally sustain. |
| 2 | **Swift Boots (疾步之靴)** or **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Swift Boots for roaming S2 support. Resistance vs heavy CC. |
| 3 | **Rising Sun (旭日初光)** | S2 spam procs Rising Sun's shield on Mozi + lowest-HP ally. |
| 4 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + anti-ADC passive. |
| 5 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic Defense + spell shield. |
| 6 | **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** | 20% CDR. Combined with support item aura = 35-40% CDR. "Mobile machine gun." |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Magic Penetration, Movement Speed, Attack Speed (smoother 4th AA).**

### Setup 1: "Hunt 88-Pen" (Mid Lane / Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +16% AS, **+10% MS**. Smoothest 4th AA punch timing.

### Setup 2: "Harmony Sustain" (Support)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +88 Magic Penetration, +6% AS, **+450 Max Health**, +52 HP/5s, +4% MS. Safer laning and roaming.
