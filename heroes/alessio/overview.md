# Alessio（萊西奧） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Aerial Bombardier)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 火鷹船長 (Firehawk Captain)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (莱西奥)

> Alessio ("Firehawk Captain") is a uniquely designed burst-bombardment Marksman. His AAs deal AoE splash damage. His S2 launches him into the air — **he ignores terrain while airborne** and AAs/S3 extend his airtime. His S3 is a targeted missile barrage with **%missing HP execute damage.** Standing still reduces his AA interval by 25%.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Stand Still = Faster AAs

When Alessio stands still, his AA interval is **reduced by 25%.** In safe positions (behind frontline, free-farming), stand still and turret. When kiting, use the standard "AA → step → AA" rhythm.

### S2: Smoke Jump (Aerial + Stealth)

S2 launches Alessio into the air. While airborne: **ignores terrain, AAs and S3 extend airtime.** The smoke cloud on landing grants **Stealth to Alessio AND allies inside it** — enemies can't target you.

### S1: Empowered Shots

S1 empowers the next 3 AAs with larger AoE + bonus damage. **Pre-cast S1** and wait for CD to nearly reset before engaging — you can fire 4-5 empowered shots in one trade.

### S3: %Missing HP Execute + Unstoppable

S3 locks onto an enemy and fires homing missiles dealing **%missing HP damage (execute).** If cast from the ground: grants **Unstoppable** — use to tank CC.
