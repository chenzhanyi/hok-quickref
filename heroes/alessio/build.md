# Alessio Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** AAs deal AoE %Max HP damage. S3 has %missing HP execute. Needs **Critical Rate + Attack Speed + Physical Penetration.**

---

## Recommended Crit-Speed Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early AS for smoother AA rhythm. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots or defensive items.* |
| 2 | **Shadow Blade (影刃)** | **Core #1!** On-crit AS + MS. Keeps Alessio airborne longer and kiting smoother. |
| 3 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | Crit damage spike. Empowered AoE AAs now devastate. |
| 4 | **Dawn (破晓)** | Marksman capstone. 40% Penetration + AA damage amp. *(If behind: build Cloud Piercer (穿云弓) first.)* |
| 5 | **Blood Weeper (泣血之刃)** | 25% Lifesteal. AoE splash lifesteal stacks per target — one wave = full HP. |
| 6 | **Sun Bow (逐日之弓)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Sun Bow:** +Range — hit enemies from extreme distance. **Pure Sky:** active DR vs burst. |
