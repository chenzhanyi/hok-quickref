# Alessio Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Stand Still When Safe (25% Faster AAs)

Passive reduces AA interval by 25% when standing still. When your frontline is protecting you or you're free-farming: **don't move — just fire.** Maximum DPS. When kiting: standard "AA → step → AA."

### 2. S2: Aerial God Mode

S2 launches you airborne — you ignore terrain. AA and S3 **extend your airtime.** When Assassins dive: S2 up → AA + S3 from the sky → they can't touch you. Land in smoke → Stealth → escape.

### 3. Pre-Stack S1 for Burst

S1 empowers 3 AAs. Cast S1 early → wait for CD to nearly reset → engage. You'll get 3 empowered AAs, then S1 off CD again = 5-6 empowered shots in one trade.

### 4. S3: Ground Cast = Unstoppable

S3 from the ground grants Unstoppable during the cast. When Musashi S3, Lu Bu knockup, or any CC is incoming: **S3 their face** — you'll tank the CC and return fire.

### 5. S3: %Missing HP = Cleanup Tool

S3 deals %missing HP damage. Save it for **after** your initial burst, not before. The lower they are, the harder S3 hits.

## Signature Combos

### Aerial Bombardment: **S1 → S2 → AA (air) → S3 (air) → AA (air)**

1. S1 (empower)
2. S2 launch (airborne)
3. AA from the sky (extend airtime)
4. S3 from the sky (extend airtime further)
5. AA until landing → smoke stealth

### Anti-Dive: **S3 (ground) → S2 → AA (air)**

1. Assassin dives → S3 their face (Unstoppable + damage)
2. S2 launch → you're airborne
3. AA from the sky → they can't reach you
4. Land → smoke stealth → walk away

## Advanced Tips

- **AoE splash lifesteal stacks per target hit.** One empowered AA into a minion wave = massive HP restore.
- **S2 can cross any wall.** Use for creative gank paths and escapes over the thickest terrain.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs melee divers (S2 counters them). Struggles vs long-range poke that hits you in the air.
