# Ma Chao Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs high **CDR** for frequent spear throws, **Movement Speed** (which directly increases damage), and **semi-tank durability**. Full damage builds get deleted. CN server consensus: **MS semi-tank bruiser**.

---

## Recommended Semi-Tank Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** or **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** | Jungle Ma Chao: Red for burst, Purple for tankiness. Lane Ma Chao: skip jungle item, take Sprint. |
| 2 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | Early 15% CDR. Ma Chao desperately needs CDR for frequent spear throws. *Late game: swap to Resistance Boots when CDR overflows.* |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core Fighter item: Attack, Penetration, Health, CDR. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | **Ma Chao's mobility core.** Empowered dashes perfectly trigger Frost Grip's AoE slow, making him impossibly sticky. Also provides CDR and Physical Defense. |
| 5 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Taking damage grants up to **+10% Movement Speed and +10% damage**. For Ma Chao, MS = damage. This is an offense+defense hybrid item. |
| 6 | **Doomsday (末世)** or **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Doomsday:** on-hit HP damage vs tanky comps. **Star Breaker:** percentage Penetration. **Pure Sky:** active DR for survival. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Physical Penetration, Movement Speed (damage), Attack Speed (smoother dash animations).**

### Setup 1: CN Server "Hunt 100-Pen Speed" (Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, **+10% Movement Speed**, +10% Attack Speed.

### Setup 2: "Lifesteal Sustain" (Newcomer Friendly)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×7 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×3 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, +100 Physical Penetration, +7% Movement Speed, +7% Attack Speed, **+4.8% Physical Lifesteal**. Dash heals + rune lifesteal = near-infinite sustain.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Ma Chao is extremely sensitive to Attack Speed thresholds and Movement Speed feel. The safest setup:

1. Open **Preparation (备战)** → **Change Build (更换)**
2. Select **Recommended / Pro Player (推荐/大神配置)**
3. Copy the **CN #1** or **KPL pro** Ma Chao setup
4. Always take **Sprint** as summoner spell on lane Ma Chao
