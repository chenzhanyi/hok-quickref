# Ma Chao（馬超） — Assassin / Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin / Fighter (Carry Skirmisher)
- **Lane:** Jungle / Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 冷暉之槍 (Spear of Cold Dawn)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 马超)

> Ma Chao is an extremely high-ceiling carry Fighter/Assassin. His core mechanics revolve around **Spear Throwing & Retrieving** — his empowered dash-attack damage scales directly with **Movement Speed**. Every point of Movement Speed is a damage amplifier.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Movement Speed = Damage

Each ability cast increases Movement Speed (stacks up to 3 times). Ma Chao's empowered basic attack damage scales with his **total Movement Speed**. Any item, buff, or summoner spell that grants MS is a direct damage boost.

### Empowered Dash-Attack

Picking up a thrown Coldlight Spear grants an empowered basic attack — a **dash toward the joystick direction**. Hitting enemies with the dash **restores HP and reduces all ability cooldowns by 1 second**.

### Ultimate: Ten Thousand Spears (S3)

S3 recalls all thrown spears from the ground. Flying spears damage enemies in their path and deal **bonus damage based on target's missing Health (execute)**.

### "Hold a Spear" Principle

Never be spear-less. Before ganking, teamfighting, or trading, throw a spear at your feet (S1 or S2), pick it up, and hold the empowered attack. One in hand + one ready on the ground = continuous dashing.
