# Ma Chao Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Movement Speed = Damage

Every ability cast grants stacking Movement Speed (max 3 stacks). Ma Chao's empowered dash-attack damage scales directly with his total MS. **Anything that gives movement speed is a damage item for Ma Chao.**

### 2. Empowered Dash Mechanics

Pick up a thrown spear → empowered basic attack → **dash in the joystick direction**. This is NOT auto-targeted — you must aim with the left joystick. Dash hits **restore HP and reduce all cooldowns by 1 second.**

### 3. Always Hold a Spear ("存枪")

Never engage without a spear in hand:
- Throw S1 or S2 at your feet
- Pick it up
- Start the fight with one empowered dash ready

### 4. Release the Attack Button, Use Left Joystick Only

The empowered dash goes toward your **left joystick direction**, not toward a locked target. For precise dashes, release the right attack button and aim purely with the left joystick.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take ultimate whenever available.** S2 can be precisely placed at your feet — it's the core tool for instant pickup and fluid kiting.

---

## Signature Combos

### Standard Double Dash: **Hold Spear → AA → S2 → AA**

1. Pre-hold a spear (empowered AA)
2. Dash (AA) toward enemy
3. Mid-dash, throw S2 at your feet or enemy's feet
4. Step forward to pick up → second dash (AA) immediately

### Ultimate Infinite Combo: **Hold Spear → AA → S3 → AA → S3 → AA**

1. Hold spear → dash (AA)
2. Throw spear behind enemy + press S3 (recall)
3. Spear flies back through enemy (execute damage on path)
4. Catch returning spear → dash (AA) again
5. Repeat S3 → AA cycle. As long as your rhythm is clean, infinite dashing.

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Sprint summoner spell is core:** Sprint massively boosts your MS → directly converts to damage. Always take Sprint on lane Ma Chao.
- **Practice the rhythm:** Ma Chao's ceiling is entirely in your dash timing. One missed pickup = combo over.
- **River combat advantage:** Ma Chao's MS scaling makes him extra dangerous near river speed shrines.
- **Matchup:** Destroys immobile backline carries. Struggles vs heavy point-and-click CC (Daji, Zhang Liang).
