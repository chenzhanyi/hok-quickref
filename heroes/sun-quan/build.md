# Sun Quan Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Damage fully scales with **Magic Attack.** Needs semi-tank stats for melee brawling. CN server consensus: **AP semi-tank bruiser.**

---

## Recommended Semi-Tank Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Sun Quan must melee AA for shields — CC-lock = death. |
| 2 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | S3 water zone + S1 multi-hit endlessly triggers Mask's %HP burn. King of cost-efficiency. |
| 3 | **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | **Absolute core!** AP + Max HP. Converts AP to dual defenses (up to 250 each). With enhanced AA shields, you're tank-level durable. |
| 4 | **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** | S3 water zone's multi-tick stacks 10 penetration layers instantly. |
| 5 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** or **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** | Physical or Magic defense based on enemy comp. |
| 6 | **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** or **Splendor (辉月)** | **Tome:** max AP — Prophet's Hour defenses also hit cap. **Splendor:** Stasis vs burst. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Magic Lifesteal, Magic Penetration, Max HP.**

### Setup 1: "Harmony Tank AP" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +88 Magic Penetration, +450 Max Health, +4% MS, +52 HP/5s. Safer early laning + strong mid-game.

### Setup 2: "Lifesteal Skirmish"

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Greed (贪婪) | ×7 | Magic Lifesteal +1.6% |
