# Sun Quan（孫權） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Magic Fighter / AP Bruiser)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane / Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 定旌之謀 (Strategist of the Banner)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (孙权)

> Sun Quan, leader of the Eastern Wu faction, is a dominant all-round Magic Fighter/Mage-Tank. His core mechanics revolve around **Sword Seals** — stacking marks via S1/S2, then detonating them with enhanced AAs for massive **%Max HP shield + heal.** His S3 creates a persistent water zone that slows, damages, and grants him bonus MS + DR. He excels at zone control and extended skirmishes.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Sword Seal Stacking: Skill → AA Rhythm

S1 and S2 apply Sword Seals. At 3 stacks: enhanced AA deals bonus damage + **%Max HP heal + shield.** Never dump all abilities — always: **Skill → AA → Skill → AA → Skill → AA.**

### S3: Water Zone (Your Arena)

S3 creates a persistent water zone that slows enemies, deals continuous Magic Damage, and grants Sun Quan **bonus MS + %DR while inside.** Also grants **Unstoppable** during the cast. Use to: create your own arena, block enemy retreat paths, isolate frontline from backline.

### River/Water Combat Advantage

Sun Quan gains bonus MS and DR near rivers or in his own S3 water zone. Always fight near Dragon/Baron pit or in river corridors.
