# Sun Quan Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Skill → AA Rhythm (Never Dump All Skills)

S1 and S2 apply Sword Seals. Each enhanced AA at 3 stacks grants **%Max HP heal + shield.** Always: S1 → AA → S2 → AA → S1 → AA. Dumping all skills at once means you miss 2 shields.

### 2. S3: Unstoppable Cast + Zone Control

S3 grants Unstoppable during cast and leaves a water zone. The zone: slows enemies, deals continuous Magic Damage, grants Sun Quan **bonus MS + %DR.** Use S3 to: create your arena, block enemy retreat, isolate enemy frontline from backline.

### 3. Fight Near Water

Sun Quan gains bonus MS + DR near rivers or inside S3's water. Always force fights at Dragon/Baron pit or river corridors. Never fight in dry jungle if you can avoid it.

### 4. S3: Anti-Dive Tool

When an Assassin (Lanling Wang, Jing) dives you: **S3 immediately.** The Unstoppable cast ignores their CC, and the water zone slows them while you gain DR. Then S2 away → S1 + enhanced AA for the counter-kill.

### 5. S2: Gap-Close + Wall Hop

S2 dashes forward (can cross thin walls) with a brief stun. Use to: engage from bush, escape over walls, or reposition for S1 angle.

---

## Signature Combos

### Mid-Range Engage: **S2 → S1 → AA → S3 → AA**

1. S2 dash + stun
2. S1 + AA (3 seals → shield + heal)
3. S3 water zone (enemy is slowed, you gain DR)
4. AA finish inside your zone

### Anti-Dive Counter: **S3 → S2 → S1 → AA**

1. Assassin dives → S3 (Unstoppable + water zone)
2. S2 dash away (create distance)
3. S1 + enhanced AA (shield + heal)
4. They're slowed in water, you're shielded — turn and kill

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 water zone persists for several seconds.** Pre-cast it on objectives (Dragon/Baron) to zone enemies away.
- **Sword Seals have a generous stack window.** You can stack on one target, then switch to another with the enhanced AA.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in extended fights near water. Struggles in dry, open areas.
