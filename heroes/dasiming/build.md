# Dasiming Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Semi-tank drain bruiser. Needs **Attack + Penetration + Tankiness.** Not an AS Hero — tether is continuous. Full damage gets deleted before tether ramps. CN server consensus: **semi-tank smite jungler.**

---

## Recommended Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** | HP stacking + burn aura. Highest winrate. Tankiness lets tether ramp. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. CC breaks tether flow. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core damage. CDR + flat Penetration + Health. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | Enhanced AA or final tether tick triggers AoE slow. Enemies can't escape the chain. |
| 5 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Stacking MS + damage while taking hits. Perfect for extended tether brawls. |
| 6 | **Phoenix Eye (不死鸟之眼)** or **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** | **Phoenix Eye:** amplifies S3 heal + tether drain — "medical miracle." **Eternal Night:** vs burst magic. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Zero Attack Speed needed. **Physical Penetration + Attack + Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Stealth 100-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% MS. Max early jungle invade power.

### Setup 2: "Harmony Sustain" (Safer)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, +100 Physical Penetration, **+452 Max Health**, +52 HP/5s, +4% MS. Tankier early game.
