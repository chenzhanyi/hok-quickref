# Dasiming Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. AA Tether: Move, Don't Mash

Dasiming's AA is a continuous tether. **Don't keep pressing the AA button** — press once, then focus entirely on movement. The tether persists for up to 3 seconds while you freely reposition. The final tick is the burst.

### 2. S2 → AA: Multi-Tether (Drain Everyone)

S2 leaves marks. When enemies step on them, your next AA **tethers ALL marked enemies simultaneously.** In teamfights: S2 into the enemy cluster → AA → you're draining 3+ enemies at once.

### 3. S1: Wall-Hack Jungle Rotations

S1 creates a path. If it crosses terrain: **you walk through the wall.** Use for: fastest jungle clears, creative gank angles, escapes over walls. Master the path placement — this is Dasiming's signature mobility.

### 4. S3: Clone the Enemy ADC

S3 summons a clone of any enemy Hero hit. **Always S3 the enemy ADC or fed carry.** Their clone attacks with a portion of their stats — an S3'd Hou Yi clone can solo-kill Hou Yi's own Support.

### 5. Tether Ignores Dashes and Flash

The soul chain tether is hard to break. Enemies who Flash or dash stay tethered unless they go beyond max range. Use this to tower-dive: tether outside tower → walk in as the tether ticks → final tick kills → walk out.

## Signature Combos

### Long-Range Gank: **S1 → S2 → AA → S3 → AA**

1. S1 wall-hack path into lane
2. S2 mark on the enemy
3. AA tether (continuous damage while you walk with them)
4. S3 slam (clone + heal)
5. AA finish

### Teamfight: **S2 → AA → S3 → S1**

1. S2 into enemy cluster (marks multiple)
2. AA multi-tether (draining everyone)
3. S3 (clone their ADC + massive heal)
4. S1 wall-hack to chase or escape

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Tether works on towers?** No. But you can tether enemies from outside tower range and walk in for the final tick.
- **S3 clone inherits on-hit effects.** Items like Doomsday and Blood Weeper apply through the clone.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile comps. Struggles vs burst that kills before tether ramps.
