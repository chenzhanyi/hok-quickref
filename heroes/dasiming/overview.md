# Dasiming（大司命） — Fighter / Tank

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter / Tank (Soul Chain Jungler)
- **Lane:** Jungle / Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 肅歸之戈 (Spear of Solemn Return)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (大司命)

> Dasiming is a mythic-level jungle dominator. His AAs are a **soul chain tether** — continuous damage while he moves freely (no attack-move needed). His S1 creates a path that lets him **ignore terrain and pass through walls.** His S3 slams down and **summons a clone of any enemy Hero hit** to fight for him. He's a sustain-drain brawler, not a burst Assassin.

---

## Core Mechanics

### AA: Soul Chain Tether (Move While Attacking)

Dasiming's AA is a tether that continuously damages enemies for up to 3 seconds. **You don't need to keep pressing AA** — just hold the joystick to move and the chain persists. The final tick deals massive burst damage. The tether is difficult to break with dashes/Flash — only extreme range breaks it.

### S1: Terrain-Ignoring Path

S1 creates a path. Dasiming gains massive MS on it. **If S1 crosses a wall, he can walk through it.** This makes him one of the fastest junglers for rotations and escapes.

### S2: Mark → Multi-Tether

S2 strikes the ground, leaving marks. Enemies stepping on marks cause Dasiming's next AA to **tether multiple enemies simultaneously.** Farm multiple jungle camps or drain multiple enemies in teamfights.

### S3: Clone Summon

S3 slams down (AoE damage + heal). **Any enemy Hero hit spawns a clone** that fights for Dasiming — the clone has a portion of the original's Attack. S3 an enemy ADC and their clone shreds their own team.
