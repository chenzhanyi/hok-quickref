# Sikong Zhen（司空震） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Magic Bruiser / Thunder Lord)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 雷霆之王 (Thunder King)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (司空震)

> Sikong Zhen is a dominant melee Magic Fighter (also a strong Jungler). He builds Energy via AAs to refresh S1/S2, and every ability grants an enhanced AA. His S3 transforms him into the Thunder Lord: during the 1.5s ascension, he's **Unstoppable, terrain-ignoring, and at full Energy.** All damage is full Magic Damage.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Energy + Enhanced AA Cycle

Each ability grants an enhanced AA. Every 6 Energy = S1/S2 refreshed. The rhythm: **Ability → Enhanced AA → Ability → Enhanced AA.** Never dump two abilities without the AA between them.

### Melee vs Ranged AA

- **Melee AA (point-blank):** Faster attack speed = faster Energy. Use for: jungle, wave clear, 1v1 brawling.
- **Ranged AA (distance):** Slower but AoE chain lightning. Use for: poke, safe farming.
- **Melee Enhanced AA:** Grants a **shield.** Ranged Enhanced AA: **chain AoE damage.**

### S3: 1.5s Ascension (Unstoppable)

S3 ascends for 1.5s: **Unstoppable, ignores terrain, full Energy.** Use to dodge CC (Musashi S3, Lu Bu S3) or chase over walls. Post-landing: 10s of double Energy gain from AAs.
