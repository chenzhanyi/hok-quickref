# Sikong Zhen Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure Magic Damage. AA-reliant — needs **Attack Speed + Magic Attack.** Two paths: Jungle (most common) and Clash Lane.

---

## Recommended Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Rune Greatsword (符文大剑)** | Blue jungle knife: AP stacking + enhanced AA. |
| 2 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | AS for fast Energy stacking. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs CC.* |
| 3 | **Golden Sword (金色圣剑)** | **Core #1!** AS + on-hit Magic Damage. Max stacks = massive AS. Perfect for enhanced AA spam. |
| 4 | **Lightning Dagger (闪电匕首)** or **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | **Lightning Dagger:** chain lightning + AS (pure damage). **Prophet's Hour:** AP + dual defenses (semi-tank). |
| 5 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP spike. Enhanced AAs + S3 damage skyrockets. |
| 6 | **Devourer's Tome (噬神之书)** or **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | **Devourer's Tome:** Lifesteal for extended brawls. **Void Staff:** Penetration vs Magic Cloak. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Attack Speed (AA rhythm), Magic Penetration, Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "32% AS Speed" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1.6%, Critical Rate +0.5% |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** **+32% Attack Speed**, +64 Magic Penetration, +10% MS, +5% Crit. Fastest Energy stacking. *Note: 0% Lifesteal — buy Vampiric Scythe early.*

### Setup 2: "Lifesteal Sustain" (Safe Lane)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Greed (贪婪) | ×7 | Magic Lifesteal +1.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×3 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +25% AS, +64 Magic Penetration, **+11.2% Magic Lifesteal**, +3% MS. Healthier trades vs Kuang Tie, Sun Ce.
