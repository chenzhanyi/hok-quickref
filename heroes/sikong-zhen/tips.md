# Sikong Zhen Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Ability → AA Rhythm (Never Skip the AA)

Every ability grants an enhanced AA. **Never cast two abilities without the AA between them.** Correct: S1 → AA → S2 → AA. Wrong: S1 → S2 (wasted enhanced AA). This is the #1 Sikong Zhen mistake.

### 2. Melee AA for Speed, Ranged for Safety

- **Melee (point-blank):** Faster AA = faster Energy = faster ability refreshes. Always melee when farming or trading up close.
- **Ranged (distance):** Slower but safer. Use for poke and when kiting.

### 3. Melee Enhanced AA = Shield

Melee enhanced AA grants a shield. When trading: S1 → dash in → melee enhanced AA → shield absorbs counter-damage → continue trading.

### 4. S3 Ascension: Dodge Everything

S3's 1.5s ascension is Unstoppable + terrain-ignoring. Use reactively: Musashi S3? S3. Lu Bu S3? S3. Collapsed on? S3 over a wall.

### 5. Pre-Stack Energy Before S3

Before teamfighting: AA minions/jungle to 4-5 Energy. S3 in → you're full Energy on landing → immediate enhanced AA spam. With post-S3 double Energy gain, you chain abilities indefinitely.

## Signature Combos

### Basic Trade: **S1 → AA → S2 → AA**

1. S1 dash + enhanced AA (melee = shield)
2. S2 push + enhanced AA
3. Energy builds → S1 refreshed → repeat

### Full Energy Burst: **S1 → AA → S1 → AA → S2 → AA**

1. Start at full Energy: S1 + AA
2. S1 refreshed (Energy spent) + AA
3. S2 + AA
4. Maximum DPS cycle

### S3 Teamfight Entry: **AA×4 (pre-stack) → S3 → S1 → AA → S2 → AA → S1...**

1. Pre-stack 4-5 Energy on minions
2. S3 ascend (dodge CC, reposition)
3. Land with full Energy + double Energy gain
4. S1+AA+S2+AA+S1+AA... infinite cycle

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3's double Energy gain lasts 10 seconds.** Maximize AA output during this window.
- **Ranged enhanced AA chains to nearby enemies.** Use when enemies are clustered.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in extended fights. Struggles vs burst that kills before enhanced AA shields stack.
