# Taiyi Zhenren Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Gold Passive: Stick to Your Carry

Nearby minion/monster deaths grant **+20% Gold** to Taiyi and allies. Early through mid-game, glue yourself to your ADC. Even passive farming generates a ~1,000 Gold lead. This is Taiyi's win condition.

### 2. S1 Infinite Refresh

Full-charge S1 explosion that hits an enemy Hero = **instant S1 cooldown refresh**. Chain explosions = infinite CC. The combo: S1 (charge) → S2 (hook) → S1 auto-explodes → S1 again immediately.

### 3. S2-Flash Engage

S2's tentacle has limited range, but **Flash mid-flight extends range or changes trajectory**. Combined with a charging S1: **S1 (charging) → Flash into enemies → manual S1 detonate → S2 hook → S1 recharge**.

### 4. S3 Resurrection: The Golden Window

S3 lasts ~3 seconds. Timing is everything:
- **Too early (ally >50% HP):** enemies stop attacking, buff expires wasted
- **Too late:** ally dies before cast completes
- **Perfect:** right before the killing blow — Zhuge Liang S3 about to land, Wukong bonk incoming, Marco S3 spinning

### 5. S1 + Flash = Undodgeable Engage

S1 starts charging → **Flash directly onto enemy backline** → manually detonate S1. Instant AoE stun with zero warning. Follow with S2 to lock down survivors.

## Signature Combo

### Perfect Chain CC: **S1 (charge) → Flash → S1 (detonate) → S2 → S1 (recharge)**

1. Start S1 charging from bush or fog of war
2. Flash into enemy cluster
3. Manual S1 detonate — AoE stun
4. S2 hook to lock down priority target
5. S1 (instantly refreshed from first explosion) → second stun

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3's resurrection scales with AP.** More AP = more HP on revive. But full AP Taiyi is too squishy — hybrid or tank is safer.
- **Don't waste S3 on yourself if you're out of position.** Save it for the carry.
- **S1 charging makes noise.** Charge from bushes or behind walls to mask the audio cue.
- **Matchup:** God-tier with hyper-carry ADCs. Struggles vs teams that can instantly delete before S3 activates.
