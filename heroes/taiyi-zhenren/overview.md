# Taiyi Zhenren（太乙真人） — Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Support (Economy / Resurrection)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 鍊金大師 (Alchemy Grandmaster)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 太乙真人)

> Taiyi Zhenren is a strategically invaluable "economy + resurrection" Support. His passive grants nearby allies **bonus Gold**, and his S3 is the game's only ally-resurrection ability — a portable "second life." A well-played Taiyi single-handedly shifts the economic balance of the game.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Gold Printer

When nearby non-Hero units die, Taiyi and nearby allies gain **+20% bonus Gold**. Stick to your ADC/Marksman. Even if they just farm normally, they'll be ~1,000 Gold ahead of the enemy carry by mid-game. This is Taiyi's entire identity — **win through economic advantage.**

### S1: Infinite Refresh

S1 charges up and explodes. **If the full-charge explosion hits an enemy Hero, S1's cooldown instantly refreshes.** Land your explosions and you can chain-control indefinitely.

### S2: Hook + S2-Flash

S2 shoots a tentacle that latches onto enemies, allies, or objects (Shouyue wards). **Can be combined with Flash** to extend range or change trajectory mid-flight.

### S3: Resurrection (Timing Is Everything)

S3 places a buff on nearby allies. If they die within ~3 seconds, they **resurrect with %Max HP.** The #1 skill check for Taiyi players:
- **Too early:** enemies see it and stop attacking; the buff expires wasted
- **Too late:** ally dies before cast completes
- **Perfect timing:** right before the fatal hit lands
