# Taiyi Zhenren Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs high **CDR** (frequent S1/S3), enough **tankiness** (walks into enemies to land S1), and **Movement Speed**. Full tank support.

---

## Recommended Support Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎)** or **Shadow Aegis · Star Spring (极影·星泉)** | Shadow Aegis aura: AS + CDR + Mana regen. **Redemption:** group shield. **Star Spring:** sustained lane healing for ADC. |
| 2 | **Swift Boots (疾步之靴)** | Movement Speed to land S1 explosions. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC.* |
| 3 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Reduces enemy AS/MS on-hit — survival after diving in. |
| 4 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic Defense + spell shield. Prevents getting poked out before engaging. |
| 5 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** | Massive HP pool. Taiyi's S3 revive scales with **%Max HP** — more HP = healthier revive. Continuous regen for map presence. |
| 6 | **Rising Sun (旭日初光)** or **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** | CDR filler. **Rising Sun:** shields ally on ability hit. **Glacial Storm:** 20% CDR + Physical Defense. |

## Quick In-Game Setup

Taiyi's S3 resurrection scales with AP, but full AP builds are too fragile in the current meta. Stick to tank and let the Gold passive carry. Copy the **CN #1 Taiyi** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
