# Lu Bu Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** True Damage ignores armor — Lu Bu needs **zero Penetration items** (no Shadow Axe, no Star Breaker). His damage items are: **Attack, Critical, Attack Speed.** Semi-tank is meta.

---

## Recommended Semi-Tank True Damage Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Lu Bu must cast S1 and S2 — getting CC-locked = no Enchant = dead. |
| 2 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Taking hits grants stacking MS + damage — perfect for Lu Bu's frontline brawling. |
| 3 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | Attack + CDR. Active DR prevents getting burst during S3 landing. **Can be used while CC'd.** |
| 4 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Lu Bu's soul item!** Massive Attack. +30% vs <50% HP — stacked with True Damage, one Enchanted AA deletes squishies. |
| 5 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic Defense + spell shield. Counters burst Mages. |
| 6 | **Shadow Blade (影刃)** or **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** / **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | **Shadow Blade:** AS + Crit for snowballing True Damage DPS. **Tank items:** for frontline initiation. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Attack Speed (AA smoothness), Max Health (lane sustain), Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Red Moon AS Kite" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1.6%, Critical Rate +0.5% |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, Cooldown Reduction +0.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +16% Attack Speed, +0.5% Critical Rate, +375 Max Health, +6% CDR, +16 Physical Attack, **+10% MS**. Silky-smooth Enchanted AA kiting.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Lu Bu's level 1 matters. Vs weak laners: S1 for early trade. Vs lane bullies: S2 for safe farming. Once Pure Sky + Siege Breaker are online, Lu Bu becomes a late-game god. Copy the **CN #1 Lu Bu** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
