# Lu Bu（呂布） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (True Damage / Drain Tank)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 無雙之魔 (Peerless Demon)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 吕布)

> Lu Bu is a uniquely perennial Clash Lane king. His signature: **True Damage.** After S1 or S3 hits an enemy Hero, his weapon becomes **Enchanted** — all subsequent AAs and abilities deal True Damage that ignores armor and shields. He's the ultimate tank shredder and one of the few Heroes who can 1v5 when fed.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S1: Enchant Weapon (Your Entire Identity)

S1 sweeps in a wide arc. **If it hits an enemy Hero, Lu Bu's weapon becomes Enchanted** — all damage becomes True Damage. **If S1 misses or S3 doesn't hit anyone, Enchant expires.** Never fight without Enchant active.

### S2: Absorb Shield → HP

S2 drains shield from targets (max 3 — Minions, Monsters, Heroes). **The shield slowly converts to HP.** In lane: S2 three minions → walk away → repeat. Infinite sustain without recalling.

### S3: Strategic Ultimate

S3 leaps to a target area, **knocking up and granting massive DR.** The arena edge slows and heals Lu Bu on contact. Uses: engage, escape (only long-range mobility), zone control, bush-check.

### S1 + Flash

S1 has a noticeable wind-up. **Press S1, then Flash during the swing** to reposition the arc. Surprise-enchant enemies who thought they were safe.

### Attack Range Advantage

Lu Bu's AA range is longer than most melee Fighters. After enchanting, kite at max range — Mulan and Fuzi can't reach you while you True-Damage them for free.
