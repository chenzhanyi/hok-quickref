# Lu Bu Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Never Fight Without Enchant

S1 must hit an enemy Hero to Enchant. If S1 misses: **disengage immediately.** You deal Physical Damage (reduced by armor) without Enchant. Wait for S1 cooldown (~5s), then try again.

### 2. S2 Sustain: Shield → HP

S2 absorbs shield from up to 3 targets. The shield **converts to HP over time.** In lane: S2 three minions → retreat → repeat. This lets Lu Bu sustain indefinitely without recalling. The original "lane bully sustain king."

### 3. S1 + Flash: Surprise Enchant

S1 has a wind-up. Enemies at max range will walk away. Solution: **Press S1 → Flash mid-swing** toward the enemy. The arc repositions to your Flash destination. Guaranteed Enchant on targets who thought they dodged.

### 4. S3: Don't Blindly Initiate

In high ELO, raw S3 is easily dodged. Better uses:
- **Follow-up:** After an ally lands CC
- **Counter-engage:** When enemies commit to a dive
- **Zone control:** Block escape paths in jungle corridors
- **Escape:** S3 over walls when collapsed on

### 5. AA Range Kiting

Lu Bu's AA range is longer than most melee Fighters. After enchanting, **AA at max range** — Mulan, Fuzi, and other melee Heroes can't reach you while you deal True Damage for free.

## Signature Combos

### Lane Trade: **S2 → S1 → AA → AA → retreat**

1. S2 minion wave + enemy (max shield)
2. S1 enchant (hits enemy + wave)
3. AA ×2 with True Damage at max range
4. Walk away before they can trade back

### Teamfight Perfect: **S1 (enchant) → S3 → AA → S2 → AA**

1. S1 enchant off enemy frontline
2. S3 directly onto enemy backline (knockup + enchant refresh)
3. AA — True Damage with Siege Breaker execute
4. S2 for shield → AA finish

## Advanced Tips

- **Level 1: skill S1 vs weak laners, S2 vs lane bullies (Sun Ce, Kuang Tie).**
- **S3 grants massive DR on landing.** Use to tank tower shots during dives.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs tanks and immobile comps. Struggles vs hyper-mobile kiting Heroes.
