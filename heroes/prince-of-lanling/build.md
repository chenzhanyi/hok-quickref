# Prince of Lanling Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure burst. Skills have massive **Bonus AD scaling** — stack Physical Attack. Early-game monster, falls off late. Must guarantee one-shot on squishies.

---

## Recommended Jungle Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Standard Assassin jungle item: Attack, Penetration, CDR, MS. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Mandatory. Lanling is paper — getting CC'd on engage = instant death. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core first major item. 15% CDR, flat Penetration, Health. Early gank power spike. |
| 4 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Lanling's soul item!** Massive AD amplifies all skills. +30% vs <50% HP **stacks multiplicatively with S2's 24% missing-HP execute** — absurd kill threshold. |
| 5 | **Master's Power (宗师之力)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Master's Power:** S2→AA one-shots. **Pure Sky:** active DR for teamfight dives. |
| 6 | **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** or **Fate's Edge (名刀·司命)** | **Star Breaker:** 40% Penetration vs armor. **Fate's Edge:** fatal-damage immunity. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Zero Attack Speed or Critical needed. **Pure Physical Penetration + Attack + Movement Speed.**

### Setup: "Stealth 100-Pen" (Universal Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, **+10% Movement Speed**. Combined with passive's +20% MS toward enemies, Lanling is the fastest jungler in early game.

### Micro-Adjustment: "Reave Sustain"

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×8 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×2 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |
*(Red + Green unchanged)*

**Total:** +100 Physical Penetration, +8% MS, **+3.2% Physical Lifesteal**. Healthier jungle clears without recalling.
