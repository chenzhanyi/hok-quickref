# Prince of Lanling（蘭陵王） — Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin (Stealth / Psychological Warfare)
- **Lane:** Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 暗影刀鋒 (Shadow Blade)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 兰陵王)

> The Prince of Lanling is a strategically terrifying Stealth Assassin. His core mechanic is near-permanent invisibility — he applies psychological pressure just by existing. Enemy carries see the "!" warning over their heads and must play defensively or risk instant deletion. He's an early-game monster who can snowball games out of control before the enemy team can scale.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Face-Toward-Enemy Speed Boost

Moving **toward an enemy Hero** grants **+20% Movement Speed** (trail effect behind him). This passive doubles as a **bush-check tool**: walk toward a suspicious bush — if you suddenly speed up, someone's inside.

### Stealth: The "!" Mind Game

Lanling's S3/ultimate grants long-duration stealth. Enemies within a certain radius see a **flashing "!" above their head**. If Lanling stays inside that radius for >3 seconds, stealth breaks. **Sometimes the threat of engagement is stronger than engaging.** Walk near enemies to zone them off CS, then leave without attacking. The psychological damage is real.

### S2: Delayed Execute

S2 marks enemies. Follow-up AA/ability **stuns for 1s and heals Lanling**. When the mark expires, it deals **24% of the target's missing HP as Physical Damage** — a built-in execute. After a full combo, walk away confidently even if they're at 5% HP. The mark will finish them.

### Post-Stealth AS Boost

Breaking stealth grants **+50% Attack Speed** briefly. When farming jungle: enter stealth → immediately break it → enjoy the AS boost for faster clears.
