# Prince of Lanling Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Psychological Warfare: The "!" Is Your Weapon

When you're stealthed near enemies, they see "!" above their heads. Sometimes **don't attack.** Just walk near them. They'll: forfeit CS, burn Flashes, huddle under tower. You zone them without spending a single cooldown. This is Lanling's highest-level skill.

### 2. Passive = Free Bush-Check

Moving toward an enemy grants +20% MS. When approaching a suspicious bush at an angle, if you suddenly speed up → someone's inside. No ability needed.

### 3. S2 Execute: Walk Away Confidently

S2 mark deals **24% missing HP damage** on expiration. After a full combo, if the enemy is at ~5-10% HP, just walk away. The mark will kill them. Don't overstay for one extra AA and get collapsed on.

### 4. Fastest Guaranteed Kill Combo: **S2 → S1 → S3 → AA**

S2 mark (slow) → S1 immediately (guaranteed stun because S2 was already applied) → S3 dash through → AA. This combo has zero delay — enemies can't Flash or Stasis between hits. The most reliable deletion sequence.

### 5. Post-Stealth AS Boost for Farming

Breaking stealth grants +50% AS. When clearing jungle: stealth → immediately break stealth with an ability → enjoy the AS boost. Significantly faster clears.

### 6. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take S3 whenever available.** S2 is core: more damage, lower CD, more healing.

## Signature Combos

### Guaranteed Delete: **S2 → S1 → S3 → AA**

1. Stealth → approach
2. S2 mark + slow
3. S1 — guaranteed stun (S2 mark already applied)
4. S3 dash through
5. AA — they're dead or the S2 mark will finish them

### Long-Range Chase: **S2 → S3 → S1 → AA**

1. S2 mark from range
2. S3 long dash (stuns + closes gap)
3. S1 + AA to finish
*Risk: if S3 misses, you're exposed with no escape.*

## Advanced Tips

- **Lanling is an early-game monster, late-game minion.** End games before 15 minutes or you fall off hard.
- **Don't engage tanks.** Your mission is the enemy ADC and Mage. Nothing else matters.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile squishies. Struggles vs tanky comps late game.
