# Feyd Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Wall Energy Management

Wall jumps consume energy (max 4 jumps). Running out = you fall off and are vulnerable. **Always track your remaining jumps.** Don't greed for one more jump — land with S1 before energy runs out.

### 2. S2 → S1 → Hit → S2: The Infinite Cycle

Wall-S1 hitting an enemy **refunds S2 cooldown.** Cycle: S2 (wall) → S1 dive → hit enemy → S2 immediately back up → repeat. This is how Feyd becomes untouchable.

### 3. Wall Vision: Never Get Ambushed

On walls, Feyd gains **elevated vision** that reveals enemies in bushes below. Use walls to scout enemy jungle safely. You'll see them before they see you.

### 4. S3: Watch for the Red Execute Line

S3 marks enemies. When their HP drops below the execute threshold, a **red indicator appears** on their health bar. Reactivate S3 → they die. Don't waste S3's second cast before the red line appears.

### 5. S3 + Siege Breaker = 50% HP Execute

Siege Breaker (+30% vs <50% HP) stacks with S3's execute. Enemies die at half HP instead of quarter HP. Rush Siege Breaker after Shadow Axe.

## Signature Combos

### Long-Range Backline Dive: **S2 (wall) → S1 (dive) → AA → S1 (dash) → S3 → S3 (execute)**

1. S2 mount wall → reposition behind enemy
2. S1 dive from wall → knockup + shield
3. Enhanced AA
4. S1 second cast (dash to stick)
5. S3 sweep + mark
6. Wait for red line → S3 execute

### Harass Cycle: **S2 → S1 → hit → S2 → S1 → hit → S2...**

1. S2 up → S1 down → hit (S2 CD refunded)
2. S2 back up immediately
3. Repeat until enemy is low → S3 execute

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Feyd is weak in base-defense fights (few walls).** Push lanes aggressively to fight in enemy jungle — more walls = your domain.
- **Hard CC (Donghuang, Liang) knocks you off walls.** Avoid these matchups or wait for their CC to be spent.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in jungle-heavy maps. Struggles in open-base scenarios.
