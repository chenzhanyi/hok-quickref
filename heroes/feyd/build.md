# Feyd Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Skill-burst Assassin. Needs **CDR** (wall mount frequency) and **Physical Penetration** (S3 execute threshold). CN server consensus: **CDR Penetration burst.**

---

## Recommended Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Attack, Penetration, CDR. Standard Assassin jungle item. |
| 2 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR for S2 wall mount. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC.* |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core. CDR + flat Penetration + Health. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Frost Grip:** wall-S1 → AA triggers AoE slow. **Pure Sky:** active DR for dives. |
| 5 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Feyd's soul item!** +30% vs <50% HP. Stacks with S3 execute — enemies die at half HP. |
| 6 | **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** or **Fate's Edge (名刀·司命)** | Penetration vs armor, or fatal-damage immunity. |
