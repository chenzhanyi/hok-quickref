# Feyd（暃） — Assassin

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin (Wall-Runner / Execute)
- **Lane:** Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 靈玉之子 (Child of Spirit Jade)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (暃)

> Feyd is a uniquely designed "wall-running" Assassin. His S2 lets him **mount and run along walls** — gaining massive MS, vision advantage, and immunity to bush ambushes. From walls, his S1 becomes a dive-bomb with knockup. His S3 is a two-stage execute: sweep → mark → reactivate for **%HP execute** that kills enemies below a threshold.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Wall Mode (S2): Your Entire Identity

S2 mounts walls. On walls: massive MS, **elevated vision (anti-bush)**, and S1 transforms.
- **Wall energy:** Up to 4 jumps between walls. Running out of energy or getting hard-CC'd = you fall off and are vulnerable.
- **S1 from wall:** Dive down → **knockup + shield + enhanced AA.** Second S1 cast: dash in a direction.
- **Hitting enemies with wall-S1 refunds S2 cooldown.** Cycle: S2 → S1 down → hit → S2 back up → repeat.

### S3: Two-Stage Execute

S3 sweeps (marks enemies). Reactivate: multi-slash with **%HP execute.** When enemy HP drops below a threshold, a **red execute indicator appears** — reactivate S3 and they die. Walk away confidently.
