# Niu Mo（牛魔） — Tank / Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Tank / Support (Hard Engage / Bodyguard)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 精英酋長 (Elite Chieftain)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 牛魔)

> Niu Mo is a classic hard-engage Tank Support with massive AoE CC and a powerful defensive passive. He's a universal pick that pairs well with burst ADCs and Assassins. His signature "S3 + Flash" can single-handedly win teamfights by knocking up the entire enemy backline.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Dual Defense Aura

When an ally is within 500 range, Niu Mo AND the lowest-HP nearby ally gain **massive Physical + Magic Defense.** Always stay near your carry — the passive is the difference between them surviving a dive or getting one-shot.

### S1: Weaken Enemy Output

S1 sweeps, reducing the **Physical AND Magic damage output** of enemies hit for 3 seconds. Always tag the enemy Assassin or carry with S1 during teamfights.

### S2: Charge + Knockup

S2 charges forward with a brief wind-up, knocking up and slowing enemies. Cast from bushes to hide the animation, or chain after ally CC.

### S3: The "Big Flash" (Signature Play)

S3 channels then slams the ground, creating a fissure that knocks up and leaves a burning trail (slow + damage). The channel is highly telegraphed — enemies WILL dodge it. The solution: **S3 + Flash.** Press S3, then Flash during the slam animation toward enemies. The knockup and fissure reposition with you.

### Reverse S3 + Flash

If enemies expect the forward engage: **aim S3 backward**, then Flash into them at the last instant. They'll think you whiffed — then they're airborne.
