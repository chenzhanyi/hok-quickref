# Niu Mo Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure tank Support. Max dual defenses + CDR + team utility. The tankier you are, the more S3+Flash engages you can survive.

---

## Recommended Full-Tank Support Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Guard · Redemption (近卫·救赎)** | **Guard aura:** team dual defense (stacks with Niu Mo's passive). **Redemption active:** group shield. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity to survive CC and cast S3. |
| 3 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Reduces enemy AS by 40% on-hit — ADC/Assassin counter. |
| 4 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic Defense + spell shield vs burst Mages. |
| 5 | **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** | **20% CDR** + Physical Defense. More S2 and S3 casts per fight. |
| 6 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** or **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** | **Overlord's:** massive HP + regen for sustained fights. **Eternal Night:** burst-heal vs magic. |
