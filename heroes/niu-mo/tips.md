# Niu Mo Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S3 + Flash: The Only Engage You Need

S3's channel is slow and telegraphed. Enemies will walk out. The fix: **Press S3 → Flash during the slam animation toward enemies.** The knockup and fissure reposition with your Flash. This is undodgeable.

### 2. Reverse S3 + Flash (Mind Game)

If enemies are watching for the forward S3+Flash: **aim S3 backward**, let them think you missed → **Flash into them at the last instant.** They drop their guard, then they're airborne. Higher-ELO mind game.

### 3. Stay Glued to Your Carry

Passive only works within 500 range. If you wander off to ward or chase, your ADC loses massive dual defenses. **Laning phase: stay attached to your marksman.** Your passive makes them deceptively tanky.

### 4. S1 Tag the Biggest Threat

S1 reduces enemy damage output by a significant amount for 3 seconds. Priority targets:
- Enemy Assassin diving your carry
- Enemy Mage about to burst
- Enemy ADC outputting freely
Don't waste S1 on the tank.

### 5. S3 Fissure: Zone Control

Even if S3 misses, the burning trail persists for several seconds, dealing damage + **heavy slow.** In jungle corridors, use S3 to block escape paths or separate the enemy frontline from their backline.

## Signature Combos

### Perfect Teamfight Engage: **S3 + Flash → S2 → S1**

1. S3 + Flash → AoE knockup on enemy backline
2. S2 charge → second knockup + slow (chain CC)
3. S1 sweep → weaken enemy damage output
4. Team cleans up

### Defensive Counter-Engage: **S2 → S1 → S3**

1. Enemy Assassin dives your ADC
2. S2 knock them away
3. S1 sweep → reduce their damage
4. S3 at your ADC's feet → zone + damage + slow

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S2 can cross thin walls.** Use to escape or rotate through jungle.
- **S3's fissure blocks paths.** In Dragon/Baron pit fights, S3 across the entrance to trap enemies inside.
- **Matchup:** Universal — fits any team comp. Struggles vs hyper-mobile teams that easily dodge S3 (even with Flash).
