# Ying Zheng（嬴政） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Artillery / Wave Clear King)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 王者獨尊 (Imperial Sovereign)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 嬴政)

> Ying Zheng is a uniquely high-output, long-range Magic artillery. He has some of the best wave clear and high-ground defense in the game. His AAs are penetrating line attacks that deal mixed Physical/Magic damage — making him the hardest-hitting AA Mage in mid lane. His S3 fires **50 flying swords over 5 seconds** with +20% MS, letting him reposition while outputting.

---

## Core Mechanics

### AA: Penetrating Line Attack

Ying Zheng's AAs pierce through all enemies in a line, dealing mixed damage. **Always weave AAs between abilities** — his early-game AA DPS rivals Marksmen.

### S2: Shield + Anti-Assassin

S2 grants a shield that provides **bonus Magic Attack + MS while active.** On activation: **clears enemy Physical Attack equipment bonus** for 2 seconds. When an Assassin dives you: S2 → their damage plummets → walk away.

### Keep S2 Active Always

S2's buffs (AP + MS) persist while the shield holds. If the shield breaks: recast immediately. Always rotate with S2 active.

### S3: 50 Swords + 20% MS

S3 fires 50 swords over 5 seconds. You can **move freely during S3.** Uses: siege towers (force defenders off), check bushes (hit sound effect), steal objectives (high tick rate), zone enemies (nobody walks into 50 swords).
