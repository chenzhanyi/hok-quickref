# Ying Zheng Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs **CDR** (S3 uptime), **Magic Penetration**, and **MS** (compensate for no mobility). S3's 50 ticks synergize with Mask + Sunset Flow.

---

## Recommended Artillery Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR for frequent S2/S3. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC.* |
| 2 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | MS + AoE explosion. S1 + S3 both trigger it perfectly. |
| 3 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP boost. Stacks with S2's AP buff for massive mid-game spike. |
| 4 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** or **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** | **Mask:** S3's 50 ticks endlessly trigger %HP burn. **Sunset Flow:** 50 ticks instantly stacks 10 penetration layers. |
| 5 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Penetration vs late-game Magic Cloak. |
| 6 | **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** or **Splendor (辉月)** | **Tome:** max AP + damage amp. **Splendor:** Stasis vs heavy dive. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Attack Speed (AA DPS), Magic Penetration, Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "32% AS Speed" (CN Standard — Strongly Recommended)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1.6%, Critical Rate +0.5% |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** **+32% Attack Speed**, +10% MS, +64 Magic Penetration, +0.5% Crit. Smoothest AA weaving in the game.

### Setup 2: "88-Pen Burst" (Skill-Focused)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +16% AS, +10% MS. Heavier skill burst, less AA DPS.
