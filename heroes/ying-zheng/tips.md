# Ying Zheng Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Always Keep S2 Shield Active

S2's AP + MS buffs persist while shielded. If the shield breaks: recast immediately. Rotate with S2 on for constant MS + damage boost.

### 2. S2 Anti-Assassin: Strip Their AD

S2 activation **removes enemy Physical Attack equipment bonuses** for 2 seconds. When Lanling Wang, Jing, or any AD Assassin dives you: S2 → their damage drops ~40% → walk away or S3 counter-kill.

### 3. AA Constantly (You Hit Like a Marksman)

Ying Zheng's AAs pierce and deal mixed damage. In lane and skirmishes: **AA between every ability.** Early-game AA DPS is higher than most Mages' entire kits.

### 4. S3: Move While Firing (ABSOLUTELY Critical)

S3 lets you **move freely while shooting 50 swords.** Use this to: chase enemies (run forward during S3), kite enemies (run backward), dodge skillshots (strafe). Never stand still during S3.

### 5. S3 Zone Control: Force Enemies Off Turrets

When your team sieges: S3 directly at the enemy tower. Enemies must either eat 50 swords or abandon the tower. Either way, your team wins.

---

## Signature Combos

### Standard: **S2 (active) → S1 → S3 → AA weave**

1. Ensure S2 is active
2. S1 slow at enemy's feet
3. S3 — 50 swords while walking toward them
4. AA between swords to finish

### Anti-Dive: **S2 → S1 (at feet) → S3 (backward) → AA**

1. Assassin dives → S2 (strip their AD)
2. S1 at your feet (slow them)
3. S3 while walking AWAY (they eat swords chasing you)
4. AA as they retreat

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 has 50 ticks.** Mask of Agony and Sunset Flow proc dozens of times during one S3.
- **S3 hit sound = bush check.** Fire S3 into suspicious bushes. If you hear hits → enemies inside.
- **Matchup:** God-tier at wave clear and sieging. Struggles vs long-range engage that catches him before S2 activates.
