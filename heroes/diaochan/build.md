# Diaochan Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Must reach **40% CDR** (S2 dance engine). Must have **Lifesteal** (melee skirmishing). Must build **semi-tank** — full AP gets deleted on engage. CN server consensus: **Semi-Tank CDR Lifesteal.**

---

## Recommended Semi-Tank Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR rush. Dance steps must flow. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC.* |
| 2 | **Devourer's Tome (噬神之书)** | **Diaochan's soul item!** Massive Magic Lifesteal. Combined with runes = ~40% spell vamp. 1v5 sustain. |
| 3 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** or **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical defense. **Berserker's:** stacking MS + damage while taking hits — perfect for prolonged dancing. |
| 4 | **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | AP + Max HP. Converts AP to dual defenses. Incredible cost-efficiency for melee Mage. |
| 5 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** or **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** | **Mask:** percentage HP burn on multi-hit. **Sunset Flow:** penetration stacking vs magic resist. |
| 6 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** or **Splendor (辉月)** | **Magic Cloak:** vs burst Mages. **Splendor:** Stasis for dodging fatal damage when S2 is on cooldown. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Diaochan has True Damage — she needs less Magic Penetration than other Mages. Core: **CDR + Magic Lifesteal.**

### Setup 1: "10% CDR + 16% Lifesteal" (Classic CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Compassion (怜悯) | ×10 | Cooldown Reduction +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Greed (贪婪) | ×10 | Magic Lifesteal +1.6% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +24 Magic Penetration, **+16% Magic Lifesteal**, **+10% CDR**. With Calm Boots = near 25% CDR at level 1.

### Setup 2: "Hybrid Tank" (Safer Early Game)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Compassion (怜悯) | ×10 | Cooldown Reduction +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Greed (贪婪) | ×5 | Magic Lifesteal +1.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×5 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +24 Magic Penetration, +10% CDR, **+8% Magic Lifesteal**, **+225 Max Health**, +2% MS. Better early survival vs aggressive junglers.
