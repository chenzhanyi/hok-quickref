# Diaochan（貂蟬） — Assassin / Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin / Mage (Sustained Skirmisher)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 絕世舞姬 (Peerless Dancer)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 貂蝉)

> Diaochan is a sustained-damage Magic Skirmisher who thrives in prolonged fights. She doesn't one-shot — she **dances inside her S3 circle**, stacking her passive marks to trigger chain explosions of **True Damage + 90% slow + self-healing.** With max CDR and Lifesteal, a well-played Diaochan can 1v5.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: 4-Stack Explosion

Abilities stack marks. At **4 stacks: AoE explosion** dealing True Damage + 90% slow + self-heal. If multiple enemies are marked together, chain explosions delete entire teams.

### S2: Invulnerability Frame + CD Reset

S2 dashes and fires 3 petals. **The dash animation has a brief invulnerability window** — dodge Zhuge Liang S3, tower shots, Musashi S3. **If ANY petal hits an enemy, S2 cooldown is reduced by 4 seconds.** If all petals miss, S2 has a long cooldown and you're dead.

### S3: Circle of Infinite Dancing

S3 creates a circle. **While Diaochan is inside, S1 and S2 cooldowns are massively reduced.** The circle also applies marks on creation and expiration.

### Diagonal Dancing

Never dash straight forward or backward. **Dash diagonally or in a triangle** around enemies — stay in the circle, dodge skillshots, and keep petals hitting.
