# Diaochan Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S2 Invulnerability Frame = Your Survival

S2's dash has a brief **invulnerability window** (the disappear-reappear frames). Use to dodge:
- Zhuge Liang S3 (ultimate lock-on)
- Tower shots
- Musashi S3, Wukong bonk, Daji charm
Mastering S2 timing is what separates Diaochan gods from feeders.

### 2. S2 Petals Must Hit — NEVER Miss

S2's 3 petals reduce S2 cooldown by **4 seconds per hit.** If all 3 miss (dashing into a wall, no enemies nearby), S2 goes on full cooldown — and you die. Always dash TOWARD enemies, through them, or at angles where at least 1 petal connects.

### 3. Diagonal Dancing (Never Straight)

Never dash forward/backward in a straight line. Always dash **diagonally or in a triangle** around enemies. This keeps you in your S3 circle, dodges enemy skillshots (Gan & Mo swords), and maintains petal contact.

### 4. S3 Circle: Your Dance Floor

S3 massively reduces S1/S2 cooldowns while you're inside. **Never leave your circle during a fight.** If enemies try to kite you out, let them go — you're useless without the circle. Wait for S3 cooldown, then fight again on YOUR terms.

### 5. Grouped Enemies = Free Penta

If enemies cluster together, S1 and S2 mark multiple targets simultaneously. Chain explosions trigger every 4 stacks — with 3+ enemies, the chain reactions delete everyone instantly.

## Signature Combo

### Standard Dance: **S2 → S1 → S3 → S2 → S1 → S2 → S1...**

1. S2 dash in (stack marks + CD reset)
2. S1 (more marks)
3. S3 circle (marks on creation + super CDR)
4. Inside circle: alternate S2 → S1 → S2 → S1
5. Diagonal dashes only. Never leave the circle.

### Chase: **S2 → S1 → S3 (at max range toward enemy)**

1. S2 + S1 to stack marks and damage
2. Place S3 at max range IN the enemy's escape direction
3. The circle edge reduces your cooldowns immediately
4. S2 again to chase into the circle

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 applies marks on creation AND expiration.** Time your burst around these free stacks.
- **CDR is everything.** 40% CDR + S3 circle = near-infinite dashes.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs melee-heavy comps that cluster. Struggles vs long-range poke that outranges her circle.
