# Loong Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Know Your Element Combos

AAs inherit the last two elements cast. Always know what combo you're running:
- **Fire+Fire:** Max damage. Farming, objectives, all-in.
- **Water+Water:** Sustain. AA minions to full HP/Mana.
- **Wind+Wind:** Knockback AAs. Anti-dive, self-peel.
- **Fire+Water:** Best all-purpose — damage + sustain in one.

### 2. S3: Save for Escape, Not Engage

S3 Dragon Flight is untargetable + terrain-ignoring + extreme speed. **Never use it to initiate** — you'll land and die. Save it for: escaping Assassin dives, dodging fatal burst, chasing fleeing enemies when you're safe to commit.

### 3. S3 Landing: Fear Mechanic

S3's landing deals AoE damage + **fear (enemies can't act).** When chasing: time the landing to fear the enemy, then finish with Fire+Fire AAs.

### 4. Wind + Fire = Kite God

When an Assassin is chasing you: Wind (knockback) → Fire (bonus damage). AA once = knockback + bonus damage. Kite backward. Repeat. They never reach you.

### 5. Water + Water: Never Recall

AA minions with Water+Water active = restore HP + Mana. Loong almost never needs to recall. Sustain through everything.

## Signature Combos

### Standard Burst: **Fire → Water → AA**

1. Fire = bonus Physical Damage on AA
2. Water = HP/Mana restore on AA
3. AA = both effects combined
4. Best trade combo. Damage + sustain.

### Anti-Dive: **Wind → Fire → AA → S3**

1. Wind = knockback
2. Fire = bonus damage
3. AA = knockback + damage
4. If they re-engage: S3 Dragon Flight → escape

### Tower Dive Execute: **Fire → Water → AA → S3 → S3 (land)**

1. Fire+Water → burst trade
2. Enemy Flashes under tower
3. S3 → fly over tower (untargetable)
4. S3 land → fear + damage → kill

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Element switching has no cooldown between elements.** You can rapidly cycle Fire-Water-Fire-Water for sustained damage + sustain.
- **S3 landing can be aimed during flight.** Track enemy movement and land ahead of them.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs melee divers (Wind counters them). Struggles vs long-range poke that out-ranges your AAs.
