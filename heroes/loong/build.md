# Loong Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Burst-caster ADC, not a turret. Skills and elemental AA effects CAN crit. Needs **Physical Penetration + Critical Rate + Attack.** Less AS than traditional ADCs.

---

## Recommended Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early AS for smoother AA feel. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs CC.* |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | CDR + flat Penetration + Health. Perfect for skill-based ADC. |
| 3 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | **Power spike!** Elemental AA effects CAN crit. Infinity Edge makes Fire+Fire AAs devastating. |
| 4 | **Shadow Blade (影刃)** or **Master's Power (宗师之力)** | **Shadow Blade:** on-crit AS + MS. **Master's Power:** enhanced AA burst after element swap — one AA deletes. |
| 5 | **Dawn (破晓)** | Marksman capstone. Penetration + AS + AA damage amp. |
| 6 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Siege Breaker:** execute — stacks with S3 landing fear combo. **Pure Sky:** active DR for survival. |

## Quick In-Game Setup

Loong's element combos take practice. Drill Fire+Water (standard) and Wind+Fire (anti-dive) in Training Mode. Copy the **CN #1 Loong** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
