# Loong（敖隱） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Burst Caster ADC / 4-Dimensional Dragon)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 凌霄真龍 (Soaring True Dragon)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (敖隐)

> Loong is a uniquely designed "4-Dimensional True Dragon" Marksman. His core mechanic: **three elemental Dragon Souls (Fire, Water, Wind)** — his AAs gain dual-element effects based on his last two casts. He weaves between burst damage (Fire), sustain (Water), and self-peel knockback (Wind). His S3 transforms him into a dragon: **untargetable, terrain-ignoring flight** — the ultimate escape/execute tool.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Three Dragon Souls: Last Two Casts Define Your AAs

Loong doesn't have traditional abilities. He toggles between elements. AAs inherit effects from the last TWO elements cast:

- **Fire + Fire (双火):** Max DPS. AA deals bonus Physical Damage. Use for: wave clear, jungle, burst.
- **Water + Water (双水):** Sustain. AA restores HP + Mana. Use for: laning, recovering, extended fights.
- **Wind + Wind (双风):** Self-peel. AA knocks enemies back. Use for: anti-dive, creating distance.
- **Fire + Water:** Burst + sustain. Best general-purpose combo.
- **Fire + Wind:** Burst + knockback. Kite and damage.
- **Water + Wind:** Sustain + knockback. Survival mode.

### S3: Dragon Flight (Untargetable)

S3 transforms Loong into a flying dragon: **untargetable, ignores terrain, extreme speed.** Use reactively — when an Assassin dives or when collapsed on. The landing deals AoE damage + **fear (disables enemy actions).**
