# Mayene（姬小滿） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Combo Brawler / Four Stances)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 武道奇才 (Martial Prodigy)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (姬小满)

> Mayene is a dominant all-round Fighter with a unique **"combo input" system.** S1 and S2 each have **2 charges.** Pressing two skills in sequence triggers a different combo move — she has **four distinct combo variations** covering burst, %HP damage, displacement, and invulnerability.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Four Combo Variations (Press Two Skills in Order)

- **1+1 (Pull + Kick Knockup):** S1 pull → S1 flying kick (AoE knockup). Burst + CC. **Best for: teamfight engage, squishy deletion.**
- **1+2 (Pull + Slam %HP):** S1 pull → S2 slam with **%Max HP damage + self-heal.** **Best for: tank dueling, jungle objectives.**
- **2+1 (Roll + Roundhouse Kick):** S2 roll → S1 instant teleport behind enemy + kick them backward. **Best for: long-range pursuit, tower ganks, repositioning enemies.**
- **2+2 (Roll + Invulnerable Heal):** S2 roll → S2 leap upward. **Fully untargetable + massive HP restore.** **Best for: tower dive de-aggro, dodging burst, escape.**

### S3: Charge Limit Break

S3 grants: +MS, Slow Resist, **+1 charge to S1 and S2, and raises charge cap to 3.** Pop S3 before teamfights for three consecutive combos.
