# Mayene Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Skills have massive base damage. Needs **CDR** (more charges = more combos), **Physical Penetration**, and **semi-tank** for melee brawling. Zero Critical needed.

---

## Recommended Clash Lane / Jungle Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** or **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** | Clash: CDR boots for frequent combos. Jungle: HP stacking. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core #1. CDR + flat Penetration + Health. |
| 3 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | Combo AAs perfectly trigger AoE slow. CDR + Defense. |
| 4 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Power spike!** Active 50% DR. Combined with 2+2 invulnerability, Mayene survives everything. |
| 5 | **Star Breaker (碎星锤)** or **Siege Breaker (破军)** | **Star Breaker:** 40% Pen vs tanks. **Siege Breaker:** execute vs squishy teams. |
| 6 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Physical or Magic defense. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Zero Attack Speed or Critical needed. **Physical Penetration + Attack + Movement Speed.**

### Setup: "Stealth 100-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% MS. Combo innately has AS — Stealth gives more AD than Hunt.

### Micro-Adjust: "Harmony Sustain" (vs Lane Bullies)

| Slot | Rune | Qty |
|------|------|-----|
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 |

**Total:** +452 Max Health, +52 HP/5s, +4% MS. Better sustain vs Sikong Zhen, Kuang Tie.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Mayene's 4-combo system takes practice. Drill all four in Training Mode. Copy the **CN #1 Mayene** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
