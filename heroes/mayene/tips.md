# Mayene Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Know Your Four Combos

S1 and S2 have 2 charges. The SEQUENCE matters:
- **1+1:** AoE knockup burst. Squishy killer.
- **1+2:** %Max HP slam + heal. Tank buster.
- **2+1:** Roll → behind-enemy roundhouse kick. Reposition tool.
- **2+2:** Roll → invulnerable leap + heal. Escape/Dodge.

### 2. S3 Before Combos (Charge Limit Break)

S3 adds +1 charge and raises cap to 3. **Always S3 before engaging** — you can chain 3 combos instead of 2. S3 → 2+1 (engage) → 1+1 (burst) → 2+2 (escape).

### 3. 2+2: The Ultimate Dodge Tool

2+2 makes you fully untargetable + heals massively. Use to: dodge Zhuge Liang S3, tower shots, Guitarist S3, or any burst. Also: dive tower → kill → 2+2 to de-aggro.

### 4. 2+1: Tower Dive Kidnap

2+1 teleports behind the enemy and kicks them backward. From the side of a tower: 2+1 → kick the defender OUT of tower range → your team deletes them.

### 5. Track Your Charges

No charges = useless. Never chase with 0 charges. In lane: use combos, then back off and wait for charges to refill. S3 resets them.

## Signature Combos

### Full Teamfight Cycle: **S3 → 2+1 → 1+1 → 2+2**

1. S3 (charge limit break)
2. 2+1 (roll into backline, kick carry toward your team)
3. 1+1 (AoE knockup burst on the displaced carry)
4. 2+2 (invulnerable escape)

### Lane Trade: **1+2 → 2+2**

1. 1+2 (%HP damage + heal)
2. 2+2 (untargetable → safe retreat)
3. Repeat until enemy is low → finish with 1+1

## Advanced Tips

- **1+2's %HP damage makes Mayene a Jungler too.** She shreds Dragon and Baron.
- **2+1 can be aimed.** The kick direction depends on your joystick angle.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile tanks and squishies. Struggles when charges are wasted.
