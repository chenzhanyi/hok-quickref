# Mai Shiranui Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Always Land S2 Before Committing

S2 shreds Magic Defense + slows. If S2 misses: **don't go in.** Wait for the cooldown (~2s) and try again. An S2-less engage does 30-40% less damage and the enemy isn't slowed for your follow-up.

### 2. Post-Ability Roll-Dash (Never Stand Still)

After every ability: push the left joystick to roll. This costs zero Energy and grants bonus MS. Constant rolling makes Mai nearly impossible to catch or skillshot.

### 3. S1-Flash / S3-Flash (Off-Screen Engage)

Both S1 and S3 can be **Flash-extended**. Cast the ability, then Flash during the animation — the hitbox extends to your Flash destination. This lets you engage from outside enemy vision range. The #1 high-ELO Mai mechanic.

### 4. Energy Management

Don't spam fans blindly. Two missed S2s = empty Energy = you're a minion. When Energy is below half:
- Play safe
- Poke with S2 until one lands (restores Energy)
- Only then commit to S1/S3

### 5. Passive AA: Free Engage/Disengage

The 5-second enhanced AA is a **charging knockback**. Use to:
- Push enemies into your tower
- Knock Assassins away when they dive you
- Extra gap-close when S1/S3 are on cooldown

## Signature Combos

### Standard Delete Combo: **S2 → Roll → S1 → AA → S3 → S2**

1. S2 fan (defense shred + slow)
2. Roll forward
3. S1 flying kick (knockup)
4. Enhanced AA (Ninja Bee — push back)
5. S3 charge (massive AoE burst + knockback)
6. S2 fan again (cooldown refreshed) — finish

### Off-Screen S3-Flash Engage: **S3 + Flash → AA → S1 → S2**

1. S3 from fog of war → **Flash mid-animation**
2. Enemy backline gets AoE knocked back
3. Enhanced AA push
4. S1 knockup chain-CC
5. S2 finish

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3's knockback can push multiple enemies.** Aim to hit 2-3 squishies when they're grouped.
- **Roll-dash through enemies.** The roll can pass through units — use to juke skillshots or reposition behind enemies.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile squishies. Struggles vs point-and-click CC that stops her roll chain.
