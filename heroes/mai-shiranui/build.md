# Mai Shiranui Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure burst. No sustained damage — she's a one-rotation killer. **Max AP, max Penetration, max Lifesteal** for survival during dives.

---

## Recommended Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Arcane Boots (秘法之靴)** | Flat Magic Penetration + Mana regen. S2 fans hit like trucks early. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC.* |
| 2 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | Core burst. S2 fan triggers AoE explosion on hit. +Movement Speed for better kiting. |
| 3 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP spike. Two fans chunk squishies to 30% HP. |
| 4 | **Devourer's Tome (噬神之书)** | **25% Magic Lifesteal** + Health. Mai dives into melee range — the lifesteal keeps her alive during S3 engages. |
| 5 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Magic Penetration. Mandatory when enemies build Magic Cloak. |
| 6 | **Splendor (辉月)** or **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** | **Splendor:** Stasis after S3 dive — wait for team or cooldowns. **Sage's Tome:** max AP — one fan deletes. |
