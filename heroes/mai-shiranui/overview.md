# Mai Shiranui（不知火舞） — Assassin / Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin / Mage (Magic Burst Diver)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 明媚烈焰 (Bright Flame)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 不知火舞)

> Mai Shiranui is an extremely high-ceiling, high-burst Magic Assassin. She has some of the best mobility and one-shot potential in the game. After every ability, she can **roll-dash** by pushing the left joystick — giving her unparalleled repositioning. Her S2 fans shred Magic Defense, enabling devastating all-in combos.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Post-Ability Roll-Dash

After every ability cast, push the left joystick to perform a **free roll-dash** with bonus Movement Speed. This is her core mobility — chain abilities + rolls to dance around enemies.

### Passive: Enhanced AA (Ninja Bee)

Every 5 seconds, Mai's next AA becomes a **charging dash that knocks enemies back**. Use to reposition, push enemies into towers, or as an extra gap-closer.

### S2: Fan (Defense Shred)

S2 fan deals high damage at long range AND **reduces the target's Magic Defense + slows them**. **Always land S2 before committing to a full combo** — the defense shred makes all follow-up damage significantly stronger.

### Energy Management

Mai has no Mana — she uses Energy. Abilities cost Energy; hits restore it. Missing two fans in a row or whiffing S3 leaves you **Energy-starved and useless**. When Energy is low, play safe and poke with S2 until you land one.

### S1/S3 + Flash (Blind-Spot Engage)

Both S1 (flying kick) and S3 (forward charge) can be combined with **Flash** to extend range dramatically. S1-Flash or S3-Flash lets you engage from off-screen — the ultimate surprise deletion tool.
