# Luban Dashi（魯班大師） — Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Support (Strategic Playmaker)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 神匠 (Divine Craftsman)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 鲁班大师)

> Luban Dashi is a top-tier strategic Support. His core mechanics revolve around **Mechanical Arm Link** (S2 grants allies a long-range teleport to him) and **Forceful Recall** (S3 pulls multiple enemies toward him). He's the game's premier engage playmaker and the ultimate babysitter for immobile carries.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S2: Assistant Reinforcement (God-Tier Protection)

Luban Dashi links a shield to an ally. The ally can press the **interact button** to **teleport directly to Luban Dashi, ignoring terrain**. This gives immobile carries (Luban No. 7, Hou Yi, Angela) instant self-peel and chase potential. Also enables rapid team rotations through walls.

### S3: Forceful Recall (Fatal Engage)

Luban Dashi fires mechanical arms that connect to enemies. On recast or after channeling, **all connected enemies are pulled toward him and knocked up**. Massive AoE teamfight control.

### "Slingshot Combo" (Famous Tactic)

S2 links a long-range mage (Jiang Ziya, Gan & Mo) charging their ultimate. The mage releases their skill and **immediately presses interact to teleport** — forcibly changing the skill's origin point for cross-screen blind-spot bombardment.
