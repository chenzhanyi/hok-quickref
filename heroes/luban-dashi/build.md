# Luban Dashi Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs high **CDR** for frequent S2 links and S3 engages, plus high **Defense + Max Health** to survive Flash-initiated dives. Full-tank CDR support.

---

## Recommended Full-Tank CDR Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎)** or **Shadow Aegis · Star Spring (极影·星泉)** | **Shadow Aegis line** for CDR. **Redemption active:** group shield vs burst. **Star Spring active:** sustain healing for extended lane/jungle skirmishes. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity is mandatory — getting CC-locked during engage cancels everything. |
| 3 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | High Physical Defense + Max Health. Reduces enemy Marksman and Assassin Attack Speed. |
| 4 | **Rising Sun (旭日初光)** or **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** | Further CDR stacking. **Rising Sun:** shields lowest-Health ally on hit — perfect for Luban's protector role. **Glacial Storm:** more armor + AoE Frost shockwave when taking burst. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense. Eternal Night vs burst (heal back); Magic Cloak vs sustained magic poke. |
| 6 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** or **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | **Overlord's Platemail:** massive Health + out-of-combat regen — Luban never needs to recall, infinite map presence. **Sage's Sanctuary:** resurrection for final teamfight. |
