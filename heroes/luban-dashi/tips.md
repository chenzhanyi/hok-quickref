# Luban Dashi Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S2: The Ultimate Babysitter Tool

Luban Dashi links a shield to an ally. The ally presses the **interact button** to teleport to him, ignoring terrain. This is the strongest repositioning tool in the game:
- **Save immobile carries** (Luban No. 7, Hou Yi, Angela) from divers
- **Enable aggressive chases** — link your diver, they go in, you pull them out
- **Rapid rotations** — pull teammates through walls for surprise ganks

### 2. S3: Instant-Cast vs Delayed Pull

S3 connects arms to enemies. You can either:
- **Let it channel:** Connect more targets for a bigger pull
- **Instant recast:** vs mobile enemies, tap S3 again immediately to pull before they Flash or dash out

### 3. Slingshot Combo (Signature Tactic)

Link S2 to a long-range mage (Jiang Ziya, Gan & Mo). They charge ultimate from deep. On release, they **press interact** — the ultimate's origin point teleports with them, creating cross-map blind-spot kills.

### 4. Shield Stacking

Hitting enemies with abilities or having shields broken grants Luban Dashi a stackable absorption shield. When contesting mid-lane early, weave S1 and auto-attacks to trigger passive — makes you surprisingly tanky in early skirmishes.

## Signature Combos

### Long-Range Team Engage: **S2 (link carry) → S1 / Flash → S3 → Carry Interacts**

1. S2 link your burst carry (Gao Jianli, Su Lie, or fed Marksman)
2. **S1 speed boost / Flash** into the enemy backline
3. S3 connect and **instant-pull** multiple enemies
4. Your linked carry presses interact → teleports directly into the cluster → team-wipe

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Remind teammates about the interact button.** In solo queue, many players don't know they can teleport to you. Ping or quick-chat before fights.
- **Duo queue synergy:** Luban No. 7, Di Renjie, or Gao Jianli paired with Luban Dashi = unfair dominance.
- **Vision play:** Use S2 to pull your scout/roamer back to safety after they check bushes.
- **Matchup:** Excels against dive-heavy comps (S2 saves carries). Struggles against poke comps that don't commit.
