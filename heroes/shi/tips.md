# Shi Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S1 Accuracy = Everything

Shi's entire kit revolves around landing S1. It has a noticeable cast delay — skilled opponents will dodge it easily. Always **cast S2 first** to slow the enemy, making S1 nearly undodgeable. Then S1 → pull.

### 2. Pull Direction Dictates Outcomes

- **Ganking:** Pull enemies toward your team or into your tower
- **Self-defense vs divers:** Pull Assassins (Lam, Jing, Fey) **away** from yourself
- **Damage maximization:** Pull enemies into your S2 orb center (inner circle = double damage + bonus S1 interaction damage)

### 3. Ultimate Maximizes Control

S3 grants **+80% decaying MS**, **resets S2**, and **doubles S1 pulls to 2**. Never use S1's second pull until you've positioned the enemy perfectly — the 5-second window gives you time to adjust.

### 4. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take ultimate whenever available.** S2 is Shi's core wave-clear and poke tool — maxing it enables fast roaming.

## Signature Combos

### Core Gank Combo: **S2 → S1 → S3 → S1 (pull) → S1 (pull) → S2**

1. S2 slow → S1 bind
2. **Immediately S3** (refreshes S2, enhances S1 to 2 pulls)
3. First S1 pull — drag enemy toward team
4. Second S1 pull — drag them further into death
5. Refreshed S2 to finish

### Double Orb Burst: **S1 → S2 → S3 → S2 → S1 (pull)×2**

1. S1 bind
2. Drop first S2 orb
3. S3 — refresh S2, drop second orb (two orbs bombarding simultaneously)
4. Pull enemy back and forth between both orbs for maximum damage

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Don't hold S1.** The cooldown is short. Constantly fish for binds from bushes or on enemy movement paths — one hit = one kill window.
- **Bush camping:** Shi is terrifying from fog of war. Enemies can't dodge what they can't see coming.
- **Peel for your ADC:** S1 is just as powerful defensively. Pull divers off your backline.
- **Matchup:** Excels against immobile mages and marksmen. Struggles against long-range artillery (Gan & Mo) who outrange her S1.
