# Shi Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Two paths — **Semi-Tank Utility (mainstream)** and **Full Damage Burst**. Most high-ranked CN players prefer utility: Shi's job is landing S1 pulls; her team provides the damage. Semi-tank massively improves survival.

---

## Build 1: Semi-Tank Utility (Recommended)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | Early CDR for frequent S1 fishing and roaming. |
| 2 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | Utility Mage's core item. S2's multi-hit damage perfectly triggers Mask's percentage HP burn repeatedly. |
| 3 | **Frost Breath (凝冰之息)** | Massive control amplification. S2 + Frost Breath makes enemies nearly immobile inside the orb — S1 becomes undodgeable. |
| 4 | **Rising Sun (旭日初光)** or **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Defensive flex. Rising Sun: shields self + lowest ally on hit (perfect for protector role). Ominous Premonition: vs heavy physical dive. |
| 5 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense + spell shield. Prevents getting one-shot by magic assassins (Mai Shiranui, Shangguan Wan'er). |
| 6 | **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** or **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | Penetration filler. Sunset Flow's multi-hit passive stacks instantly from S2 ticks. |

## Build 2: Full Damage Burst (Solo Carry)

| Order | Item |
|-------|------|
| 1 | Calm Boots |
| 2 | Echo Staff (回响之杖) |
| 3 | Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒) |
| 4 | Mask of Agony (痛苦面具) |
| 5 | Void Staff (虚无法杖) |

Best when your team lacks magic damage. A late-game double-orb combo with Savant's Wrath can solo-kill enemy carries.

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Movement Speed (roaming), Magic Penetration, CDR.**

### Setup 1: CN Server "Harmony 88-Pen" (Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +4% Movement Speed, +364 Max Health, +6% Attack Speed. Early Health prevents getting burst when fishing for S1.

### Setup 2: "Hunt Max Speed" (Aggressive Roaming)

## Mindset Tip

Don't be stingy with S1. The cooldown is short — constantly fish from bushes or on enemy movement paths. One landed S1 = one kill window for your team.
