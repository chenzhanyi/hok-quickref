# Shi（西施） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Control / Utility)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 幻紗之靈 (Spirit of Illusory Silk)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 西施)

> Shi is a strategically dominant control Mage. Her core mechanic revolves around S1's **"Silk Bind"** — a forced movement command that controls enemy Hero positioning. She's the team's absolute engagement tool and a nightmare for squishy carries.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S1: Silk Bind (纱缚·抽丝)

Shi throws silk to a target area. If it hits an enemy Hero, within 5 seconds she can press S1 again to **force that Hero to walk in a chosen direction**. This is one of the most oppressive forced-movement abilities in the game.

### S2: Flowing Silk (流纱·聚束)

Throws an energy orb that deals continuous AoE damage (inner circle deals double). Explodes after 2 seconds.

### Ultimate: Undistracted Heart (心无旁骛)

On activation: grants decaying **+80% Movement Speed for 1 second**, **instantly resets S2 cooldown**, and **doubles S1's control to 2 pulls** instead of 1.

### Pull Direction Matters

- **Ganking/Engage:** Pull enemies toward your team or into your tower
- **Self-defense:** Pull diving Assassins (Lam, Jing, Fey) away from yourself, or into your S2 orb center for bonus damage
