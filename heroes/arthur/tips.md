# Arthur Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. 40% CDR = Infinite Silence

At 40% CDR: S1 = 3s cooldown. S1 buff = 3s duration. You can perma-run with S1 active and **Silence enemies twice in one engage.** This is Arthur's entire identity — enemies literally can't cast abilities.

### 2. Hit-and-Run, Not All-In

Arthur is NOT a "dive and die" Hero. His playstyle: **S1 in → Silence → S2 spin → S3 → S1 OUT.** Let passive heal you, then repeat. Three cycles of this and the enemy team is frustrated to death.

### 3. S1 Wall-Hop Escape

S1's enhanced AA has a dash-lunge. If a jungle camp is on the other side of a wall: S1 → AA the camp → you lunge through the wall. Free escape over any thin wall.

### 4. Passive: Never Recall

After any trade: walk to a bush, stand still for a few seconds. Passive + any HP regen item = full HP. Arthur almost never needs to recall.

### 5. Pre-Cast S1 Before Engaging

S1's buff lasts 3 seconds. Pre-cast S1 before entering a fight → AA (first Silence) → S1 is already close to off cooldown → S1 again → AA (second Silence). Double Silence in <3 seconds.

## Signature Combos

### Standard Engage: **S1 → AA (Silence) → S2 → S3 → AA → S1 (retreat)**

1. S1 in → AA Silence
2. S2 spin (damage while they can't cast)
3. S3 knockup + %HP damage
4. AA
5. S1 out (retreat)
6. Heal with passive → repeat

### Bush Ambush: **S1 (pre-cast) → AA → S2 → S3 → S1 → AA**

1. Pre-cast S1, wait in bush
2. AA (Silence)
3. S2 + S3 while they're Silenced
4. S1 (off cooldown) → AA (second Silence)

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S1 Silence is Arthur's entire kit.** Without 40% CDR, you can't chain-Silence. Prioritize CDR items.
- **Don't stand and AA trade.** Arthur's AA DPS is low. Hit abilities, then retreat. Hit-and-run only.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs ability-reliant Heroes (Jing, Diaochan, Luna). Struggles vs auto-attack bruisers who don't care about Silence.
