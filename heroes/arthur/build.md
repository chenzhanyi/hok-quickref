# Arthur Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Must hit **40% CDR** for infinite S1 Silence. Semi-tank skirmisher — hit-and-run, not all-in. CDR > Penetration > Tankiness.

---

## Recommended Clash Lane Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Arthur needs to keep running — getting CC-locked stops S1 cycle. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | CDR (15%) + flat Penetration + Health. Core damage item. |
| 3 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | **Arthur's soul item!** CDR (10%). S1's enhanced AA perfectly triggers AoE slow. Enemies can't escape after Silence. |
| 4 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Taking hits grants stacking MS (up to 10%) + damage. Arthur gets faster as he fights. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense. Eternal Night vs burst; Magic Cloak vs sustained poke. |
| 6 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** or **Blood Rage (血魔之怒)** | **Pure Sky:** CDR (10%) → now at 40% CDR cap. Active DR for survival. **Blood Rage:** massive shield at low HP. |

### CDR Breakdown

| Source | CDR |
|--------|-----|
| Shadow Axe | 15% |
| Frost Grip | 10% |
| Pure Sky | 10% |
| Runes (Compassion) | 10% |
| **Total** | **40% (cap)** |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **CDR, Movement Speed, Physical Penetration.**

### Setup 1: "10% CDR 100-Pen Speed" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Compassion (怜悯) | ×10 | Cooldown Reduction +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +20 Physical Attack, +36 Physical Penetration, **+10% CDR**, **+10% MS**, +10% AS. Combined with items = 40% CDR cap.

## Quick In-Game Setup

Arthur's 40% CDR is non-negotiable. Without it, S1 has gaps. Copy the **CN #1 Arthur** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**.
