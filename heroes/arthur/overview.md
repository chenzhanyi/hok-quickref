# Arthur（亞瑟） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Silence Skirmisher / Anti-Carry)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★☆☆☆☆
- **Title:** 聖騎之力 (Holy Knight's Might)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 亚瑟)

> Arthur is the game's iconic all-rounder Fighter. He has free passive HP regen, and his S1 is one of the most oppressive abilities in the game — a **Silence** on a 3-second cooldown (at 40% CDR). He's the ultimate "anti-fancy" Hero: Jing, Diaochan, and Luna all fear the Silence lock.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Infinite Silence at 40% CDR

At max CDR (40%): S1 cooldown = **3 seconds.** S1's speed boost + enhanced AA last **3 seconds.** This means **permanent S1 uptime.** You can double-Silence enemies in a single engage, preventing them from casting anything.

### Passive: Free HP Regen

Passive restores %Max HP every 2 seconds. After trading, don't recall — just walk around in a bush and heal to full for free.

### S1: Wall-Hop via AA

S1's enhanced AA has a small **dash-lunge.** If a jungle monster or minion is on the other side of a thin wall: S1 → AA the target → you lunge through the wall. Free escape or engage.

### S3: %Max HP Execute + AoE

S3 deals **%Max HP damage** (lower HP = harder it hits visually) + knockup. Leaves a burning circle on the ground. Combo with S1 Silence to keep enemies standing in the fire.
