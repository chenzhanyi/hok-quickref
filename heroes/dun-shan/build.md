# Dun Shan Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs high **Movement Speed** (roaming for engage angles), max **CDR** (frequent S2 shoulder throws), and **pure tank stats**. Shield blocks projectiles; raw HP/defense handles the rest.

---

## Recommended Roaming Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎)** or **Shadow Aegis · Wolf Rush (极影·奔狼)** | **Shadow Aegis line** for CDR + Mana regen. **Redemption active:** group shield for protection. **Wolf Rush:** team speed boost for rushing engages when your comp lacks initiation. |
| 2 | **Swift Boots (疾步之靴)** | Maximum roaming speed for finding S2+Flash angles. *Swap to Resistance Boots late game vs heavy CC.* |
| 3 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | High Physical Defense + Max Health. Reduces enemy Attack Speed on-hit — counters Marksmen and Physical Assassins. |
| 4 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Massive Magic Defense + spell shield. Even though shield blocks projectiles, AoE ground magic (Shen Mengxi S3, Zhou Yu fire) bypasses it — this is your safety net. |
| 5 | **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** | Physical Defense + **20% CDR**. Massive S2 uptime for constant shoulder-throw engages. |
| 6 | **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** or **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** | **Sanctuary:** resurrection for final teamfights. **Overlord's Platemail:** massive HP + regen for infinite map presence. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Max Health (early tankiness), CDR (frequent engages), Movement Speed.**

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, Cooldown Reduction +0.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** **+1,162 Max Health**, +23 Physical Defense, +4% Movement Speed, +6% CDR, +10% Attack Speed.

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
