# Dun Shan Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Shield: What It Blocks (and What It Doesn't)

**Blocks (S1/S3 immune):** All projectile auto-attacks, Zhuge Liang S3, Diaochan orbs, Hou Yi arrows, Angela S2, Daji charm.
**Does NOT block (shield is useless):** Zhou Yu fire, Wang Zhaojun freeze, Zhong Kui hook, Lu Bu true damage, ground-targeted AoE.

### 2. S2 + Flash: The Signature Engage

S2 has a brief charge forward. **Flash during the charge** to extend range massively. This "Flash Shoulder Throw" can kidnap enemy carries from a screen away and dump them into your team.

### 3. S3 Ultimate: Zone Control

S3 creates a giant shield wall that pushes enemies back. Can't move during. Use in:
- **Narrow jungle corridors:** Split enemy frontline from backline
- **Dragon/Baron pit:** Trap enemies against the wall
- **After S2 Flash:** Immediately S3 after throwing to trap the victim

### 4. S4 Repair Turret

Merge into allied turrets: you're invulnerable, the turret heals + gains defense. Use to:
- Defend high ground while behind
- Escape death when low HP
- Stall for teammates to respawn

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take ultimate whenever available.** S2 lower cooldown = more shoulder throws = more kills.

## Signature Combo

### Flash Kidnap Combo: **S2 → Flash → S3 → S1**

1. S2 charge toward enemy
2. **Flash mid-charge** to extend range
3. S2 completes — enemy thrown behind you into your team
4. **Immediately S3** to push them further and wall them in
5. S1 shield to block any rescue attempts

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Energy management:** Each blocked projectile costs 1 Energy. Against rapid-fire Marksmen (Hou Yi, Marco), your shield drains fast. Lower it preemptively to recharge.
- **Counter-pick hero:** Dun Shan is a hard counter to projectile-reliant teams. If the enemy has 3+ projectile Heroes, lock him in.
- **Avoid vs:** Zhou Yu, Wang Zhaojun, Lu Bu — their AoE ground damage ignores your shield entirely.
