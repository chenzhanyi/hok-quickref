# Dun Shan（盾山） — Tank / Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Tank / Support (Anti-Projectile / Engage)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 無盡之盾 (Endless Shield)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 盾山)

> Dun Shan is a top-tier utility Tank who counters most Marksmen and Mages in the game. His core strength is **blocking all projectile-based attacks** (auto-attacks, spell projectiles), combined with devastating Flash-engage crowd control. He can also **repair allied turrets** while invulnerable.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Shield: Blocks Projectiles (S1 / S3)

Dun Shan's shield (S1 hold or S3 ultimate) completely blocks all projectile attacks — basic attacks, Zhuge Liang's ultimate, Diaochan's orbs, Hou Yi's arrows. **Each blocked projectile costs 1 Energy.** Energy runs out → shield drops.

### What the Shield Does NOT Block

Non-projectile ground/overhead abilities pierce the shield: Zhou Yu's fire, Wang Zhaojun's freeze, Zhong Kui's hook, Lu Bu's true damage. Know when to lower the shield.

### Repair Turret (S4)

Dun Shan can merge into an allied turret, becoming **invulnerable** while healing the turret and boosting its defenses. Use when: defending high ground while behind, or escaping death at low HP.

### S2: Shoulder Throw (Core Engage)

S2 charges forward and **throws an enemy over his shoulder** behind him. Combined with Flash, this is one of the most dangerous engage tools in the game.
