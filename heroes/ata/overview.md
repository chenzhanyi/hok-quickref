## Core Mechanics

### Grey HP (Sustain Core)

When Ata takes damage, his HP bar turns **grey** instead of disappearing. As long as grey HP remains, **any damage dealt to enemies (including Minions and Monsters) converts grey HP back to green**. Never flee at low HP вҖ” S1 into a minion wave and watch your HP refill instantly.

### Ability Highlights

- **S1 (Bouncing Slam):** Leap вҖ” **Unstoppable during the jump**. Main damage, heal, and CC-ignore tool.
- **S2 (Hook Slam):** Hook that pulls. If it hits a wall, Ata charges to the wall and **stuns enemies against it**.
- **S3 (Terrain Wall):** Creates an impassable wall. **Pairs with S2** вҖ” even without natural terrain, S3вҶ’S2 creates a wallвҶ’stun combo anywhere.
