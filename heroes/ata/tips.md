# Ata Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Grey HP = Never Retreat

Damage taken becomes grey HP. Dealing damage converts grey → green. At low HP, don't run — **S1 into a minion wave or enemy cluster** and watch your HP refill instantly. The more enemies, the more healing.

### 2. S1 Unstoppable Timing

S1's jump grants **Unstoppable**. Time it against Daji's charm, Zhang Fei's roar, or any hard CC. If you're airborne, you're immune.

### 3. S3 → S2 Wall Combo

S3 creates a wall. S2 hooks enemies and slams them into walls for a stun. **Even with zero natural terrain**, cast S3 → S2 to create a wall and immediately stun enemies against it.

### 4. Smite Laning (Aggressive Start)

If playing Clash Lane with Smite (tank-jungle build):
- Level 1: immediately clear the back two minions
- S1 hits both enemy laner + minion wave simultaneously
- Almost nobody out-trades you at level 1

### 5. S3: Cage AND Escape

- **Cage:** Cut off enemy backline from frontline in teamfights
- **Escape:** Drop S3 behind you to block chokepoints
- **Cancel:** Press S3 again to remove the wall (prevents trapping allies)

---

## Signature Combos

### Wall-less Stun: **S3 → S2 → AA → S1**

1. S3 create wall in open ground
2. S2 hook → slam enemy into S3's wall (stun)
3. AA pull enemy back
4. S1 Unstoppable slam for damage + massive grey HP conversion

### Bush Ambush: **S2 → Flash → S1 → S3**

1. S2 charge from bush (gap-close)
2. Flash to extend range/extend stun
3. S1 Unstoppable bounce for damage
4. S3 wall to trap fleeing enemies

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Double burn aura:** Giant's Grip + Blazing Cape = two overlapping burn auras. This continuously triggers grey HP conversion even when just standing near enemies.
- **Don't cancel S3 too late:** Allies can be trapped by your wall. Cancel early.
- **Matchup:** Destroys melee-heavy comps (more targets = more healing). Struggles against kite-heavy ranged teams.
