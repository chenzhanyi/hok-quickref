# Ata Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Ata's damage and healing both scale with **Max Health**. Pure tank = high damage + high sustain. CN server consensus: **Smite Tank build** (Jungle/Clash flex).

---

## Recommended Smite Tank Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** | Core mandatory item. Massive Max Health. Double burn aura (jungle item + Blazing Cape) **continuously triggers grey HP conversion** — Ata heals just by standing near enemies. |
| 2 | **Boots of Fortitude (影忍之足)** or **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Fortitude: 15% basic attack DR for lane duels. Resistance: Tenacity vs heavy CC. |
| 3 | **Blazing Cape (红莲斗篷)** | Burn aura stacks with Giant's Grip (post-rework, both auras coexist). Clear speed, jungle speed, and passive grey HP regen all spike dramatically. |
| 4 | **Phoenix Eye (不死鸟之眼)** or **Succubus Cloak** | **Ata's signature item!** Passive: "Every 10% HP lost increases all healing received by 6%." Perfectly synergizes with grey HP conversion — at low HP, one S1 bounce refills most of your HP bar. |
| 5 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Counters enemy Marksmen and Physical Assassins. Reduces attacker's Attack Speed and Movement Speed on-hit. |
| 6 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** or **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | **Overlord's Platemail:** post-rework provides in-combat %HP regen — further amplifies the "unkillable disruptor" playstyle. **Sanctuary:** resurrection for final fights. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Zero need for Attack Damage or Critical. Core stats: **Max Health** (damage + tankiness + healing).

### Universal "Thousand-HP Tank" (Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |
| 🟢 Green | Void (虚空) | ×10 | Max Health +37.5, Cooldown Reduction +0.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** **+1,162 Max Health**, +23 Physical Defense, +52 HP/5s, +6% CDR, +4% Movement Speed, +10% Attack Speed. Level 1 dominance in lane and jungle skirmishes.

## Quick Tip

Ata's core three items — Giant's Grip, Blazing Cape, Phoenix Eye — have **identical icons** across global and CN servers. If you can't read the names, just match the icons. In-game, copy the CN #1 or pro player Ata setup via **Preparation → Recommended → Copy**.
