# Luban No. 7 Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Never Move During Sweep

Sweep deals %Max HP damage. **Moving cancels Sweep immediately.** When Sweep activates: stand still for the full 3-tick barrage. The damage difference between full Sweep and cancelled Sweep is enormous.

### 2. S2: Save for Self-Peel, Not Poke

S2 knocks back enemies at point-blank. **This is your only survival tool.** When an Assassin (Lanling Wang, Lam, Han Xin) dives you: S2 their face → they're pushed away → Flash → run. Never waste S2 randomly — when it's on cooldown, you're dead.

### 3. Pre-Stack Sweep for Tower Siege

Sweep out-ranges turrets. Pre-stack by AA'ing air 3-4 times, or cast any ability. Walk forward → free Sweep on the tower from outside its range. Repeat until tower falls.

### 4. Full Damage Rotation: **AA (Sweep) → S1 → Sweep → S3 → Sweep → S2 (hold) → Sweep**

1. Pre-stack Sweep with 4 AAs
2. S1 → second Sweep
3. S3 → third Sweep
4. S2 held for when they engage → fourth Sweep + knockback
5. This rotation deletes entire frontlines

### 5. S1/S3: Free Vision

S1 and S3 reveal the area. Before face-checking any bush: S1 or S3 into it first. S3 also serves as a zone tool in teamfights.

## Signature Combo

### Maximum Burst Rotation: **AA×4 → S1 → AA → S3 → AA → S2 → AA**

1. AA air 4 times → Sweep (first barrage)
2. S1 → Sweep (second barrage)
3. S3 → Sweep (third barrage)
4. S2 (save for peel or finish) → Sweep (fourth barrage)
5. Four Sweeps = dead enemy team

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S2 deals %missing HP damage.** Use to finish low-HP enemies anywhere on the map. Watch the minimap.
- **Sweep's %HP damage scales with Attack.** Infinity Edge + Dawn make your Sweep shred 20%+ Max HP per barrage.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs tanks and immobile teams. #1 target for enemy Assassins — needs a dedicated bodyguard.
