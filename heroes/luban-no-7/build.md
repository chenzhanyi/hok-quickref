# Luban No. 7 Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Sweep's %Max HP damage scales with **Physical Attack** (every 100 AD = +1% Max HP). Stack **Attack + Crit + some tankiness** to survive. Semi-tank is meta in CN server.

---

## Recommended Semi-Tank Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early AS for smooth passive stacking. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC.* |
| 2 | **Shadow Blade (影刃)** | AS + Crit. On-crit MS compensates for Luban's slow movement. |
| 3 | **Infinity Edge (无尽战刃)** | **Absolute core!** Crit Damage + high AD. Sweep's %HP damage scales with AD — this is the power spike. |
| 4 | **Blood Weeper (泣血之刃)** | 25% Lifesteal. One Sweep heals to full HP. Essential for turret-style survival. |
| 5 | **Dawn (破晓)** | Marksman capstone. Penetration + AS + AA damage amp. Sweep shreds tanks. |
| 6 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Berserker's:** defense + stacking MS/damage. **Pure Sky:** active DR vs burst. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Critical Rate, Physical Penetration, early Lifesteal.**

### Setup 1: "5 Reave 5 Hunt" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×3 | Critical Rate +0.7%, Critical Damage +3.6% |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | Critical Rate +1.6% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Reave (夺萃) | ×5 | Physical Lifesteal +1.6% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×5 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +9 Physical Attack, +64 Physical Penetration, +13.3% Crit Rate, +10.8% Crit Damage, **+8% Lifesteal**, +5% AS/MS.

### Setup 2: "10 Reave" (Max Lane Sustain — Solo Queue)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Peerless (无双) | ×3 | +10.8% Crit Dmg |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×7 | +1.6% Crit Rate |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | +64 Pen |

Enable **"Avatar Lock"** and **"Lowest Absolute HP"** targeting in settings. Luban is the #1 target — proper targeting keeps you alive.
