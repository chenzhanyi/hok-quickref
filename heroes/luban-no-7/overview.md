# Luban No. 7（魯班七號） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Turret / %HP Shredder)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 機關造物 (Mechanical Marvel)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 鲁班七号; nickname: "Little Luban")

> Luban No. 7 is one of the highest sustained-DPS Marksmen and the #1 target for enemy Assassins. His passive "Sweep" triggers after abilities, dealing **%Max HP Physical Damage** that scales with his Attack. He shreds tanks faster than any other ADC. His S2 is a global execute + point-blank knockback — his only self-peel tool.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive Sweep: %Max HP Shred

After any ability or every 4th AA, Luban enters "Sweep" — a 3-tick barrage dealing **%Max HP Physical Damage.** The % scales with his Physical Attack (every 100 AD = +1%). **Never move during Sweep** — movement cancels it immediately.

### S2: Global Execute + Self-Peel

S2 fires a global-range rocket. Deals **%missing HP damage.** Also **knocks back enemies at point-blank range.** When an Assassin dives you: S2 their face → they're knocked back → Flash away. **Never waste S2 for poke when enemies are missing.**

### Turret Siege from Range

Sweep out-ranges turrets. Pre-stack passive on air → walk forward → Sweep the tower from outside its range. Free tower damage with zero risk.

### S1/S3: Bush Check + Free Sweep Trigger

S1 and S3 provide vision. Throw them into bushes before face-checking. Both also trigger Sweep — use S3 at the start of teamfights for a free Sweep + AoE zone.
