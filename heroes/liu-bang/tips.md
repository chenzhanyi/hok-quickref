# Liu Bang Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. 4-1 Split-Push: Your Entire Identity

Mid-late game, push the lane **opposite** the current objective. When a teamfight breaks out, S3 onto your most threatened ally. You create an unwinnable situation for the enemy: send 1 person to stop you (you trade evenly), send 2+ (your team gets a 4v3 advantage). This is why Liu Bang is a pro-play staple.

### 2. Watch the Minimap Constantly

Use three-finger camera drag to watch fights while split-pushing. Don't wait for allies to ping — proactively scan for who's getting dove or who's about to engage. The best Liu Bangs S3 BEFORE the ally takes damage, not after.

### 3. Pre-Cast S1 During S3 Channel

During S3's channel (~2.5s), **press S1 to shield yourself**. When you land, this shield transfers to your protected ally. Land → S2 stun → detonate S1. You arrive with full burst ready.

### 4. S1 Pre-Cast from Bush

In lane: charge S1 from bush → S2 dash out → detonate S1 → AA. This undodgeable combo chunks half a squishy's HP. The enemy never sees it coming.

### 5. HP Stacking = Damage + Tankiness

Both S1 and passive AA scale with **%Max HP**. Every HP item is a damage item. Blazing Cape + Overlord's Platemail + Ice Grip = tanky AND devastating.

## Signature Combos

### Lane Trade: **S1 (pre-cast) → S2 (charge) → S2 (stun) → S1 (detonate) → AA**

1. S1 shield (hold it)
2. S2 charge toward enemy
3. S2 connects → stun
4. Detonate S1 → AoE burst
5. Enhanced AA → half their HP gone

### Perfect Save + Turn: **S3 (target ally) → S1 (self-shield during channel) → Land → S2 → S1 (detonate) → AA**

1. S3 onto your endangered carry
2. S1 during channel
3. Land with shield on carry
4. S2 stun the attacker
5. Detonate S1 + AA → reverse-kill

## Advanced Tips

- **S3's Damage Reduction can save allies through execute ults** (Zhuge Liang S3, Kongming S3).
- **Don't S3 the initiator who's going to die anyway.** Save it for your hyper-carry.
- **Matchup:** God-tier in coordinated play. Solo queue: ping before you S3 so allies know help is coming.
