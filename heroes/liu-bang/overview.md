# Liu Bang（劉邦） — Tank / Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Tank / Support (Global Teleport / Split-Pusher)
- **Lane:** Roaming / Clash Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 雙面君主 (Dual-Faced Sovereign)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 刘邦)

> Liu Bang is a strategically invaluable global-teleport Tank. His entire identity revolves around the **4-1 split-push**: pressure a side lane alone, and when a teamfight breaks out, S3 instantly teleports to an ally anywhere on the map — granting them massive Damage Reduction + Shield, then landing to turn the fight. His damage and shields scale entirely with **Max HP**, so building tanky makes him hit harder.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S3: Global Teleport (Tactical Core)

S3 targets an ally anywhere on the map. During channel: grants them **massive Damage Reduction + Shield**. Liu Bang then teleports to them, granting nearby allies **Dual Defense buffs**. This is the ultimate "save the carry" and "turn a losing fight" ability.

### HP = Damage

S1 shield damage and passive enhanced AA damage both scale with **%Max HP**. Stacking HP makes Liu Bang tankier AND hit harder — the perfect feedback loop.

### S1: Shield + Detonate

S1 grants a shield. If it's not broken, **recast to detonate** for AoE Magic Damage. In lane: pre-cast S1 from bush → S2 charge in → detonate S1 → AA. Undodgeable burst.

### S2: Charged Dash + CC

S2 is a charged dash. Longer charge = longer distance + longer stun. Your primary engage and escape tool.
