# Liu Bang Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** All damage and shields scale with **Max HP**. Pure HP stacking = tankier + harder-hitting. Add CDR to reduce S3 cooldown.

---

## Recommended Clash Lane / Support Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Blazing Cape (红莲斗篷)** | **Liu Bang's mandatory first item!** Physical Defense + Max HP. Burn aura solves his slow wave clear and synergizes with his melee style. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Prevents CC-lock when caught split-pushing or landing from S3. |
| 3 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** or **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | **Frost Grip:** enhanced AA after abilities perfectly triggers AoE slow. **Berserker's Armor:** stacking MS + damage for split-push escapes. |
| 4 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** | Highest Max HP in the game. Directly amplifies S1 shield/damage + passive AA. Continuous regen = never recall. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | **Eternal Night:** burst-heal vs magic. **Magic Cloak:** sustained magic defense + spell shield. |
| 6 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** or **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | **Pure Sky:** active DR — stacks with S3's DR for near-immunity. **Sanctuary:** resurrection. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Max Health (damage + shields), Attack Speed (faster passive AA), Movement Speed (split-push kiting).**

### Setup 1: "Thousand-HP Attack Speed" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Destiny (宿命) | ×10 | Max Health +33.7, Physical Defense +2.3, Attack Speed +1% |

**Total:** **+712 Max Health**, +23 Physical Defense, **+20% Attack Speed**, **+10% MS**, +6% CDR.
