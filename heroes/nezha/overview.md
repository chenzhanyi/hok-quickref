## Core Mechanics

### Passive: True Damage Burn + Anti-Heal

All damage applies **Fire Lotus Seal** вҖ” continuous True Damage + **15% Healing Reduction**. This hard-counters sustain/lifesteal Heroes (Cheng Yaojin, Cai Yan, lifesteal ADCs).

### S1: Movement Speed per Enemy Hit

S1 spins and hits nearby enemies. **Each enemy Hero hit grants a Movement Speed stack** (max 5). Fight near minion waves for max stacks.

### S2: Dash + 15-20% Damage Reduction

S2 rolls behind the enemy and grants **15-20% DR**. Has 2 charges. **Don't burn both instantly.** Use first charge вҶ’ enjoy 3s of DR вҶ’ use second charge right before DR expires or when enemy dashes away.

### S3: Global Lock-On + Engage

Reveals ALL enemies (cancels their recalls), then fly to a chosen target. **Landing grants massive Tenacity + Fire Lotus Seal.** The threat of S3 alone forces enemies to play differently вҖ” you don't always need to fly.

### Split-Push Strategy (4-1)

Nezha is the best split-pusher because his S3 lets him join any fight instantly. Push a side lane вҶ’ when a teamfight breaks out вҶ’ S3 the enemy backline. This creates an unstoppable numbers advantage.
