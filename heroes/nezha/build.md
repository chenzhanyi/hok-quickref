# Nezha Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs **tankiness** (survive S3 dive), **Physical Penetration** (S1 + passive damage), and **Movement Speed**. CN server consensus: **heavy semi-tank bruiser**.

---

## Recommended Clash Lane Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity stacks with S3 landing bonus — easily hits Tenacity cap. CC becomes nearly irrelevant. |
| 2 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Nezha's only core damage item. CDR, Health, flat Penetration. Enough burst to solo-kill squishies on S3 engage. |
| 3 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | Nezha constantly weaves abilities + AAs. Perfectly triggers Frost Grip's slow on-hit. Massive stickiness + Physical Defense. |
| 4 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Taking damage (inevitable when S3 diving) grants stacking **MS + damage output**. Combined with S1's MS stacks, Nezha becomes impossibly fast. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | **Eternal Night:** burst-heal vs magic burst — perfect for S3 dives into heavy magic teams. **Magic Cloak:** sustained magic defense. |
| 6 | **Blood Rage (血魔之怒)** or **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | **Blood Rage:** massive shield at low HP + bonus on-hit damage. Lets Nezha survive focus-fire in the enemy backline. **Sanctuary:** resurrection. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core needs: **Physical Penetration, Movement Speed, Max Health.**

### Setup 1: "Stealth 100-Pen" (Highest Winrate)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, **+100 Physical Penetration**, +10% Movement Speed. Max S3 landing burst.

### Setup 2: "Harmony Sustain" (vs Lane Bullies)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×10 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +29 Physical Attack, +100 Physical Penetration, **+450 Max Health**, +52 HP/5s, +4% Movement Speed. Survive Kuang Tie, Ji Xiaoman, Sikong Zhen in lane.
