# Angela（安琪拉） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Burst / Bush Ambusher)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 暗夜蘿莉 (Dark Lolita)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 安琪拉)

> Angela is a classic burst Mage with terrifying ambush potential. Her S1 fires 5 fireballs — if all 5 hit the same target, it instantly stacks half her passive. Her S3 grants a massive shield + **Unstoppable** during the channel, letting her face-tank Assassin dives and retaliate. She's the queen of bush camping: S2 → S1 → S3 deletes anyone who walks past the wrong bush.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Stack Stacks = Exponential Damage

Abilities stack burns (max 10). Each stack amplifies subsequent damage. **The more stacks, the more exponential the damage.** A fully-stacked S3 deals 2-3x the base damage.

### S1: 5 Fireballs — Make Them All Hit

S1 fires 5 fireballs. **If all 5 hit one target, instant 5 passive stacks.** Close-range S1 on a stunned enemy = maximum damage.

### S2: Slow Projectile — Predict Movement

S2's fireball travels slowly. Don't aim where they are — **aim half a step ahead of their movement path.** From bush, it's nearly undodgeable.

### S3: Unstoppable + Shield (Anti-Dive)

S3 grants a massive shield + **Unstoppable (CC-immune)** during the entire channel. When an Assassin dives (Lanling Wang, Wukong, Li Bai): **don't run — S3 their face.** The shield + CC immunity means you out-trade them. Press S3 again after 0.5s to cancel early.

### S3 + Flash: Reposition the Laser

During S3, you can **Flash to reposition the laser.** If the enemy Flashes out of range: Flash to extend the beam onto them.
