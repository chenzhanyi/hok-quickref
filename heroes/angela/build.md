# Angela Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Pure burst. Instant deletion. **Magic Penetration + high AP + Movement Speed** for rotations and bush camping.

---

## Recommended Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Arcane Boots (秘法之靴)** | Flat Magic Penetration + Mana regen. Early kill pressure. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots vs heavy CC.* |
| 2 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | Core burst. S1/S3 triggers AoE explosion. +MS compensates for no mobility. |
| 3 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP boost. After this, one combo deletes any squishy. |
| 4 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Penetration vs late-game Magic Cloak. |
| 5 | **Devourer's Tome (噬神之书)** or **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** | **Devourer's Tome:** massive spell vamp — S3 healing is absurd. **Sage's Tome:** max AP + damage amp for one-shot potential. |
| 6 | **Splendor (辉月)** | Stasis for survival when S3 is on cooldown. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Magic Penetration, Movement Speed, mild Attack Speed.**

### Setup 1: "88-Pen Hunt Speed" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +16% AS, **+10% MS**. Max rotation speed for bush camping.

### Setup 2: "Harmony Sustain" (vs Poke Lanes)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +88 Magic Penetration, +6% AS, **+450 Max Health**, +52 HP/5s, +4% MS. Better survival vs Gan & Mo, Mai Shiranui.
