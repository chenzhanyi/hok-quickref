# Angela Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Bush Camping Is Your Identity

Angela is the queen of bush ambushes. Hide in any bush near enemy rotation paths. Enemy walks past: **S2 (point-blank, undodgeable) → S1 (all 5 hit) → S3.** Even full tanks die to this. Rotate between bushes after each kill — the enemy will fear every bush on the map.

### 2. S2: Predict, Don't React

S2 travels slowly. Against aware enemies, aim **half a step ahead** of their movement. From bush or fog of war, it's undodgeable.

### 3. S3: Anti-Dive, Not Just Damage

When an Assassin dives you: **S3 immediately.** The massive shield + Unstoppable means you tank their entire burst + CC, then melt them during the channel. Never panic and run — Angela wins stand-and-fight trades.

### 4. S3 + Flash: Beam Extension

During S3, Flash to reposition the laser. If the enemy Flashes or dashes out of range, Flash-chase them with the beam. Also: S3 from bush → Flash into the open = surprise death ray.

### 5. S1 Close-Range = Maximum Stacks

S1's 5 fireballs spread out. At range: 1-2 hit. **At close range: all 5 hit one target.** After S2 stun, walk forward and point-blank S1 for instant 5 passive stacks → S3 for the amplified finish.

## Signature Combos

### Bush Delete: **S2 → S1 → S3**

1. Enemy walks past your bush
2. S2 at point-blank (guaranteed stun)
3. S1 close-range (all 5 fireballs hit)
4. S3 channel — dead before they recover from stun

### Anti-Dive Reversal: **S3 → S2 → S1**

1. Assassin dives you
2. S3 immediately (shield + Unstoppable — tank their burst)
3. S2 at your feet (stun them in the laser)
4. S1 to finish

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S3 grants Unstoppable for the ENTIRE channel.** Daji charm, Mozi cannon — irrelevant.
- **Cancel S3 early (press again after 0.5s).** Don't commit to the full channel if it'll miss.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile squishies. Struggles vs tanky dive comps that survive her combo.
