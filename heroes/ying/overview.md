# Ying（雲纓） — Assassin / Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin / Fighter
- **Lane:** Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★☆
- **Title:** 燎原之心 (Heart of the Prairie Fire)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 云缨)

> Ying is a high-skill-cap skirmisher whose core mechanic revolves around **Spear Intent (枪意)** — each ability cast stacks one layer (max 3), and her enhanced basic attack consumes all layers to unleash a unique effect while reducing Ability 1 & 2 cooldowns by 1.5s.

---

## Skills & Spear Intent Mechanics

### Passive: Spear Intent (枪意)

Casting any ability grants 1 stack of Spear Intent (max 3). Her next basic attack consumes all stacks:

| Layer | Name | Effect | Use Case |
|-------|------|--------|----------|
| **1 Stack** | Firm Intent · Truce (坚意·止戈) | Rapid flurry of strikes, gains **Shield + 30% Damage Reduction** | Jungle clear, wave clear, turret pushing, close-range duels |
| **2 Stacks** | Keen Intent · Citybreaker (锐意·摧城) | Forward thrusting combo, **restores HP** (more healing at lower HP) | Mid-range poke, sustain, attrition trades |
| **3 Stacks** | True Intent · Prairie Fire (真意·燎原) | Leaps into the air (+200% move speed), channels then slams down a flame path. **Execute damage** (bonus when target HP < 50%) | Long-range finisher, teamfight edge harassment, securing kills |

> **Tip:** During the 3-stack enhanced attack, pull the left joystick in the **opposite direction** to change the slam direction.

### Abilities

- **Ability 1 (S1):** AoE slash + knockup. Main crowd control tool.
- **Ability 2 (S2):** Charge forward with slow. Can be cancelled. **Max this first** for lower CD and higher move speed.
- **Ability 3 / Ultimate (S3):** Two-part charge + sweep. **Grants crowd-control immunity (Unstoppable) and high damage reduction** during both phases. Use reactively against enemy CC.
