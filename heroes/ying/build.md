# Ying Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Ying is a **CDR-dependent, semi-tank skirmisher**. Full damage builds will get deleted. CN server consensus: **bruiser-assassin hybrid**.

---

## Recommended Jungle Build (Most Common)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Greedy Bite (贪婪之噬)** | Physical Attack + Physical Penetration. Core jungle item. |
| 2 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | Early CDR to cap. *Swap to Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴) or defensive items late game when CDR overflows.* |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | HP + Attack + Penetration. Essential for deleting squishies. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | **Ying's perfect item!** Her enhanced AAs (Spear Intent) **fully trigger Frost Grip's slow + on-hit effects**. Massive stickiness and durability spike. |
| 5 | **Siege Breaker (破军)** | Extreme Physical Attack. **Stacks with Ying's 3-stack execute passive** (< 50% HP bonus damage). One slam, one kill. |
| 6 | **Flex Defensive Slot** | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** vs heavy magic burst; **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** for active DR vs physical burst; or **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** for balanced durability. |

### Late-Game Swaps

- Once **Greedy Bite** is fully stacked and CDR overflows, sell **Calm Boots** for:
  - **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** — Tenacity vs heavy CC
  - **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** — Resurrection
  - **Fate's Edge (名刀·司命)** — Fatal damage immunity

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Ying does **NOT need critical chance** as a core stat. **Physical Penetration, Movement Speed, and a touch of Attack Speed / Lifesteal** are the unanimous choices among top CN players.

### Setup 1: CN Server "100-Pen Hybrid" (High Burst)

The most popular setup. Early-game damage is oppressive.

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats |
|------|------|-----|-------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×7 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 each |
| 🔴 Red | Legacy (传承) | ×3 | Physical Attack +3.2 each *(or just 10× Mutation)* |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 each |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×7 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% each |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×3 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% each |

**Total:** ~100 Physical Penetration, high base Physical Attack, +10% Movement Speed, +3% Attack Speed.

### Setup 2: Global / Balanced "Micro-Crit" (Solo Queue Safe)

Used by some Global "Ying" players. Small crit chance for lucky burst during extended AA chains.

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats |
|------|------|-----|-------|
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×6 | Critical Rate +1.6% each |
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×4 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 each |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 each |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% each *(or mix in Hunt)* |

**Total:** ~9.6% Critical Rate, solid Penetration, +10% Movement Speed. The crit chance occasionally spikes during Ying's multi-hit 1-stack and 2-stack enhanced AAs.
