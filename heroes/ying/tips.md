# Ying Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Understand the Three Spear Intent Layers

Ying's entire kit orbits around **Spear Intent (枪意)** — each ability cast adds 1 stack (max 3). Your enhanced basic attack consumes all stacks and reduces S1 & S2 cooldowns by 1.5s.

- **1 Stack (Firm Intent · Truce):** Rapid strikes + **Shield & 30% DR**. Use for **jungle clear, wave clear, turret push**, and close-range brawls when outnumbered.
- **2 Stacks (Keen Intent · Citybreaker):** Thrust combo + **HP restore** (higher heal when lower HP). Use for **mid-range poke, attrition trades, and sustain**.
- **3 Stacks (True Intent · Prairie Fire):** Airborne leap (+200% MS) → charged flame slam with **execute damage** (bonus when enemy HP < 50%). Use for **long-range finishers and teamfight edge harassment**.

> During 3-stack enhanced attack, pull the **left joystick opposite** to redirect the slam.

### 2. Pre-Stack Spear Intent

- Ying's abilities **stack Spear Intent even when cast into empty air**.
- S2 still grants a stack even if you **cancel the charge**.
- Before ganking or teamfighting, hide in a bush and **pre-stack 1–2 layers** by whiffing abilities.

### 3. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take ultimate whenever available.**

- Leveling S1 only adds negligible base damage.
- Leveling S2 **significantly reduces cooldown** and **increases movespeed cap** — far more value for Ying's skirmish-heavy playstyle.

### 4. Ultimate = Unstoppable + Damage Reduction

Ying's S3 (both the charge and sweep phases) grants **Unstoppable (霸体) and high damage reduction**. Use it reactively to:

- **Ignore enemy CC** — tank through Daji, Mai Shiranui, Zhang Fei, etc.
- **Turn fights** — absorb burst while dealing damage.
- **Escape** — use the two dashes to disengage over walls.

## Signature Combos (CN Server / High-Rank)

### Burst Gank / Delete Backline: **2 → 3 → 3 → 1 → A**

1. **S2 charge** — gap-close with slow
2. **S3 first cast** — dash + push
3. **S3 second cast** — sweep + push again
4. **S1** — knockup to lock them down
5. **Enhanced AA (3 stacks)** — airborne execute slam to finish

> Classic one-shot combo. Deleting squishies from full HP.

### Skirmish / Sustain Duel: **2 → A → 1 → A** (repeat)

- Weave 1-stack and 2-stack enhanced attacks while cycling S2 → S1.
- Constant shields, healing, and CD refunds wear down opponents in extended fights.
- Ideal against tanks and bruisers.

### Max CDR Double True Intent: **2 → 1 → 3 → A → 1 → 3 → 2 → A**

- At **40% CDR** (late game), you can fit **two 3-stack flame slams** in one combo cycle.
- Requires specific itemization (see [build.md](build.md)).
- Devastating in late-game teamfights.

## Advanced Tips

- **Turret pressure:** 1-stack enhanced AA provides a shield — use it to tank 1–2 turret shots while sieging.
- **Vision deny:** S2 charge can go through thin walls for unexpected gank angles.
- **Flame path zoning:** 3-stack slam leaves a burning trail — use it to block escape routes, not just for damage.
- **Cooldown management:** Every enhanced AA reduces S1 & S2 cooldowns by 1.5s. Aggressively weave autos between abilities — never just dump all skills.
- **Teamfight positioning:** Stay at the edge with 3-stack intent charged. Leap in only when an enemy drops below 50% HP for the execute.
