# Eclipse Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Eclipse is squishy and roams constantly. Needs max **CDR**, max **Movement Speed**, and enough **tankiness** to survive rushing into 5 enemies.

---

## Recommended Tempo-Setting Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Wolf Rush (极影·奔狼)** | **Core support item.** Wolf Rush active: team-wide speed burst. Stacks with S3 + S1 for lightning-fast engages. |
| 2 | **Swift Boots (疾步之靴)** | Must be the fastest Hero on the map. Swift Boots + S1 = unmatched roaming. |
| 3 | **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** | **20% CDR** + Physical Defense. Frost shockwave on burst helps Eclipse escape after pulling. |
| 4 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic Defense + spell shield. Prevents getting poked out while charging engages. |
| 5 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Reduces enemy AS/MS on-hit — helps survival after diving in. |
| 6 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** | **Eternal Night:** vs magic burst. **Overlord's Platemail:** infinite sustain — never recall, constant map pressure. |

## Battlefield Mindset

Eclipse is **team-dependent**. If your carries are scaling champions (Garo, Daji), they may lack early damage to follow your engages. Ping "Attack" before going in. If your team doesn't respond, don't force it — wait for the right window.
