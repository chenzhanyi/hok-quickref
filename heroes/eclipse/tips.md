# Eclipse Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S1: Don't Auto-Attack During Stealth

S1 Camouflage cancels if you AA or cast other abilities. Correct flow:
1. **S1 → touch enemies (defense shred applied)**
2. **Then S2 → pull**
Never AA during S1 — it breaks the stealth.

### 2. S2 Fake-Out Charge

S2 has a 2-second delay before pulling. Without Flash, don't run straight at enemies — they'll just walk away. Instead:
1. Start S2 from a bush
2. Let it charge for ~1.5 seconds
3. S1 or Wolf Rush (support item) to dash into enemies at the last instant
4. S2 pops right as you arrive — instant pull

### 3. S3: Vision + Stealth = Anti-Ambush

S3 reveals the nearest enemy Hero. Use proactively:
- Before face-checking Dragon/Baron pit
- When Lanling Wang or Ake might be nearby
- To spot bush campers before your team walks in

### 4. S2 + Flash: Undodgeable Engage

S2 → charge for 1.5s → **Flash directly onto the enemy backline** at the last instant. S2 pops, stunning everyone. The Flash makes this nearly impossible to react to.

### 5. Communication Is Everything

Eclipse is useless if teammates don't follow up. When you're about to engage: **ping "Attack" or "On my way."** If your Mid/ADC are scaling champions (Garo, Daji), they may not have enough damage early — adjust your aggression accordingly.

## Signature Combo

### Lightning War Engage: **S3 → Wolf Rush → S1 → S2 → Flash**

1. **S3:** Team-wide stealth + MS
2. **Wolf Rush (support item):** Extra team speed burst
3. **S1:** +40% MS, tag enemies for defense shred
4. **S2:** Charge for 1.5s
5. **Flash:** Dive into enemy backline at the last instant
6. **S2 pops:** Multiple enemies pulled + stunned → team cleanup

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S2 shield stacks per Hero pulled.** A 3-man pull = massive team shield.
- **Counter-pick into immobile comps.** Eclipse destroys teams without dashes. Struggles vs highly mobile teams that dodge S2.
- **Don't over-engage without S3.** Eclipse's engage without team stealth is predictable and easily dodged.
