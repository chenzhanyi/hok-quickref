# Eclipse（鬼谷子） — Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Support (Engage / Tempo-Setter)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 萬物有靈 (Spirit of All Things)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 鬼谷子)

> Eclipse is an extremely aggressive, tempo-setting Support with one of the game's few team-wide Camouflage mechanics. He excels at leading his team in stealth for surprise "blitzkrieg" engages. His signature: group stealth → group pull → group stun → team-wipe.

---

## Core Mechanics

### S1: Camouflage + Defense Shred

Enter Camouflage with +40% MS. **Enemies you touch during stealth lose 25% Physical & Magic Defense.** Don't just use S1 to travel — use it to tag enemies before your team engages.

### S2: Group Pull + Stun

After 2 seconds, pulls all enemies in range toward Eclipse + **1s stun**. If an enemy Hero is pulled, all nearby allies gain a stackable shield. This is your game-winning ability.

### S3: Team-Wide Stealth + Vision

Grant Camouflage + 30% MS to ALL nearby allies. Also **reveals the nearest enemy Hero** — hard-counters Lanling Wang, Ake, and bush ambushes.

### Golden Rule: Don't AA During S1

Auto-attacking or casting other abilities **cancels S1's Camouflage**. Correct sequence: S1 → touch enemies to shred defense → THEN S2 to pull.
