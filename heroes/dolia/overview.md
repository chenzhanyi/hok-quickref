# Dolia（朵莉亞） — Support

## Overview

- **Role:** Support (Cooldown Refresher / Water Mage)
- **Lane:** Roaming
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 人魚之歌 (Mermaid's Song)
- **Region:** CN server exclusive (朵莉亚)

> Dolia is a strategically invaluable utility Support with a unique **Mermaid Form** (water/land dual environment). Her game-breaking mechanic: **S3 refreshes the longest-cooldown ability of a targeted ally** — effectively giving them a second ultimate. She's the ultimate "combo enabler."

---

## Core Mechanics

### Mermaid Form (Water Environment)

When Dolia enters the river OR her own S2 water zone, she transforms into Mermaid Form: **+MS, %HP + %Mana regen per second, and AAs become AoE water blades.** Stay in water whenever possible.

### S1: Bush Check + Knockback

S1 fires 4 sound waves. **In water: the 4th wave becomes a massive knockback + stun.** The detection range is enormous — fire S1 toward suspicious bushes. If the wave changes behavior: enemies inside. Safest bush-check in the game.

### S3: Refresh Ally's Longest Cooldown

S3 refreshes the **longest-cooldown ability** of a targeted ally (usually their ultimate). Bind to a specific ally via the S3 UI wheel. **If unbonded, auto-targets nearest ally with longest CD.**

### Best S3 Partners

S3 enables "double ultimate" game-breaking combos with: **Lu Bu** (double sky slam), **Guitarist** (double nuclear concert), **Wu Zetian** (double global stun), **Shangguan** (double aerial assassination), **Wukong** (double staff), **Bai Qi** (double AoE taunt). Avoid pairing with heroes whose ults aren't game-changing (Marco, Arli, Han Xin).
