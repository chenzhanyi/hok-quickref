# Dolia Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S3: Bind the Right Ally

S3 refreshes the longest-cooldown ability of the targeted ally. **Always bind your strongest ultimate combo partner.** Bind in fountain at match start via S3 wheel. Priority: Lu Bu > Guitarist > Wu Zetian > Shangguan > Bai Qi. Don't bind Marco, Arli, or Han Xin — their ults aren't game-changing.

### 2. S1 in Water = Knockback + Stun

S1's 4th wave becomes a knockback + stun when cast in water. Always: **S2 (create water) → S1 immediately.** The control is massive. Use to peel Assassins off your carry.

### 3. Stay in Water for Infinite Sustain

In Mermaid Form: %HP + %Mana regen per second. Between fights: stand in river or S2 water zone. Never recall for Mana or HP — Dolia sustains forever.

### 4. S1: Best Bush-Check in the Game

S1's detection range is enormous. Fire S1 toward any suspicious bush. If the wave changes (4th wave triggers knockback) → enemies inside. Zero risk, infinite range.

### 5. 40% CDR Is Mandatory

S3 shares cooldown with the ally's ability. If your CDR is low, S3 takes forever. Stack: Shadow Aegis aura (15%) + Calm Boots (15%) + runes (6-10%) = 40% cap.

## Signature Combo

### Anti-Dive Peel: **S2 → S1 → S3**

1. S2 create water at your feet (or you're in river)
2. S1 → 4th wave knockback + stun the diving Assassin
3. S3 your ADC (refreshes their escape/ultimate)

### Lane Sustain: **S2 → stand in water**

1. S2 at your feet
2. Stand there. HP + Mana regen.
3. Never recall.

## Advanced Tips

- **S1's 4th wave is directional.** Face the enemy when casting for maximum knockback distance.
- **Heino + Dolia = Time Freeze at level 15.** Dolia's S3 refreshes Heino's S3.
- **Matchup:** God-tier with high-impact ultimate allies. Struggles when team has no strong ultimate to refresh.
