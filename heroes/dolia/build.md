# Dolia Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Must hit **40% CDR.** S3 shares cooldown — low CDR = S3 takes forever. Needs tankiness (survive to cast S3). CN server consensus: **max CDR tank Support.**

---

## Recommended Support Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎)** or **Shadow Aegis · Star Spring (极影·星泉)** | Shadow Aegis aura: **+15% CDR** for Dolia + team. **Redemption:** group shield. **Star Spring:** sustained healing. |
| 2 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | +15% CDR. Combined with aura = 30% CDR. Add runes = 40% cap. |
| 3 | **Rising Sun (旭日初光)** | +10% CDR + Physical Defense + HP. S1 spam procs shield on Dolia + lowest-HP ally. |
| 4 | **Frost Breath (凝冰之息)** | S1's 4 waves apply **massive AoE perma-slow.** Enemies can't escape. |
| 5 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Physical defense vs ADCs. Magic defense vs burst Mages. |
| 6 | **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** or **Prophet's Hour (时之预言)** | Resurrection (Dolia must survive to cast S3) or AP + dual defenses. |

### CDR Breakdown

|--------|-----|
