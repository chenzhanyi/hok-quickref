## Core Mechanics

### S2 Passive: Stacking HP

Every kill or assist permanently grants **+180 Max HP** (max 20 stacks = +3,600 HP). Even a full-damage Kui becomes as tanky as a pure tank by late game. This is why Kui scales so well.

### S2 Hook (The Signature)

Long-range hook that pulls the first enemy hit. Combined with **Flash**, you can hook backward during the cast to pull enemies from absurd distances вАФ into your tower or team.

### S3 Ultimate: Only Stuns Hooked Targets

S3 deals multi-hit damage and **stuns enemies marked by S2**. If you S3 without hooking first, enemies are only slowed and can walk out easily. **Always S2 вЖТ S3.**

### The Threat Is Stronger Than the Hook

A missed hook = you're useless for ~12 seconds. Sometimes the smartest play is to **hold your hook** вАФ your mere presence forces enemies to play defensively, forfeiting CS and position. Don't feel pressured to throw it.
