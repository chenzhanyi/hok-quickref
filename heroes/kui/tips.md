# Kui Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. The Threat Is Stronger Than the Hook

A Kui with hook OFF cooldown is terrifying. Enemies must constantly dodge, forfeiting CS and positioning. The moment you throw and miss, you're a walking target for 12 seconds. **Don't feel pressured to throw it.** Sometimes just walking forward menacingly wins the lane.

### 2. S2 + Flash: Insane Pull Range

Cast S2, then **Flash backward during the cast animation**. The hook travels from your original position but the pull destination is your NEW position. This lets you drag enemies from off-screen into your tower. The most satisfying mechanic in the game.

### 3. S2 Hook: Jungle Invade Level 1

Kui's classic level-1 cheese: walk to the enemy's Blue/Red buff. When the monster is at ~50% HP, **hook it over the wall**. The monster resets and starts healing. The enemy jungler loses 10+ seconds. Repeat at every buff spawn. This alone can win games.

### 4. S3: Only Stuns Hooked Targets

S3 stuns ONLY enemies who were recently hooked (S2). If you raw S3 without hooking, enemies are just slowed and can walk out. **Always S2 → S3.** If you miss the hook, save S3 for after the next hook lands.

### 5. S3 + Flash: Reposition Mid-Ultimate

S3 roots Kui in place, but **you can Flash during S3**. Use this to: catch multiple enemies who tried to walk out, or redirect the stun onto a priority target.

## Signature Combos

### Standard Delete Combo: **S2 → S1 → S3**

1. S2 hook → pull enemy to you
2. S1 immediately (deals double damage at close range + slow)
3. S3 — enemy is hooked = stunned, can't escape
4. Team follows up → dead

### Bush Ambush: **S3 (start from bush) → Flash → S2 → S1**

1. Start S3 from bush (enemies walk into it)
2. Flash during S3 to reposition onto priority targets
3. When S3 ends, enemies scatter
4. S2 hook one fleeing survivor
5. S1 finish

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Hook priority targets only.** Hooking the enemy tank into your team can lose the fight.
- **S2 passive makes you a late-game monster.** With 20 stacks, you have +3,600 HP. Full AP Kui with tank HP is terrifying.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile squishies. Struggles when enemies have multiple dashes to dodge hooks.
