# Kui Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Two distinct paths. **Tank Support** (highest winrate, team-oriented) and **Full AP Burst** (solo carry, one-hook kills).

---

## Build A: Tank Support (Recommended)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Shadow Aegis · Redemption (极影·救赎)** or **Guard · Star Spring (近卫·星泉)** | Shadow Aegis aura. **Redemption:** group shield. **Star Spring:** sustained healing. |
| 2 | **Swift Boots (疾步之靴)** | Movement Speed is everything — find hook angles. *Late: swap to Resistance Boots.* |
| 3 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Reduces enemy AS on-hit. |
| 4 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** or **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense. Eternal Night vs burst; Magic Cloak vs sustain poke. |
| 5 | **Glacial Storm (极寒风暴)** | **20% CDR** + Physical Defense. More hooks = more kills = more passive stacks. |
| 6 | **Overlord's Platemail (霸者重装)** or **Sage's Sanctuary (贤者的庇护)** | Overlord's: massive HP + passive HP stacking = 10,000+ HP. Sanctuary: resurrection. |

---

## Build B: Full AP Burst (Mid Lane / Fun)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | Calm Boots (冷静之靴) / Arcane Boots (秘法之靴) | CDR or Penetration. |
| 2 | Echo Staff (回响之杖) | AoE explosion on hook hit. |
| 3 | Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒) | AP spike — one hook = half HP. |
| 4 | Void Staff (虚无法杖) | Penetration vs magic resist. |
| 5 | Sage's Tome (贤者之书) | Massive AP + damage amp. |
| 6 | Splendor (辉月) or Ardent Dominator (炽热支配者) | Stasis after hooking for team follow-up. |

## Quick Mindset Tip

Don't force hooks. Your hook's THREAT is often more valuable than the hook itself. Walk forward, zone enemies, and only throw when you're confident. A patient Kui wins more games than an impatient one.
