# Princess Frost Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Needs **CDR** (frequent S2+S3), **Magic Penetration**, and **semi-tank survivability** (must stay alive to control fights).

---

## Recommended Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | CDR for frequent S1/S2. More freezes = more kills. |
| 2 | **Mask of Agony (痛苦面具)** | CDR + multi-tick %HP burn. S3's continuous damage endlessly triggers Mask. |
| 3 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | Burst + MS. Enhances S1 poke and roaming speed. |
| 4 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage AP spike. After this, one combo deletes squishies. |
| 5 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Penetration vs late-game magic resist. |
| 6 | **Sage's Tome (贤者之书)** or **Splendor (辉月)** | **Tome:** max AP + damage amp. **Splendor:** Stasis vs heavy dive. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Magic Penetration, CDR, Movement Speed.**

### Setup 1: "Harmony 88-Pen" (CN Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Mind's Eye (心眼) | ×10 | Attack Speed +0.6%, Magic Penetration +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Harmony (调和) | ×10 | Max Health +45, Move Speed +0.4%, HP/5s +5.2 |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, **+88 Magic Penetration**, +450 Max Health, +4% MS, +6% AS. Near true-damage vs squishies with no magic resist.

### Setup 2: "10% CDR Spam"

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Nightmare (梦魇) | ×10 | Magic Attack +4.2, Magic Pen +2.4 |
| 🟢 Green | Compassion (怜悯) | ×10 | Cooldown Reduction +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×10 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +42 Magic Attack, +24 Magic Penetration, +10% CDR, +10% MS, +16% AS. With Calm Boots = ~25% CDR at level 1. S2 available every few seconds.
