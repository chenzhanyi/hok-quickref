# Princess Frost Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. S2: Never Cast at Moving Feet

S2 has a delay. Enemies walking will dodge it. Technique: **S1 slow first → predict their escape path → S2 ahead of them.** Or cast from bush (they can't see the cast animation).

### 2. S3: Zone, Don't Just Kill

S3's massive AoE is a zoning tool as much as a damage tool. Use S3 to:
- Block enemy retreat paths in jungle corridors
- Protect your ADC (drop S3 on top of them when dived)
- Zone enemies off Dragon/Baron
- Force enemies to walk around — splitting their formation

### 3. Passive Shield: Trade Aggressively

Your shield absorbs damage and explodes when broken. Walk forward with shield up, AA the enemy laner — their trade damage hits your shield, you get free poke in return. Once shield breaks, play safe until it regenerates.

### 4. S1 Bush Check

S1 has wide detection. Throw S1 into suspicious bushes. If enemies are inside: the slow procs OR your passive shield breaks. Never face-check — always S1 first.

### 5. Anti-Dive S3 + S2

When an Assassin dives you: **S3 at your feet → S2 yourself.** The Assassin must stand in the blizzard to attack you. If S2 freezes them, they take amplified damage and die in the storm.

---

## Signature Combos

### Standard Lockdown: **S1 → S2 → S3**

1. S1 slow (makes S2 undodgeable)
2. S2 freeze (predict ahead)
3. S3 full damage on frozen target

### Bush Ambush: **S2 → S1 → S3**

1. S2 from bush (zero cast-animation warning)
2. S1 + S3 immediately
3. Frozen target takes amplified damage — squishies die before thawing

### Self-Defense: **S3 → S2 → S1**

1. Assassin dives you
2. S3 at your feet (they must stand in it)
3. S2 yourself (freeze them in the storm)
4. S1 finish

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Frozen targets take bonus S2 damage.** Always follow a successful freeze with S1+S3 for maximum burst.
- **S3's slow stacks with items (Frost Breath).** Enemies in your blizzard are practically rooted.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs immobile comps and objective fights. Struggles vs hyper-mobile Assassins who dash past S2.
