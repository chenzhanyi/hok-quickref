# Princess Frost（王昭君） — Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Mage (Zone Control / Teamfight Engine)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 冰雪之華 (Frost Bloom)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 王昭君)

> Princess Frost is a dominant zone-control Mage with massive AoE CC and high burst. She's a "teamfight engine" — her S2 freeze and S3 blizzard can single-handedly win fights. More than raw damage, her strength is in **prediction, zoning, and battlefield control.**

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Frost Shield

After exiting combat, gains a shield. When broken, deals AoE damage + slow. This is your "trading tool" — walk forward with shield up, AA the enemy, the shield absorbs trade damage.

### S2: Freeze (Soul Skill)

S2 has a delay — **never cast directly at a moving enemy's feet.** Technique: S1 slow first → predict their escape path → S2 there. From bush or after S1, it's nearly undodgeable.

### S3: Blizzard (Zone Control)

Massive AoE with continuous damage. Don't just use for kills — use to: **zone enemies off objectives, protect your ADC, block choke points, or force enemies to walk around it.**

### S1: Bush Check + Slow

S1 has wide detection range + slow. Approaching a suspicious bush? S1 first. If the slow procs or your shield breaks, someone's inside.
