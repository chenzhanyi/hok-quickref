# Menki（夢奇） — Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Fighter (Dual-Scaling Brawler)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 入夢之靈 (Dream Spirit)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 梦奇)

> Menki received a rework and became an extremely flexible Hero with a unique **Mass mechanic**. As Mass increases, Menki grows larger and gains massive Dual Defense + Physical Attack. At full Mass he's a walking fortress; at zero Mass he's one of the fastest Heroes in the game. Two viable playstyles: **AD Physical Bruiser** (mainstream) and **AP Magic Poke** (situational).

---

## Core Mechanics

### Mass (Energy) Management

- **High Mass = Large body + high Dual Defense + high Physical Attack.** Use S1 to gain shield + Mass. Stay at high Mass for fights.
- **Zero Mass = Small body + extremely fast Movement Speed.** Never cast S1 when rotating — stay small until you reach the fight.

### S2: Bubble Boomerang

S2 throws a bubble that returns. If Menki catches the returning bubble, it **upgrades into an execute bomb** — recast for massive burst damage.

### S3: 360-Degree Ring Dash

S3 makes Menki dash in a circular arc. During the dash: **Slow-immune**, gains Mass, damages enemies in path. Recast to stop early and **knockup**. Use for: backline dives, escapes, flanking.
