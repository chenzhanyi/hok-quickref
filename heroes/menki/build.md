# Menki Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Two viable paths. **AD Bruiser** (mainstream, highest winrate) leverages Mass-granted Physical Attack. **AP Poke** (situational) uses S2/S3 burst vs kite-heavy teams.

---

## Build A: AD Physical Bruiser (Recommended)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** or **Lightning Dagger (闪电匕首)** | Jungle: tank knife for HP. Clash Lane: Lightning Dagger for wave clear + AS. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. Swap to Shadow Boots vs full-AD teams. |
| 3 | **Shadow Axe (暗影战斧)** | Core Fighter item: CDR, Penetration, Health. |
| 4 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | S1 shield → AA perfectly triggers AoE slow + bonus damage. Enemies can't escape. |
| 5 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Taking hits grants MS + damage output. Perfect for extended brawls. |
| 6 | **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** or **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** | Pure Sky vs Physical burst. Eternal Night vs Magic burst. |

---

## Build B: AP Magic Poke (Situational)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | Calm Boots (冷静之靴) | CDR for S2 spam. |
| 2 | Echo Staff (回响之杖) | MS + burst on S2/S3 hits. |
| 3 | Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒) | Percentage AP spike. |
| 4 | Mask of Agony (痛苦面具) | S2 multi-hit triggers percentage burn. |
| 5 | Void Staff (虚无法杖) | Magic Penetration. |
| 6 | Sage's Tome (贤者之书) | Max AP for S3 one-shot potential. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

### Setup 1: "AS Brawler" (AD Standard)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×5 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🔴 Red | Red Moon (红月) | ×5 | Attack Speed +1.6%, Critical Rate +0.5% |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Hunt (狩猎) | ×7 | Attack Speed +1%, Move Speed +1% |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×3 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** ~15% Attack Speed, high Physical Penetration, +10% Movement Speed. Silky-smooth AA weaving at high Mass.

### Setup 2: "Dream Crit 100-Pen" (Pure Burst)

| Slot | Rune | Qty | Stats per Rune |
|------|------|-----|----------------|
| 🔴 Red | Mutation (异变) | ×9 | Physical Attack +2, Physical Pen +3.6 |
| 🔴 Red | Calamity (祸源) | ×1 | Critical Rate +1.6% ("one dream crit") |
| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |
| 🔵 Blue | Stealth (隐匿) | ×10 | Physical Attack +1.6, Move Speed +1% |

**Total:** +43 Physical Attack, +96 Physical Penetration, +1.6% Critical Rate, +10% Movement Speed. That one lucky crit at full Mass deletes health bars.

## Quick Tip

When rotating from base or between lanes, **stay at zero Mass** — you're one of the fastest Heroes. Only cast S1 to build Mass when you're about to fight.
