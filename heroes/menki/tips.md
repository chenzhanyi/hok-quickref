# Menki Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Mass Management: Small to Travel, Big to Fight

- **Rotating between lanes:** Stay at **zero Mass** — you're one of the fastest Heroes in the game. Never cast S1 while traveling.
- **About to fight:** Spam S1 to build Mass → large body + high dual defense + high Physical Attack. You effectively start fights with two extra items of stats.

### 2. S2 Boomerang Upgrade

S2 throws a bubble that returns. **Walk into the returning bubble** to catch it — it upgrades into an **execute bomb**. Recast S2 to throw the bomb for massive burst damage. This is your highest-damage ability.

### 3. S3: Engage AND Escape

S3's circular dash is **Slow-immune**. Two uses:
- **Engage:** Dash behind enemy backline → recast to knockup → S1 → AA → S2 execute
- **Escape:** When collapsed on, S3 toward your tower — the arc lets you bypass terrain and enemies

### 4. S1 Shield Spam = Immortality

S1 grants a shield + Mass on a very short cooldown. In extended fights: S1 → AA → S1 → AA. The constant shield refreshing makes you deceptively tanky.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S1 first, then S2. Take ultimate whenever available.** S1's short-CD shield is Menki's brawling core.

## Signature Combos

### Backline Dive: **S2 → S3 → S3 (recast) → S1 → AA → S2 (execute)**

1. S2 slow + stack Mass
2. S3 circular dash into enemy backline
3. S3 recast to knockup
4. S1 shield + AA
5. S2 execute bomb to finish

### Extended Brawl: **S1 → AA → S2 → AA → S1 → AA**

1. S1 shield → AA
2. S2 bubble → AA while it travels
3. Catch returning bubble (upgrade)
4. S1 shield again → AA
5. S2 execute bomb

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Full Mass at level 1 is absurdly strong.** You can 1v1 almost any laner if you start with S1 and keep Mass high.
- **S3 can bypass walls.** The circular arc lets you dash through terrain for creative ganks and escapes.
- **AP build is viable but situational.** Use vs kite-heavy teams where AD can't reach.
- **Matchup:** Destroys melee brawlers. Struggles vs long-range kiters who never let you close distance.
