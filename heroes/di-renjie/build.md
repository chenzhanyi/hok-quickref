# Di Renjie Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Two viable paths. **On-Hit Semi-Tank** (highest winrate — tanky, unkillable, anti-Assassin) and **Crit Burst** (max DPS, needs protection).

---

## Build A: On-Hit Semi-Tank (Recommended — Highest Winrate)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Berserker's Boots (急速战靴)** | Early AS for fast passive stacking. |
| 2 | **Lightning Dagger (闪电匕首)** | On-hit chain lightning. Blue/Red cards + passive AA spam trigger it constantly. |
| 3 | **Doomsday (末世)** | On-hit %HP damage. Perfect synergy with Di Renjie's high AS + card throws. |
| 4 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Defense + stacking MS/damage when hit. Makes Di Renjie a legitimate "Fighter." |
| 5 | **Blood Rage (血魔之怒)** or **Frost Assault (寒霜袭侵)** | **Blood Rage:** massive shield at low HP + bonus on-hit damage. **Frost Assault:** slow on-hit vs mobile teams. |
| 6 | **Magic Cloak (魔女斗篷)** | Magic defense vs burst Mages. |

## Arcana / Runes (铭文)

Core: **Attack Speed, Movement Speed, mild Lifesteal.**

| 🟢 Green | Eagle Eye (鹰眼) | ×10 | Physical Attack +0.9, Physical Pen +6.4 |

## Quick In-Game Setup

Di Renjie's AS thresholds significantly affect kiting feel. Copy the **CN #1 Di Renjie** setup via **Preparation → Recommended**. Choose runes based on build (On-Hit vs Crit).
