# Di Renjie Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Pre-Stack 5 Passive Before Every Fight

Passive grants 30% AS + 25% MS at 5 stacks. Before any fight: AA minions, monsters, or even air to reach 5 stacks. The MS is what lets you kite circles around enemies. Never start a fight at 0 stacks.

### 2. S2: Your Lifeline — Never Waste It

S2 cleanses ALL debuffs. Save it for: Li Yuanfang's S1 mark (cleanses before detonation), Daji charm, Wang Zhaojun freeze, Luna's mark, Dr Bian's poison, tower shots. **Never S2 for wave clear or poke damage.**

### 3. S3: Shred Their Tank — Then Everyone Focuses

S3 reduces target's dual defenses by **50%.** In teamfights: S3 the enemy frontline (tank/bruiser). Your entire team now deals near-true damage to them. The tank melts in seconds.

### 4. Blue/Red Card Bush Check

Every 3rd AA throws a special card. AA into suspicious bushes. If you see blue card's slow trail or hear the hit sound → enemies inside. Free, safe bush-check without abilities.

### 5. S3 → AA → S2 → AA → S1 → AA

The perfect duel combo: S3 (stun + shred) → AA → if enemy CC's you → S2 (cleanse) → AA → S1 (finish). Unbeatable in 1v1.

## Signature Combo

### Counter-Kill vs Assassin: **S3 → AA → S2 → AA → S1**

1. Assassin dives you
2. S3 their face (stun + 50% defense shred)
3. AA while kiting backward
4. They use CC → S2 cleanse immediately
5. Continue AA → S1 to finish

---

## Advanced Tips

- **S2 has a tiny invulnerability window.** With perfect timing, you can dodge tower shots and even Zhuge Liang S3.
- **On-Hit build is meta.** It makes Di Renjie tanky enough to survive Assassin dives while still dealing high DPS.
- **Matchup:** God-tier vs debuff-reliant Heroes (Li Yuanfang, Dr Bian). Struggles vs instant burst from stealth.
