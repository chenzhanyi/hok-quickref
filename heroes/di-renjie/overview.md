# Di Renjie（狄仁傑） — Marksman

## Overview

- **Role:** Marksman (Kiting King / Self-Cleanse ADC)
- **Lane:** Farm Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆
- **Title:** 斷案大師 (Master Detective)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 狄仁杰)

> Di Renjie is called the "true Fighter" among Marksmen. He has the best self-cleanse in the game (S2 removes ALL debuffs), mixed Physical/Magic damage, and a stacking passive that grants up to **30% AS + 25% MS**. His S3 shreds **50% of the target's dual defenses.** Two viable builds: On-Hit Semi-Tank (highest winrate) and Crit Burst.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Always Keep 5 Stacks

Each AA grants +6% AS +5% MS (max 5 stacks = **30% AS + 25% MS**). Always pre-stack on minions/air before fighting. The MS is his kiting engine.

### S2: Self-Cleanse (Never Waste)

S2 removes ALL debuffs: stuns, slows, Li Yuanfang's S1 mark, Dr Bian's poison, Luna's mark — everything. **Never use S2 for damage or wave clear.** Save it for enemy CC or when an Assassin dives you.

### S3: 50% Dual Defense Shred

S3 stuns AND **reduces target's Physical + Magic Defense by 50%.** Even a full-tank Zhang Fei becomes paper. Always S3 the enemy frontline before your team focuses them.

### Blue/Red Card: Bush Check

Every 3rd AA throws a blue (penetrate + slow) or red (AoE explode) card. AA bushes from range — if you see the slow effect or hear the hit sound, enemies are inside.
