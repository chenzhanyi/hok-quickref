# Dun Tips & Tricks

---

## Core Gameplay Tips

### 1. Passive: Never Retreat Below 50% HP

When HP drops below 50%, Dun enters lifesteal state for 8s: **every AA/ability hit restores 4.5% Max HP.** Don't run — fight! AA minions, monsters, or enemies. One wave can take you from 10% to 80% HP. This is the "medical miracle" that turns losing fights into reverse-kills.

### 2. S2 True Damage + S3 CDR

S2 grants a shield + 3 empowered AAs (True Damage). **Each empowered AA reduces S3 cooldown by 1 second.** Always S2 → AA×3 in fights — you'll have S3 back up almost immediately.

### 3. S3: Engage AND Escape

S3's dash is **CC-immune** during travel. Use to:
- **Engage:** S3 onto enemy backline (ignore their CC)
- **Escape:** S3 over walls when collapsed on
- **Outplay:** S3 through Daji's charm, Mozi's cannon

### 4. S1 Stage 2 Unlock

S1 stage 2 (AoE knockup) only unlocks if stage 1 hits something. Hit minions to unlock stage 2, then zone the enemy with the threat of the knockup.

### 5. Skill Priority

**Max S2 first, then S1. Take S3 whenever available.** S2's shield and True Damage are your laning and jungling foundation.

## Signature Combos

### Long-Range Engage: **S3 → S1 → AA → S2 → AA×3 → S1 (stage 2)**

1. S3 onto enemy (stun)
2. S1 stage 1 (slow)
3. AA
4. S2 (shield + True Damage)
5. AA×3 (True Damage + reduce S3 CD)
6. S1 stage 2 (AoE knockup)

### Bush Ambush: **S1 → AA → S2 → AA×3 → S1 (stage 2) → S3**

1. S1 from bush (slow)
2. AA → S2 shield + True Damage AAs
3. S1 stage 2 knockup
4. S3 to chase or finish

---

## Advanced Tips

- **Passive + Eternal Night = absurd healing.** The two effects stack, making Dun nearly unkillable at low HP.
- **S3 crosses walls.** Use it to escape ganks or steal objectives.
- **Tank Jungle Dun is meta in pro play.** Giant's Grip + Blazing Cape dual-burn clears camps absurdly fast.
- **Matchup:** Excels in extended brawls. Struggles vs burst that kills before passive triggers.
