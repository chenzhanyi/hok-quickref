# Dun Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Two viable paths: **Clash Lane Flash** (bruiser) and **Jungle Smite** (tank — currently meta in pro play). Both leverage his passive + S2 True Damage.

---

## Build A: Clash Lane Flash Bruiser

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity + magic defense. Keeps Dun mobile during engages. |
| 2 | **Blazing Cape (红莲斗篷)** | Physical Defense + Max HP. Burn aura compensates for Dun's slow wave clear. |
| 3 | **Frost Grip (冰痕之握)** | S2's 3 empowered AAs perfectly trigger AoE slow + bonus damage. Maximum stickiness. |
| 4 | **Berserker's Armor (暴烈之甲)** | Taking hits grants stacking MS + damage output. Dun gets stronger as fights go on. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** | Burst-heal on magic damage. Stacks with passive lifesteal — absurd low-HP survival. |
| 6 | **Blood Rage (血魔之怒)** or **Pure Sky (纯净苍穹)** | **Blood Rage:** massive shield at low HP + bonus on-hit. Perfect synergy with passive. **Pure Sky:** active DR for teamfight dives. |

---

## Build B: Jungle Tank Smite (Pro Meta)

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Giant's Grip (巨人之握)** | HP stacking + burn aura. |
| 2 | **Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴)** | Tenacity. |
| 3 | **Blazing Cape (红莲斗篷)** | Double burn with Giant's Grip = absurdly fast jungle clear. |
| 4 | **Lightning Dagger (闪电匕首)** | Attack Speed for faster S2 True Damage procs + passive lifesteal trigger rate. |
| 5 | **Eternal Night (永夜守护)** | Magic defense + burst heal. |
| 6 | **Ominous Premonition (不祥征兆)** | Physical Defense + anti-ADC passive. |
