# Dun（夏侯惇） — Tank / Fighter

## Overview

- **Role:** Tank / Fighter (All-Rounder Bruiser)
- **Lane:** Clash Lane / Jungle
- **Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆
- **Title:** 不羈之風 (Unruly Wind)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 夏侯惇)

> Dun is an extremely versatile, ultra-tanky Clash Lane Fighter/Jungler. He has everything: CC, mobility, healing, shields, and True Damage. He's often called the "perfect Hero" in the CN server for his complete kit. His signature mechanic is his **passive lifesteal** — when HP drops below 50%, his AAs and abilities restore massive HP, letting him reverse-kill from seemingly certain death.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Passive: Vampiric Regeneration (Reverse-Kill Engine)

When HP drops **below 50%**, Dun enters a state where **every AA and ability hit restores 4.5% of his Max HP** for 8 seconds. Never retreat when this triggers — fight minions, monsters, or enemies and watch your HP refill. This is the "medical miracle" mechanic.

### S2: True Damage + Shield + CDR

S2 grants a **12% Max HP shield** and empowers the next 3 AAs with **True Damage**. Each empowered AA also **reduces S3 cooldown by 1 second**. S2 is your bread and butter — max it first.

### S3: Pseudo-Unstoppable Engage

S3 throws a chain blade and dashes to the target. **During the dash, Dun is CC-immune.** Stuns enemies at the impact point. Also crosses walls — use for engage AND escape.

### S1: Two-Stage Knockup

S1's first hit must connect (Hero, Minion, or Monster) to unlock the second stage — a wide AoE knockup. Poke minions to unlock stage 2, then threaten the enemy laner with the knockup.
