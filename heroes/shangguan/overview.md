# Shangguan（上官婉兒） — Assassin / Mage

## Overview

- **Role:** Assassin / Mage (Dive Burst)
- **Lane:** Mid Lane
- **Difficulty:** ★★★★★
- **Title:** 驚鴻之筆 (Startling Brushstroke)
- **Region:** Global (CN server name: 上官婉儿)

> Shangguan is an extremely high-skill-cap Magic Assassin. Her core mechanics are **Ink Collision** (S1 + S2 intersecting explosions) and her ultimate's **5-dash unlock → invincible aerial barrage**. Many beginners think she just needs to go airborne — but the real burst comes from ground-level ink collision explosions.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Ink Collision (True Burst Source)

When S1 and S2 intersect, or when Shangguan dashes (S3) over ink, they trigger an **explosion that restores HP**. Mastering ground-level collisions is what separates good Shangguans from button-mashers.

### Enhanced Auto-Attack (Early Laning)

Every third auto-attack becomes a penetrating line shot. Use this for efficient wave clear and lane poke before level 4.

### S3 "Brake" (Pause Mid-Dash)

During S3's dash sequence, **release the left joystick** — the dash timer **pauses (up to 3 seconds)**. Use this gap to: weave auto-attacks, wait for S1/S2 cooldowns, or reposition.

### S3 Collision Damage

The first 4 dashes deal massive damage if they **pass through enemy Heroes**. Don't just dash for distance — dash THROUGH targets.
