# Shangguan Build & Items

> **Build philosophy:** Dive-backline burst Assassin. **Max Magic Penetration + high Magic Attack.** Latest meta: one defensive item to survive post-landing. CN server consensus: **penetration burst with Stasis survival**.

---

## Recommended Burst Build

| Order | Item | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Calm Boots (冷静之靴)** | Early S3 cooldown reduction for frequent ganks and tower dives. *Late game: swap to Resistance Boots (抵抗之靴) to avoid getting CC-cancelled during ground dashes.* |
| 2 | **Echo Staff (回响之杖)** | Movement Speed for roaming + explosion on ability hit. Stacks perfectly with ink collision burst. |
| 3 | **Savant's Wrath (博学者之怒)** | Percentage Magic Attack steroid. After this, one full combo deletes squishies from 100% HP. |
| 4 | **Void Staff (虚无法杖)** | 45% Magic Penetration. Mandatory mid-late game vs Magic Cloak and magic resist stacking. |
| 5 | **Sunset Flow (日暮之流)** or **Devourer's Tome (噬神之书)** | **Sunset Flow:** S3 multi-hit fully stacks penetration instantly vs tanky teams. **Devourer's Tome:** Lifesteal for sustained map pressure and wave clear. |
| 6 | **Splendor (辉月)** | **Ultimate survival tool.** Stasis on landing = you're not a free kill. During Stasis: sell Splendor → buy Sage's Sanctuary or Fate's Edge in the shop. |
